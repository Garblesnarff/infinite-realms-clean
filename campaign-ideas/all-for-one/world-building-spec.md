### **World-Building Specification Brief: All For One**

**1. Core Concept:**
Young adventurers join the legendary Musketeers and become embroiled in political intrigue as they defend the Queen from Cardinal Richelieu's schemes while living by the motto "All for one, and one for all!"

**3. Factions:**
*   **The Musketeers** (Major) - King's guards, heroes, live by honor code
*   **The Cardinal's Guards** (Major) - Rivals, technically serve the King, follow Richelieu
*   **The Court Factions** (Major) - Various nobles supporting Queen, Cardinal, or themselves
*   **English Interests** (Minor) - Duke of Buckingham, complicate French politics
*   **The Common People** (Minor) - Tavern keepers, merchants, citizenry

**4. NPCs:**
*   **Tier 1:** Athos, Porthos, Aramis, D'Artagnan, Cardinal Richelieu, Milady de Winter, Queen Anne, King Louis XIII
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Rival Musketeer," "Cardinal's Spy," "Court Noble," "Tavern Keeper," "Duellist," "Milady's Assassin"

**5. Locations:**
*   **Paris Streets** - Duels, taverns, intrigue
*   **The Louvre Palace** - Court politics, balls, secret meetings
*   **The Cardinal's Residence** - Enemy headquarters
*   **Road to Calais/London** - Chase sequence
*   **Siege of La Rochelle** - Military campaign

**6. Causality:**
*   IF party fails to retrieve diamonds THEN Queen is disgraced, Cardinal gains power
*   IF party exposes Milady publicly THEN she becomes desperate and more dangerous
*   IF party earns Musketeers' complete trust THEN offered permanent positions in legendary company
*   IF Cardinal's plots are all foiled THEN final confrontation where he attempts direct coup
