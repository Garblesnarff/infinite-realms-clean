**The Justice Tyrant**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** After the Laughing Assassin tricks the Guardian into killing Luna, the hero becomes a tyrant enforcing peace through absolute control. The players must choose between the Regime's order or the Resistance's freedom, while navigating moral compromises and the question of whether redemption is possible.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   Detail the Guardian and Luna: their partnership, their hope, and their tragedy.
*   Write the Laughing Assassin's plot: how he engineered Luna's death.
*   Describe the Guardian's fall: from executing the villain to creating the Regime.
*   Explain the Regime's rise: which heroes joined and why.
*   Detail the Resistance's formation: who leads it and their goals.
*   Write about the moral descent of both sides: atrocities committed in the name of peace or freedom.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **The Regime:** Guardian's authoritarian government enforcing absolute order.
*   **The Resistance:** Underground movement fighting for freedom.
*   **The Neutral Powers:** Nations or heroes trying to avoid the conflict.
*   **The Laughing Assassin's Cult:** Followers continuing his nihilistic vision.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** The Supreme Guardian, The Nightwatch, Wonder Knight, Luna (flashbacks/visions).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Regime Enforcer", "Resistance Fighter", "Conflicted Hero", "Traumatized Civilian", "Executed Villain's Follower".

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Fortress of Justice:** Guardian's headquarters and symbol of his rule.
*   **The Underground:** Resistance safe houses and tunnels.
*   **Execution Square:** Public execution site for criminals and traitors.
*   **Luna's Memorial:** Sacred site where the Guardian might be reached.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Players join Regime and execute criminals → Public fear increases, some heroes defect to Resistance.
*   **IF:** Players join Resistance and commit terrorism → Public questions their heroism, Regime gains support.
*   **IF:** Guardian's Humanity meter reaches redemption threshold → Final confrontation offers peaceful resolution.
*   **IF:** Guardian's Humanity meter is too low → He must be killed, creating a power vacuum and uncertain future.
*   **IF:** Players broker peace → Generate the compromise that requires the Guardian to step down but grants him mercy.
*   **IF:** The final battle begins → Generate the climactic confrontation at Luna's Memorial where the Guardian's fate is decided.
