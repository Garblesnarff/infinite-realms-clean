### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

**Gearless**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In a dystopian future where boxers fight wearing powered exoskeletons called "Gear," the protagonist is a nameless underground fighter from the slums who takes the name "Joe" and challenges the system by fighting Gearless—with only fists and spirit. Sponsored by a washed-up former trainer, they enter the Megalonia tournament where elite compete for the championship. Their journey from fixed underground matches to the highest stage becomes a statement against class divide—proving raw human spirit can overcome enhanced technology and corporate-backed champions.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the emergence of Gear technology in boxing and how it changed the sport.
    *   Write about the class divide—slum city controlled by yakuza vs corporate elite districts.
    *   Describe Megalonia tournament history and how it crowns the world's greatest Megaloboxer.
    *   Explain Yuri's rise to champion and his philosophy of perfect technique with perfect technology.
    *   Detail underground boxing's role in slum economy and organized crime control.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Slum Underground** (Major): Illegal fighting rings controlled by yakuza, where Joe started.
    *   **Corporate Boxing League** (Major): Sanctioned Megalobox with corporate sponsors and media coverage.
    *   **Fujimaki Corporation** (Major): Powerful sponsor controlling multiple top fighters and Gear development.
    *   **Independent Fighters** (Minor): Those like Joe who fight without corporate backing.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Nanbu Gansaku (coach), Yuri (champion), Fujimaki (corporate boss), Sachio (street kid).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Corporate Fighter" (sponsored boxers with resources), "Underground Fighter" (illegal circuit competitors), "Yakuza Boss" (organized crime control), "Former Champion" (washed-up legends).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Slum City:** Lower district with industrial decay, neon-lit streets, poverty, crime, underground fighting rings.
*   **Underground Arena:** Illegal boxing venue in warehouse, betting crowds, yakuza presence, no rules.
*   **Joe's Gym:** Barely-equipped training facility, aging equipment, poverty but determination.
*   **Megalonia Stadium:** State-of-the-art championship arena, corporate luxury boxes, massive LED screens, represents the pinnacle.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Joe wins Gearless → **THEN:** Becomes symbol to working class, inspires others, threatens corporate control of sport.
*   **IF:** Accumulated injury reaches critical → **THEN:** Must choose between continuing Gearless or accepting defeat.
*   **IF:** Defeats corporate-sponsored fighter → **THEN:** Corporation targets them, tries to buy or break them.
*   **IF:** Reaches Megalonia finals → **THEN:** Face Yuri in ultimate test of human vs enhanced perfection.
*   **IF:** Joe's style inspires others → **THEN:** More fighters try Gearless, beginning movement against mandatory enhancement.
