# The Spice Wars - Creative Brief

## Art Style and Visual Tone
- **Primary Style**: Epic desert fantasy-sci-fi
- **Arrakis**: Endless dunes, harsh sunlight, blue-within-blue spice eyes
- **Fremen Culture**: Stillsuits, desert robes, tribal aesthetics
- **Imperial Pomp**: Ornate architecture, ceremonial dress, baroque excess
- **Harkonnen Brutality**: Industrial nightmare, oil and darkness

## Color Palettes
- **Arrakis**: Orange desert, blue sky, spice-tinted air
- **Atreides**: Green and black, noble colors
- **Harkonnen**: Red and black, blood and oil
- **Fremen**: Earth tones, blue eyes, practical colors
- **Empire**: Gold and purple, imperial majesty

## Music and Sound Design
- **Main Theme**: Epic orchestral with Middle-Eastern influences
- **Desert**: Haunting vocals, wind instruments
- **Combat**: Heavy percussion, war horns
- **Spice Vision**: Ethereal, reality-bending sounds
- **Sandworm**: Deep bass rumble building to roar

## Cinematic Moments
- **Arrival on Arrakis**: First sight of endless desert
- **Sandworm Riding**: Mounting and riding Shai-Hulud
- **Betrayal Night**: Harkonnen attack, Duke's death
- **Water of Life**: Psychedelic vision sequence
- **Battle of Arrakeen**: Desert warfare at epic scale
- **Duel for Empire**: One-on-one combat with galactic stakes

## Artistic Inspirations
- Dune (2021 film - Denis Villeneuve)
- Lawrence of Arabia (desert cinematography)
- Game of Thrones (political intrigue)
- Apocalypse Now (descent into extremism)
