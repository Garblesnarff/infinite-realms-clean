# Neural Drift: Creative Brief

## Art Style: Psychic Horror

**Drift Visualization:** Overlapping consciousnesses, translucent minds merging, beautiful and invasive

**Sentinels:** Biomechanical, neural interfaces visible, looking partially alive, disturbing organic-tech fusion

**Deep Drift Space:** Surrealist mindscape, Escher-like impossibility, memories as physical locations

**Kaiju:** Ethereal, psychic energy more than flesh, reality-distorting presence

## Colors

**Primary:** Neural Purple (#9370DB), Drift Blue (#4169E1), Warning Red (#DC143C), Medical White (#F0F0F0)

**Psychic Space:** Shifting iridescent, impossible colors, synesthesia visualization

**Burnout:** Brain Gray (#696969), Void Black (#000000)

## Sound

**Drift Sequence:** Overlapping thoughts, whispers building, heartbeats synchronizing, white noise to harmony

**Deep Drift:** Ambient psychic hum, distance voices, memory echoes, unsettling beauty

**Burnout:** High-pitch ringing, thoughts fragmenting, silence then screaming

## Cinematic Moments

**First Drift:** Minds touching, overwhelming intimacy, seeing through partner's eyes, losing boundary

**Memory Bleed:** Partner's past becoming yours, confusion about which memories are real

**Full Conversion:** Individual dissolving into collective, peaceful horror, smile as self dies
