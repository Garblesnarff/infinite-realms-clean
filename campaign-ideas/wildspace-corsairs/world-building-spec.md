**Wildspace Corsairs**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party inherits a spelljammer ship—magical vessels sailing between worlds—and becomes space corsairs. They navigate Wildspace, battle alien threats, explore exotic planets, and seek the legendary Spelljammer while opposing the Arcane organization's monopoly on spelljamming knowledge. Age of sail adventure in infinite space.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the nature of crystal spheres—enclosed solar systems with impenetrable shells.
    *   Write the history of spelljamming magic and how ancient civilizations traveled stars.
    *   Describe the Arcane organization and how they monopolize spelljamming knowledge.
    *   Explain the Phlogiston—the rainbow flow between crystal spheres where fire is deadly.
    *   Detail the legend of the Spelljammer—massive sentient ship seeking worthy crew.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Arcane** (Major): Monopolistic organization controlling spelljamming magic through licensing and force.
    *   **Neogi Slaver Fleets** (Major): Evil eel merchants trafficking in sentient beings across spheres.
    *   **Mind Flayer Nautiloids** (Major): Illithid ships hunting for brain-eating victims.
    *   **Rock of Bral Merchants** (Minor): Neutral traders maintaining asteroid city as safe port.
    *   **Giff Mercenaries** (Minor): Hippo warriors hiring out as ship security.
    *   **Independent Corsairs** (Minor): Free captains refusing Arcane control.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Prince Andru (Bral governor), Captain Teldin Moore (legendary explorer), Arcane Triumvirate (monopolist leaders), Sseth'ik (neogi slaver), Spelljammer's Voice (ship avatar).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Giff Gunner" (hippo mercenary), "Hadozee Rigger" (flying sailor), "Plasmoid Crew" (ooze person), "Pirate Captain" (space raider), "Arcane Agent" (enforcer).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Rock of Bral:** Massive asteroid city serving as neutral port for all space-farers.
*   **The Party's Ship:** Customizable spelljammer vessel with crew quarters and upgrades.
*   **Exotic Planets:** Fire worlds, water spheres, garden paradises, each with unique civilizations.
*   **The Phlogiston:** Rainbow flow between spheres, flammable and dangerous.
*   **The Spelljammer:** City-sized living manta ray ship, legendary and sentient.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party operates without Arcane license → **THEN:** Harassment escalates to open combat.
*   **IF:** Ship is damaged → **THEN:** Must repair at port, vulnerability to ambush.
*   **IF:** Crew morale drops → **THEN:** Mutiny risk, poor performance in combat.
*   **IF:** Party spreads spelljamming knowledge → **THEN:** Arcane monopoly breaks, new age of exploration begins.
*   **IF:** Party claims the Spelljammer → **THEN:** Becomes target of every major power in Wildspace.
*   **IF:** Air envelope is breached → **THEN:** Suffocation hazard, emergency protocols needed.
