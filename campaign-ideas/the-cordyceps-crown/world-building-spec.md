**The Cordyceps Crown**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Twenty years after a mutant Cordyceps fungus turned most of humanity into violent infected, the party are smugglers hired by the Fireflies to escort immune teenager Ellie across the ruined country. What starts as a simple job becomes a surrogate parent-child relationship, culminating in an impossible choice: let the Fireflies kill Ellie to maybe save humanity, or doom the world to save one girl you've come to love.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Cordyceps outbreak—how the fungus mutated to infect humans.
    *   Write the history of the first weeks—Outbreak Day, military response, quarantine zones.
    *   Describe the fall of civilization and the formation of FEDRA (military dictatorship).
    *   Explain the Fireflies—rebel group seeking to restore democracy and find a cure.
    *   Detail the infection stages—Runner, Stalker, Clicker, Bloater (years of progression).

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **FEDRA** (Major): Military dictatorship controlling Quarantine Zones, oppressive but maintains order.
    *   **The Fireflies** (Major): Rebel group seeking democracy and a cure, increasingly desperate and ruthless.
    *   **Hunters** (Minor): Bandits who ambush travelers, cannibalize or enslave victims.
    *   **Tommy's Settlement** (Minor): Safe community in Wyoming trying to rebuild civilization properly.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Ellie (immune girl), Marlene (Firefly leader), David (cannibal preacher), Tess (partner who dies).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Runner" (early infected), "Clicker" (blind echolocator), "Bloater" (tank infected), "Hunter" (desperate bandit), "FEDRA Soldier" (QZ military).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Boston QZ:** Walled city section, military checkpoints, ration lines, smuggling tunnels, hanging bodies.
*   **The Outskirts:** Overgrown Boston ruins, Cordyceps growth everywhere, abandoned subway tunnels.
*   **Pittsburgh:** Hunter-controlled ambush city, skyscraper battles, sewer traversal, trapped streets.
*   **University of Eastern Colorado:** Abandoned Firefly lab, evidence of research, bandit ambush.
*   **Lakeside Resort:** Winter cabins in Colorado, hunting grounds, nearby cannibal camp.
*   **St. Mary's Hospital (Salt Lake):** Firefly headquarters, operating rooms, the site of final choice.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party bonds deeply with Ellie → **THEN:** The final choice to save her feels inevitable, moral weight increases.
*   **IF:** Party is brutal to enemies → **THEN:** Ellie becomes more hardened, accepts violence easier.
*   **IF:** Party shows mercy when possible → **THEN:** Ellie retains more hope, struggles with necessary violence.
*   **IF:** Party chooses to save Ellie → **THEN:** Massacre Fireflies, doom humanity's cure, lie to Ellie.
*   **IF:** Party chooses humanity → **THEN:** Let Ellie die, but cure may not even work, lose found family.
*   **IF:** Ellie survives → **THEN:** She knows party lied but chooses to accept it because she chose them too.
