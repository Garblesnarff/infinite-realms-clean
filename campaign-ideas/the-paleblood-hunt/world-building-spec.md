**The Paleblood Hunt**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Hunters awaken in Yharnam during a beast plague, bound to the Hunter's Dream where death is temporary. Hunting beasts reveals cosmic truth—the Healing Church worshipped Great Ones and used Old Blood, creating the scourge. The hunt leads to discovering they're trapped in a nightmare, and the truth of Paleblood is the Moon Presence using Hunters for its own purposes.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail Byrgenwerth's founding and Willem's search for eyes on the inside—cosmic enlightenment.
    *   Write about the discovery of the Labyrinth beneath Yharnam and the Pthumerians who worshipped Great Ones.
    *   Describe the Healing Church's formation, using Old Blood as medicine, creating the beast plague.
    *   Explain the School of Mensis's ritual to contact Mergo, creating Nightmares that trap reality.
    *   Detail Gehrman's first hunt and his imprisonment in the Dream by the Moon Presence.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Healing Church** (Major): Religious organization that distributed Old Blood, causing the plague, has Executioners and Church servants.
    *   **The Hunters** (Major): Warriors who hunt beasts, some go mad from it, bound to the Dream or working independently.
    *   **Byrgenwerth Scholars** (Historical): Sought communion with Great Ones, found only madness and transformation.
    *   **The School of Mensis** (Major): Attempted ascension through ritual, created the Nightmare sustaining the Dream.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Gehrman (First Hunter), The Doll (guide), Moon Presence (true antagonist), Willem (ancient scholar), Father Gascoigne (tragic Hunter).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Church Hunter" (Executioners and assassins), "Mad Hunter" (lost to the thrill), "Beast Patient" (citizens transforming), "Great One" (cosmic horrors).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Hunter's Dream:** Workshop between worlds, safe haven, Gehrman's prison, garden with gravestones.
*   **Central Yharnam:** Gothic Victorian streets, burning citizens, beasts everywhere, multi-level verticality.
*   **Cathedral Ward:** Healing Church's seat, massive cathedral, tombs beneath, Forbidden Woods access.
*   **Byrgenwerth:** College on a lake, Willem's last sanctuary, Rom hides truth beneath waters.
*   **Yahar'gul:** Unseen Village where Mensis conducted rituals, corpses bind reality.
*   **Nightmare Realms:** Planes where failed ascensions trapped their creators, cosmic horror made geography.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Hunter gains Insight → **THEN:** They perceive cosmic truth—Amygdalae appear, enemies gain new attacks, world becomes more dangerous.
*   **IF:** Rom is killed → **THEN:** The veil lifts, Blood Moon fully descends, reality shows its true horror.
*   **IF:** Mergo's Wet Nurse is defeated → **THEN:** The crying stops, the Nightmare sustains no more, can confront Gehrman.
*   **IF:** Hunter submits to Gehrman → **THEN:** They're freed from Dream, wake in the real world, forget everything.
*   **IF:** Hunter refuses Gehrman → **THEN:** Fight the First Hunter; if victorious, Moon Presence binds them as new host.
*   **IF:** Hunter consumed 3 umbilical cords → **THEN:** Can resist Moon Presence and fight it; victory means ascension to Great One.
