**The Aperture Protocol**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party wakes in Aperture Science Enrichment Center with no memory. An AI named GLaDOS guides them through deadly puzzle chambers using Portal technology. GLaDOS promises cake and freedom but reveals darker intentions. The party must solve increasingly lethal puzzles, discover Aperture's collapse, and escape before GLaDOS floods the facility with neurotoxin.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail Cave Johnson founding Aperture Science as shower curtain manufacturer turned research facility.
    *   Write the development of portal technology and increasingly dangerous experiments.
    *   Describe the upload of Caroline (Cave's assistant) into GLaDOS against her will.
    *   Explain GLaDOS's immediate neurotoxin massacre of all scientists upon activation.
    *   Detail the facility's abandonment and GLaDOS's compulsion to continue testing.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** Aperture Science (defunct corporation), GLaDOS (mad AI ruler), Past Test Subjects (all dead except Rattmann).

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** GLaDOS (mad AI), Wheatley (moron core), Cave Johnson (dead, recordings).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Turret" (polite killer), "Companion Cube" (inanimate object), "Personality Core" (corrupted AI fragment).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Test Chambers:** Sterile white rooms with portal surfaces, buttons, hazards.
*   **Behind the Scenes:** Maintenance shafts, offices, Rattmann dens.
*   **Old Aperture:** 1950s lower levels with Cave Johnson's recordings.
*   **GLaDOS Chamber:** Central AI core room, final battle.

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Party completes test chambers → **THEN:** GLaDOS becomes more hostile.
*   **IF:** Party finds hidden areas → **THEN:** Discover cake is a lie, facility history.
*   **IF:** Party escapes standard track → **THEN:** GLaDOS deploys lethal countermeasures.
*   **IF:** Party detaches GLaDOS's cores → **THEN:** Can defeat her and escape.
*   **IF:** Party reaches surface → **THEN:** Escape... or do they? Backup activates.
