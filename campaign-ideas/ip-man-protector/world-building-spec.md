### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

**Ip Man: Protector of the Art**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The Jade Empire has been invaded by the Iron Shogunate, and the city of Foshan is under military occupation. The party are masters or students of the Wing Chun school who must preserve Chinese martial arts' dignity and honor while protecting their community from oppression, ultimately standing up to General Miura in a contest that will determine the fate of their school and culture.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the history of Wing Chun and its philosophy of economy of motion and simultaneous defense/attack.
    *   Write the story of the Iron Shogunate's invasion and their policy regarding occupied territories' martial arts.
    *   Describe Foshan before and after occupation—from prosperous martial arts hub to oppressed city.
    *   Explain General Miura's background as a martial artist who respects the arts but serves an oppressive regime.
    *   Detail the cultural significance of Chinese martial arts as identity and resistance.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Wing Chun School (The Players)** - Goal: Preserve martial arts and protect community. Assets: Traditional knowledge, community support, Master Ip's leadership.
    *   **The Iron Shogunate** - Goal: Cultural subjugation and military control. Assets: Military might, legal authority, resources.
    *   **The Underground Resistance** - Goal: Help masters escape and preserve knowledge. Assets: Secret networks, smuggling routes.
    *   **The Collaborators** - Goal: Survive by serving occupiers. Assets: Favor with Shogunate, knowledge of resistance.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Master Ip, General Miura, Colonel Sato, Li Zhao (collaborator), Master Liu.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Shogunate soldier," "Coal mine worker," "Underground resistance member," "Chinese master in hiding," "Exhibition match fighter."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Wing Chun School:** Closed dojo where secret training happens at night.
        *   Landmarks: Wooden dummy courtyard, Master Ip's office, secret basement training room.
        *   Encounters: Surprise inspections, midnight training sessions, meeting resistance contacts.
        *   Plot Hooks: "The wooden dummies contain hidden messages," "A student left evidence that could expose everyone."
    *   **The Exhibition Hall:** Where humiliating matches occur.
        *   Landmarks: The ring, observation box for Shogunate officers, holding cells.
        *   Encounters: Fighting for survival, witnessing brutality, Sato's challenges.
        *   Plot Hooks: "Winners are sometimes recruited by Miura," "Fight records document all Chinese techniques."
    *   **The Final Duel Ground:** Formal arena for Ip vs Miura confrontation.
        *   Landmarks: Traditional combat platform, witness areas, ceremonial gates.
        *   Encounters: The climactic duel, philosophical debate, potential ambush if party loses.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** Master Ip defeats Miura with honor → Schools reopen, culture preserved.
    *   **IF:** The party kills Shogunate officers before the duel → Massacre of civilians, immediate escalation.
    *   **IF:** Li Zhao is redeemed → He provides intelligence that saves lives.
    *   **IF:** The party trains the next generation successfully → Young masters carry on the tradition regardless of the duel's outcome.
    *   **IF:** Sato is defeated humiliatingly → Miura becomes more personal in his opposition, seeing it as defending his nation's honor.
