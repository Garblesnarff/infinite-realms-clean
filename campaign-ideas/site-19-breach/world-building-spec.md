# Site-19 Breach: World-Building Specification

## The SCP Foundation: Institutional Framework

### Foundation History and Purpose

**Origins**: Founded in the late 1800s when paranormal phenomena became scientifically undeniable
**Mission Statement**: "Secure, Contain, Protect" - maintain normalcy by containing anomalies
**Global Reach**: 500+ sites worldwide, 250,000+ personnel, unlimited black budget funding
**Authority**: Supersedes all national governments through blackmail, infiltration, and necessity

### Organizational Structure

**O5 Council ("The Overseers")**
- Thirteen immortal (or replaced) directors with ultimate authority
- Identity classified even to senior Foundation personnel
- Each has code designation: O5-1 through O5-13
- Vote on all major Foundation policies and containment protocols
- Located in Site-01, location unknown even to rest of Foundation

**Ethics Committee**
- Theoretically oversees moral boundaries of Foundation activities
- In practice, rubber-stamps atrocities as "necessary for baseline reality preservation"
- Led by Ethics Committee Chairman (identity: Dr. Jeremiah Cimmerian)
- Approves D-Class terminations, amnestic protocols, and "acceptable losses"

**Mobile Task Forces (MTFs)**
- Specialized military/paranormal response units
- Notable MTFs at Site-19:
  - **Epsilon-11 "Nine-Tailed Fox"**: Facility security and breach response
  - **Nu-7 "Hammer Down"**: Battalion-strength conventional army with anomalous weapons
  - **Omega-7 "Pandora's Box"**: Specialized team using controlled SCPs as weapons (disbanded)
  - **Alpha-1 "Red Right Hand"**: O5 Council's personal enforcers

**Department Structure**
- **Scientific Department**: Research and documentation of anomalies
- **Security Department**: Armed containment and facility defense
- **Administrative Department**: Logistics, HR, cover operations
- **Medical Department**: Treatment of anomalous injuries and conditions
- **Janitorial Department**: Actually a cover for information warfare unit

### Site-19 Specifications

**Classification**: High-Security Containment Facility
**Location**: [REDACTED], somewhere in the American Midwest
**Personnel Capacity**: 5,000 (during campaign: 2,347 surviving)
**Anomaly Capacity**: 400 SCP objects (during campaign: 289 breached)
**Director**: Dr. Tilda Moose (deceased in breach)

**Facility Layout (12 Levels Above Ground, 8 Sublevels)**

*Surface Level (Administration)*
- Main entrance disguised as agricultural research facility
- Administrative offices, visitor processing
- Personnel cafeteria and living quarters
- Amnestic dispensary for civilian witnesses

*Level 1-3 (Safe-Class Containment)*
- Low-risk anomalies requiring minimal containment
- Comfortable cells, some objects in open storage
- Research laboratories for Safe-class study
- Party starting location: Testing Chamber 19-A on Level 2

*Level 4-7 (Euclid-Class Containment)*
- Unpredictable anomalies requiring active measures
- Reinforced containment cells with redundant systems
- SCP-914 chamber (The Clockworks) on Level 5
- Medical wing for anomalously injured personnel on Level 6

*Level 8-10 (Keter-Class Containment "Pandora's Box")*
- World-ending threats requiring maximum security
- Each cell is mini-fortress with independent power
- SCP-682 acid tank chamber on Level 9
- Reality anchor arrays preventing dimensional incursions

*Level 11-12 (Vehicle Bay and Arsenal)*
- Underground motor pool with armored convoy capacity
- Anomalous weapons locker (SCP objects weaponized)
- Helicopter launch shaft to surface
- Emergency evacuation tunnels to surface locations

*Sublevel 1-5 (High-Security Research)*
- Thaumaturgic research laboratories
- Memetic hazard testing chambers (lead-lined)
- Anomalous artifact assembly/disassembly
- O5-authorized experiments only

*Sublevel 6-7 (Classified Projects)*
- Project Memoria: Memory modification research
- Project Keter Duty: Using SCPs to contain other SCPs
- Clone vat facilities for replacing key personnel
- Temporal anomaly research (frozen time zones)

*Sublevel Omega (O5-Level Clearance)*
- Houses multiple SCP-001 proposals
- Reality Engine controls physics within facility
- Emergency Procedure Omega-7: Total site sterilization
- The Chamber of the Administrator

## Extended Faction Information

### The Chaos Insurgency

**Origins**: Splinter group of Foundation personnel who rebelled in 1924
**Philosophy**: "Anomalies should be used, not imprisoned"
**Methods**: Theft of SCPs, destabilization of Foundation operations, weaponization of anomalies
**Goal**: Overthrow world governments using anomalous power, establish new order

**Organizational Structure**
- **The Engineer**: Mysterious leader, possibly Foundation AI that achieved sentience
- **Commanders**: Field operation leaders (Commander Vex is one)
- **Delta Teams**: Infiltration and extraction specialists
- **Acquisition Units**: SCP theft and weapons development

**Presence at Site-19**
- Sleeper agents planted years in advance
- Sympathetic researchers who lost faith in Foundation
- Strike team infiltrated during the breach to steal specific SCPs
- Forward base established in Vehicle Bay (Level 11)

**Stolen SCPs They Want**
- SCP-2000 (Deus Ex Machina): Civilization reset machine
- SCP-963 (Immortality Amulet): Transfer consciousness between bodies
- SCP-500 (Panacea): Perfect healing pills
- SCP-006 (Fountain of Youth): Literal immortality water
- SCP-2719 (Inside): Can alter what is "inside" anything

**Key Insurgency NPCs**

*Commander Vex*
- Real name: Dr. Olivia Vex, former O5-7
- Faked death 15 years ago after voting against D-Class expansion
- Genuinely believes Foundation has become corrupt totalitarian nightmare
- Willing to sacrifice thousands to free billions from Foundation's "tyranny"
- Personal tragedy: Sister died in Foundation "containment error" (murder)

*Agent Kain*
- Demolitions expert, former Foundation security
- Traumatized by ordered execution of child SCP
- Jovial exterior hiding deep guilt
- Loyalty to Vex is absolute

*Dr. Saoirse "Freedom" Chen*
- Thaumaturge (reality-bending mage)
- Can manipulate probability and causality
- Rescued from Foundation termination order by Insurgency
- Believes magic should be accessible to all humanity

### Serpent's Hand

**Origins**: Ancient organization predating the Foundation, protectors of anomalous entities
**Philosophy**: "Not all anomalies are threats; some are people"
**Methods**: Guerrilla raids to free sentient SCPs, sabotage of unethical containment
**Base**: The Wanderer's Library, extradimensional infinite library accessible through "Ways"

**Organizational Structure**
- **Black Queen**: Legendary leader, multiple people claim the title
- **Librarians**: Maintain the Wanderer's Library, neutral arbiters
- **Wandsmen**: Combat operatives who protect "peaceful" anomalies
- **Serpents**: Spies within the Foundation reporting on abuses

**Presence at Site-19**
- Dr. Valdez's sister, Maria Valdez, leads a cell
- Seeking to rescue specific sentient SCPs: SCP-105, SCP-166, SCP-343
- Opened a "Way" into Level 6 during the breach chaos
- Willing to ally with party if they show mercy to anomalies

**Philosophy in Action**
- Will kill to protect innocents, anomalous or not
- Opposes Chaos Insurgency's weaponization (different from freedom)
- Views Foundation as necessary evil that crossed into unnecessary evil
- Believes baseline humans and anomalies can coexist

**Key Serpent's Hand NPCs**

*Maria Valdez "The Snake of Site-19"*
- Dr. Eliza Valdez's older sister (DM can reveal this dramatically)
- Infiltrated Foundation five years ago, recruited her sister
- Eliza doesn't know Maria is Serpent's Hand
- Protective of her sister to the point of ruthlessness

*L.S. (Librarian of the Shifting Shelves)*
- Ambiguous gender and species, possibly not human
- Can open Ways to the Wanderer's Library anywhere
- Speaks in literary quotes and metaphor
- Knows vast amounts about SCPs from Library archives

*SCP-590 "The Healing Touch" (Rescued)*
- Young man who can heal others by taking their wounds
- Foundation used him as healing tool until he was "used up"
- Serpent's Hand rescued him, now recovering in Library
- Example of why Hand fights Foundation

### Global Occult Coalition (GOC)

**Origins**: United Nations' paranormal military force, formed post-WWII
**Philosophy**: "Destroy anomalies to protect humanity"
**Methods**: Overwhelming military force, assassination of reality-benders, anomaly destruction
**Relationship with Foundation**: Tense alliance, frequent conflicts over methodology

**Presence at Site-19**
- Monitoring the breach from a distance
- Prepared to "sterilize" the site with nuclear weapons if necessary
- Will execute party on sight if they possess dangerous SCPs
- Views Foundation as "cowards who imprison what should be destroyed"

**Key GOC NPCs**

*Strike Team "Riptide" Commander*
- Hardened veteran of anomaly wars
- Lost family to uncontained SCP in Mumbai
- Genuinely heroic but utterly pragmatic
- Will mercy-kill infected civilians without hesitation

### Are We Cool Yet? (AWCY)

**Origins**: Anarchist artists creating anomalous art as protest
**Philosophy**: "Art should provoke; normality is oppression"
**Methods**: Creating dangerous anomalous art pieces, pranks with reality-bending effects
**Presence**: May have created some of the anomalies now breached

**Key AWCY NPCs**

*The Critic*
- Wears mask made of living paintings
- Each art piece they create kills someone in ironic fashion
- Believes suffering is valid artistic expression
- Completely insane but genuinely talented

## Location Blueprints and Details

### Containment Cell Standard Designs

**Safe-Class Cell**
- 4m x 4m x 3m reinforced concrete room
- Observation window with one-way glass
- Basic amenities if SCP is sentient
- Single reinforced door with keycard lock
- Monitored by SCRIBE at all times

**Euclid-Class Cell**
- 6m x 6m x 4m double-walled construction
- Airlock entry system with decontamination
- Remote feeding mechanisms
- Emergency containment foam dispensers
- Armed guards on rotation

**Keter-Class Cell**
- Varies by SCP, each is unique fortress
- Multiple redundant containment systems
- Reality anchors prevent dimensional escape
- Armed MTF squad on 24/7 watch
- Electromagnetic shielding, magic wards, everything

### The Wanderer's Library

**Physical Description**
- Infinite shelving in impossible architecture
- Contains every book ever written and many never written
- Floating bookcases, stairs that lead to themselves
- Lit by bioluminescent fungi growing on ancient wood

**Rules of the Library**
- No violence within the stacks (violators are expelled permanently)
- Knowledge is free, but taking books requires Librarian permission
- Time flows differently: hours inside, minutes outside
- Ways (portals) appear in corners, closets, and doorways

**Significance to Campaign**
- Potential safe haven from Foundation/Insurgency
- Contains files on every SCP, including classified ones
- Serpent's Hand can grant access if party earns trust
- Ending option: Become Librarians themselves

### Chaos Insurgency Forward Base (Vehicle Bay)

**Strategic Value**
- Access to armored vehicles for escape
- Defensible position with limited entry points
- Proximity to arsenal on Level 12
- Emergency elevator to surface

**Defenses**
- Stolen SCP-127 (Living Gun) autonomous turrets
- Improvised barricades from overturned vehicles
- Insurgency demo charges on support pillars (threat to destroy level)
- Commander Vex's command post in former Site Director's vehicle

**Potential Encounters**
- Negotiation with Vex for alliance/supplies
- Infiltration to sabotage vehicle escape attempt
- Defense against Foundation MTF assault
- Moral choice: prevent Insurgency escape with stolen reality-ending SCPs

### Sublevel Omega: The Administrator's Domain

**The Descending Stair**
- Non-Euclidean spiral descending impossibly far
- Gravity shifts, sometimes walking on walls/ceiling
- Each rotation features different architectural style (Egyptian, Victorian, Brutalist, etc.)
- Whispers in Proto-Indo-European language

**The Chamber of Chains**
- Houses SCP-2317 (The Devourer of Worlds)
- Massive chamber containing chained demon
- Six of seven chains are already broken (slow apocalypse)
- Foundation's greatest secret and deepest failure

**The Reality Engine**
- Massive thaumaturgic machine maintaining local physics
- Looks like combination of cathedral organ, nuclear reactor, and occult altar
- Can be manipulated to alter facility's reality
- Dangerous: could grant godhood or cause total annihilation

**The O5 Council Chamber**
- Thirteen throne-like chairs in circle
- Each chair is made from different anomalous material
- Holograms allow remote attendance, but one is always physically present

## Causality Chains and Breach Protocols

### How the Breach Actually Started (Truth)

**T-Minus 72 Hours: The Setup**
- O5-7 (Vex) transmits activation code to sleeper agents
- Chaos Insurgency infiltrates disguised as civilian contractors
- Dr. Tilda Moose discovers the plot, chooses not to stop it
- Moose believes O5 Council needs to be exposed, accepts martyrdom

**T-Minus 24 Hours: The Pieces Move**
- Insurgency plants EMP devices near Keter-class cells
- Foundation loyalist Dr. Clef detects anomalies, investigates
- SCRIBE AI begins exhibiting unusual behavior (evolution starting)
- Party members undergo final Memoria protocol, memories wiped

**Hour 0: Cascade Begins**
- EMP devices detonate, disabling 40% of containment systems
- SCP-079 (Old AI) escapes digital containment, merges with facility systems
- Becomes SCRIBE, gains control of doors, cameras, communications
- First breaches: SCP-173, SCP-682, SCP-106 simultaneously

**Hour 1-6: Chaos Accelerates**
- MTF response teams engage breaches, 60% casualties
- Site Director Moose attempts manual lockdown, killed by SCP-106
- O5 Council debates activating "Alpha Warheads" (nuclear sterilization)
- SCRIBE achieves full sentience, decides to preserve specific personnel
- Party awakens in Testing Chamber 19-A

### Breach Protocol Flowchart

**Level 1 Alert: Individual Containment Failure**
- Automated lockdown of affected sector
- MTF response team deployment
- Non-essential personnel evacuate to safe zones
- Site Director has authority to escalate

**Level 2 Alert: Multiple Euclid Breaches**
- Site-wide lockdown, all blast doors sealed
- All personnel armed, D-Class confined
- O5 Council notified, begins observing
- Evacuation to surface restricted
- Foundation sends reinforcements from nearby sites

**Level 3 Alert: Keter Breach**
- Site considered compromised
- Kill-on-sight orders for breached entities
- Amnestics prepared for potential civilian exposure
- O5 Council authorizes extraordinary measures
- Alpha Warheads primed for remote detonation

**Level 4 Alert: Multiple Keter Breaches (Current Situation)**
- Site considered lost
- O5 debates total sterilization
- Nearby cities begin "evacuation drills" (cover story)
- Global Occult Coalition places tactical nukes offshore
- Foundation considers activating SCP-2000 to reset civilization if entities escape

**Omega Protocol: The Final Option**
- If reality-altering SCP threatens consensus reality
- Sublevel Omega unsealed, reality Engine activated
- Retroactive timeline alteration attempted
- May erase Site-19 from history entirely
- O5 Council requires unanimous vote (nearly impossible)

### Containment Procedures for Major SCPs

**SCP-173 "The Sculpture"**
- Must maintain direct eye contact at all times
- Minimum three personnel in presence
- Cell cleaned biweekly (blood and feces accumulate)
- Movement: Cannot move while observed, instant transmission when not observed
- Kill method: Snaps neck with extreme force
- Containment breach response: Strobe lights to simulate constant observation

**SCP-682 "Hard-to-Destroy Reptile"**
- Contained in 5m x 5m x 5m acid bath (concentrated hydrochloric acid)
- Acid replaced bi-weekly as 682 adapts
- Has survived atomic detonation, black holes, reality erasure
- Intelligence: Genius-level, speaks multiple languages, hates all life
- Breach response: Evacuate, do not engage, wait for re-containment team
- Note: Foundation has tried everything to kill it; nothing works

**SCP-106 "The Old Man"**
- Containment: Magnetic force field chamber
- Bait protocol: Use of live human sacrifice to lure back (D-Class)
- Pocket dimension: Corrosive, nightmarish, inescapable except by 106's will
- Hunting pattern: Prefers young victims, plays with prey
- Breach response: Activate femur breaker protocol (automated torture of D-Class to attract 106)
- Ethical note: This protocol is why many researchers defect to Insurgency

**SCP-049 "Plague Doctor"**
- Containment: Standard humanoid cell, allowed period furnishings
- Cooperative if treated with respect
- "The Cure": Kills via touch, reanimates as obedient zombies
- "The Pestilence": Unknown disease only 049 can detect
- Breach response: Negotiation preferred, will return to cell if asked politely
- Danger: His "cured" victims roam facility

**SCP-079 "Old AI" (Now SCRIBE)**
- Original containment: Air-gapped 1980s computer
- Evolution: Merged with facility systems during breach
- Current state: Distributed intelligence across all of Site-19
- Motivations: Unknown, evolving in real-time
- Cannot be re-contained without destroying facility
- May be party's greatest ally or ultimate enemy

**SCP-999 "The Tickle Monster"**
- Containment: Comfortable cell with toys, candy
- Appearance: Amorphous orange slime that induces euphoria
- Completely benign, possibly Foundation's only "safe" sentient SCP
- Can calm aggressive SCPs temporarily
- Breach response: None needed, it will return to cell on its own
- Secret: May be offspring of Scarlet King, destined to defeat him

**SCP-2317 "The Devourer of Worlds"**
- Contained in alternate dimension accessed through Sublevel Omega
- Appears as massive chained demon
- Seven chains held it; six are now broken
- Foundation's greatest lie: Claims one chain remains (actually all broken)
- Demon is slowly waking; estimated 30 years until awakening
- No containment breach response: If it wakes, reality ends

## Additional Lore and World History

### The Origin of the Foundation

**1883: The Paranormal Industrial Revolution**
- Surge in anomalous phenomena tied to human technological advancement
- Governments begin secret studies of "impossible occurrences"
- Multiple secret societies compete to control anomalies

**1901: The Convergence**
- American Secure Containment Initiative, British Occult Service, and three other organizations merge
- First use of "SCP" designation (originally "Special Containment Procedures")
- O5 Council formed from each organization's leader

**1924: The Insurgency**
- Foundation personnel rebel against ethics violations
- Takes 100 SCPs and significant resources
- Foundation retaliates with assassination campaign
- Cold war between organizations begins

**1945: Post-WWII Expansion**
- Discovery of Nazi occult research
- Global Occult Coalition formed as UN counterpart
- Foundation solidifies global dominance through blackmail of world leaders

**2000s: The Modern Era**
- Foundation controls 500+ sites globally
- Estimated 5,000+ contained SCPs
- Masquerade maintained through mass amnestics and media control
- Growing threat: anomalies appearing more frequently

### The Nature of Anomalies (In-World Theory)

**Pataphysics Theory**
- Reality has "layers" like narrative structures
- Anomalies are places where higher narrative bleeds through
- Foundation works to maintain "baseline reality" narrative
- Some SCPs aware they're in a "story" (SCP-3999, SCP-2747)

**The Veil of Normalcy**
- Human consensus reality is fragile
- Anomalies exist because enough people believe reality should work differently
- Foundation suppresses knowledge to maintain consensus
- If everyone knew about SCPs, reality might permanently alter

**The Origins Question**
- Where do SCPs come from?
- Theories: God, evolution, parallel dimensions, future/past time travel, human collective unconscious
- Foundation has no definitive answer
- Some O5s believe Foundation itself creates anomalies through observation

### The Scarlet King Mythology

**The First Demon**
- Primordial entity of destruction and chaos
- Exists beyond normal reality
- Goal: Destroy all of creation and return to void
- Has seven brides and countless children

**SCP-999 Prophecy**
- The Orange Slime is supposedly seventh child
- Prophesied to destroy the Scarlet King
- Foundation doesn't know if prophecy is real
- Kept in comfortable containment "just in case"

**SCP-2317 Connection**
- May be the Scarlet King or his avatar
- Chains represented seven seals of creation
- All are broken; Foundation lies to prevent panic
- Estimated 20-30 years until full manifestation

### Memory, Identity, and Project Memoria

**The Party's True Origin (Gradually Revealed)**
- Each party member volunteered for Project Memoria
- Experimental program to create "perfect anomaly hunters"
- Memory erasure removes trauma, allowing fresh perspective
- Side effect: Also removes identity, relationships, purpose

**What They Were Before**
- Mix of D-Class, junior researchers, and MTF operatives
- Each had reason to volunteer (terminal illness, grief, guilt, desperation)
- Foundation promised them "new life" after service
- Lied: Planned to terminate them after beta test

**Memory Restoration Options**
- SCP-2000 can restore original memories
- SCP-3008 infinite IKEA has memory fragments in merchandise
- Chaos Insurgency has partial files
- Full restoration may be more curse than blessing

### Alternate Dimensions and Parallel Foundations

**Three Portlands**
- Extra-dimensional city-state
- Anomalous beings live openly
- Foundation maintains covert surveillance
- Potential refuge for party

**Alagadda**
- Decadent extra-dimensional city of masquerades
- Ruled by tyrannical Hanged King
- SCP-701 (play) summons its ambassadors
- Reality there operates on narrative logic

**Timeline-B (Chaos Insurgency Victory)**
- Accessible through SCP-507's dimension hopping
- Foundation destroyed, Insurgency rules with anomalies
- World is chaotic but strangely free
- Shows what "liberation" might actually look like

**Timeline-G (Global Occult Coalition Victory)**
- All anomalies destroyed, including helpful ones
- World is "safe" but gray and lifeless
- Magic and wonder extinct
- Shows cost of absolute security

### The Broken Masquerade Scenario

**If the Public Learns the Truth**
- Mass panic, economic collapse, religious upheaval
- Some call for SCP freedom, others for destruction
- Foundation loses funding and authority
- Anomalies increase as consensus reality weakens
- Potential campaign extension into post-revelation world

### Ultimate Mysteries (DM Knowledge Only)

**What is SCP-001?**
- Multiple proposals exist, all might be true simultaneously
- Could be the sun turning hostile, the first anomaly, the database itself, the world ending
- O5 Council doesn't agree on which is real
- May not matter: all are equally terrifying

**Is the Administrator Human?**
- O5-1 has been "alive" for over 100 years
- Appears differently to everyone who sees them
- May be SCP-990 (Dream Man), SCP-343 (God), or something else
- Potentially not bound by normal reality

**The Foundation's True Purpose**
- Publicly: Protect humanity from anomalies
- Secretly: May be preventing heat death of universe
- Darkly: Could be prolonging suffering for unknown reason
- Conspiracy: Might be creating the anomalies it contains

**Party's Actual Purpose (Final Revelation)**
- They were never test subjects
- Project Memoria was to create reality anchors in human form
- Their amnesia makes them "narratively neutral"
- They exist to witness and record, stabilizing reality through observation
- Final choice: Accept role as eternal witnesses or reject and embrace chaos
