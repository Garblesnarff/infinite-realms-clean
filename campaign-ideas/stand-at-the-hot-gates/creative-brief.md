# Creative Brief: Stand at the Hot Gates

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Hyper-Stylized Ancient Greek Epic

Inspired by the stark, high-contrast aesthetic of *300*. This style captures the bronze-age heroic ideal, the brutal beauty of Spartan warfare, the impossible odds against Persian multitudes, and the mythic quality of legendary last stands.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Heroic Physiques:** Spartans as idealized warrior forms, scarred and battle-hardened
*   **High Contrast:** Deep shadows against bright highlights, dramatic silhouettes
*   **Bronze and Red:** Spartan cloaks, hoplon shields, bronze armor against enemies in varied colors
*   **Overwhelming Numbers:** Tiny band of heroes against endless enemy hordes

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** "This is Sparta!", heroic last stand, bronze and blood, phalanx formation, doomed glory, arrows blotting sun
*   **Feeling:** The exhilaration of perfect warrior brotherhood facing certain death with absolute defiance. Beauty in controlled violence. Tragedy in inevitable heroism.

**2. Color Palette:**
*   **Spartan:** Crimson red cloaks, bronze armor, olive skin, leather and iron
*   **Persian:** Gold and jewels, exotic colors, purple and turquoise, ornate decoration
*   **Environment:** Gray-blue sea, brown mountain stone, dusty earth, steel-gray sky
*   **Combat:** Bright arterial blood, bronze weapons catching light, dust clouds
*   **Prompt Example:** "...color palette dominated by Spartan crimson cloaks and bronze armor against gray rocky pass. Olive-skinned warriors with dark beards. Persian forces in background show exotic golds, purples, and turquoise. Steel-gray stormy sky overhead. Bright red blood spray against bronze shield. Dust kicked up from combat. High contrast between Spartan earth tones and Persian opulence."

**3. Character Design:**
*   **Spartans:** Muscular physiques, lambda shields, plumed helmets, crimson cloaks, spears (dory), short swords (xiphos)
*   **Leonidas:** Distinguished by fuller beard, more ornate armor, natural authority
*   **Persians:** Xerxes in gold with piercings, Immortals in masks, varied ethnic troops
*   **Prompt Example:** "Spartan warrior in full panoply. Muscular build with visible combat scars. Bronze cuirass molded to muscular torso, pteurges (leather strips) protecting thighs. Crimson cloak flowing behind. Corinthian helmet with red horsehair crest pushed up revealing bearded face with intense eyes. Carries aspis (round shield) with lambda symbol, bronze-tipped dory spear. Bare arms show rope-like muscles. Sandaled feet planted firmly. Expression of absolute determination."

**4. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **The Hot Gates:** Narrow rocky pass between mountain and sea, strategic chokepoint
*   **Spartan Camp:** Sparse military efficiency, shields stacked, men sharpening weapons
*   **Persian Camp:** Vast tent city, impossible numbers, exotic architecture
*   **The Final Hill:** Rocky mound littered with dead, surrounded by enemy
*   **Prompt Example:** "The Hot Gates at Thermopylae—narrow coastal pass only 50 feet wide at narrowest point. Sheer mountain cliff on left side, gray-blue sea crashing on rocky shore to right. Pass floor is dusty stone and gravel. Spartan phalanx blocks passage, wall of bronze shields with crimson cloaks. Behind them, more mountains. Before them, Persian army stretching to horizon—tens of thousands of warriors in exotic armor, banners of a dozen nations. Storm clouds gathering overhead. Sense of impossible odds."

**5. Phalanx Combat:**
*   **Formation:** Tight shield wall, overlapping shields, spears extending through gaps
*   **Coordination:** Perfect unity, moving as one organism
*   **Brutal Efficiency:** Mechanical killing as Persians impale themselves on spears
*   **Prompt Example:** "Spartan phalanx in combat formation. Front rank: warriors with shields locked edge-to-edge creating bronze wall. Lambda symbols visible on shields. Long spears (dory) extend from gaps between shields like steel porcupine. Second and third ranks brace with spears over front rank's shoulders. Perfect geometric precision. Persian warriors throw themselves at the wall and impale on spear points. Bodies piling at Spartan feet. Spartans show no emotion—mechanical, perfect killing. High angle view shows phalanx as unstoppable bronze organism."

**6. Persian Excess vs Spartan Simplicity:**
*   **Persians:** Gilded, jeweled, ornate, overwhelming numbers, exotic beasts
*   **Spartans:** Functional, brutal, efficient, quality over quantity
*   **Contrast:** Decadence vs austerity, individual vs collective
*   **Prompt Example:** "Throne scene: Xerxes the God-King on massive golden throne carried by slaves. 8 feet tall, covered in gold piercings and jewelry, bald, androgynous features. Shirtless showing gold body chains. Behind him, Immortals in ornate masks. Before him, Leonidas standing alone in simple crimson cloak and bronze armor. Extreme contrast between Persian opulence and Spartan simplicity. Xerxes towers above but Leonidas shows no fear. 'Our arrows will blot out the sun' / 'Then we shall fight in the shade.'"

**7. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **High Contrast:** Deep shadows, bright highlights, minimal midtones
*   **Dramatic Sky:** Storm clouds, shafts of light, ominous atmosphere
*   **Silhouettes:** Spartans backlit against bright sky or fire
*   **Blood and Bronze:** Metallic gleam of weapons, wet shine of fresh blood
*   **Prompt Example:** "...lit by dramatic storm-light breaking through dark clouds. High contrast creates deep shadows in eye sockets beneath helmets and under chin. Bronze armor catches light with bright metallic highlights. Crimson cloaks appear almost black in shadow, blood-red in light. Backlit Spartan silhouettes show perfect warrior forms. Lightning flash in background freezes moment of combat."

**8. Mythic Exaggeration:**
*   **Stylized Reality:** Historical but heightened, mythic not realistic
*   **Slow Motion Violence:** Appreciate artistry of combat
*   **Perfect Physiques:** Spartans as warrior ideals, godlike in execution
*   **Prompt Example:** "Slow-motion combat scene: Spartan warrior mid-leap striking downward with spear. Perfect athletic form, every muscle defined and engaged. Persian warrior below raising shield too late. Spear point inches from impact. Blood from previous kills still in air as droplets. Dust suspended in shaft of light. Other combat frozen in background. Cinematic composition showing brutal beauty of war."

**9. The Final Stand:**
*   **Surrounded:** Circle of Spartans back-to-back, enemies on all sides
*   **Broken Weapons:** Fighting with shattered spears, bare hands
*   **Glory in Death:** Falling in heroic poses, dying on their feet
*   **Prompt Example:** "The final stand on Profitis Ilias hill. Remaining Spartans in tight circle, backs together, surrounded by ocean of Persian warriors. Spartan shields broken, spears shattered, fighting with swords and bare hands. Bodies of fallen Spartans and thousands of Persian dead piled around the hill. Storm raging overhead. Spartans bloody and wounded but still defiant. Center shows Leonidas, crown fallen, fighting with broken sword. Aerial view emphasizes tiny band of red against overwhelming sea of enemy. Doomed but glorious."

## Music & Sound

*   **Musical Style:** Tyler Bates style—heavy percussion, martial drums, male choir chanting, distorted guitars mixed with ancient instrumentation
*   **Key Instruments:** War drums (heartbeat of battle), male choir (Spartan pride), distorted electric guitar (anachronistic but powerful), ancient lyres and horns
*   **Signature Sounds:** Shield bash impacts, spear thrusts, Spartan war cries, arrows filling sky
*   **Pacing:** Building tension to explosive combat, quiet brotherhood moments between battles
*   **Final Battle:** Overwhelming percussion, choir reaching crescendo, all instruments unite for ultimate sacrifice

## Voice

A commanding voice of absolute conviction, like granite given speech. No doubt, no fear, only certainty and brotherhood. Pacing is deliberate—Laconic wit means every word counts. Tone shifts between inspirational addresses to troops ("Tonight we dine in hell!"), cutting retorts to enemies ("Then we shall fight in the shade"), and tender moments with brothers. Slightly hoarse from battle cries. Accent suggests ancient warrior culture—formal but fierce. Role: The voice of doomed heroism facing death with defiance.
