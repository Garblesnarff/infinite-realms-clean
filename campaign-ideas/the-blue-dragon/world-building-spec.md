# The Blue Dragon - World-Building Specification

## The Southwestern Territories

Desert kingdom where magical alchemy thrives. Major city (Albuquerque equivalent) with surrounding desert perfect for hidden laboratories.

### The Drug Trade

**Blue Sky**: Magical narcotic of unprecedented purity
- Effects: Enhanced magical ability, euphoria, severe addiction
- Market: Displaces traditional drugs
- Signature: Distinctive blue coloration
- Creator: The Blue Dragon (Walter's alias)

### Criminal Organizations

**The Salamanca Family**: Violent cartel
- Tuco: Unstable muscle
- Hector: Paralyzed patriarch
- Twins: Silent enforcers
- Culture: Traditional, brutal

**Los Pollos Hermanos (The Golden Roosters)**: Gus's empire
- Front: Restaurant chain
- Reality: Drug distribution network
- Leader: Gustavo "Gus" Fring
- Style: Professional, patient, ruthless

**Law Enforcement**:
- DEA equivalent pursuing Blue Dragon
- Hank leading investigation
- Institutional challenges and politics
- Personal connection complicates case

### Character Arcs

**Walter's Transformation**:
- Session 1: Sympathetic victim
- Session 10: Morally compromised
- Session 20: Unrecognizable monster
- Session 40: "I did it for me"

**Jesse's Tragedy**:
- Starts criminal, becomes conscience
- Manipulated and damaged by Walter
- Multiple redemption attempts, all fail
- Eventually escapes or dies trying

**Skyler's Horror**:
- Discovers truth gradually
- Complicity vs. survival
- Protection of children
- "I am the danger... to you"

### The Chemistry

Magical alchemy replacing traditional chemistry. Same precision, same danger, same addictive perfection. Science as power. Knowledge as weapon.

### The Moral Questions

- When does providing for family become excuse?
- How much evil for good reason?
- Is transformation inevitable or chosen?
- Can you ever really go back?
- What's the cost of pride?

### The Ending

Everyone loses. Walter dies or escapes but has lost everything. Jesse broken but free. Hank dead or destroyed. Family shattered. Empire fallen.

But the blue dragon flies forever in legend.

The consequences? They last forever.
