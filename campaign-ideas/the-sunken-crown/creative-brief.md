# The Sunken Crown - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Underwater Archaeological Adventure
**Inspiration**: Atlantis: The Lost Empire, Tomb Raider, Uncharted, Indiana Jones
**Aesthetic**: Ancient grandeur beneath the waves, mysterious ruins, exotic architecture
**Tone**: Adventure and wonder tinged with danger and hubris

## Color Palettes
- **Ruins**: Weathered stone (blues, greens from algae), faded gold trim, coral overgrowth
- **Azurian Style**: White marble, gold inlay, azure blue mosaics, elegant geometry
- **The Crown**: Pure gold, gemstone centerpiece (sapphire), divine radiance
- **Titan's Presence**: Sickly purple-black, reality distortion effects, oily darkness

## Voice Direction
**Dr. Stormsinger**: British accent, passionate enthusiasm, scholarly expertise
**Baron Goldwater**: Aristocratic, condescending, cultured villain
**Captain Krait**: Hissing serpentine speech, dangerous and unpredictable
**Last Azurian**: Echoing, ancient, speaks in archaic formal language
**Thalax (Titan)**: Multiple voices overlapping, promises and threats, seductive power

## Cinematic Moments
- **First Ruins Vista**: Massive city revealed through murky water, scope and grandeur
- **Crown Chamber**: Dramatic reveal, Crown floating in magical stasis
- **Titan's Eye Opens**: Horror moment, realize prison is failing
- **Final Choice**: Party holding Crown, multiple factions demanding it, Titan rising
- **Epilogue**: Show consequences of choice weeks later
