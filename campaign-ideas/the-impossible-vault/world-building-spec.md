**The Impossible Vault: Eleven Masters, One Score**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** The players are recruited by a charismatic mastermind to pull off the impossible: robbing the Grand Celestial Casino's legendary vault during the city's most prestigious magical tournament. To succeed, they must assemble a crew of eleven specialists—each an expert in their field—and execute a plan so audacious that no one would believe it could work. As they navigate the casino's elaborate security systems, corrupt nobility, and rival criminal organizations, the heist becomes a delicate dance of timing, teamwork, and misdirection. But the real challenge isn't just stealing the fortune—it's doing it with style and getting everyone out alive.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts. These entries will form the historical and cultural bedrock of the world.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the founding of the Grand Celestial Casino and its rise to become the most prestigious gambling establishment in the realm.
    *   Write the history of Lord Midas Goldweaver's family and how they built their fortune through legitimate business before turning to corruption.
    *   Describe the tradition of the Grand Magical Tournament and its significance to the city's culture and economy.
    *   Explain the evolution of casino security from simple guards to sophisticated magical wards and surveillance systems.
    *   Detail famous heists in the city's history and how they shaped modern security practices.
    *   Write about the legendary "Castellan Catastrophe"—the financial scandal that destroyed Darian's family and drove him to crime.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below. Each profile must contain the specified fields.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Casino Security Consortium** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** To protect the casino's assets and maintain its reputation as impenetrable.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Military-style command structure with specialized divisions for magical and mundane security.
        *   **Public Agenda:** We ensure the safety and security of all casino patrons and assets.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** To develop security systems so advanced they can be sold to governments and military organizations.
        *   **Assets:** Cutting-edge surveillance, elite security forces, powerful magical wards.
        *   **Relationships:** Allied with law enforcement; antagonistic toward all criminal organizations.
    *   **The Underworld Alliance** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** To control the city's criminal enterprises and expand influence into legitimate business.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Council of crime lords representing different criminal specialties.
        *   **Public Agenda:** We provide services that the law won't or can't.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** To infiltrate and control the casino from within, making it a money laundering operation.
        *   **Assets:** Network of criminals, corrupt officials, and information brokers.
        *   **Relationships:** Competitive with independent operators; hostile toward the Casino Security Consortium.
    *   **The Specialists' Guild** (Minor)
        *   **Goals:** To protect the interests and reputations of expert criminals-for-hire.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Loose association where reputation and proven expertise determine standing.
        *   **Public Agenda:** We are independent professionals who provide specialized skills to those who can afford them.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** To maintain high standards that prevent law enforcement crackdowns on the profession.
        *   **Assets:** Diverse expertise, professional networks, code of conduct.
        *   **Relationships:** Neutral toward most factions; willing to work for the highest bidder while maintaining professional ethics.

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below, populating all specified sub-sections.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **The Grand Celestial Casino:** The magnificent target of the heist.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Gaming Floor, The Tournament Arena, The VIP Suites, The Security Center, The Vault of Stars.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Wealthy patrons, casino staff, security personnel, tournament competitors, corrupt nobility.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Gambling, luxury accommodations, fine dining, entertainment, magical services.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** A high-roller recognizes one of the crew, security conducts a random inspection, a tournament match creates a perfect distraction, a drunk patron reveals useful information, a rival crew member is spotted.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The vault contains a magical artifact that grants wishes." "Lord Goldweaver cheats using enchanted cards." "The casino is built on ancient ruins with secret passages."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Crystal chandeliers, magical displays, well-dressed crowds), Sound (Cards shuffling, coins clinking, magical chimes), Smell (Expensive perfumes, fine cigars, wine).
    *   **The Vault of Stars:** The legendary repository beneath the casino.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Vault Door, The Security Checkpoints, The Artifact Chamber, The Emergency Seals.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Elite vault guards, automated magical defenses, emergency response teams.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** None—strictly forbidden access.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** A guard rotation happens early, a magical ward activates unexpectedly, a pressure plate is triggered, a security camera swivels toward movement, an alarm sounds in a distant section.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The vault has never been breached in two hundred years." "There's a backdoor that only the original builders knew about." "The vault's magical defenses can kill intruders instantly."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Gleaming metal, glowing runes, laser grids), Sound (Humming wards, mechanical locks, footsteps echoing), Smell (Ozone, metal, recycled air).
    *   **The Crew's Hideout:** The warehouse where the team plans and practices.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Planning Room, The Practice Vault, The Equipment Storage, The Living Quarters.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** The eleven specialists and their support staff.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Planning resources, training facilities, specialized equipment.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** A specialist reveals a hidden talent, equipment malfunctions during practice, someone's past catches up to them, a disagreement threatens team cohesion, a breakthrough in the plan.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "This warehouse was used for a famous heist decades ago." "Someone in the crew is being watched by law enforcement." "There's hidden treasure in the warehouse from a previous operation."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Blueprint-covered walls, makeshift vault replica, scattered equipment), Sound (Tools clanging, people arguing, plans being discussed), Smell (Coffee, sawdust, mechanical oil).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes. For each "IF" condition, the agent must pre-generate the narrative and environmental consequences for the "THEN" outcome.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** The players successfully recruit all eleven specialists.
    *   **THEN:** They gain access to the full range of heist capabilities. Generate scenarios where managing eleven strong personalities creates interpersonal conflicts.
    *   **IF:** The players are discovered during reconnaissance.
    *   **THEN:** Casino security is heightened and the crew must adapt. Generate additional security measures and increased paranoia from Lord Goldweaver.
    *   **IF:** The players befriend Lyra Nightingale and earn her trust.
    *   **THEN:** They gain crucial inside information but put her in danger. Generate moral dilemmas around using her information versus protecting her.
    *   **IF:** The players execute the heist during the tournament finals.
    *   **THEN:** They benefit from maximum distraction but face time pressure. Generate chaotic tournament events that both help and hinder the heist.
    *   **IF:** The players reveal their true plan at the critical moment.
    *   **THEN:** They gain the element of surprise against opponents who thought they knew the plan. Generate reactions from NPCs who realize they've been outplayed.
    *   **IF:** A specialist is captured or compromised.
    *   **THEN:** The crew must improvise without their expertise. Generate scenarios where other specialists step up or the plan must be radically altered.
