**Wasteland Rebellion**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Wasteland rebels with weaponized tanks fight Water & Power corporation's monopoly on water in post-apocalyptic desert. Led by punk Tank Girl and allied with bioengineered kangaroo Rippers, they raid corporate facilities, liberating water and slaves while blowing stuff up with gleeful abandon.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail how Water & Power deliberately created the apocalypse to control survivors.
    *   Write the history of the Ripper creation program and their escape/abandonment.
    *   Describe the water wars and how W&P established total monopoly.
    *   Explain Tank Girl's origin and why she fights against authority.
    *   Detail Ripper City's founding and their culture combining military discipline with freedom.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Water & Power Corporation** (Major): Megalithic company controlling all water, corporate tyranny.
    *   **The Rippers** (Major): Bioengineered kangaroo warriors seeking freedom and purpose.
    *   **Wasteland Rebels** (Major): Scattered resistance groups united by Tank Girl.
    *   **Water Slaves** (Minor): Captive laborers in W&P facilities awaiting liberation.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Tank Girl (anarchic leader), Booga (Ripper warrior), Kesslee (corporate enforcer), The Colonel (Ripper leader), Jet Girl (mechanic).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Ripper Warrior" (kangaroo soldier), "W&P Enforcer" (corporate soldier), "Wasteland Rebel" (punk fighter), "Water Merchant" (resource trader).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Tank Girl's Mobile Base:** Camp centered on her weaponized tank, punk aesthetic.
*   **Ripper City:** Underground bunker with military organization and freedom philosophy.
*   **The Pipeline:** Massive water distribution network, target for raids.
*   **Aquifer Complex:** W&P fortress headquarters over last natural water.
*   **The Wasteland:** Harsh desert where water is life and death.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Raid W&P facility → **THEN:** Free water and slaves, decrease oppression, W&P retaliates.
*   **IF:** Ally with Rippers → **THEN:** Gain army and advanced tech, access to Ripper City.
*   **IF:** Destroy W&P infrastructure → **THEN:** Water flows freely, settlements thrive, corporate control weakens.
*   **IF:** Kesslee enhanced → **THEN:** More powerful but less human, final boss form.
*   **IF:** Aquifer Complex destroyed → **THEN:** Water monopoly broken, wasteland begins recovery.
