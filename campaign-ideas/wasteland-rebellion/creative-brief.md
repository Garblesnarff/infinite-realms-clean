# Creative Brief: Wasteland Rebellion

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Post-Apocalyptic Punk Comic Book (Tank Girl-inspired)

Inspired by *Tank Girl*—blending Mad Max wasteland with punk aesthetic, comic book violence with crude humor, tank warfare with kangaroo mutants. Over-the-top action where explosions solve problems and attitude is armor.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Comic Book Action:** Bold outlines, exaggerated violence, sound effect words
*   **Punk Wasteland:** Leather, mohawks, safety pins, graffiti on tanks
*   **Kangaroo Warriors:** Bioengineered mutants in military gear, anthropomorphic
*   **Tank Vehicles:** Heavily weaponized, customized, character in their own right

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Post-apocalyptic punk, water wars, tank action, kangaroo mutants, crude humor, anarchic freedom
*   **Feeling:** Mad Max meets punk rock. Survival through rebellion. Chaos as virtue.

**2. Color Palette:**
*   **Wasteland:** Desert yellows, sand browns, rust oranges, bleached sky blues
*   **Punk Accents:** Bright spray paint, neon against decay, vivid contrasts
*   **Water:** Precious blue, glowing and valuable
*   **Military:** Olive drab, camouflage, steel grays
*   **Prompt Example:** "...vibrant color palette mixing desert wasteland—sand yellows, rust oranges, bleached blue sky—with punk bright accents: hot pink graffiti, neon green mohawks, electric blue water. A weaponized tank painted with chaotic designs and spray paint. The wasteland is harsh but the characters add explosive color."

**3. Character Design:**
*   **Tank Girl:** Short hair or mohawk, tank top, shorts, boots, weaponized accessories
*   **Rippers:** Anthropomorphic kangaroos in military gear, muscular, martial arts weapons
*   **W&P Soldiers:** Corporate uniforms, impersonal armor, faceless enforcers
*   **Wasteland Rebels:** Punk scavengers, makeshift armor, weapons from salvage
*   **Prompt Example:** "A punk warrior woman with cropped hair, wearing tank top with band logo, cut-off shorts, heavy boots. Covered in tattoos and scars. Gun belt with multiple weapons. Standing atop a weaponized tank painted with graffiti. Desert wasteland behind her. Confident, anarchic posture. Comic book style with bold outlines."

**4. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **The Wasteland:** Endless desert, rock formations, abandoned ruins, harsh sun
*   **W&P Facilities:** Corporate-clean installations in dirty wasteland, water tanks
*   **Ripper City:** Underground bunker with military aesthetic, makeshift but organized
*   **Tank Interiors:** Cramped, weapon-filled, personalized with punk decorations
*   **Prompt Example:** "A post-apocalyptic desert wasteland stretching to horizon. Rock formations and ruins of old civilization. A massive water pipeline runs across the landscape, guarded by corporate facilities—clean white buildings incongruous in the wasteland. Dust storms in distance. Harsh sunlight creates stark shadows. A tank convoy approaches, kicking up dust."

**5. Tank Warfare:**
*   **Heavily Armed:** Mounted guns, missile launchers, armor plating, spikes
*   **Customized:** Each tank unique with paint jobs, modifications, personality
*   **Action Shots:** Tanks mid-jump, firing weapons, explosive impacts
*   **Comic Book Violence:** Exaggerated explosions, visible shockwaves, debris
*   **Prompt Example:** "A massive weaponized tank leaping over a sand dune, all four treads off the ground. The tank is covered in punk graffiti, spikes welded to armor, multiple gun turrets firing. Huge orange explosion behind it. Comic book style with motion lines. Sound effect text: 'BOOM!' The tank's operator visible in hatch, mohawk flowing, yelling triumphantly."

**6. The Rippers:**
*   **Anthropomorphic Kangaroos:** Muscular, human-like proportions, kangaroo features
*   **Military Gear:** Combat vests, bandoliers, weapons, tactical equipment
*   **Martial Artists:** Posed for combat, using kicks and Australian weapons
*   **Personality:** Each distinct despite being same species
*   **Prompt Example:** "An anthropomorphic kangaroo warrior standing seven feet tall. Muscular build, kangaroo head and tail. Wearing military combat vest with ammunition, combat boots. Holding large Australian-themed weapon. Battle scars visible. Noble warrior pose. Desert background. Comic book style rendering with bold outlines and dramatic shading."

**7. Water & Power Corporate:**
*   **Sterile vs. Wasteland:** Clean uniforms in dirty world
*   **Authoritarian:** Faceless soldiers, corporate logos everywhere
*   **High-Tech:** Advanced weapons contrasting primitive wastelander equipment
*   **Kesslee:** Increasingly cybernetic, chrome implants, inhuman
*   **Prompt Example:** "Water & Power corporate facility—pristine white building with huge company logo. Corporate soldiers in clean uniforms and impersonal armor standing guard. Water tanks behind barbed wire. Everything orderly and controlled. Contrasts sharply with wasteland around it. Oppressive, sterile, the enemy of chaos."

**8. Action & Violence:**
*   **Comic Book Style:** Exaggerated impacts, visible force lines, dynamic angles
*   **Sound Effects:** "POW," "BOOM," "CRASH" in bold lettering
*   **Explosions:** Huge, over-the-top, oranges and reds, shockwaves visible
*   **Heroic Poses:** Characters mid-action in impossible but cool positions
*   **Prompt Example:** "Dynamic action scene with extreme low angle. Tank Girl mid-flip through air, firing dual pistols. Below, enemy vehicle explodes with massive orange fireball. Comic book style with motion blur, speed lines. Sound effect text: 'BLAM BLAM!' and 'BOOM!' Debris frozen mid-air. Her expression is gleeful despite danger."

**9. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Harsh Desert Sun:** Strong overhead light, deep shadows, high contrast
*   **Explosion Lighting:** Orange-red glow illuminating scenes
*   **Comic Book Cel-Shading:** Flat colors with bold outlines, minimal gradients
*   **Dramatic Angles:** Low angles for heroism, high angles for overwhelm
*   **Prompt Example:** "...lit by harsh desert sunlight creating strong shadows and high contrast. Comic book cel-shading with flat colors and bold black outlines. An explosion provides secondary orange lighting. Dramatic low angle makes characters appear heroic and imposing. The lighting is clear and graphic, perfect for action sequences."

## Voice

An energetic, irreverent narration style like someone gleefully recounting the most violent, chaotic story possible. The pacing is fast and explosive, matching tank combat. The tone shifts between crude humor and genuine rebellion, treating authority with contempt and chaos with reverence. Lots of sound effects and action descriptions. Celebrates violence against oppression. Role: The voice of punk anarchism winning through superior firepower and attitude.
