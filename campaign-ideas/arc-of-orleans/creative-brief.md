# Creative Brief: Arc of Orleans

## Art Style: Medieval Epic (The Messenger meets Henry V)

Inspired by Joan of Arc's story in *The Messenger* and the medieval warfare of *Henry V*. This style captures chivalric pageantry dying under longbow arrows, inspired leadership against impossible odds, and the birth of French national identity.

### Core Principles:
*   **Emphasize Transformation:** Show chivalry dying, inspiration rising, nation being born.
*   **Joan's Presence:** Capture her absolute conviction and inspirational effect.
*   **Medieval Warfare:** Portray both chivalric ideals and brutal reality.

### Color Palette:
French royal blue and gold, English red and white, the armor gleam of chivalry, the mud and blood of Agincourt, the pure white of Joan's banner, the orange-red of her execution flames.

### Character Design:
French knights in full plate, English longbowmen in practical gear, Joan in armor with her banner, common soldiers in varied equipment, noble commanders in heraldic splendor.
