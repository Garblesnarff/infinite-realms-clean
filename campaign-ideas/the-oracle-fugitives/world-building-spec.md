### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**The Oracle Fugitives**

**1. Core Concept:**
Crime eliminated through three clairvoyant Oracles. Party member named future murderer. Must prove innocence while uncovering conspiracy.

**2. Lore:**
*   Precrime system's creation and 99.9% success rate.
*   The Oracles—kidnapped psychics drugged to see the future.
*   The city of Farsight—utopian on surface, police state beneath.

**3. Factions:**
*   **Precrime Division** — Elite agents preventing crimes before they happen.
*   **The Resistance** — Those who reject predetermination.
*   **The Corrupt Leadership** — Manipulating predictions for power.

**4. Locations:**
*   **Farsight City** — Sleek, surveilled metropolis.
*   **Oracle Temple** — Where seers are kept in sensory deprivation.
*   **The Underground** — Resistance hideouts outside scanner range.

**5. Causality:**
*   IF party acts predictably, THEN Precrime captures them.
*   IF party proves free will by defying prediction, THEN system's authority crumbles.
