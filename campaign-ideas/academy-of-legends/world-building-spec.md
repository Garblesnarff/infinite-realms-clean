### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

**Academy of Legends**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In a world where 80% possess Quirks—unique superpowers—the party enrolls in Beacon Academy to become professional heroes. They must master their Quirks, compete in tournaments, and face the rising League of Villains seeking to destroy the Symbol of Peace and break society. Through rivalry and sacrifice, they learn what "Plus Ultra" truly means.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the emergence of Quirks—when and how superpowers first appeared in humanity.
    *   Write the history of hero society's formation and the laws governing Quirk usage.
    *   Describe All Might's rise as the Symbol of Peace and how he maintained order for decades.
    *   Explain the villain All For One's history and his clash with successive holders of One For All.
    *   Detail the creation of Hero Rankings and how they influence society.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Hero Society** (Major): Professional heroes maintaining order, ranked publicly, licensed to use Quirks.
    *   **The League of Villains** (Major): Organized villains seeking to break hero society's foundation.
    *   **The Hero Public Safety Commission** (Minor): Government body regulating heroes and covering up problems.
    *   **The Meta Liberation Army** (Minor): Radicals believing Quirks should be used freely without regulation.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** All Might (Symbol of Peace), Shouta Aizawa (Eraserhead teacher), Tomura Shigaraki (villain leader), Endeavor (flawed #2 hero), All For One (ancient villain).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Professional Hero" (various ranks), "League Villain" (unique Quirks), "Rival Student" (Class 1-A/B), "Nomu" (bio-engineered monsters).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Beacon Academy:** Training facilities, dorms, simulation zones, sports festival arena.
*   **USJ (Unforeseen Simulation Joint):** Multi-environment disaster training facility.
*   **Kamino Ward:** Industrial district, site of All Might vs. All For One battle.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** A character overuses their Quirk → **THEN:** Temporary dysfunction, physical strain, potential awakening.
*   **IF:** All Might retires → **THEN:** Hero society destabilizes, villains grow bolder, power vacuum.
*   **IF:** Party achieves provisional licenses → **THEN:** Can engage villains professionally, higher stakes encounters.
