### **World-Building Specification Brief: The Prophesied Child**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Ordinary heroes discover a prophesied infant destined to destroy the evil sorceress-queen Bavmorda and must protect the child while journeying to restore the good sorceress Fin Raziel, gathering unlikely allies and proving that chosen heroism matters more than destined power.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the ancient prophecy about the marked child and Bavmorda's downfall
    *   Explain Bavmorda's rise to power and her conquest of the kingdoms
    *   Describe Fin Raziel's battle with Bavmorda and her transformation curse
    *   Write about the kingdom of Tir Asleen and why Bavmorda cursed it into frozen time
    *   Detail the nature of magic in this worldâ€”rituals, components, gestures, spoken spells
    *   Explain the fairy folk's relationship with the human kingdoms and their ancient pacts

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:**
    *   **Bavmorda's Empire** (Major) - Sorceress-queen and armies, goal: destroy prophesied child, maintain power
    *   **The Protectors** (Major) - Party and allies guarding the child, goal: fulfill prophecy, defeat tyranny
    *   **The Fairy Kingdoms** (Major) - Fairy Queen and her subjects, goal: maintain neutrality unless properly bargained with
    *   **Tir Asleen (Frozen)** (Minor) - Cursed kingdom awaiting restoration, goal: break free and reclaim glory
    *   **The Resistance** (Minor) - Hidden rebels in conquered lands, goal: survive and await hope
    *   **Raziel's Circle** (Minor) - Former students of Raziel still resisting Bavmorda, goal: restore their teacher

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Bavmorda, Fin Raziel, Madmartigan, General Kael, The Fairy Queen, Elora Danan (the child)
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Franjean and Rool (Brownies)," "Death Knight Commander," "Bavmorda's Witch Apprentice," "Tir Asleen Knight," "Fairy Warrior," "Village Elder," "Madmartigan's Former Companion," "Raziel's Student"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Starting Village** - Humble farming community, destroyed early to establish stakes
*   **The Fairy Forest** - Magical woodland with bioluminescent plants, home to fairy folk
*   **The Cursed Island** - Where Raziel is trapped, isolated from magical energy sources
*   **Tir Asleen** - Grand kingdom frozen mid-celebration, every person and object suspended in time
*   **Nockmaar Castle** - Black fortress with skull decorations, ritual chambers, and death knight barracks
*   Each location represents different fantasy archetypes and challenges

**6. Causality Chains**
*   IF the child is captured by Bavmorda THEN she completes the banishment ritual and darkness wins
*   IF the party gathers all restoration components THEN Raziel can be restored to human form
*   IF Raziel is restored THEN she can break Tir Asleen's curse and challenge Bavmorda
*   IF the party gains the Fairy Queen's favor THEN fairy folk become powerful allies
*   IF they defeat General Kael THEN Bavmorda's armies lose their strongest commander
*   IF Madmartigan stays with the party THEN his skill becomes crucial in final battles
*   IF they hold the child toward Bavmorda during her dark ritual THEN innocence reflects evil back
*   IF Bavmorda is destroyed THEN her curses break, kingdoms are freed, prophecy is fulfilled
