# World-Building Specification: The Wheel of Naraka

**1. Core Concept:** Players are trapped in the karmic cycle of death and rebirth. They die, suffer in Naraka proportionate to karma, are reborn in one of six realms, and repeat. They must accumulate wisdom across lifetimes to escape samsara and achieve liberation.

**2. Lore Primer:**
*   Hindu and Buddhist concepts of karma, dharma, and samsara
*   The six realms of existence and their characteristics
*   Yama and the judgment process
*   Naraka hells (hot and cold) and their purposes
*   The Bardo/intermediate state
*   Moksha (Hindu liberation) and Nirvana (Buddhist enlightenment)
*   The Bodhisattva path and compassion
*   Reincarnation and past-life karma
*   The nature of suffering and attachment
*   Enlightenment and transcendence

**3. Factions:**
*   **Yama's Court** - Judges and administrators of karma
*   **Bodhisattvas** - Enlightened helpers delaying final liberation
*   **The Trapped Souls** - Beings cycling through realms
*   **Mara's Temptations** - Forces of delusion and attachment
*   **The Liberated** - Those who escaped, offering guidance

**4. NPCs:**
*   **Tier 1:** Yama, Chitragupta, Bodhisattva Guide, Mara, Players' Past Lives
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** Beings in various realms, souls at different karmic levels, spirits and gods

**5. Locations:** Hot Narakas, Cold Narakas, Yama's Court, Six Realms, Wheel of Samsara, Bodhi Tree, The Bardo

**6. Mechanics:**
*   Karmic balance tracking
*   Multiple lives system
*   Six realms rebirth
*   Enlightenment progress
*   Attachment mechanics
*   Bardo choices
*   Past-life echoes

**7. Themes:** Karma and consequence, the cycle of suffering, learning across lifetimes, attachment as source of suffering, compassion and wisdom, transcendence, whether escape is possible or necessary.
