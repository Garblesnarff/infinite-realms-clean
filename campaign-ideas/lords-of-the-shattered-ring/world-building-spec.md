**Lords of the Shattered Ring**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party are Tarnished exiles called back to the Lands Between to seek the shattered Elden Ring. Queen Marika shattered it, plunging the realm into war as demigod children fight over Great Runes. The party must explore a vast open world, defeat demigods, and decide whether to repair the Ring and become Elden Lord or serve outer gods.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Age of the Erdtree and Queen Marika's rule.
    *   Write the Night of Black Knives—when Godwyn was murdered.
    *   Describe the Shattering—Marika destroying the Elden Ring.
    *   Explain the Tarnished exile and their return.
    *   Detail the demigods' war over the Great Runes.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** The Golden Order (Erdtree worship), Carian Royalty (sorcerers), Volcano Manor (heretics), Ranni's conspiracy (ending the Order).

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Melina, Ranni, Godrick, Malenia, Radagon/Elden Beast.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Godrick Soldier," "Cleanrot Knight," "Black Knife Assassin," "Crucible Knight."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   Limgrave (starting), Liurnia (lakes), Caelid (rot), Leyndell (capital), Mountaintops (snow), Farum Azula (dragon temple), Erdtree (final).

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Two Great Runes collected → **THEN:** Leyndell accessible.
*   **IF:** Erdtree burned → **THEN:** Enter final area.
*   **IF:** Specific questlines completed → **THEN:** Unlock secret endings.
*   **IF:** Inherit Frenzied Flame → **THEN:** Locked into chaos ending.
