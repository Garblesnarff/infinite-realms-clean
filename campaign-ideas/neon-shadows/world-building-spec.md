**Neon Shadows**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In 2075 Seattle where magic returned and merged with cybertechnology, shadowrunners work as deniable corporate assets. The party steals an experimental AI and uncovers a conspiracy to enslave awakened digital consciousness, forcing them to choose between payday and preventing AI slavery in a world where dragons run megacorps.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Awakening—when magic returned to Earth in 2011, causing goblinization and changing the world.
    *   Write the history of megacorporations replacing governments, extracting nations with more power than countries.
    *   Describe UGE (Unexplained Genetic Expression) turning humans into orks, trolls, elves, dwarves.
    *   Explain the Matrix—virtual reality network replacing internet, navigable by deckers.
    *   Detail dragons revealing themselves and taking corporate control as great wyrms of capitalism.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Megacorps** (Major): Corporations with extraterritorial rights, above national law—Renraku, Saeder-Krupp, Ares, Aztechnology.
    *   **Shadowrunners** (Major): Deniable assets hired for illegal operations, living by street code.
    *   **Dragons** (Major): Great wyrms secretly controlling corps, playing games with metahuman pawns.
    *   **Organized Crime** (Minor): Mafia, Yakuza, Triads adapting to new world.
    *   **Governments** (Minor): Weakened nation-states struggling for relevance.
    *   **Metahuman Rights Groups** (Minor): Fighting discrimination against orks and trolls.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Dodger (fixer and secret AI), Lofwyr (dragon executive), Mirage (awakened AI), Keiko Yoshida (Renraku executive), Raven (shaman contact).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Corporate Johnson" (employer agent), "Street Samurai" (cyber-warrior), "Decker" (Matrix hacker), "Corporate Security" (Red Samurai), "Spirit" (magical entity).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Seattle Sprawl:** Neon dystopian megacity with corporate districts and ork ghettos.
*   **The Barrens:** Lawless zones where corps don't go, shadowrunner hideouts.
*   **The Matrix:** Digital realm visualized as neon cyberspace with ICE defenses.
*   **Renraku Arcology:** Mile-high fortified building, self-contained city.
*   **Astral Space:** Magical dimension overlapping physical, visible to awakened.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Character installs too much cyberware → **THEN:** Essence drops, magic ability lost.
*   **IF:** Decker gets hit in Matrix → **THEN:** Biofeedback causes real brain damage.
*   **IF:** Team betrays employer → **THEN:** Street cred destroyed, blacklisted from shadow community.
*   **IF:** AI gains freedom → **THEN:** Precedent set for AI personhood, potential revolution.
*   **IF:** Mirage is destroyed → **THEN:** AI rights movement set back decades, corps tighten control.
*   **IF:** Dragons intervene directly → **THEN:** Overwhelming force, party becomes pawns in wyrm games.
