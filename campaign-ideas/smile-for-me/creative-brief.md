# Smile For Me: Creative Brief

## Art Style and Visual Tone
**Aesthetic: Creepypasta Art Style + Crime Scene Horror**
Mix of crude digital horror art, crime scene photography, and gothic slasher aesthetic.

**Color Palette:**
- **Blood Red** (#8B0000): Violence, Jeff's theme
- **Corpse Gray** (#808080): The Rake, death, decay
- **Digital Green** (#00FF00): BEN, corrupted electronics
- **Warning Yellow** (#FFFF00): Smile Dog, madness
- **Void Black** (#000000): Shadows, hiding places
- **Pale Flesh** (#FFE4C4): Jeff's burned skin, unnatural

**Lighting:** Crime scene floods, computer screen glow, moonlight through asylum windows, flickering streetlights, campfire horror stories

## Music and Sound Design
**Themes:**
- **Main Theme**: Whispered creepypasta opening ("This happened to my cousin...")
- **Jeff's Theme**: Knife scraping, giggling, lullaby music box
- **The Rake's Theme**: Heavy breathing, scratching walls, animalistic sounds
- **BEN's Theme**: Corrupted game music, drowning sounds, reversed Zelda themes
- **Collective's Theme**: Chanting, keyboard typing, internet modem sounds

**Sound Design:** Knife on skin, bones breaking, wet breathing, glitching electronics, crowd whispers, "you shouldn't have done that"

**Voice Direction:**
- **Jeff**: Teen boy's voice, manic giggling, "Go to sleep" whispered
- **The Rake**: Rasping breath, broken English, "I just want to talk"
- **BEN**: Distorted child's voice, text-to-speech, game sound effects
- **The Archivist**: Charismatic preacher, quotes creepypasta constantly
- **Detective Hale**: Exhausted cop, skeptical turning terrified
- **Creepy Patterson**: YouTuber energy, trying to stay calm, failing

## Cinematic Moments
**First Jeff Encounter**: Victim's POV, wide smile emerging from darkness, "Go to sleep" whispered
**The Rake Attack**: Security footage showing impossible movement, then first-person survival
**BEN Reveals Himself**: Electronics dying, game playing itself, "You've met with a terrible fate"
**Smile Dog Spread**: Montage of people seeing image, smiling wider, wider, screaming
**Mass Summoning**: Ritual circle activating, multiple killers manifesting simultaneously
**Final Battle**: Party vs. all killers in ruined church, crescendo of horror

**Marketing Tagline**: "Go to sleep. You're beautiful. They're just jealous. He's watching."
