# Punk Rock Rebellion - Creative Brief

## Art Style and Visual Tone

**Overall Aesthetic:** Urban decay meets DIY culture. Graffiti everywhere, safety pins holding reality together, everything repurposed and reclaimed. Think 1970s-80s punk documentaries meets post-apocalyptic wasteland, but with vibrant street art and defiant energy.

**Visual Influences:**
- 1970s-80s punk photography
- Street art and graffiti culture
- Post-apocalyptic urban settings
- Anarchist zines and DIY aesthetics
- Punk show photography (crowded venues, energy, chaos)

## Color Palettes

**Primary:** Black (#000000), white (#FFFFFF), safety-pin silver (#C0C0C0)
**Accents:** Spray paint colors: hot pink (#FF69B4), toxic green (#00FF00), blood red (#8B0000)
**Environmental:** Concrete gray (#808080), rust (#B7410E), broken glass (#E0E0E0)

## Music and Sound Design

**Ambient:** Distant punk shows, sirens, breaking glass, chanting
**Combat:** Fast, aggressive punk rock (Ramones, Sex Pistols, Dead Kennedys, Black Flag)
**Social Scenes:** Acoustic punk, folk-punk (Against Me!, Mischief Brew)
**Tense Moments:** Post-punk, dark wave
**Triumphant:** Anthemic punk rock

**NPC Themes:**
- **Johnny Rotten-Note:** Aggressive UK punk
- **Sid Vicious:** Classic punk rock
- **Green Dawn:** Crust punk, folk-punk
- **Theory:** Anarcho-punk, political
- **Bubblegum:** Pop-punk, melodic

## Voice Direction

**Johnny:** British accent, angry, spitting words
**Sid:** Gruff but gentle, older punk's wisdom
**Green Dawn:** Earnest, passionate, environmentalist rhetoric
**Theory:** Intellectual, uses big words, condescending accidentally
**Bubblegum:** High energy, cheerful, refuses to be dismissed
**Corporate Commander:** Smooth, corporate, hints of past punk
