**Escape from Zagreus**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party are demigods escaping the Underworld despite Hades' attempts to stop them. Through roguelike runs, they receive boons from Olympian gods, fight through multiple biomes, and discover they're searching for Zagreus's mother Persephone. The family dysfunction is revealed, and they must reunite the family or forge their own path.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Greek pantheon and Underworld structure.
    *   Write Hades and Persephone's marriage and growing distance.
    *   Describe Zagreus's birth and Persephone's near-death.
    *   Explain why Hades kept the truth hidden.
    *   Detail the Olympians' interest in helping Zagreus.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** House of Hades (order), Olympians (chaos), Shades (the dead), Chthonic gods (underworld).

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Hades, Persephone, Nyx, Megaera, Thanatos, Achilles.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Shade," "Fury Sister," "Elysium Hero," "Satyr."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   Tartarus (prison), Asphodel (lava), Elysium (paradise), Temple of Styx (poison), House of Hades (hub), Persephone's Garden (goal).

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Multiple successful escapes → **THEN:** Persephone story unlocks.
*   **IF:** High relationship with NPCs → **THEN:** Unlock secrets and bonuses.
*   **IF:** Defeat Hades enough times → **THEN:** He respects Zagreus, family reunites.
