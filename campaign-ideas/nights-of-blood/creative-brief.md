# Creative Brief: Nights of Blood

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Gothic Punk with Modern Noir (Vampire: The Masquerade-inspired)

Inspired by the aesthetic fusion of gothic horror and 1990s punk in *Vampire: The Masquerade*, film noir, and urban decay. This style captures eternal beings hiding among mortals, blood-soaked political intrigue, and the tension between humanity and the Beast.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Duality:** Beautiful monsters, elegant violence, humanity within horror
*   **Gothic-Punk Fusion:** Victorian elegance mixed with modern urban decay
*   **Nighttime Always:** Everything occurs at night—no daytime scenes
*   **Blood and Politics:** Violence is elegant, politics are deadly

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Gothic-punk, personal horror, vampire society, eternal night, blood politics, Beast within, Masquerade
*   **Feeling:** The struggle to remain human while being a predator. Beauty concealing monstrosity. Power corrupting over centuries.

**2. Color Palette:**
*   **Night Dominance:** Deep blacks, midnight blues, blood reds
*   **Gothic Elements:** Deep purples, crimson, bone white, tarnished silver
*   **Punk Touches:** Neon signs, urban decay, graffiti colors
*   **Blood:** Always present—crimson on lips, staining clothes, pooling
*   **No Daylight:** Every scene is night, artificial light only
*   **Prompt Example:** "...a color palette of deep blacks and midnight blues dominating. Blood red accents on lips, staining elegant clothes. Gothic purples and crimson velvet. Neon signs provide harsh artificial light. Everything is nighttime—no sunlight ever. Urban decay meets Victorian elegance."

**3. Character Design:**
*   **Kindred:** Pale skin (corpse-like), elegant clothing mixing periods, predatory beauty
*   **Clan Variations:** Ventrue in suits, Toreador in artistic fashion, Nosferatu hideously deformed, Brujah in punk gear
*   **The Beast:** Eyes flash red when hungry/angry, fangs extend, inhuman grace
*   **Humanity Loss:** Characters become more corpse-like as Humanity drops
*   **Fashion:** Mix of period clothing—Victorian coats with modern jeans, timeless elegance
*   **Prompt Example:** "A Ventrue vampire in expensive three-piece suit, but corpse-pale. Eyes occasionally flash red with hunger. Fangs barely visible. Moves with inhuman grace. Behind them, modern Chicago at night—neon and decay. Blood stains their sleeve cuff. Expression shows the struggle between human conscience and predatory instinct."

**4. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Elysium:** Grand locations like art museums, theaters, with vampires in formal attire
*   **Urban Night:** Modern city streets at night, neon lights, shadows deep
*   **Gothic Architecture:** Old churches, mansions, crypts mixed with modern buildings
*   **Havens:** Personal domains—some elegant, some decrepit, all windowless
*   **Anarch Territory:** Urban decay, graffiti, punk aesthetic, squatted buildings
*   **Prompt Example:** "Chicago at eternal night. Skyscrapers lit by office lights and neon. Dark alleys where predators hunt. An old gothic church stands among modern buildings. Vampires move through shadows. The Chicago Art Institute serves as Elysium—elegant vampires in period and modern dress discussing politics. Everything bathed in artificial night light."

**5. Vampire Powers and Violence:**
*   **Disciplines:** Supernatural speed blurs, mind control shown through glazed eyes, blood magic with crimson energy
*   **Feeding:** Elegant horror—fangs in neck, blood drinking, victim in thrall or terror
*   **The Beast:** Monstrous transformation—eyes fully red, features distort, movement becomes animalistic
*   **Violence:** Elegant but brutal—stake through heart, decapitation, burning, always theatrical
*   **Prompt Example:** "A vampire using Celerity—supernatural speed. Multiple motion-blurred images show them moving impossibly fast. Their opponent uses Presence—glowing aura of command. Another vampire feeds from mortal's neck, blood trickling down. In background, a Nosferatu's monstrous features are half-hidden in shadow. Gothic horror meets modern violence."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Noir Lighting:** High contrast, dramatic shadows, single light sources
*   **Neon and Artificial:** Harsh fluorescent, colorful neon, no natural light
*   **Candlelight:** In traditional settings, warm but Gothic
*   **Eyes Glow:** Vampire eyes reflect light like predators, glow red when Beast emerges
*   **Prompt Example:** "...lit with film noir dramatic contrast. Harsh neon light from club signs casts red and purple. Deep shadows hide predators. Candlelight in Elysium creates warm but Gothic atmosphere. A vampire's eyes reflect light—predatory eye-shine. When angry, eyes glow fully red. No sunlight anywhere—eternal night."

## Voice

A seductive yet tormented voice, cultured but hiding monstrosity. The pacing is measured, aristocratic, with sudden passion. The tone shifts between elegant discourse and bestial hunger. Occasional slips into archaic language from older vampires. Underlying theme of struggling to remain human while being a monster. The voice carries centuries of experience but also loss of innocence. Whispers about blood, power, and the Beast. Role: The voice of immortal predators hiding in plain sight.
