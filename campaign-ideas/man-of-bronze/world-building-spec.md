### **World-Building Specification Brief: Man of Bronze**

**1. Core Concept:**
A team of specialists led by a superman trained from birth to be perfect tackle threats too strange for normal authorities using science, gadgets, and coordinated teamwork, operating from an Arctic fortress and Manhattan headquarters.

**2. Lore & History:**
*   Detail Doc Savage's training from infancy—physical perfection, mental disciplines, scientific mastery
*   Explain the Fortress of Solitude's construction and purpose
*   Describe the Lost Tribe of Mayans and Doc's sworn duty to protect them
*   Write about Doc's father and the legacy he left
*   Detail the rise of superscience in this world—where impossible becomes possible
*   Explain the ethical philosophy behind the "reform through brain surgery" for criminals

**3. Factions:**
*   **Doc Savage and the Fabulous Five** (Major) - Heroic specialists, science-based problem solvers
*   **The Reformed Criminals** (Minor) - Villains "cured" at the Fortress, some relapse
*   **Mad Scientists** (Major) - Pervert science for personal gain, create superweapons
*   **The Lost Tribe** (Minor) - Mayan descendants, guard ancient technology
*   **International Criminals** (Major) - Masterminds seeking world domination
*   **Law Enforcement** (Minor) - Work with Doc, sometimes reluctantly
*   **Scientific Community** (Minor) - Peers who respect or envy Doc's genius

**4. NPCs:**
*   **Tier 1:** Doc Savage, Monk Mayfair, Ham Brooks, Long Tom Roberts, Renny Renwick, Johnny Littlejohn, John Sunlight, Patricia Savage
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Mad Scientist," "Reformed Criminal," "Tribal Elder," "Police Inspector," "Henchman," "Scientific Rival," "Inventor," "Foreign Agent"

**5. Locations:**
*   **Fortress of Solitude** - Arctic hideaway, laboratory, library, prison, invention vault
*   **86th Floor Headquarters** - Manhattan skyscraper, communication hub, garage
*   **The Lost Valley** - Prehistoric preserve, dinosaurs, ancient ruins
*   **Phantom City** - Underground metropolis, advanced technology
*   **Doc's Garage** - Specialized vehicles and equipment
*   **Villain Lairs** - Mad science laboratories, death trap facilities

**6. Causality:**
*   IF villain escapes Fortress prison THEN returns more dangerous and seeking revenge
*   IF superweapon falls into wrong hands THEN global catastrophe imminent
*   IF team splits up THEN each specialist vulnerable in others' fields
*   IF Doc's identity compromised THEN allies and Lost Tribe endangered
*   IF non-lethal philosophy broken THEN team dynamic and moral authority shattered
*   IF Fortress secrets revealed THEN every criminal seeks its technology

**7. MacGuffins & Artifacts:**
*   **Mayan Gold Artifacts** - Ancient power sources
*   **Death Ray Prototype** - Too dangerous to use, locked in Fortress
*   **Superscience Inventions** - Anesthetic gas, mercy bullets, super-flashlights
*   **The Autogyro** - Advanced aircraft
*   **Reform Machine** - Controversial brain surgery device
*   **Ancient Tablets** - Contain lost scientific knowledge

**8. Superscience Inventions:**
*   Death rays, invisibility devices, mind control helmets, robot servants, dinosaur DNA weapons, seismic machines, weather control, submarine cars, bulletproof fabrics, super-strength serums, time viewers
