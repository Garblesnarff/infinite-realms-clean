# Journey to the Western Paradise - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Chinese Fantasy Opera

**Visual Style:** Traditional Chinese art meets wire-fu action. Ink painting aesthetics, vibrant colors, impossible martial arts.

**Color Palettes:**
- **Heaven:** Gold, jade, cloud white, celestial blue
- **Earth:** Earthy browns, bamboo green, rice paddy patterns
- **Demon Realms:** Dark purples, poisonous greens, blood reds
- **Sun Wukong:** Orange-gold fur, red/gold outfit, crimson cape

**Music:** Traditional Chinese (erhu, pipa, dizi) meets orchestral. Peking opera influences. Each character has instrument theme.

**Character Themes:**
- Sun Wukong: Energetic percussion, monkey sounds, mischievous
- Tang Sanzang: Gentle Buddhist chants, peaceful
- Zhu Bajie: Comic bassoon, food sounds
- Sha Wujing: Steady drums, reliable rhythm

**Voice Direction:**
- Sun Wukong: Cocky, theatrical, Jackie Chan energy
- Tang Sanzang: Gentle, naive, compassionate
- Zhu Bajie: Gluttonous, lustful, cowardly but lovable
- Guanyin: Serene, motherly, infinite patience

**Cinematic Moments:**
- Sun Wukong's cloud-somersault (clouds stretching beneath feet, impossible speed)
- Staff extending from needle-size to mountain-tall
- 72 transformations mid-combat
- Buddhist sutras manifesting as golden light restraining demons
