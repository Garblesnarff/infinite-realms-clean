**Breath of the Slayer Corps**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In Taisho-era fantasy realm, demons hunt humans at night. The party joins the Demon Slayer Corps after their families are slaughtered, mastering Breathing Techniques to hunt demons and seek a cure for their transformed loved ones. They must face the Twelve Demon Moons and ultimately the Demon King who seeks to conquer sunlight.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the origin of demons—how Muzan became the first demon a thousand years ago.
    *   Write the history of the Demon Slayer Corps and their centuries-long war.
    *   Describe the development of Breathing Techniques from Sun Breathing.
    *   Explain why demons cannot walk in sunlight and Muzan's obsession with overcoming this.
    *   Detail the tragic past of the original Sun Breathing user who nearly killed Muzan.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Demon Slayer Corps** (Major): Secret organization hunting demons, led by Ubuyashiki family.
    *   **The Twelve Demon Moons** (Major): Muzan's strongest demons ranked Upper and Lower.
    *   **The Swordsmith Village** (Minor): Craftsmen who forge nichirin blades.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Ubuyashiki (Corps Master), The Nine Hashira (Pillars), Muzan Kibutsuji (Demon King), Transformed Family Member, Six Upper Moons.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Corps Member" (various ranks), "Lower Moon Demon," "Wild Demon," "Swordsmith," "Civilian."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Fujikasane Mountain:** Final Selection site with captured demons, wisteria barriers.
*   **Infinity Castle:** Dimensional fortress with impossible architecture, shifting rooms.
*   **Swordsmith Village:** Hidden by misdirection, hot springs, forges.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party unlocks Total Concentration Constant → **THEN:** Permanent stat boost, can fight Upper Moons.
*   **IF:** Party member manifests Demon Slayer Mark → **THEN:** Massive power boost but lifespan reduced.
*   **IF:** Transformed family member walks in sunlight → **THEN:** Muzan targets them, becomes key to cure/his evolution.
*   **IF:** Muzan conquers sunlight → **THEN:** Emergency endgame—he becomes invincible, must be killed before sunrise.
