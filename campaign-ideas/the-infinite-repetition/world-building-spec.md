# World-Building Specification: The Infinite Repetition

## Core Concept
Pop Art dimension of mass-produced reality where repetition creates truth and uniqueness is heresy. Quest for the Original in world of infinite copies.

## Lore
- **Origin**: Created when commercial culture achieved critical mass and became dimension
- **The Warhol Factory**: Silk-screening deity manufacturing repetitive reality
- **The Original**: Legendary unique artifact, only non-reproduced item
- **Mass Production Philosophy**: Replication as equality, uniqueness as inequality

## Factions
- **The Factory Workers** (maintain repetitive reality)
- **The Original Hunters** (seek the unique)
- **The Marilyn Collective** (embrace duplication as identity)
- **Unique Una and the Singular Resistance** (fight for individuality)

## NPCs
- **Grid Guide Gary #23**: Identical guide (one of thousands)
- **Andy's Echo**: Manufacturing god
- **The Marilyn Collective**: Thousand Marilyn copies
- **Unique Una**: Only non-duplicate, hunted
- **The Original**: One-of-a-kind artifact

## Locations
Seven commercial realms based on Pop Art icons plus Grid City and Warhol Factory.

## Mechanics
- **Repetition Tolerance**: Madness from too much sameness
- **Copy Detection**: Spot differences in identical things
- **Commercial Navigation**: Brands as maps
- **Serial Combat**: Identical enemies
- **Limited Edition Powers**: Uniqueness grants bonuses
