### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**The Titan Vanguard**

**1. Core Concept:**
Massive Titans emerge from ocean rift. Party pilots giant Warforged Colossi via mental Drift. Must close the rift to save the world.

**2. Lore:**
*   The Breach—planar rift opening to alien dimension.
*   Titans—massive aberrations designed as terraforming bioweapons.
*   The Drift—mental bonding technology allowing two pilots to control one Colossus.
*   The Vanguard—international military organization defending humanity.

**3. Factions:**
*   **The Vanguard** — Colossus pilots and support staff, humanity's defenders.
*   **The Politicians** — Short-sighted leaders who want to defund the program.
*   **The Alien Hive** — Extradimensional conquerors sending Titans.

**4. Locations:**
*   **Vanguard Headquarters (The Shatterdome)** — Massive base housing Colossi.
*   **Coastal Cities** — Battlegrounds where Titans emerge.
*   **The Breach** — Ocean rift at the sea floor.
*   **The Alien Homeworld** — Glimpsed through the rift.

**5. Causality:**
*   IF Titans aren't stopped, THEN cities are destroyed, civilians die.
*   IF Drift compatibility is low, THEN pilots fail in combat.
*   IF the rift is sealed, THEN Titan attacks end, world is saved.
