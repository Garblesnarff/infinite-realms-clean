### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

**Red Winter Rising**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The players participate in the Russian Revolution as the Romanov dynasty falls, the Bolsheviks seize power promising peace and bread, and Russia descends into brutal civil war between Reds and Whites while Lenin builds history's first communist state through revolutionary terror.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail tsarist Russia's problems that led to revolution: autocracy, poverty, WWI disaster.
    *   Write about the February Revolution's spontaneous uprising and Romanov abdication.
    *   Describe Lenin's April Theses and Bolshevik strategy to seize power.
    *   Explain the Civil War: Reds, Whites, foreign intervention, brutality on all sides.
    *   Detail War Communism, Red Terror, and building the Soviet state.
    *   Write about the Revolution's contradictions: liberation rhetoric and authoritarian reality.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Bolsheviks** (Major): Goals—Socialist revolution, one-party rule, new world order. Revolutionary idealism and authoritarian methods.
    *   **The Whites** (Major): Goals—Restore order, defeat Bolshevism. Diverse coalition of monarchists, democrats, opportunists.
    *   **The Mensheviks/SRs** (Minor—Suppressed): Goals—Democratic socialism. Betrayed by Bolshevik authoritarianism.
    *   **The People** (Major—Suffering): Goals—Peace, bread, land. Caught between warring factions.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Vladimir Lenin, Tsar Nicholas II, Leon Trotsky, Alexandra Kollontai
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Bolshevik Revolutionary", "Red Army Soldier", "White Officer", "Peasant", "Worker", "Secret Police", "Revolutionary Idealist"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Winter Palace:** Tsarist symbol stormed by revolution.
*   **Smolny Institute:** Bolshevik headquarters organizing new state.
*   **Ekaterinburg:** Romanov execution site.
*   **Kronstadt:** Revolutionary sailors rebelling against Bolshevik betrayal.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Triggers:** IF February Revolution THEN tsar falls. IF October Revolution THEN Bolsheviks seize power. IF Romanovs executed THEN Civil War intensifies. IF War Communism implemented THEN famine results. IF Kronstadt rebels THEN Revolution's betrayal becomes undeniable.
