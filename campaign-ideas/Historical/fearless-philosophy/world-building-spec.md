### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**Fearless: The Philosophy of Strength**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Huo Yuanjia's arrogance leads to tragedy when he accidentally kills an opponent and is secretly poisoned. The party supports him through a spiritual journey learning that philosophy matters more than strength, culminating in an international tournament where they must prove this while uncovering the poisoning conspiracy.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail early 1900s Shanghai and the tension between Chinese tradition and foreign influence.
    *   Write about the philosophy of Chinese martial arts—protecting the weak vs. proving the strong.
    *   Describe the various international martial arts styles competing in the tournament.
    *   Explain the business conspiracy behind weakening Chinese martial artists.
    *   Detail the founding philosophy of Jingwu Association.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:**
    *   **Jingwu Association** - Goal: Teach martial philosophy alongside technique.
    *   **Foreign Business Interests** - Goal: Weaken Chinese cultural institutions to ease colonization.
    *   **Traditional Chinese Schools** - Goal: Maintain cultural pride while adapting to modern threats.
    *   **Honorable Foreign Martial Artists** - Goal: Cultural exchange and mutual respect.

**4. NPC Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Huo Yuanjia, Moon, Master Chin, Tanaka, Anno.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Jingwu student," "Foreign martial artist (various styles)," "Shanghai businessman," "Rural villager."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Rural Village:**
        *   Landmarks: Moon's family farm, meditation pond, training field with simple equipment.
        *   Encounters: Farm work teaching humility, philosophical conversations, healing from poison.
    *   **The International Arena:**
        *   Landmarks: Main platform, style-specific training areas, diplomatic reception halls.
        *   Encounters: Tournament brackets, philosophical debates, uncovering conspiracy evidence.
    *   **Jingwu Association:**
        *   Landmarks: Training halls, philosophy classroom, Huo's office with calligraphy.
        *   Encounters: Teaching students, planning for tournament, investigating poisoning.

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Huo completes his philosophical transformation → Can defeat Anno despite being poisoned.
*   **IF:** The conspiracy is exposed → Foreign sponsors lose credibility, Tanaka becomes ally.
*   **IF:** Party shows respect to defeated opponents → Gain their friendship and support.
*   **IF:** Huo dies from poison → His legacy lives through Jingwu and inspires the party to continue.
*   **IF:** Party defeats opponents with arrogance → Perpetuates cycle of conflict, hollow victory.
