# The Art of War - World-Building Specification

## Spring and Autumn Period (771-476 BCE)
Zhou Dynasty collapsing, regional states (Wu, Chu, Yue, etc.) warring for dominance. Philosophy flowering: Confucius, Laozi contemporaries.

## Kingdoms
**Wu**: Southeastern state, naval power, Sun Tzu's employer
**Chu**: Large central state, Wu's main rival
**Yue**: Southern neighbor, eventually destroys Wu
**Jin, Qi, Others**: Northern powers, occasional allies/enemies

## Warfare Evolution
Transitioning from chariot warfare to infantry. Professional armies replacing aristocratic warriors. Strategy becoming science.

## Philosophy Schools
**Legalism**: Law and order through strict rules (Sun Tzu influenced)
**Confucianism**: Ethics, ritual, social harmony
**Taoism**: Natural way, going with flow (Sun Tzu's water metaphors)
**Military Science**: Sun Tzu pioneering field

## The Art of War Content

**13 Chapters, Core Principles:**
1. Planning: Calculate before fighting
2. Waging War: Speed and economy
3. Strategic Attack: Win without fighting
4. Positioning: Invincibility through defense
5. Momentum: Timing and force combination
6. Weak/Strong Points: Appear where unexpected
7. Maneuvering: Indirect approach
8. Variation: Adapt to situation
9. Army on March: Reading terrain/enemy
10. Terrain: Six types, tactical use
11. Nine Situations: Psychological states
12. Attack by Fire: Destructive tactics
13. Spies: Intelligence superiority

## Historical Impact
- Used by: Mao Zedong, Vietnam's Vo Nguyen Giap, Japanese samurai
- Business: Applied to competition, negotiation
- Sports: Coaching strategy
- Translation: Available in every major language
- Influence: 2,500 years of military thought

## Causality Chains
1. **Concubine Demonstration**: If Sun Tzu shows mercy, not appointed general
2. **Fu Chai's Folly**: If listens to Sun Tzu, Wu survives
3. **Sparing Goujian**: If Yue King killed, different history
4. **Book Completion**: If unwritten, strategic knowledge lost
5. **Student Disciples**: If untaught, wisdom dies with Sun Tzu
