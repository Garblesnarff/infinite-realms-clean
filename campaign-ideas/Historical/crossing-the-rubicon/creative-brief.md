# Crossing the Rubicon - Creative Brief

## Art Style
**Roman Epic Grandeur**: Classical sculpture aesthetics, Roman architecture, legion formations. Inspired by: HBO's *Rome*, Colleen McCullough's novels, classical Roman art.

## Visual Design
**Caesar**: Balding, athletic, charismatic. Purple-bordered toga (consul), golden laurel wreath, scarlet paludamentum (general's cloak). Evolution: Ambitious politician → Conquering general → Purple-clad dictator.

**Roman Legions**: Lorica segmentata (segmented armor), gladius, scutum (shield), disciplined formations.

## Color Palettes
- **Roman**: SPQR red, imperial purple, toga white
- **Gaul**: Tribal earth tones, woad blue war paint
- **Civil War**: Roman red vs. Roman red (tragedy), Senate gold
- **Assassination**: Toga white bloodied, marble red-stained

## Music
**Epic orchestral**: Roman military marches, triumphal fanfares, tragic strings (civil war). Instruments suggesting antiquity: Horns, drums, classical chorus.

## Voice Direction
**Caesar**: Commanding, patrician Latin accent (British RP equivalent), intelligent, capable of warmth and steel. Arrogant confidence masking calculation.

## Cinematic Moments
- Alesia's double walls: Engineering marvel
- Rubicon crossing: Single fateful step
- Pharsalus: Roman killing Roman
- Caesar and dead Pompey: Respect for fallen rival
- Ides of March: 23 knives, betrayal, "Et tu?"
