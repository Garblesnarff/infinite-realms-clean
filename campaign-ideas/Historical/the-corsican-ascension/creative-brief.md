# The Corsican Ascension - Creative Brief

## Art Style
**Napoleonic Epic**: Romantic paintings style (Jacques-Louis David, Antoine-Jean Gros), grand battle panoramas, imperial splendor. Inspired by: Period paintings, epic films like *War and Peace*.

## Visual Design
**Napoleon**: Evolution from thin artillery officer → Imperial regalia. Iconic: Bicorne hat, grey coat, hand-in-vest pose.
**Battles**: Massive scale, thousands of troops, smoke, thunder, organized chaos.

## Color Palettes
- **Imperial**: Navy blue, gold trim, white (French tricolor)
- **Battle**: Gunpowder smoke grey, blood red, mud brown
- **Glory**: Golden victory light, marble white
- **Exile**: Atlantic grey, isolation blues

## Music
**Romantic orchestral**: Beethoven-era (initially dedicated Eroica to Napoleon), military marches, la Marseillaise, dramatic symphonic scope. Battles: Thunderous percussion, brass fanfares. Tragedy: Strings, minor keys.

## Voice Direction
**Napoleon**: French accent, authoritative, rapid-fire thinking, passionate. Evolution: Idealistic → Imperious → Resigned.

## Cinematic Moments
- Toulon: Young Napoleon's first triumph
- Austerlitz: The sun breaking through ("The Sun of Austerlitz")
- Retreat from Moscow: Frozen horror
- Waterloo: La Garde Meurt (The Guard Dies)
- St. Helena: Caged eagle dictating memoirs
