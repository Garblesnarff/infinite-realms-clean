# Campaign Bible: War of the Elements

## 1. DEEP LORE & HISTORY
[TAG: LORE_CORE]

### Origin Myth: The Astra-Panopticon
In the Beginning, there was only the Screaming Chaosâ€”a roiling, sentient entropy that sought to devour itself. To impose order, the Prime Architect (an entity now forgotten) did not create the world; they trapped the Chaos. The twelve Constellations are not merely stars; they are the great rivets of a cosmic cage called the *Astra-Panopticon*. Zodiactica exists on the "lid" of this cage. The magic of the Zodiac is the waste-heat of the cage's bars holding back the entropy. The "Void" is not an invader; it is the prisoner waking up and picking the lock.

### Timeline of Zodiactica
1.  **Year 1 (The Great Riveting):** The stars are hammered into place; the Screaming Chaos is silenced.
2.  **Year 400 (The Age of Mud):** Before the elements separated, Zodiactica was a sludge-filled purgatory.
3.  **Year 890 (The First Schism):** Fire and Water attempt to divorce; the continent cracks, forming the Steam Jungle.
4.  **Year 1050 (The Treaty of Sol):** The Twelve Signs sign the compact of eternal interdependence.
5.  **Year 1500 (The Day of Floating Mountains):** Gravity briefly reversed in Libra territories; cities were tethered to the ground by chains.
6.  **Year 2020 (The Weeping of Virgo):** A plague of uncontrollable empathy swept the land; armies laid down weapons to hug.
7.  **Year 2300 (The Invention of the Calendar):** Time is standardized, angering the Retrograde Historians.
8.  **Year 2495 (The Birth of Unity):** The first multi-elemental child is rumored to have been born and immediately eaten by a griffin (False Rumor).
9.  **Year 2500 (3 Months Ago - The Elemental Schism):** The stars dim. The lock is picked. The factions fracture.
10. **Year 2500 (Current Day):** The Modality Refuge is declared the last neutral ground.

### The Metaphysical "Why"
The conflict exists because the **Cognitive Anchor** has slipped. The collective belief of the mortals powers the Constellations. By convincing the elements to hate one another, the Void Cult is starving the stars of belief, thereby weakening the cage's rivets.

**Type:** Defensive Siege Oligarchy

**Asset:** The Deep-Iron Veins (Indestructible metal).
**Rivals:** The Ethereal Covenant, The Blazing Concordat.

### The Ethereal Covenant (Canon)
**Type:** Technocratic Spy Network
**Leader:** Twins Castor & Pollux (Gemini)
**Asset:** The Cloud-Roads (Instant travel).
**Rivals:** The Granite Alliance, The Tidal Dominion.

### The Tidal Dominion (Canon)
**Type:** Emotional Hivemind Collective
**Leader:** Dreamer Neptus (Pisces)
**Asset:** The Memory Water (Liquid information storage).
**Rivals:** The Blazing Concordat, The Ethereal Covenant.

### The Ophiuchus 13th (Original)
**Type:** Bio-Magical Cult
**Leader:** Serpent-Bearer Asclepius
**Asset:** Serum of Amalgamation (Allows user to graft monster parts).
**Rivals:** All Canon Factions.
**Goal:** To forcefully stitch the world back together by stitching *people* together.

### The Retrograde Historians (Original)
**Type:** Temporal Terrorists
**Leader:** Prefect Zyx (Living time backwards)
**Asset:** Chrono-Dust (Ages weapons to dust instantly).
**Rivals:** The Ethereal Covenant (Gemini specifically).
**Goal:** Restore the timeline by erasing the last 50 years.

### The Cusp Walkers (Original)
**Type:** Planar Smugglers
**Leader:** "Monday" (No fixed form)
**Asset:** Phase-Keys (Open doors in solid walls).
**Rivals:** Granite Alliance (Border control).
**Goal:** Profit from the borders; they exist in the seconds between sign changes.

### The Ephemeris Auditors (Original)
**Type:** Magical Bureaucracy
**Leader:** Head Auditor Quill
**Asset:** Red Tape (Literal magical tape that freezes foes).
**Rivals:** Blazing Concordat (Too chaotic).
**Goal:** To audit the magic usage of the war and issue fines for "Elemental Waste."

### The Society of Weeping Spoons (Original)
**Type:** Surrealist Dadaist Rebels
**Leader:** The Great Utensil
**Asset:** Potions of Absurdity (Turns lava into bubbles).
**Rivals:** Serious people.
**Goal:** To prove the war is a joke by turning battlefields into banquets.

### The Void-Touched Sommeliers (Original)
**Type:** Nihilistic Hedonists
**Leader:** Baron Empty
**Asset:** Void Wine (Erases memories).
**Rivals:** Tidal Dominion (Pollutes their water).
**Goal:** To drink the world into oblivion, one sip at a time.

## 4. LOCATIONS
[TAG: LOCATIONS_MAIN]

### Zone 1: The Scorch (Fire Territories)
*   **The Solar Citadel:** A palace of gold and brass, blindingly bright. **Sensory:** Smell of melting metal, sound of constant trumpets.
*   **The Ash Plains:** Grey dunes of bone and ash. **Sensory:** Taste of soot, silence except for wind.
*   **The Magma Arteries:** Rivers of lava used for transport. **Sensory:** Radiant heat, bubbling thick liquid.
*   **The Phoenix Nests:** High peaks where birds burn. **Sensory:** Screeching, smell of ozone.
*   **The Spark-Market:** Where fire is sold in jars. **Sensory:** Crackling, dry heat.

### Zone 2: The Bedrock (Earth Territories)
*   **The Iron Vaults:** Underground cities. **Sensory:** Claustrophobic, smell of wet stone and rust.
*   **The Crystal Gardens:** Plants made of gems. **Sensory:** Chiming sounds when wind blows, refraction of light.
*   **The Mud-Wallows:** Trench warfare zones. **Sensory:** Sucking sounds, smell of rot and clay.
*   **The Geometric Canyons:** Perfectly square canyons carved by Virgo. **Sensory:** Unnatural echoes, vertigo.
*   **The Stalactite Throne:** The inverted palace. **Sensory:** Dripping water, cold drafts.

### Zone 3: The Zephyr (Air Territories)
*   **Cloud-9:** A casino floating on a cumulus. **Sensory:** Thin air, sound of slot machines and harp music.
*   **The Whispering Bridges:** Invisible bridges made of wind. **Sensory:** Vertigo, roaring wind in ears.
*   **The Lightning Spire:** Power plant. **Sensory:** Hair standing on end, ozone smell.
*   **The Vacuum Pockets:** Dead zones with no air. **Sensory:** Silence, suffocation panic.
*   **The Glass Libraries:** Floating archives. **Sensory:** Reflection of sky, sound of turning pages.

### Zone 4: The Abyss (Water Territories)
*   **The Coral Catacombs:** Underwater graveyard. **Sensory:** Muffled sound, pressure, salty taste.
*   **The Mirror Surface:** Perfectly calm lake. **Sensory:** Uncanny stillness, smell of lilies.
*   **The Boiling Trench:** Where fire meets water. **Sensory:** Steam burns, hissing sound.
*   **The Tear-Falls:** Waterfalls of pure sorrow. **Sensory:** Hearing sobbing in the water, damp cold.
*   **The Ink-Depths:** Scorpio's dark zone. **Sensory:** Total darkness, bioluminescent flashes.

### Zone 5: The Modality Refuge (Neutral)
*   **The Cardinal District:** Military sharp lines. **Sensory:** Marching boots, smell of polished steel.
*   **The Fixed District:** Bunkers. **Sensory:** Heavy doors slamming, smell of stew.
*   **The Mutable District:** Tents and shifting walls. **Sensory:** Incense, confusion, laughter.
*   **The Void-Alley:** Slums where reality flickers. **Sensory:** Static noise, greyscale vision.
*   **The Star-Plaza:** Center of the city. **Sensory:** Hope, smell of street food.

## 8. MECHANICS
[TAG: RULES_CUSTOM]

### Elemental Affinity & Combo System
*   **Affinity:** Players pick a Primary Element. They gain Resistance to it but Vulnerability to the opposite.
*   **Combo:**
    *   Fire + Earth = **Magma** (Restrained + Fire Dmg).
    *   Air + Water = **Storm** (Push 10ft + Lightning Dmg).
    *   Fire + Air = **Plasma** (Penetrates AC).
    *   Earth + Water = **Mud** (Difficult Terrain).

### The Constellation Dimming (Global Clock)
*   **Stage 1 (100-75%):** Normal Magic.
*   **Stage 2 (75-50%):** Spells cost +1 slot level or HP.
*   **Stage 3 (50-25%):** Magic has 50% fail chance. Shadows grow longer.
*   **Stage 4 (<25%):** Only Cantrips work. Void monsters spawn freely.

### Sanity: "The Void Call"
*   When witnessing Void horrors, make WIS save.
*   **Failure:** Gain "Void Point." At 5 points, player becomes an NPC Cultist (temporarily).

## 9. SESSION GUIDE
[TAG: DM_GUIDE]

### Roadmap (Compressed 1-12)
*   **Session 1: The Schism.** Party meets at the border. Combat with mixed elementals. Run to Refuge.
*   **Session 2: The Modality Refuge.** Establish base. Meet Faction Recruiters. Choose path.
*   **Session 3: Infiltration.** Enter Fire or Earth territory. Stealth mission.
*   **Session 4: The Horror.** Discover the first Amplifier. Combat with a boss using it.
*   **Session 5: The Heist.** Steal the Amplifier. Chase scene (Lava chase or Airship chase).
*   **Session 6: The Betrayal.** The faction turns on them. Party is outlawed.
*   **Session 7: The Water/Air Connection.** Go to the opposing faction. Realize they are manipulated too.
*   **Session 8: The Void Reveal.** Find a Void Cult cell. Fight Nihil's lieutenant.
*   **Session 9: The Dreamscape.** Enter Pisces' mind to free the Water signs. Surreal dungeon.
*   **Session 10: The Summit.** Diplomacy skill challenge. Unite the leaders.
*   **Session 11: The Void Temple.** Dungeon crawl in the anti-space. Boss: The Un-Thing.
*   **Session 12: The Re-Riveting.** Final Boss: Nihil/Void Entity. Use Amplifiers to seal the cage.
