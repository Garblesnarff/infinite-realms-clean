# Fate of the Eternal City - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Roman Epic Grandeur

**Visual:** Classical Roman art meets Renaissance paintings of Aeneid. Maritime adventure, Mediterranean beauty, Italian landscapes.

**Palettes:**
- **Troy:** Fire orange, ash black, destruction red
- **Carthage:** Purple (Tyrian dye), gold, marble white
- **Underworld:** Shadow gray, Styx black, Elysium gold
- **Italy:** Fertile green, Roman red, bronze

**Music:** Latin choruses, Roman martial drums, Mediterranean strings. Tragic opera influences.

**Themes:**
- **Aeneas:** Duty over desire, noble suffering
- **Dido:** Passion, betrayal, tragedy (heartbreaking strings)
- **Rome:** Triumphant brass, destiny manifesting

**Voices:**
- **Aeneas:** Duty-bound, weary (Russell Crowe)
- **Dido:** Passionate queen (Cate Blanchett)
- **Jupiter:** Ultimate authority (James Earl Jones)

**Cinematic:**
- Troy burning (flashback)
- Dido's suicide pyre seen from ships
- Underworld katabasis
- Final duel with Turnus
