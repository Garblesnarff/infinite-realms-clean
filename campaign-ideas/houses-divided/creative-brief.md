# Creative Brief: Houses Divided

## Visual Art Direction
- War-torn landscapes divided by visible borders
- Each house has distinct architectural style
- No-Man's-Lands are devastated wastelands
- Ruins of Thirteenth House in center

**House Visual Themes:**
- First: Individual monuments, personal expression
- Second: Golden wealth, vaults, treasure
- Third: Roads, messages, communication towers
- Fourth: Homes, families, hearths
- Fifth: Colorful art, creativity everywhere
- Sixth: Military efficiency, labor camps
- Seventh: Partnership symbols, paired architecture
- Eighth: Gothic death imagery, transformation
- Ninth: Libraries, temples, academies
- Tenth: Hierarchical towers, capitals
- Eleventh: Communal circles, gathering spaces
- Twelfth: Hidden, shadowy, mysterious

## Music Direction
- Each house has distinct cultural music
- War drums underlying all themes
- Peace negotiations: Twelve themes attempting harmony
- Thirteenth House theme: All twelve combined

## Voice Acting
- Each leader embodies their philosophy
- Passionate belief in their approach
- None purely villainous - all think they're right
- House-Walkers: Weary mediators

## Atmosphere
- Political thriller meets military campaign
- No clear good guys or bad guys
- Tragedy of ideological warfare
- Hope of rediscovering unity

## Marketing Taglines
- "Twelve territories. Twelve truths. One war."
- "When philosophy becomes war, mediators become heroes."
- "The forgotten house holds the key to peace."
