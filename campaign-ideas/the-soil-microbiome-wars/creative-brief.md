# Creative Brief: The Soil Microbiome Wars

## Visual Art Direction
- **Color Palette:** Earth browns, bacterial colony colors (white, yellow, pink), contamination sickly green, mineral grays, organic matter dark brown
- **Environments:** Soil particles as massive boulders, root systems as living cities, fungal hyphae as bridges/highways, water films as coating
- **Characters:** Rod/spherical bacteria, filamentous actinomycetes, branching fungal hyphae, segmented nematode worms
- **Scale:** Pore spaces as caverns, root hairs as towers

## Music and Sound Design
- **Main Theme:** Underground, earthy, pulsing with hidden life
- **Bacterial themes:** Different species have different musical motifs based on function
- **War Theme:** Chemical warfare represented by discordant mixing of competing motifs
- **Fungal Theme:** Slow, spreading, network-like interconnected melodies
- **Environmental:** Soil shifting, water moving, root growth, decomposition

## Voice Acting
- **Root-Mother Rhiza:** Ancient, plant-connected wisdom
- **Invader-Prime:** Aggressive, enhanced, believes in dominance
- **Mycelial Elder:** Vast consciousness, speaks for entire network
- **Rootwise:** Enthusiastic gnome geomancer
