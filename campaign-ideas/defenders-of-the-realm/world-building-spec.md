**Defenders of the Realm**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** When extraplanar entities known as the Void-Touched begin abducting citizens across the realm, the Defenders Initiative forms as humanity's last hope. The party conducts guerrilla operations against superior alien technology, researching captured equipment, and upgrading their hidden base. Every mission carries lethal risk with permanent character death, and the invaders grow stronger monthly, but hope lies in turning captured technology against the enemy.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the First Contact event—when and where the Void-Touched invasion began.
    *   Write the history of the Defenders Initiative's formation and its secret coalition of nations.
    *   Describe previous alien invasions the Ethereals have conducted on other worlds and their fate.
    *   Explain the Ethereals' desperate search for genetic perfection and why humans are valuable.
    *   Detail the technology gap between human ballistic weapons and alien plasma weaponry.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Defenders Initiative** (Major): Secret international military force fighting the invasion with guerrilla tactics.
    *   **The Ethereal Collective** (Major): Dying alien race seeking genetic salvation through harvesting other species.
    *   **The Council of Nations** (Minor): Political body funding the Defenders while dealing with panic and politics.
    *   **EXALT** (Minor): Human paramilitary organization collaborating with aliens for promised power.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Commander Verin (strategic leader), Dr. Kahleen Soren (chief researcher), Chief Engineer Dravin (equipment builder), The Ethereal Overmind (invasion mastermind), Agent Kael (deep cover operative).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Defender Soldier" (replaceable squad members), "Sectoid" (psionic gray aliens), "Muton" (elite shock troops), "Chrysalid" (zombie creators), "Cyberdisc" (robot platforms), "Ethereal" (psionic masters).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Crucible:** Multi-level underground fortress with command center, research labs, engineering workshops, psionic training chambers, barracks, medical bay, hangar for interceptor craft.
*   **UFO Crash Sites:** Smoking wreckage with alien survivors, recoverable technology, and hazardous alien materials.
*   **Alien Base:** Underground facility with stasis pods holding abductees, genetic labs, power cores, and heavy defenses.
*   **The Temple Ship:** Massive orbital command vessel with hangar bays, stasis chambers, power core, and Ethereal sanctum.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** A soldier dies in combat → **THEN:** Permanently lost, replacement recruited at lower level, emotional impact on squad.
*   **IF:** Party ignores abduction missions → **THEN:** Regional panic increases, nations withdraw funding, mission difficulty rises.
*   **IF:** Research is neglected → **THEN:** Party falls behind technology curve, enemies become overwhelming.
*   **IF:** The Crucible is successfully defended → **THEN:** Operations can continue, but repairs cost resources and time.
*   **IF:** Temple Ship is destroyed → **THEN:** Invasion ends but alien tech is lost forever.
*   **IF:** Temple Ship is captured → **THEN:** Invasion ends and humanity gains alien technology but risks future corruption.
