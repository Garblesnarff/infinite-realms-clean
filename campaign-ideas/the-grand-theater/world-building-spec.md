# World-Building Specification: The Grand Theater

## Core Concept
Baroque dimension where reality is staged opera. Party written into five-act tragedy by Divine Director. Must perform to reach Dramatic Crescendo and transcend script.

## Lore
- **Origin**: Created when Baroque drama achieved such intensity it became reality
- **The Divine Director**: God who writes all existence as theatrical performance
- **Eternal Opera**: Five-act tragedy playing in infinite loop
- **The Dramatic Crescendo**: Perfect emotional climax moment with reality-breaking power

## Factions
- **The Stage Crew** (maintain theatrical reality)
- **The Prima Donnas** (jealous lead performers)
- **The Hecklers** (punish bad acting)
- **The Audience** (cosmic entities whose reactions alter reality)

## NPCs
- **Stage Manager**: Cynical immortal guide
- **Divine Director**: God of drama
- **Prima Donna**: Jealous lead
- **Baroque Horrors**: Death, Time, Vanity as theatrical monsters
- **The Hecklers**: Bad performance punishers

## Locations
Five-act structure through Infinite Stage, Costume Halls, Palace of Mirrors, Orchestra Pit, Director's Box, Prompt Book library.

## Mechanics
- **Performance Score**: Drama quality = success
- **Theatrical Roles**: Archetypes grant powers/limits
- **Dramatic Timing**: Initiative by appropriateness
- **Chiaroscuro Combat**: Extreme light/shadow only
- **Audience Reaction**: Cosmic approval grants bonuses
- **Script Deviation**: Freedom vs. Heckler attacks
