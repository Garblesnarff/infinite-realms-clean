# Creative Brief: The Merchant and the Harvest Deity

## Art Style: Medieval Economic Romance (Spice and Wolf-inspired)

Captures the rustic beauty of medieval trade routes, the earthy warmth of harvest imagery, and the elegant interplay between a clever merchant and a wise wolf deity.

### Core Principles:

*   **Medieval Authenticity:** Grounded medieval settings with realistic trade goods, clothing, and economics.
*   **Harvest Imagery:** Wheat fields, autumn colors, abundance mixed with the passage of seasons.
*   **Wolf and Woman:** Holo's dual nature visualized through wolf ears, tail, and occasional full transformation.

### Detailed Style Guide:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:** Witty, sophisticated, warm, intellectually engaging, romantic, rustic, timeless.

**2. Color Palette:** Earthy harvest tonesâ€”golden wheat, autumn oranges and browns, forest greens. Rich medieval textiles. Holo's amber eyes are striking.

**3. Character Design:**
*   **Holo:** Beautiful woman with wolf ears and tail, long hair, amber eyes, medieval traveling clothes. Can transform into a massive wolf. Often depicted eating or drinking with great enjoyment.
*   **Lawrence:** Practical medieval merchant, simple but well-maintained clothes, kind eyes, often shown with ledgers or trade goods.

**4. Backgrounds:** Medieval trade routes, market towns, guild halls, inns, wheat fields, northern wilderness, ruins of ancient settlements.

**5. Lighting:** Natural, warm lighting emphasizing autumn and harvest imagery. Firelight in inns. Golden hour for romantic moments.

## Voice

**Narrator Voice:** Sophisticated, warm, and knowing. The voice of someone recounting a timeless tale of travel, trade, and love. Intellectual but accessible, with appreciation for both economic cleverness and romantic warmth. Studio-quality with ambient sounds of medieval marketplaces and rustling wheat.
