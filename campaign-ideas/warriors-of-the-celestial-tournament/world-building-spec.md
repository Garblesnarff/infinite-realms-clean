**Warriors of the Celestial Tournament**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Martial artists harness ki for superhuman abilities. The party defends Earth from escalating threats—alien warlords, androids, demons, gods. Through training arcs, transformations, and power level increases, they grow from village defenders to universal champions competing in the Tournament of Power where losing means erasure.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Saiyan race—warrior aliens who grow stronger from combat and transform under stress.
    *   Write the history of the Dragon Balls and their creator.
    *   Describe the hierarchy of gods—Kais, Gods of Destruction, Angels, and the Omni-King.
    *   Explain the multiverse structure with twelve universes.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** Earth's Martial Artists (defenders), Frieza Force (galactic empire), Pride Troopers (universal justice league), Gods of Destruction (cosmic balance keepers).

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Master Roshi, Frieza (emperor), Beerus (god), Whis (angel), Jiren (strongest mortal), Zeno (omni-king).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Martial Artist," "Alien Warrior," "Android," "Kai," "Tournament Fighter."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Hyperbolic Time Chamber:** Time dilation training dimension, harsh environment.
*   **Tournament of Power Arena:** Floating platform in void, universal erasure stakes.
*   **Planet Namek:** Three suns, Namekian villages, dragon balls.

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Character achieves first transformation → **THEN:** 50x power increase, golden aura, can face major villains.
*   **IF:** Character masters Ultra Instinct → **THEN:** Body moves without thought, silver form, rivals gods.
*   **IF:** Universe loses Tournament of Power → **THEN:** Entire universe erased by Zeno, only winner's universe survives.
*   **IF:** Dragon Balls wished → **THEN:** Any wish granted within limits, balls scatter and turn to stone for 1 year.
