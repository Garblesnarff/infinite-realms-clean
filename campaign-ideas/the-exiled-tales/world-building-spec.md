**The Exiled Tales**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Centuries ago, fairy tale characters were driven from their Homelands by a mysterious Adversary and now live in hiding in the mortal world's greatest city—Fabletown. The party consists of Fables navigating life as immortal exiles, dealing with community politics, investigating crimes, and eventually discovering that the Adversary who conquered their worlds is preparing to invade the mortal realm.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Exodus—how fairy tale kingdoms fell one by one to the Adversary, forcing survivors to flee to the mortal world.
    *   Write the history of Fabletown's founding in New York and the Compact that governs Fables.
    *   Describe the Farm system—why non-human Fables are confined there and the resentment it breeds.
    *   Explain the nature of Fable immortality—how their fame in mortal stories sustains them.
    *   Detail the Adversary's Empire and their systematic conquest of all Homelands.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Fabletown Government** (Major): Led by mayor and deputies, maintains order and secrecy.
    *   **The Farm Residents** (Major): Non-human Fables seeking equality and freedom.
    *   **The Adversary's Empire** (Major): Conquering force from the Homelands, led by a former Fable.
    *   **The Resistance** (Minor): Scattered rebels still fighting in occupied Homelands.
    *   **Bluebeard's Faction** (Minor): Wealthy Fables who believe money makes them above the law.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Bigby Wolf, Snow White, Prince Charming, Bluebeard, Rose Red, King Cole, The Adversary.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** Various fairy tale characters (Three Little Pigs, Jack Horner, Beauty and Beast, etc.)

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Woodland:** Luxury apartments, Bigby's office, government chambers.
    *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Elegant lobby, hidden magical wards, mix of fairy tale decor and modern furnishings), Sound (Hushed conversations, elevators, distant magic), Smell (Old wood, magic, various Fables' unique scents).
*   **Fabletown Streets:** Hidden neighborhood maintaining glamours.
*   **The Farm:** Rural property confining non-human Fables.
*   **The Homelands:** Conquered fairy tale kingdoms.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** Players solve crimes without exposing Fables to mortals.
    *   **THEN:** Community trust increases; access to resources improves.
    *   **IF:** Players support Farm equality.
    *   **THEN:** Non-human Fables become allies; political structure changes; some wealthy Fables become hostile.
    *   **IF:** Players gather intelligence on the Adversary.
    *   **THEN:** War preparation improves; resistance in Homelands grows stronger.
    *   **IF:** Fabletown is exposed to mortals.
    *   **THEN:** Campaign shifts to dealing with mortal world knowledge of fairy tales; government intervention.
    *   **IF:** The Adversary is defeated.
    *   **THEN:** Fables can return home; political restructuring; some choose to stay in mortal world.
