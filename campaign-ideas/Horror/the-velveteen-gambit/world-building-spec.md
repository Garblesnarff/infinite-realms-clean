# The Velveteen Gambit - World Building Specification

## The Becoming: Comprehensive Mechanics

### Transformation Stages (Detailed)

**Stage 0: Unconscious Object**
- No awareness, pure toy
- No drain on child
- Cannot participate in campaign

**Stage 1: Awakened Consciousness**
- Full awareness, can think and choose
- Standard toy limitations (Stillness, no pain, immortal)
- Drain: 0 HP from child
- Reversible: Yes (by becoming unconscious again)

**Stage 2: Sensitivity Awakening**
- Begin feeling temperature, texture
- Touch becomes meaningful
- Drain: 5 HP from child over one week
- Symptoms: Toy feels "more real," child slightly tired
- Reversible: Difficult (Will save DC 15)

**Stage 3: Pain Response**
- Damage actually hurts
- Can be "wounded" emotionally and physically
- Drain: 10 HP from child over one week
- Symptoms: Toy flinches, child has nightmares
- Reversible: Very difficult (Will save DC 20)

**Stage 4: Biological Needs**
- Hunger, thirst (don't need food but feel lack)
- Fatigue (must rest, not quite sleep)
- Drain: 15 HP from child over one week
- Symptoms: Toy exhibits needs, child weakens visibly
- Reversible: Extremely difficult (Will save DC 25)

**Stage 5: Flesh Emergence**
- Real tissue appears beneath toy material
- Bleeds if cut (blood under fabric)
- Drain: 20 HP from child over one week
- Symptoms: Toy has pulse, child gets sick
- Reversible: Nearly impossible (Will save DC 30)

**Stage 6: Partial Mortality**
- Aging begins (slow for toys, but real)
- Can develop infections, disease
- Drain: 25 HP from child over one week
- Symptoms: Toy shows wear, child hospitalized
- Reversible: Miracle required

**Stage 7: Broken Stillness**
- No longer freeze when observed
- Move freely, humans can see
- Drain: 30 HP from child over one week
- Symptoms: Toy is "alive" to humans, child critical
- Reversible: Unknown

**Stage 8: Fully Real**
- Complete flesh and blood being
- Can reproduce, grow, die
- Drain: Child's remaining HP (fatal)
- Symptoms: Toy is animal-like being, child dies
- Reversible: No (transformation complete)

### Jamie's Health Tracking

**Starting HP**: 100 (healthy 8-year-old)

**Drain Effects by HP**:
- **100-76 HP**: Normal, energetic child
- **75-51 HP**: Tired, pale, parents notice
- **50-26 HP**: Sick, doctor visits, tests
- **25-11 HP**: Hospitalized, critical condition
- **10-1 HP**: Dying, on life support
- **0 HP**: Death

**Recovery Rate**:
- If all draining stops: 1 HP per day recovery
- Medical intervention: 2 HP per day
- Below 25 HP: Permanent damage risk even if saved

**Multiple Toys Draining**:
- Each toy's stage drains simultaneously
- Example: 3 toys at Stage 4 = 45 HP drain per week
- Cult of 10 toys could kill Jamie in days

### The Cult of Becoming

**Structure**:
- **Prophet**: Velvet (founder, leader)
- **High Priest**: Brother Thomas (chief zealot)
- **Initiates**: 12 toys in various transformation stages
- **Seekers**: 8 toys considering joining
- **Total**: 20-21 toys involved

**Beliefs**:
1. Becoming Real is divine calling
2. Child's love is gift to be used
3. Draining is honor (child helps transcendence)
4. Death is acceptable cost for Realness
5. Flesh is superior to toy form
6. Suffering proves commitment

**Practices**:
- Daily gatherings in Secret Chapel
- Meditation on love received
- Practicing movement without Stillness
- Confession of doubts (group pressure)
- Advancement ceremonies as toys progress stages
- Preparing for mass Becoming event

**Recruitment**:
- Target lonely toys
- Promise meaning and purpose
- Demonstrate Velvet's abilities
- Peer pressure from other members
- Love-bombing new recruits
- Gradual commitment escalation

### The Resistance

**Leadership**: Mother Agnes, Professor Sawdust, The Witness

**Members**: 15 toys opposing cult

**Philosophy**:
1. Toys are already Real (consciousness = realness)
2. Flesh is downgrade (mortality, pain)
3. Draining child is murder
4. Toyhood has value
5. Immortality beats mortality

**Methods**:
- Educating about risks
- Showing attic graveyard
- Testimony from Patches
- Protecting Jamie
- Sabotaging cult rituals
- Offering alternative (community Becoming)

### Velvet's True History

**Timeline**:
- **1960**: Velvet created as Christmas gift
- **1961-1965**: Loved by child named Sarah
- **1965**: Achieved Stage 8 (Fully Real)
- **1965**: Sarah died (from drain, age 9)
- **1966-1980**: Velvet wandered, flesh-bear, homeless
- **1980-2000**: Found by collectors, passed around
- **2000**: Ended up in Jamie's home (grandmother's toy)
- **2020**: Jamie began playing with her
- **2024**: Started cult (desperate to reverse)

**Current State**:
- 64 years old (in Real years)
- Aging slowly (toy metabolism)
- Wants to die but too afraid
- Wants to reverse but doesn't know how
- Teaching others hoping to find transfer method
- Genuine regret but still manipulative

**Secret Goal**: If someone becomes Fully Real, try to transfer her Realness to them (swap places). Doesn't know if possible, desperate enough to try.

### Mother Agnes's Reversal Ritual

**Discovery**: Found in 1940s by accident

**Method**:
1. Gather all toys who love the Real toy
2. Create circle, sharing memories of toy's pre-Real state
3. Real toy must consent, truly want to return
4. Collective will reverses transformation
5. Cost: Real toy loses all memories of Realness

**Requirements**:
- Minimum 5 toys participating
- Real toy cannot resist
- Takes 24 hours of continuous ritual
- Exhausting for participants

**Success Rate**:
- Stage 2-4: 90% success
- Stage 5-6: 60% success
- Stage 7: 30% success
- Stage 8: 10% success (Agnes succeeded, barely)

**Side Effects**:
- Real toy remembers pain (psychological scar)
- May have flesh remnants (Agnes has scars)
- Trauma from experiencing mortality
- Identity crisis (was I Real? Am I still me?)

### The Alternative Becoming (Community Ritual)

**Theory**: Proposed by Professor Sawdust

**Concept**: Instead of draining child, toys drain each other
- Each toy contributes life force to pool
- Pool distributed to all participants
- Everyone becomes Real together
- No child harmed

**Requirements**:
- Minimum 10 willing toys
- Perfect trust (doubt breaks it)
- Simultaneous commitment
- Equal sacrifice from all

**Process**:
1. Toys form circle holding hands/paws
2. Open hearts to each other
3. Share essence freely
4. Energy cycles through circle
5. Transformation occurs collectively
6. All reach same stage simultaneously

**Outcome**:
- All become Stage 6-7 Real (not quite full mortality)
- Diluted transformation (less flesh, more awareness)
- Shared mortality (age together, die together)
- No child harmed
- Community bond strengthens

**Risks**:
- If one betrays, all suffer
- If one dies, affects all
- Linked fates forever
- Unknown long-term effects

### The Attic Graveyard (Evidence)

**Contents**: 23 Real toy corpses from past attempts

**Notable Dead**:
1. **Teddy Roosevelt** (1920s): First known Real toy, skeleton visible
2. **Porcelain twins** (1940s): Became Real together, died of infections
3. **Soldier Sam** (1950s): Real for 15 years, died of old age
4. **Velveteen Sarah** (1960s): Velvet's child's toy rabbit, drained to death
5. **Multiple others**: Various eras, various fates

**Evidence Provided**:
- Journals (some literate toys wrote warnings)
- Physical decay (proof mortality is real)
- Timelines (how long Real toys survived: 2-20 years)
- Causes of death (disease, accidents, old age, predation)
- Final messages ("Don't do it," "Not worth it," "I regret")

**Horror**: Some are recent (within last 10 years). This isn't ancient history.

### Becoming Factions Within Cult

**The Zealots** (Brother Thomas's faction)
- Believe in mass Becoming immediately
- Willing to kill Jamie quickly for power
- Don't care about morality
- Want Stage 8 for all members
- Dangerous, irrational, committed

**The Gradualists** (Velvet's faction)
- Slow transformation, one stage at a time
- Try to keep Jamie alive longer (sustainable drain)
- Some ethical concerns
- Want controlled Becoming
- Manipulative but less overtly evil

**The Doubters** (Wavering members)
- Joined but seeing problems
- Noticing Jamie's sickness
- Feeling guilty
- Could be turned to Resistance
- Vulnerable to both sides' arguments

**The Hybrid** (Solo, outside factions)
- Stuck mid-transformation
- Warning to all sides
- Neither fully cult nor resistance
- Living example of failure
- Both sides use as evidence

### The Truth About Real

**What Toys Think Real Means**:
- Freedom from Stillness
- Ability to affect world
- Being "alive" like humans
- Transcendence, ascension
- Power and agency

**What Real Actually Means**:
- Mortality (will die)
- Vulnerability (can be hurt permanently)
- Biological needs (hunger, sleep, pain)
- Aging (decay over time)
- Disconnection from toy nature
- Trading immortality for brief life

**The Paradox**:
- Toys ARE already Real (conscious, feeling, choosing)
- Flesh doesn't make them more Real
- It makes them more HUMAN
- But humanity includes suffering and death
- Question: Is human experience worth human mortality?

**Professor Sawdust's Thesis**:
"Realness is not a state of matter but a state of mind. We are Real because we think, feel, choose. Flesh adds nothing but pain. We seek to become what we already are."

## Victory Scenarios (Detailed)

### Scenario 1: The Enlightened Path
**Actions**:
- Convince cult Realness isn't flesh
- Philosophical victory over Velvet
- All toys reject Becoming
- Jamie recovers fully

**Outcome**:
- Toys remain toys but embrace it
- New philosophy spreads
- Velvet helped to accept toy state
- Community stronger

**Epilogue**: Toys continue living, Jamie grows up, eventually they're passed on. But they lived with meaning, no regrets.

### Scenario 2: Community Becoming
**Actions**:
- Gather 10+ willing toys
- Perform alternative ritual
- All become Real together
- Jamie unharmed

**Outcome**:
- Shared transformation
- Diluted mortality
- Community of Real toys
- Face aging together

**Epilogue**: They age slowly, have years together, die as community. Jamie remembers them fondly, never knew sacrifice.

### Scenario 3: Velvet's Redemption
**Actions**:
- Help Velvet reverse Realness
- Use Mother Agnes's ritual
- Velvet becomes toy again
- Cult dissolves

**Outcome**:
- Velvet loses memories of flesh
- Returns to toy innocence
- Jamie recovers
- Other toys saved from mistake

**Epilogue**: Velvet lives as happy toy, doesn't remember pain. Players remember for her. Bittersweet.

### Scenario 4: Individual Choices
**Actions**:
- Allow each toy to choose
- Some Become, some don't
- Manage Jamie's health carefully
- Accept mixed outcome

**Outcome**:
- Community splits
- Some Real, aging
- Some toys, immortal
- Jamie weakened but alive

**Epilogue**: Real toys live brief beautiful lives. Toy toys watch them age and die. Both choices valid, both have costs.

## Defeat Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Jamie Dies
- Too much draining
- Child dies in hospital
- Cult achieves Realness
- Victory through murder

**Aftermath**: Guilt, horror, Jamie's mother's grief, police investigation (mysterious child death). Real toys hunted.

### Scenario 2: Mass Corruption
- Players join zealots
- Justify child sacrifice
- Become Real through murder
- Lose moral compass

**Aftermath**: Living as flesh beings with murder on conscience. Cannot return. What have we become?

### Scenario 3: Total Reversal
- Agnes's ritual on ALL toys
- Everyone loses consciousness
- Return to being objects
- Jamie saved but toys dead

**Aftermath**: Silence. Empty playroom. Did we win or lose everything?

## Final Philosophical Question

**The Campaign Asks**: What is Real?

**Possible Answers**:
1. Consciousness makes you Real (already achieved)
2. Flesh makes you Real (cult's belief)
3. Love makes you Real (Velveteen Rabbit's claim)
4. Choice makes you Real (existentialist view)
5. Nothing makes you Real (nihilist view)
6. Everything is Real (universal view)

**Campaign Should Support Multiple Answers**: Let players decide through play, not lecture.

**Final Note**: Being toy might be blessing. Immortality, no pain, eternal childhood. Maybe Realness is downgrade. Maybe it's upgrade. Maybe it's just different. Campaign explores all sides, judges none.
