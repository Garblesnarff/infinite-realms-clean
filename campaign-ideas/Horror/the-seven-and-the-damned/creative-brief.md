# Creative Brief: The Seven and the Damned

## Art Style: Corporate Superhero Satire (The Boys meets Watchmen)

Inspired by contrast between polished corporate branding and ultraviolent reality. Colors are bright corporate branding (reds, blues, golds for Supes) versus gritty realism (blood, dirt, urban decay for the party). Character designs emphasize this: Supes in spandex costumes with perfect hair versus ordinary people in tactical gear covered in blood. Lighting shows sterile corporate environments versus grimy safe houses. Style emphasizes satire, violence, and moral degradation.

### Core Principles:

*   **Corporate Polish vs Gritty Reality:** Shiny Supes versus bloody vigilantes.
*   **Ultraviolence:** Gore, brutality, consequences of superhuman fights.
*   **Satire:** Mocking superhero tropes and corporate culture.
