# Creative Brief: The Hollow Kingdom

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Gothic Bug Kingdom with Infection Horror (Hollow Knight-inspired)

Inspired by the hauntingly beautiful hand-drawn aesthetics of *Hollow Knight* with Victorian gothic and insect biology influences. This style captures the melancholic beauty of a fallen civilization, the horror of orange infection, dream realm surrealism, and the tragedy of discarded vessels.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Scale:** Bugs as characters, massive in relation to their tiny world
*   **Gothic Architecture:** Carved stone, bug-scale cathedrals, shell motifs, chitin decorations
*   **Infection Visual:** Orange eyes, glowing pustules, spreading corruption
*   **Hand-Drawn Aesthetic:** Flowing lines, expressive character designs, dark backgrounds

**Color Palette:** Deep blacks, shell whites, stone grays, infection orange, dream realm whites/golds

**Prompt Example:** "A gothic bug kingdom carved from stone. Tiny bug warriors with nail swords in a massive cavern with towering shell architecture. Orange infection spreading on walls. Hand-drawn art style, dark backgrounds with dramatic lighting."
