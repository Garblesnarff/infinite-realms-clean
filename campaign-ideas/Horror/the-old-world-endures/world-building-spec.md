**The Old World Endures**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The Old World is torn by war between multiple factions: Empire, Dwarfs, Elves, Greenskins, Vampires, and more. The party leads one faction as Legendary Lord, conquering settlements and fielding armies. When Chaos invades from the northern Wastes with daemons and corruption, ancient enemies must choose between eternal rivalry or united survival against the End Times.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the War of the Beard—ancient conflict between Elves and Dwarfs destroying trust.
    *   Write the history of Sigmar founding the Empire and his elevation to godhood.
    *   Describe the Time of Three Emperors when Empire nearly collapsed to civil war.
    *   Explain the Vampire Wars when undead legions nearly conquered the Old World.
    *   Detail the first Great Chaos Incursion and how it was barely repelled.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Empire** (Major): Human empire, balanced military, gunpowder and magic, diplomatic.
    *   **Dwarf Holds** (Major): Ancient underground kingdoms, tough infantry, artillery, grudges.
    *   **Greenskin Tribes** (Major): Orcs and Goblins, endless hordes, WAAAGH! momentum.
    *   **Vampire Counts** (Major): Undead legions, corruption, necromancy, eternal aristocrats.
    *   **High Elves** (Minor): Ancient magical empire, elite troops, dragons.
    *   **Forces of Chaos** (Major Enemy): Corrupted warriors, daemons, reality warpers, apocalyptic.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Karl Franz (Empire Emperor), Thorgrim Grudgebearer (Dwarf High King), Grimgor Ironhide (Orc Warboss), Mannfred von Carstein (Vampire Lord), Archaon the Everchosen (Chaos Champion).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Empire State Troop" (infantry), "Dwarf Ironbreaker" (elite infantry), "Orc Big'un" (heavy infantry), "Vampire Blood Knight" (heavy cavalry), "Chaos Warrior" (corrupted champion), "Bloodletter" (Khorne daemon).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Altdorf:** Empire capital with Gothic baroque architecture, colleges of magic, massive walls, Emperor's palace.
*   **Karaz-a-Karak:** Eternal Dwarf capital, underground halls carved over millennia, forges, Book of Grudges throne room.
*   **Drakenhof Castle:** Vampire stronghold in Sylvania, gothic horror, perpetual night, undead armies.
*   **Black Crag:** Greenskin-conquered Dwarf hold, spawns endless Orcs, crude but massive fortifications.
*   **The Chaos Wastes:** Northern hell-realm where reality breaks down, daemons manifest, corrupted landscape.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Player expands aggressively → **THEN:** Multiple factions form coalition, must fight defensive war.
*   **IF:** Chaos corruption spreads unchecked → **THEN:** Settlements rebel, Chaos armies spawn, public order collapses.
*   **IF:** Legendary lord dies in battle → **THEN:** Wounded for several turns, army suffers morale penalty.
*   **IF:** Complete faction quest chain → **THEN:** Gain legendary items, massive bonuses, unique abilities.
*   **IF:** Archaon's invasion begins → **THEN:** All factions suffer Chaos attacks, must choose between rivalry and survival.
*   **IF:** Factions ally against Chaos → **THEN:** Combined armies possible, shared intelligence, coordinated campaigns.
*   **IF:** Everchosen defeated → **THEN:** Chaos invasion repelled, corruption fades, victory achieved.
