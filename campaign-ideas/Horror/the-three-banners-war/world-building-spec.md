**The Three Banners War**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** Tamriel is torn between three factions fighting for the Ruby Throne while Daedric Prince Molag Bal enacts the Planemeld to drag the world into Coldharbour. The players are Soulless Ones who must navigate the war and ultimately assault Oblivion itself to reclaim their souls.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Soulburst event when Emperor Varen's ritual failed and broke the barriers between Tamriel and Oblivion.
    *   Write the history of the three alliances and their formation: Daggerfall Covenant, Aldmeri Dominion, Ebonheart Pact.
    *   Describe the Daedric Princes and Molag Bal's sphere of influence—domination, enslavement, and corruption.
    *   Explain the Five Companions and their failed ritual to make Varen divine.
    *   Detail Mannimarco's betrayal and his pact with Molag Bal.
    *   Write about the nature of the Soulless condition and why certain mortals were chosen.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Daggerfall Covenant** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Unite Tamriel under Covenant rule, restore trade and prosperity.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by High King Emeric, alliance of Bretons, Redguards, and Orcs.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Bring order and economic prosperity to war-torn Tamriel.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Some leaders seek to dominate rather than unite.
        *   **Assets:** Naval superiority, merchant wealth, balanced military.
        *   **Relationships:** At war with Dominion and Pact; manipulated by Molag Bal.
    *   **The Aldmeri Dominion** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Preserve elven supremacy, reclaim the Ruby Throne for mer-kind.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by Queen Ayrenn, alliance of High Elves, Wood Elves, and Khajiit.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Restore proper order with the wise and immortal elves leading.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Thalmor extremists seek to eradicate human influence entirely.
        *   **Assets:** Superior magic, elite troops, ancient knowledge.
        *   **Relationships:** At war with Covenant and Pact; manipulated by Molag Bal.
    *   **The Ebonheart Pact** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Defend against Akaviri invasion remnants, prevent domination by other alliances.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by Jorunn Skald-King, unlikely alliance of Nords, Dunmer, and Argonians.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Unity through strength, defensive coalition.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Deep racial tensions threaten to break the Pact from within.
        *   **Assets:** Harsh warriors, Nordic strength, Dunmer magic, Argonian guerrilla tactics.
        *   **Relationships:** At war with Covenant and Dominion; manipulated by Molag Bal.
    *   **The Worm Cult (Molag Bal's Servants)** (Major Enemy)
        *   **Goals:** Harvest souls for the Planemeld, achieve daedric apotheosis under Molag Bal.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by Mannimarco, with necromantic cultists throughout Tamriel.
        *   **Public Agenda:** None—they operate in secret.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Mannimarco seeks to become a Daedric Prince, viewing mortals as sacrifices.
        *   **Assets:** Necromancy, daedric magic, Dark Anchors, undead armies.
        *   **Relationships:** Serves Molag Bal; enemies of all mortal factions.

*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**

    *   **Molag Bal:** Daedric Prince of Domination, cruel master of Coldharbour.

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **Cyrodiil (Central Battlefield):** The heartland province, now a contested war zone where three alliances clash.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** Imperial City, Ruby Throne, multiple keeps and resources, Elder Scrolls temples.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Soldiers from all three alliances, opportunists, daedric infiltrators.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Alliance war vendors, siege equipment merchants, battlefield scavengers.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Alliance skirmish, Dark Anchor drop, keep siege, scroll carrier chase, Titan attack.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Molag Bal is manipulating all three alliances." "The Imperial City has fallen to daedric forces." "An Elder Scroll holds the key to stopping the Planemeld."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (battle standards, burning keeps, Dark Anchors in sky), Sound (war drums, combat, Dark Anchor chains), Smell (smoke, blood, ozone from daedric magic).
    *   **Coldharbour (Molag Bal's Realm):** A nightmarish dimension of torture, enslavement, and impossible architecture.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Halls of Torment, The Lightless Oubliette, Slave Pens, Molag Bal's Throne.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Daedra, enslaved souls, Dremora lords, Xivkyn elite guards.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** None—survival is the only goal.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Dremora patrol, tortured soul begging for death, enslaved fighter forced to attack, reality distortion, Molag Bal's voice tempting party.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Free enough souls and they'll aid your assault." "Molag Bal can be challenged in his throne room." "Some souls have been here so long they've become daedra."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (dark stone platforms, chains everywhere, red-black sky, impossible angles), Sound (screams, chains rattling, Molag Bal's laughter), Smell (sulfur, despair, absence of life).
    *   **Daggerfall City (Covenant Capital):** A major Breton city, center of trade and Covenant power.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** Castle Daggerfall, Harbor, Mages Guild, Fighters Guild, Market Square.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Bretons, Redguards, Orcs, merchants, soldiers.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Superior trade goods, naval contracts, alliance quests.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Political intrigue, Worm Cult infiltrator, trade dispute, naval recruitment, alliance ceremony.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "High King Emeric is threatened by rivals within the Covenant." "Worm Cult activity in the crypts." "Redguard royalty seeks separate peace."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (stone buildings, blue-and-silver banners, busy harbor), Sound (seagulls, merchants, ship bells), Smell (salt air, fish, bread).
    *   **The Harborage (Secret Refuge):** A hidden cave where the Prophet guides the Soulless.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Prophet's chamber, Soul Shriven portal, ancient meditation stones.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** The Prophet, the Five Companions (when rescued), other Soulless.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Quest hub, safe rest, visions of the past and future.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Vision of past events, Companion argument, daedric scrying attempt, soul fragment recovery, prophetic dream.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The Prophet knows how to restore souls." "The Five Companions can open a portal to Coldharbour." "Your soul is being used to power the Planemeld."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (dim torchlight, ancient stones, mystical symbols), Sound (dripping water, whispered prayers, echoes), Smell (damp stone, incense, age).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** The players close multiple Dark Anchors in a region.
    *   **THEN:** Daedric presence weakens, making the Planemeld slower. Generate grateful civilian encounters.
    *   **IF:** The players achieve high reputation with one alliance.
    *   **THEN:** They're granted officer rank, commanding NPCs in Cyrodiil battles. The other alliances become hostile.
    *   **IF:** The players free all Five Companions.
    *   **THEN:** A direct portal to Coldharbour can be opened. Generate preparation montage and final speeches.
    *   **IF:** The players defeat Mannimarco before the final assault.
    *   **THEN:** The Planemeld weakens significantly. Generate his transformation into the Revenant, a ghostly recurring enemy.
    *   **IF:** The players accept Molag Bal's bargain to serve him.
    *   **THEN:** Dark ending—they become powerful daedric servants. Generate epilogue of them conquering Tamriel for their master.
    *   **IF:** The players defeat Molag Bal and reclaim their souls.
    *   **THEN:** The Planemeld ends. Their Soulless condition is cured. Generate epilogue showing the three alliances continuing their war, but with daedric threat ended.
