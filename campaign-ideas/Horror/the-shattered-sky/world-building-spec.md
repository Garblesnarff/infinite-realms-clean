# The Shattered Sky - World-Building Specification

## Factions

**The Planar Academy**: Archmage Prismara and scholars studying rifts, guiding reconstruction
**The Reality Guard**: Military led by General Bulwark, defending against invasions
**The Reality Cult**: Void Prophet's followers believe merger creates transcendence
**Elemental Courts**: Divided on helping Material Plane, some hostile, some allied
**Fey Courts**: Enjoying chaos, some help for price, others cause mischief
**Demon Lords**: Exploiting rifts for invasion, want Material Plane conquered
**Reality Refugees**: Planar natives displaced, seeking return home

## NPCs

Archmage Prismara (planar expert), General Bulwark (military leader), Void Prophet (insane visionary), Elementari (elemental noble), Feyqueen Twilight (archfey trickster), Lord Abyssal (demon lord), various planar refugees and invaders

## Keystone Fragments (7 Total)

1. Material Plane (pocket dimension in city)
2. Elemental Air (floating vault)
3. Feywild (fey court treasury)
4. Shadowfell (hidden in undead city)
5. Abyss (demon lord's hoard)
6. Far Realm (impossible location)
7. Astral Plane (drifting through silver void)

## Reality Zones

Areas where planes permanently overlap:
- **Burning Quarter**: Fire Plane, eternal flames, salamanders as citizens
- **Shadow District**: Shadowfell, twilight, ghosts walking openly
- **Dream Streets**: Far Realm, madness, impossible architecture
- **Wild Woods**: Feywild, time distortion, fey creatures
- **Storm Heights**: Air Plane, floating buildings, wind elementals

## Timeline

**Month -6**: Keystone damaged (cult sabotage)
**Month -2**: First major rift (Spirereach)
**Campaign Start**: Multiple rifts, daily openings
**Month 2**: Reality zones forming, society adapting
**Month 4**: Planar war in earnest
**Month 6**: Critical mass, reconstruction or apocalypse
