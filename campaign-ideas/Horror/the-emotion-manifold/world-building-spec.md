# World-Building Specification: The Emotion Manifold

## Core Concept
Abstract Expressionist dimension of pure emotion as form/color. Party consciousness trapped, must navigate personal psychological landscapes and unite in cathartic expression to escape.

## Lore
- **Origin**: Dimension of unexpressed collective emotion, absorbs traumatized psyches
- **The Blank Canvas**: Entity of accumulated unexpressed feeling
- **Color Field Geography**: Navigate via emotion, not physical space
- **Gestural Reality**: Actions are brushstrokes, expression is physics

## Factions
None - too abstract for organized groups. Only emotional states and personal psyche fragments.

## Entities
- **The Blank Canvas**: Absorbing final entity
- **Color Fields**: Red (rage), Blue (sorrow), Yellow (fear), Black (despair), White (catharsis)
- **Gestural Echoes**: Frozen emotional moments
- **Fragment Selves**: Split psyche aspects

## Locations
Each PC has unique emotional manifold. Shared spaces are color field zones and the White Catharsis Center.

## Mechanics
- **Emotional Navigation**: Move via feeling
- **Gestural Expression**: Communicate by painting emotions
- **Psychological HP**: Emotional integrity
- **Cathartic Combat**: Expression as attack
- **Collaborative Therapy**: Help interpret abstract traumas
