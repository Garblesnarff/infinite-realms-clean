# The Platinum Fork Trials - Creative Brief

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: High-society drama meets Lovecraftian horror of too much power
- **Art Style**: Elegant restaurants hiding darkness, beautiful presentations masking corruption
- **Tone**: Sophisticated tension, power's seductive danger

## Color Palettes
- Society: Platinum silver, pristine whites, clinical perfection
- Restaurants: Varied by establishment, each has personality
- Corruption: Purple shadows creeping into silver, tarnishing
- Desperation: Warm colors becoming garish in chefs' anxiety
- Truth: Clear whites and honest golds

## Music
- Main Theme: Elegant but menacing
- Review Scenes: Tense anticipation, pen scratching paper
- Restaurant Arrivals: Grand, varied by location
- Political Intrigue: Strings and whispers
- Power Realization: Building dread/wonder
- Final Judgment: Weight of worlds

## Voice Direction
- **Augustina Grimpalate**: Ancient authority, Judi Dench gravitas
- **Dante Sharppalate**: Cutting precision, early Gordon Ramsay cruelty
- **Marcellina Soulfire**: Desperate passion, artist on edge
- **The Rogue Critic**: World-weary revolutionary, justified extremism
- **Emperor Gustavio**: Charming manipulation, political smoothness
- **Maya Quiettaste**: Idealistic hope, youthful determination

## Cinematic Moments
- First review's magical effect manifesting
- Platinum Fork ceremony, reality-bending ritual
- Restaurant collapse from bad review
- Chefs' desperate presentations
- Power corrupting fellow critic
- Final judgment with world watching
