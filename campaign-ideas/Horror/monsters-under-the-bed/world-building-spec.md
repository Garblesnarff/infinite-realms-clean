# Monsters Under the Bed - World-Building Specification

## Monster Classification

### Tier 1: Environmental Fears
- **Shadow Creepers**: Formless dark that moves
- **Dust Monsters**: Accumulation given life
- **Sound Things**: Noise without source
- Common, weak, numerous

### Tier 2: Location Fears
- **Under-Bed Dwellers**: Classic crawlers
- **Closet Lurkers**: Things that hide
- **Window Watchers**: Faces in glass
- **Hallway Haunters**: Corridor entities
- Moderate threat, territorial

### Tier 3: Conceptual Fears
- **The Abandonment**: Fear of being left alone
- **The Stranger**: Wrong adult figures
- **The Bully**: Social fear manifest
- **The Unknown**: Fear of not knowing
- Strong, personal, psychological

### Tier 4: The Elites
- **Closet King**: Ruler of hidden things
- **The Shadow That Moves**: Assassin of dark
- **Scissor Man**: Fear of sharp things
- **Wrong Parent**: Authority figure twisted
- Boss-level encounters

### Tier 5: The Supreme
- **Boogeyman Supreme**: Ultimate childhood terror
- Shapeless, everywhere, in all darkness
- Cannot be killed traditionally
- Requires facing fear itself

## The Teddy Bear Resistance

### Structure
**Commander Teddy**: One-eyed leader, battle-scarred
**Lieutenant Blankie**: Animated security blanket
**Scout Bunny**: Worn stuffed rabbit, reconnaissance
**Soldiers**: Various childhood comfort toys

### Philosophy
"We were made to protect. We will protect."

Comfort objects given life by children's love and need. Fight monsters to defend sleeping children. Know they only exist while needed. Fighting obsolescence as children grow up.

### Resources
- Night Light Sanctuaries
- The Attic headquarters
- Weapons: Childhood imagination (wooden swords that work)
- Intelligence: Listen to children's dreams

## Realm Geography Expanded

### The Bedroom Endless Zones

**Zone 1: The Nursery**
- Youngest children's fears
- Simple monsters
- Brightest night lights
- Most innocent

**Zone 2: The Kid's Room**
- Elementary age fears
- Moderate complexity
- Mix of comfort and terror
- Where most party members start

**Zone 3: The Teen Chamber**
- Adolescent fears
- Darker, more complex
- Social terrors
- Transition to adult fears

**Zone 4: The Adult Recall**
- Adult remembering childhood
- Fears filtered through time
- Nostalgia mixed with terror
- Distorted by memory

### The Primal Dark Structure

**Layer 1: Personal Dark**
- Your specific fear of darkness
- What YOUR monsters looked like
- Manageable

**Layer 2: Cultural Dark**
- Shared cultural fears
- Monsters from folklore
- Collective unconscious

**Layer 3: Human Dark**
- Fear all humans share
- Predators, death, unknown
- Ancient, powerful

**Layer 4: The Origin Dark**
- Fear itself
- Predates humanity
- Where Boogeyman was born
- Nearly impossible to survive

## Childhood Self Encounters

### Variations by Party Member
Each PC meets their childhood self, but interaction differs:

**The Protected**: Happy child, needs defending
**The Scared**: Terrified child, needs comforting
**The Brave**: Defiant child, offers inspiration
**The Broken**: Traumatized child, needs healing
**The Lost**: Confused child, needs guidance

### Integration vs. Protection
**Protect**: Keep child safe, they remain separate
**Integrate**: Merge with childhood self, gain powers but burden
**Abandon**: Leave child behind (unthinkable but possible)
**Become**: Regress entirely (defeat condition)

## Monster Evolution Mechanics

### How Monsters Grow

**Stage 1**: Simple Childhood Fear
- Monster under bed looks like monster
- Defeated by light/comfort

**Stage 2**: Persistent Fear
- Monster adapts to countermeasures
- Requires confronting what it represents

**Stage 3**: Adult Translation
- Childhood fear becomes adult anxiety
- Under-bed monster becomes fear of failure
- Much harder to fight

**Stage 4**: Existential Horror
- Fear becomes philosophical
- Darkness becomes meaninglessness
- Cannot be defeated by simple means

## The Boogeyman's Invasion Plan

### Phase 1: Reconnaissance
- Send lesser monsters to test defenses
- Identify Dream Children (party)
- Locate dimensional weak points

### Phase 2: Corruption
- Turn comfort objects against children
- Make safe spaces unsafe
- Erode boundary between fear realm and reality

### Phase 3: Bridging
- Open permanent portals
- Flood waking world with childhood terrors
- Feed on mass panic

### Phase 4: Evolution
- Feast on adult fears
- Grow exponentially stronger
- Become unkillable
- Rule through terror

## Victory Paths Detailed

### Path 1: Destruction
- Find Boogeyman's origin point
- Destroy primal dark
- Risk: Eliminating fear entirely
- Consequences: Lose caution, safety instincts

### Path 2: Integration
- Face all personal fears
- Integrate childhood terrors
- Become whole person (child + adult)
- Monsters become inner strength

### Path 3: Succession
- Become new Night Light Keepers
- Protect children forever
- Sacrifice adult life
- Eternal guardianship

### Path 4: Evolution
- Transform fear into courage
- Monsters become protectors
- Boogeyman becomes guardian
- Requires changing fundamental nature

## Hidden Lore

### The First Child's Story
Once, long ago, first human child feared the dark. That fear was so pure, so primal, it created the Boogeyman. Every subsequent child's fear has fed it. It's as old as human consciousness.

### The Parent Connection
Shadow Parents exist because real parents were afraid tooâ€”of failing their children, of monsters hurting their babies. Parent fear created protective energy that twisted into these entities.

### The Teddy Truth
Original Teddy was made by First Child to fight First Fear. Has been fighting for millennia. Worn down but never defeated. If Teddy falls, no comfort objects can resist anymore.

### The Growing Up Curse
Dream Children are adults who never fully processed childhood fear. That unresolved trauma keeps connection open. Healing childhood wounds might close portal but also mean confronting what they've avoided for decades.

### The Cycle
There have been previous Dream Children. They either: Stayed as protectors (Teddy Bears), were consumed (became monsters), escaped (closed their portal only), or transcended (became Night Light Keepers). The party is latest in long line.
