**The Elder Dragons' Awakening**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** Six Elder Dragons awaken every few thousand years to consume the world's magic, creating cataclysms that reshape continents. The players are members of the Pact fighting to kill or contain each dragon while discovering they're part of the world's natural cycle, and destroying them all may doom Tyria.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the last Elder Dragon awakening cycle and how previous civilizations fell.
    *   Write the history of the five major races and how they overcame ancestral hatreds to form the Pact.
    *   Describe each Elder Dragon's sphere of influence: Zhaitan (death/shadow), Mordremoth (plant/mind), Jormag (ice/persuasion), Primordus (fire/conflagration), Kralkatorrik (crystal/madness), and the Deep Sea Dragon.
    *   Explain the magical ecosystem and how dragons regulate the balance of magic in Tyria.
    *   Detail the previous failed attempts to fight dragons individually before the Pact formed.
    *   Write about the nature of dragon minions and how corruption spreads.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Pact** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Defeat or neutralize Elder Dragons, protect Tyria's civilizations.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by a Marshal, with commanders from each race's military.
        *   **Public Agenda:** United defense against dragon extinction events.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Search for alternatives to killing dragons as they learn the ecological truth.
        *   **Assets:** Combined armies, airship fleets, advanced technology, diverse magic.
        *   **Relationships:** Alliance of former enemies; opposed to all dragons; neutral to smaller threats.
    *   **The Vigil** (Minor - Pact Member)
        *   **Goals:** Military action, direct combat against dragon forces.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Military chain of command, mostly Charr and Norn.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Kill the dragons through superior force.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Some members seek glory and combat for its own sake.
        *   **Assets:** Heavy armor divisions, siege weapons, fortified positions.
        *   **Relationships:** Aggressive faction within the Pact; sometimes clashes with diplomatic approaches.
    *   **The Priory** (Minor - Pact Member)
        *   **Goals:** Research dragon weaknesses, preserve knowledge.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Scholarly council, mostly Asura and Sylvari.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Knowledge defeats ignorance and tyranny.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** They discovered the truth about dragon magical balance but debate revealing it.
        *   **Assets:** Ancient texts, magical research, historical sites.
        *   **Relationships:** Diplomatic faction within the Pact; seeks non-violent solutions.
    *   **Dragon Minions - Icebrood (Jormag's Forces)** (Major Enemy)
        *   **Goals:** Serve Jormag willingly or through corruption.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by powerful champions who accepted Jormag's gifts.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Jormag offers strength and evolution, not enslavement.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Many are terrified slaves pretending to serve willingly.
        *   **Assets:** Ice magic, psychological warfare, corrupting whispers.
        *   **Relationships:** Serve Jormag; opposed to Pact; hostile to other dragon minions.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** The players kill Mordremoth.
    *   **THEN:** Wild magic surges erupt globally. Jormag and Primordus both awaken more aggressively. Magic Balance drops by 20.
    *   **IF:** Magic Balance drops below 50.
    *   **THEN:** Reality rifts begin opening. Void creatures emerge. Generate void incursion missions.
    *   **IF:** The players negotiate with Jormag instead of fighting.
    *   **THEN:** They learn crucial information about magic balance but risk corruption. Generate temptation challenges.
    *   **IF:** Aurene successfully absorbs and redeems Kralkatorrik.
    *   **THEN:** A new benevolent Elder Dragon exists. Magic Balance stabilizes. Generate epilogue showing new age of dragon-mortal cooperation.
    *   **IF:** The players successfully put dragons to sleep instead of killing them.
    *   **THEN:** Magic Balance remains stable. Dragons dream for another thousand years. Generate epilogue of watchful peace.
    *   **IF:** The players kill all remaining dragons.
    *   **THEN:** Magic Balance reaches 0. Reality begins unraveling as the Void consumes the world. Generate desperate final mission to become new dragon-champions or face extinction.
