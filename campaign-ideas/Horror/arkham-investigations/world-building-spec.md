**Arkham Investigations**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In 1928 Arkham, Massachusetts, investigators uncover a global conspiracy worshiping Great Old Ones—alien beings beyond comprehension. Each discovery costs sanity as they face Deep Ones, decode the Necronomicon, explore Antarctic ruins, and race to prevent Cthulhu's awakening. The true horror: humanity is cosmically insignificant.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Great Old Ones—alien beings worshiped as gods who slumber between stars.
    *   Write the history of the Elder Things who created Earth's life millions of years ago.
    *   Describe the Necronomicon and other forbidden tomes that drive readers mad.
    *   Explain the Deep Ones' pact with Innsmouth and their underwater city Y'ha-nthlei.
    *   Detail the cosmology: humanity is accidental, gods are aliens, reality is fragile.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Cthulhu Cult** (Major): Global cult preparing for Great Old One's awakening.
    *   **Deep Ones** (Major): Amphibious fish-people breeding with humans, serving Cthulhu.
    *   **Miskatonic University** (Minor): Academic institution with forbidden occult library.
    *   **Government Agents** (Minor): Know about horrors but suppress information.
    *   **Independent Investigators** (Minor): Individuals who stumble onto cosmic truth.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Dr. Henry Armitage (librarian guide), Zadok Allen (Innsmouth informant), Richard Upton Pickman (cult leader), Professor William Dyer (Antarctic survivor), Nyarlathotep (Outer God manipulator).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Cultist" (fanatical worshiper), "Deep One Hybrid" (transforming human), "Shoggoth" (protoplasmic horror), "Elder Thing" (ancient alien), "Government Agent" (suppressor).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Miskatonic University:** Gothic New England university with forbidden library holding occult tomes.
*   **Innsmouth:** Decaying fishing town populated by Deep One hybrids.
*   **Y'ha-nthlei:** Underwater city of cyclopean architecture where Deep Ones dwell.
*   **Antarctic Ruins:** Ancient Elder Things city with non-Euclidean geometry.
*   **R'lyeh:** Sunken city where Cthulhu sleeps, waiting for stars to align.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Character reads forbidden tome → **THEN:** Gains knowledge but loses sanity permanently.
*   **IF:** Sanity drops too low → **THEN:** Indefinite madness, phobias, eventually permanent insanity.
*   **IF:** Deep One ancestry revealed → **THEN:** Character may transform over time.
*   **IF:** Ritual is stopped → **THEN:** Temporary reprieve but cultists will try again.
*   **IF:** Cthulhu awakens → **THEN:** Reality breaks, campaign ends in apocalypse.
*   **IF:** Party learns cosmic truth → **THEN:** Gains power but questions why they fight.
