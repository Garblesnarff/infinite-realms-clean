### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

This document is the primary instruction set for the world-building AI pipeline. Each section provides explicit directives for specialized agents to generate the necessary assets for a complete and dynamic campaign world.

**The Infinity Stones**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** Thaldros the Eternal has acquired all six Infinity Stones and erased half of all life with a thought. The players must unite survivors, heroes, villains, and cosmic entities in a desperate attempt to undo the Snap and stop Thaldros before he completes his vision of a "balanced" universe. Victory may require the ultimate sacrifice.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts. These entries will form the historical and cultural bedrock of the world.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the six Infinity Stones: their origins, individual powers, and what happens when combined.
    *   Write the history of Thaldros: his origin, his philosophy, and his quest for the Stones.
    *   Describe the Snap: what it looked like, how it felt, and its immediate aftermath.
    *   Explain the cosmic order and the role of abstract entities like Death.
    *   Detail previous attempts to use or control the Infinity Stones.
    *   Write about the prophecy or visions that foretell the only path to victory.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below. Each profile must contain the specified fields.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Resistance Alliance** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Reverse the Snap and stop Thaldros.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Loose coalition led by surviving heroes, with the players as key operatives.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Unite all who oppose Thaldros and assault his sanctuary.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** The Oracle knows one of them must die to succeed.
        *   **Assets:** Diverse powers, cosmic allies, knowledge from the Oracle.
        *   **Relationships:** United against Thaldros; seeking aid from cosmic abstracts; composed of former enemies.
    *   **Thaldros's Forces** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Protect Thaldros and his vision of balance.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by Thaldros, with zealot followers and cosmic enforcers.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Ensure balance is maintained and eliminate resistance.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Thaldros seeks to impress Death and prove his love.
        *   **Assets:** The Infinity Gauntlet, overwhelming power, loyal fanatics.
        *   **Relationships:** Opposed by the Resistance; attempting to court Death; viewed as mad by cosmic entities.
    *   **The Neutral Powers** (Minor)
        *   **Goals:** Survive and observe without taking sides.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Various cosmic entities and civilizations.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Remain uninvolved in mortal conflicts.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Some secretly aid the Resistance while maintaining neutrality.
        *   **Assets:** Cosmic knowledge, powerful artifacts, safe havens.
        *   **Relationships:** Avoid Thaldros; provide limited aid to Resistance; divided internally.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Directive:** The Character Generation Agent must create full profiles for all NPCs listed. Tier 1 NPCs are unique individuals. Tier 2 & 3 are archetypes to be instantiated multiple times with unique details as needed by the simulation.
*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**
    *   **Thaldros the Eternal:** Mad titan with Infinity Gauntlet, believes genocide is salvation, in love with Death.
    *   **The Oracle of Eternity:** Keeper of cosmic knowledge, has seen all futures, knows sacrifice is required.
    *   **Death Herself:** Personification of death, disgusted by Thaldros, secretly hopes he fails.
    *   **The Forge-Master:** Ancient artificer who can create weapons to fight cosmic beings.
*   **Tier 2 & 3 (Archetypes for Generation):**
    *   "Surviving Hero" (lost loved ones, seeking vengeance or justice)
    *   "Reformed Villain" (the Snap humbled them, now ally)
    *   "Cosmic Entity" (abstract being with immense power)
    *   "Thaldros Zealot" (believes in his vision of balance)
    *   "The Snapped" (those who dissolved, existing in limbo)

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below, populating all specified sub-sections.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **The Shattered Worlds:** Planets devastated by losing half their population.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** Mass graves, empty cities, memorial sites, survivors' camps.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Traumatized survivors, scavengers, opportunists.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Basic survival resources, information on the Snap, recruitment.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Memorial service, survivor breakdown, resource conflict, Thaldros cultist, ghostly vision of the Snapped.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Someone survived who shouldn't have." "The Oracle predicted this." "Thaldros's agents are recruiting believers."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (half-empty cities, ash memorials), Sound (mourning, eerie silence), Smell (decay, emptiness).
    *   **The Forge of Creation:** Where cosmic weapons are made.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Great Forge, the Hall of Weapons, the Artificers' Workshop.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Ancient artificers, their constructs, cosmic smiths.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Weapon forging, artifact analysis, cosmic knowledge.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Forging demonstration, trial to prove worthiness, cosmic tempering ritual, artifact malfunction, visit from cosmic entity.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "A weapon exists that can harm even Thaldros." "The Forge-Master lost kin in the Snap." "Creating such weapons requires sacrifice."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (cosmic fires, impossible metals), Sound (cosmic hammering), Smell (star-metal, creation energy).
    *   **The Realm of Death:** Where Death herself resides.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Oracle's Observatory, the Threshold, Death's Garden, the River of Souls.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Death, the Oracle, spirits of the deceased, psychopomps.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Cosmic knowledge, communication with the Snapped, prophecy.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Meeting lost loved ones, Death's attention, Oracle's vision, test of worthiness, revelation of the one true path.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Death despises Thaldros's Snap." "Only one future leads to victory." "The cost is one hero's life."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (ethereal landscapes, souls visible), Sound (whispers of dead), Smell (nothing—sensation fades here).
    *   **Thaldros's Sanctuary:** A garden world of terrible peace.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Meditation Garden, the Throne, the Gauntlet Shrine, the Empty Fields.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Thaldros, his zealots, peaceful creatures (half are gone).
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** None—this is the final battlefield.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Thaldros meditation, zealot patrol, vision of "paradise," reality warping, confrontation with the mad titan.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Thaldros is at peace with what he did." "He visits the Gauntlet like a shrine." "Death refuses to meet him here."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (beautiful but eerily empty), Sound (peaceful silence, unsettling), Smell (flowers, serenity, wrongness).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes. For each "IF" condition, the agent must pre-generate the narrative and environmental consequences for the "THEN" outcome.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** The players successfully recruit five major allies.
    *   **THEN:** Unlock the assault on Thaldros's sanctuary. Generate the war council scene.
    *   **IF:** A player attempts to use an individual Infinity Stone.
    *   **THEN:** Grant immense power but impose the drawback. Generate consequences based on which Stone.
    *   **IF:** The players communicate with the Snapped.
    *   **THEN:** Generate emotional reunions and urgent pleas to reverse the Snap.
    *   **IF:** The Oracle reveals the prophecy of required sacrifice.
    *   **THEN:** Generate party conflict or acceptance. The burden weighs on whoever volunteers.
    *   **IF:** The final battle begins.
    *   **THEN:** Generate the epic assault—armies clash, heroes face Thaldros, someone must reach the Gauntlet.
    *   **IF:** A hero successfully uses all six Stones to reverse the Snap.
    *   **THEN:** Generate the sacrifice scene, the Snap reversal, and the changed universe. The Snapped return, the hero dies (or miraculously survives with permanent consequences).
