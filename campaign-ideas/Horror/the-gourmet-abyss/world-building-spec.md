# The Gourmet Abyss - World-Building Specification

## The Dungeon's True Nature

### The Sleeping God
- Ancient entity beyond comprehension
- Fell asleep 10,000 years ago
- Dungeon is its digestive system
- Monsters are antibodies/gut fauna
- Awakening would be catastrophic or transformative

### The Feast of Ages Purpose
- Created by god's last worshippers
- Intended to sustain it in sleep
- Or wake it through perfect meal
- Recipe requires ingredients from all 50 floors
- Consuming it connects you to god

### Why It Appeared 50 Years Ago
- God's sleep becoming unstable
- Manifestation of hunger
- Opens periodically throughout history
- Current era's chance to deal with it
- Closing time approaching

## Floor-by-Floor Ecology

### Floors 1-5: The Stone Galleries
- **Ecosystem**: Traditional dungeon creatures
- **Cuisine Style**: Basic, hearty, medieval
- **Transformation**: Minor physical enhancements
- **Monsters**: Giant rats (tasty), slimes (versatile), goblins (gamey)

### Floors 6-10: The Crypt Gardens
- **Ecosystem**: Undead and necrophages
- **Cuisine Style**: Preservation and fermentation
- **Transformation**: Deathly pallor, cold resistance
- **Monsters**: Zombies (with right prep), giant bats, bone marrows

### Floors 11-15: The Luminous Caverns
- **Ecosystem**: Bioluminescent creatures
- **Cuisine Style**: Raw, sushi-like, glowing foods
- **Transformation**: Bioluminescent features, darkvision
- **Monsters**: Cave fish, crystal crabs, light wyrms

### Floors 16-20: The Mushroom Forest
- **Ecosystem**: Fungal and insectoid
- **Cuisine Style**: Vegetarian, umami-rich
- **Transformation**: Fungal patches, spore communication
- **Monsters**: Myconids (ethical issues), giant insects, sentient mushrooms

### Floors 21-25: The Meat Cathedral
- **Ecosystem**: Entirely carnivorous
- **Cuisine Style**: Extreme carnivore, all-meat
- **Transformation**: Predatory features, enhanced senses
- **Monsters**: Flesh golems, blood elementals, organ beasts

### Floors 26-30: The Inverted Lake
- **Ecosystem**: Aquatic in floating water sphere
- **Cuisine Style**: Seafood, innovative preparation
- **Transformation**: Gills, webbed appendages
- **Monsters**: Dungeon krakens, abyss sharks, water mimics

### Floors 31-35: The Clockwork Gardens
- **Ecosystem**: Mechanical creatures with organic parts
- **Cuisine Style**: Fusion of meat and metal
- **Transformation**: Mechanical implants, precision
- **Monsters**: Gear beasts, clockwork predators, steam things

### Floors 36-40: The Screaming Forests
- **Ecosystem**: Predatory plants and chimeras
- **Cuisine Style**: Dangerous herbs, multi-source dishes
- **Transformation**: Plant features, photosynthesis
- **Monsters**: Carnivorous trees, chimeric abominations, botanical horrors

### Floors 41-45: The Fracture Zones
- **Ecosystem**: Reality-damaged creatures
- **Cuisine Style**: Impossible flavors, abstract cooking
- **Transformation**: Reality distortion, phase shifting
- **Monsters**: Dimensional shamblers, probability beasts, conceptual predators

### Floors 46-50: The Final Descent
- **Ecosystem**: Pure nightmare fuel
- **Cuisine Style**: Transcendent, maddening
- **Transformation**: Beyond human, approaching divine
- **Monsters**: The Sleeper's dreams given form

## Monster Cookbook Highlights

### Beginner Recipes

**Giant Rat Skewers**
- Basic protein, slightly chewy
- Effect: +1 to Survival checks for 4 hours
- Preparation: Clean thoroughly, season heavily

**Slime Gelatin**
- Versatile base ingredient
- Effect: Temporary HP, acid resistance
- Preparation: Boil to reduce, flavor with fruit

**Goblin Stew** (Controversial)
- Ethical debate: Are goblins sentient?
- Effect: +2 Stealth for 8 hours
- Preparation: Slow cook to tenderize

### Intermediate Recipes

**Hydra Fondue** (Nine Flavors)
- Each head has different taste
- Effect: Regeneration 1 HP/round for 10 minutes
- Preparation: Keep all nine flavors distinct

**Beholder Eye Sashimi**
- Each eye grants different magical effect
- Effect: Temporary magical vision
- Preparation: Must be extremely fresh, raw

**Mimic Surprise**
- Tastes like whatever it was disguised as
- Effect: Polymorph self 1/day
- Preparation: Very dangerous to obtain

### Advanced Recipes

**Reality Dragon Tartare**
- Tastes like every food simultaneously
- Effect: Temporary reality manipulation
- Preparation: Don't cook, it exists outside causality

**Concept Beast Reduction**
- Flavor is abstract idea (justice, love, entropy)
- Effect: Embody consumed concept
- Preparation: Philosophical understanding required

**Dreaming God's Neuron**
- Final floor ingredient
- Effect: See truth of universe
- Preparation: Part of Feast of Ages

## The Transformation System Details

### Physical Changes by Floor Range

**Floors 1-10**: Cosmetic only
- Eye color change, minor scarring
- Enhanced taste/smell
- Fully reversible

**Floors 11-25**: Noticeable
- Bioluminescence, scale patches
- Enhanced physical abilities
- Reversible with effort

**Floors 26-40**: Significant
- Inhuman features obvious
- Supernatural abilities manifest
- Difficult to reverse

**Floors 41-50**: Transcendent
- No longer fully human
- Reality-bending powers
- Irreversible, possibly desirable

### Psychological Changes
- Predatory instincts increase
- Surface food becomes bland
- Dungeon starts feeling like home
- Human concerns fade
- Question: Is this corruption or evolution?

## The Settlements

### Floor 10 - Last Light Village
- 500 permanent residents
- Mix of failed delvers and children born here
- Farms bats, mushrooms, rats
- Last place that still feels human
- Watching party descend with pity/hope

### Floor 30 - The Halfway Hollows
- 50 residents, all transformed
- Barely remember surface
- Serve as warning
- Can provide advanced recipes
- Some want to return, can't survive surface now

### Floor 45 - The Gourmet King's Court
- Just the King and his memories
- Servants are animated meals
- Court of ghosts from past meals
- Loneliest place in existence
- Final conversation with someone who completed journey

## Secret Truths (GM Only)

1. Eating from every floor transforms you into new god's avatar
2. Gourmet King is trying to create successor so he can finally die
3. Mad Chef is actually future party member caught in time loop
4. Slimantha is god's way of watching/helping party
5. Rival party members are reincarnations of original worshippers
6. Surface town secretly prevents successful delvers from returning
7. The Feast is test of wisdom: Those who refuse gain most power
8. Dungeon resets every 50 years, all progress lost
9. Party's cooking skill determines god's personality if awakened
10. True victory is teaching god to cook, making it self-sufficient

## Ending Variations

**Consume and Ascend**: Become new gods together
**Wake the Sleeper**: God awakens, world transforms
**Seal Forever**: Close dungeon, trap Gourmet King
**Take the Throne**: Replace Gourmet King as eternal guardian
**Return Changed**: Survive and return with knowledge
**Become the Dungeon**: Merge with ecosystem itself

Each ending affects the world differently, no purely good or bad choice.
