### **World-Building Specification Brief: The Demon Barber's Revenge**

**1. Core Concept:**
A wrongfully imprisoned barber returns to Victorian London seeking revenge on the corrupt judge who destroyed his life, murdering customers and baking them into meat pies while party decides whether to stop or enable righteous but monstrous vengeance.

**2. Lore & History:**
*   Detail Benjamin Barker's backstory—happy barber, beautiful wife, infant daughter
*   Explain Judge Turpin's crime—false imprisonment, assault on wife, stealing daughter
*   Describe the transportation system—criminals shipped to Australia for years
*   Write about Fleet Street's poverty and desperate criminal element
*   Detail Victorian class system and legal corruption—the rich are untouchable
*   Explain Mrs. Lovett's history with Barker and her unrequited love
*   Describe the asylum system where inconvenient people are hidden

**3. Factions:**
*   **The Working Poor** (Major) - Fleet Street residents, potential victims and pie customers
*   **The Corrupt Judiciary** (Major) - Judge Turpin and fellow magistrates protecting each other
*   **The Police** (Minor) - Beadle Bamford and constables, more corrupt than helpful
*   **The Street Network** (Minor) - Beggars, children, informants who see everything
*   **The Reformers** (Minor) - Idealists like Anthony who believe in legal justice
*   **The Criminal Underworld** (Minor) - Actually less dangerous than respectable society

**4. NPCs:**
*   **Tier 1:** Sweeney Todd/Benjamin Barker, Mrs. Lovett, Judge Turpin, Johanna, Anthony Hope, Beadle Bamford
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Street Beggar," "Potential Victim," "Constable," "Asylum Keeper," "Rival Barber," "Pie Customer," "Factory Worker," "Street Singer"

**5. Locations:**
*   **Fleet Street** - Grimy neighborhood of desperate poverty and industry
*   **The Barber Shop** - Sweeney's domain with its trap-door chair of death
*   **Mrs. Lovett's Pie Shop** - Below the barber, ovens and meat grinders
*   **Judge Turpin's Manor** - Respectable exterior hiding cruelty and imprisoned Johanna
*   **The Asylum** - Victorian madhouse where inconvenient truths are buried

**6. Causality:**
*   IF party discovers the truth about pies THEN face horrifying choice about what to do
*   IF party tries to legally stop Judge Turpin THEN legal system protects him completely
*   IF Sweeney completes revenge on Judge THEN may finally rest or spiral further into madness
*   IF Johanna learns her true identity THEN tragedy as family destroys itself unknowingly
*   IF party enables Sweeney THEN share moral responsibility for innocent deaths
*   IF Mrs. Lovett's lies about Lucy (Sweeney's wife) are revealed THEN final betrayal completes tragedy
