# Creative Brief: Wrath of Achilles

## Art Style: Epic Bronze Age with Mythic Grandeur

Inspired by *Troy* and Homeric epics. Captures the bronze-age warfare, the majesty of ancient cities, the larger-than-life nature of heroes, and the divine intervention of gods.

### Core Principles:

*   **Emphasize Epic Scale:** Massive walls, thousand ships, armies clashing, heroes towering
*   **Bronze Age Warfare:** Spears and shields, bronze armor, chariots, phalanx combat
*   **Mythic Heroes:** Larger-than-life warriors performing impossible feats
*   **Divine Presence:** Gods watching from clouds, divine light, supernatural intervention

### Color Palette:

*   **Troy:** Golden stone walls, blue sky, white marble, lush gardens within
*   **Greeks:** Bronze armor, dark ships, leather and iron, sun-weathered
*   **Divine:** Golden light, blue divine energy, purple for royalty, celestial effects
*   **Siege:** Fire and smoke, blood on bronze, dust of battle, sunset reds
