**The Marked Assassin**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party, framed for the Empress's murder, escape with help from loyalists and are marked by the Outsider with supernatural powers. They must assassinate conspirators, rescue the heir Emily, and restore the throne. Every kill raises Chaos—the more brutal their methods, the darker the world and ending become. Choose between revenge and mercy.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Whaling Industry and how whale oil powers Dunwall's industrial revolution.
    *   Write about the Rat Plague's origin—brought from Pandyssia, weaponized by the Lord Regent.
    *   Describe the Outsider's origin—a mortal sacrificed in ritual, became the Void God.
    *   Explain the Abbey of the Everyman—the dominant religion that considers the Outsider heretical.
    *   Detail the political coup—the Lord Regent eliminated the Empress to rule through Emily.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Loyalists** (Major): Underground resistance trying to restore Emily, includes the party.
    *   **The City Watch** (Major): Military police enforcing the Lord Regent's martial law.
    *   **The Abbey of the Everyman** (Major): Religious organization spreading propaganda, hunting heretics.
    *   **The Whalers** (Minor): Assassin cult led by Daud, marked by the Outsider, responsible for Empress's death.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** The Outsider (void god), Emily (heir), Havelock (betrayer), Samuel (boatman), Piero (inventor).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "City Watch Officer" (guards), "Overseer" (religious enforcers), "Weeper" (plague zombie), "Assassin" (supernatural killers), "Noble" (targets).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Dunwall Tower:** Royal palace on an island, site of betrayal and final confrontation.
*   **Coldridge Prison:** Fortress prison on rock island, escape sequence.
*   **The Hound Pits Pub:** Abandoned pub on shore, loyalist hideout, changes with Chaos.
*   **Office of the High Overseer:** Religious headquarters, propaganda center, archives.
*   **The Golden Cat:** Upscale brothel in rich district, Pendleton brothers' base.
*   **Boyle Estate:** Aristocratic mansion, masquerade ball setting.
*   **The Flooded District:** Plague-devastated quarter, abandoned, overrun with weepers.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party kills many people → **THEN:** Chaos rises, more rats and weepers appear, world darkens, Emily learns violence.
*   **IF:** Party uses non-lethal methods → **THEN:** Chaos lowers, fewer enemies, plague recedes, Emily learns mercy.
*   **IF:** Chaos is High at end → **THEN:** Emily becomes tyrannical ruler, plague consumes the isles, dark ending.
*   **IF:** Chaos is Low at end → **THEN:** Emily becomes wise ruler, plague ends, golden age, hopeful ending.
*   **IF:** Samuel loses faith (High Chaos) → **THEN:** He fires warning shot before final mission, adding difficulty.
*   **IF:** Party discovers all secrets with Heart → **THEN:** Full understanding of corruption, harder to justify mercy or violence.
