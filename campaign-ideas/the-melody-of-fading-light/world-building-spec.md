**The Melody of Fading Light**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** A traumatized musical prodigy who lost his ability to hear his own music meets a vibrant, dying violinist who teaches him to play from the heart. Through performances and growing love, he heals his trauma, only to face the tragedy of her inevitable death. A story about finding beauty in transience and your own voice in grief.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts. These entries will form the historical and cultural bedrock of the world.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the history of the Academy of Melodic Arts and its role in training magical musicians whose performances can cast powerful spells.
    *   Write the legend of the Three Great Bards who could move mountains with music, and how modern musicians strive to reach that level.
    *   Describe the cultural significance of the Grand Competition and how winning it establishes a bard's career and legacy.
    *   Explain the magical illness called "Fading Resonance" that slowly drains a person's life force, and why it has no known cure.
    *   Detail the story of Saki Arima, a legendary but controversial bard who pushed her students to inhuman standards and died of the same illness as Kaori.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create detailed profiles for each faction listed below. Each profile must contain the specified fields.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Academy Faculty** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Nurture the next generation of magical musicians and maintain the Academy's prestigious reputation.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Headmaster, department heads, teachers, teaching assistants.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Provide excellent musical and magical education.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Some faculty knew about Saki Arima's abusive methods but allowed them because she produced winners.
        *   **Assets:** Training facilities, connections to performance venues, ability to nominate students for competitions.
        *   **Relationships:** Respected by the musical community, some tension with healers over their inability to cure Fading Resonance.
    *   **The Performers' Circle** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Create beautiful music and advance their careers through competitions and performances.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Loosely organized around talent and reputation. Informal rivalries and partnerships.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Perform, compete, improve.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Many hide personal struggles behind perfect performances, creating a culture of suppressed emotion.
        *   **Assets:** Talent, training, access to venues and audiences.
        *   **Relationships:** Competitive but generally supportive. Rivalries are intense but respectful.
    *   **The Healers' Guild** (Minor)
        *   **Goals:** Cure diseases and extend lives through magical and mundane medicine.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Archhealer, senior healers, healers, apprentices.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Provide medical care to all who need it.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Desperately seeking a cure for Fading Resonance, which has eluded them for generations. Feel profound guilt over each patient they lose.
        *   **Assets:** Medical knowledge, healing magic, hospital facilities.
        *   **Relationships:** Allied with the Academy, frustrated by their limitations, compassionate toward patients like Kaori.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Directive:** The Character Generation Agent must create full profiles for all NPCs listed. Tier 1 NPCs are unique individuals. Tier 2 & 3 are archetypes to be instantiated multiple times with unique details as needed by the simulation.
*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**
    *   **Kaori Miyazono:** Vibrant elven violinist dying from Fading Resonance, hides her condition behind cheerfulness.
    *   **Tsubaki Sawabe:** Human fighter and childhood friend who realizes too late she loves Kousei.
    *   **Ryota Watari:** Popular human fighter/rogue, best friend, emotionally perceptive despite his casual demeanor.
    *   **Emi Igawa:** Passionate halfling bard inspired by young Kousei's performances.
    *   **Takeshi Aiza:** Serious elven bard who competed with Kousei and felt abandoned when he quit.
    *   **Saki Arima (Memory/Ghost):** Kousei's deceased mother, brilliant but abusive bard.
*   **Tier 2 & 3 (Archetypes for Generation):**
    *   "Sympathetic Academy teacher who recognizes Kousei's trauma"
    *   "Competitive bard in various competitions"
    *   "Healer treating Kaori who knows her condition is terminal"
    *   "Audience member moved to tears by performances"
    *   "Ghost/memory of Saki Arima during flashbacks"
    *   "Kaori's parents, supportive but heartbroken about her illness"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below, populating all specified sub-sections.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **The Cherry Blossom Plaza:**
        *   **Key Landmarks:** Performance pavilion, cherry blossom trees, fountain, benches where musicians practice.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Performers, audiences, couples on dates, children playing.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Free performances, inspiration, meeting place.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Kaori performing recklessly for an enraptured crowd, street musicians offering advice, cherry blossom viewing festival, first meeting location revisited triggering memories, a proposal happening nearby.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "A famous bard proposed to his love at this plaza fifty years ago." "The cherry blossoms bloom year-round due to old magic, representing eternal beauty." "Musicians who perform here under the full moon are blessed with inspiration."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (pink and white blossoms, dappled sunlight, colorful performers), Sound (music from pavilion, rustling leaves, laughter), Smell (sweet cherry blossoms, spring air).
    *   **The Grand Concert Hall:**
        *   **Key Landmarks:** Massive stage with perfect acoustics, ornate balconies, backstage preparation rooms, the "Wall of Legends" displaying past winners.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Prestigious performers, judges, wealthy patrons, Academy representatives.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Performance opportunities, reputation building, career advancement.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Seeing Saki Arima's portrait on the Wall of Legends triggering trauma, other competitors preparing nervously, a legendary bard offering cryptic advice, experiencing stage fright flashback, Kaori's ethereal spirit appearing during final performance.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "This is where Kousei had his traumatic breakdown years ago." "Saki Arima won this competition five times consecutively." "The hall is said to be haunted by the emotions of every performance ever given here."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (grand architecture, spotlight on empty stage, audience in shadow), Sound (perfect acoustics where every note rings clear, applause echoing), Smell (polished wood, formal atmosphere).
    *   **Kaori's Hospital Room:**
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The medical bed with monitoring equipment, windows overlooking the city, a small table covered in flowers and letters, her violin case in the corner.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Kaori (weakening), healers, visitors (Kousei, her parents, friends).
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Medical care, visiting hours, a quiet place for difficult conversations.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Kaori weak but smiling for visitors, healers unable to meet anyone's eyes, finding Kaori's secret letters and diary, her parents quietly crying in the hallway, Kaori asking to hear about performances she missed.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Kaori knew she was dying before she met Kousei and chose to live vibrantly in her remaining time." "She has a letter prepared for Kousei to read after she dies." "The healers are trying an experimental treatment but don't believe it will work."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (sterile white walls warmed by personal touches, flowers everywhere, medical equipment, Kaori's pale but smiling face), Sound (beeping monitors, quiet conversations, sometimes her humming melodies), Smell (antiseptic, flowers, absence of life).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes. For each "IF" condition, the agent must pre-generate the narrative and environmental consequences for the "THEN" outcome.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** Kousei completes a successful performance where he expresses genuine emotion.
    *   **THEN:** His world gains more color. Describe specific elements transitioning from grayscale to color. His magical music becomes more powerful and beautiful. Kaori expresses pride and joy.
    *   **IF:** Kousei has a traumatic flashback triggered by performances or criticism.
    *   **THEN:** The world temporarily reverts to grayscale. He may freeze during performances. Requires emotional support from Kaori or friends to recover.
    *   **IF:** Kaori's condition visibly worsens (track on hidden countdown).
    *   **THEN:** She appears more fragile, tires easily, may collapse. Other characters notice. Healers become more urgent. Kousei experiences fear and denial.
    *   **IF:** Kousei confronts his feelings about his mother's abuse and death.
    *   **THEN:** Major breakthrough in his healing. Can perform in the Grand Concert Hall without freezing. His relationship with Kaori deepens as he becomes more emotionally available.
    *   **IF:** The final performance occurs while Kaori is in the hospital, unable to attend physically.
    *   **THEN:** As Kousei plays the piece he composed for her, her spirit appears on stage with him, glowing and ethereal. They perform a duet that is both impossible and perfect. The audience sees something magical. Kaori's body dies peacefully during this moment, her spirit fading as the final note rings out.
