# The Golden Colosseum - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Roman Epic Glory

**Aesthetic:** Classical antiquity, marble and gold, epic scale, heroic realism.

**Color Palette:** Imperial purple (#800080), Roman red (#8B0000), marble white (#F5F5DC), gold leaf (#FFD700), bronze (#CD7F32), blood crimson (#DC143C), sand yellow (#F4A460).

**Design References:** Gladiator (film), Spartacus, Ben-Hur, Roman architecture, classical sculpture.

## Music: Epic Orchestral

**Combat:** Hans Zimmer's Gladiator score style, war drums, brass fanfares.
**Training:** Rhythmic percussion, building intensity.
**Political:** Strings, subtle menace.
**Victory:** Triumphant choirs, immortal glory.
**Death:** Somber horns, honorable farewell.

**Voice Direction:** Theatrical, dramatic, Shakespearean in tone, Latin-influenced pronunciation, gravitas.

## Cinematic Moments
- Gladiators entering Colosseum, crowd roaring
- Emperor's judgment (thumbs up/down)
- Epic slow-motion combat
- Yield and mercy moment
- Beast hunt chaos
- Naval battle flooding
- Final duel at sunset
- Freedom granted, walking away
