**The Phantom's Game**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In the world of supernatural Hoopball, the party includes the Phantom Sixth—a player so unnoticeable they can misdirect all attention. They join Seirin Academy to defeat the Generation of Miracles: five prodigies with god-like individual abilities. Through perfect teamwork and the Zone state—a trance where time slows and superhuman plays become possible—they prove that synchronized teamwork can overcome transcendent individual talent.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the emergence of supernatural abilities in Hoopball players and the Zone state phenomenon.
    *   Write the history of Teikō Middle School where the Generation of Miracles dominated three consecutive championships.
    *   Describe how the five Miracles split up, choosing different high schools for the ultimate challenge.
    *   Explain Akashi's decision to disband the Generation and his philosophy of absolute victory.
    *   Detail the Phantom's role as the invisible sixth member and why their presence was erased from records.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Seirin High** (Major): Newly established program built on teamwork, party's home school.
    *   **Rakuzan High** (Major): Elite academy led by Akashi, demands absolute perfection and victory.
    *   **Tōō Academy** (Major): Aggressive style built around Aomine's overwhelming individual talent.
    *   **Shūtoku High** (Minor): Traditional program with perfect execution, home to Midorima.
    *   **Yōsen High** (Minor): Defense-focused team built around Murasakibara's absolute presence.
    *   **Kaijō High** (Minor): Balanced team where Kise can copy and adapt any strategy.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Taiga Kagami (ace partner), Seijūrō Akashi (Emperor), Daiki Aomine (transcendent ace), Shintarō Midorima (perfect shooter), Atsushi Murasakibara (absolute shield), Ryōta Kise (perfect copy).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Zone User" (supernatural focus state), "Miracle Counter" (developed specifically to stop one Miracle), "Former Teikō Player" (knows the Miracles' history), "Street Baller" (raw talent without formal training).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Seirin High Gymnasium:** Modern practice facility with regulation court, training rooms, strategy boards analyzing the Miracles.
*   **Winter Cup Arena:** National tournament venue with multiple courts, broadcast facilities, massive spectator capacity.
*   **Streetball Court:** Urban outdoor court with chain nets and concrete where raw talent emerges.
*   **Training Camp:** Intensive facility for building stamina to maintain Zone state longer, brutal conditioning.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Phantom reveals their presence → **THEN:** Misdirection charges refresh, opponents become wary, team coordination improves.
*   **IF:** Player enters Zone state → **THEN:** Time perception slows, abilities multiply, but mental exhaustion accumulates.
*   **IF:** Miracle is defeated → **THEN:** They respect party, may offer advice, begin questioning Akashi's philosophy.
*   **IF:** Party achieves Perfect Zone (all members simultaneously) → **THEN:** Unlock combination plays impossible individually, can counter Emperor Eye.
*   **IF:** Akashi's true personality emerges → **THEN:** Rakuzan becomes truly coordinated team, makes them more dangerous but vulnerable to teamwork counters.
