### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

**Obey and Consume**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Working-class drifters discover special sunglasses revealing subliminal alien messages commanding humans to "OBEY" and "CONSUME." The ruling class are skull-faced aliens using mind control while draining Earth. Join the resistance to destroy the signal and awaken humanity, even if most people fight to remain asleep in comfortable ignorance.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the alien infiltration timeline and how they took control gradually.
    *   Write the history of the subliminal signal technology and its effects.
    *   Describe the creation of the resistance and discovery of the glasses.
    *   Explain why aliens are here—refugees from dying world seeking resources.
    *   Detail human collaborators and why some serve aliens knowing the truth.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Aliens** (Major): Skull-faced infiltrators controlling humanity through subliminal signals.
    *   **The Resistance** (Major): Awakened humans fighting to reveal truth and destroy the signal.
    *   **The Collaborators** (Minor): Humans who know truth but serve aliens for power.
    *   **The Sleepers** (Minor): Brainwashed majority living under subliminal control.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Nada (drifter guide), The Preacher (resistance leader), Holly (collaborator), Gilbert (scientist), Frank (skeptic friend).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Alien Infiltrator" (disguised as human elite), "Awakened Rebel" (resistance fighter), "Collaborator Guard" (knowing servant), "Brainwashed Citizen" (subliminal slave).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Homeless Camp:** Where awakened gather before raids disperse them.
*   **The Church:** Resistance headquarters disguised as religious building.
*   **The City:** Urban landscape covered in subliminal messaging.
*   **Transmitter Complex:** Alien facility broadcasting control signal.
*   **Portal Room:** Where aliens arrive from their dying homeworld.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Wear the glasses → **THEN:** See subliminal messages, identify aliens, become target.
*   **IF:** Try to awaken others → **THEN:** Most resist, some fight violently to stay asleep.
*   **IF:** Destroy local transmitter → **THEN:** Signal weakens in area, easier to awaken people.
*   **IF:** Aliens detect awakened → **THEN:** Watches alert, enforcers dispatched, elimination attempts.
*   **IF:** Broadcast truth globally → **THEN:** All humans see messages, mass awakening, chaos and liberation.
