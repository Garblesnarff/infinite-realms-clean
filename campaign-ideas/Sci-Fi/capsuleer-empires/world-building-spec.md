**Capsuleer Empires**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** The players are Capsuleers—immortal starship pilots who transfer consciousness upon death. They build corporate empires, wage economic wars, and navigate politics of four great empires in New Eden where trust is a liability.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the EVE Gate collapse that trapped humanity in New Eden.
    *   Write the history of the four empires and their conflicts: Caldari (corporate), Gallente (democracy), Amarr (religious slavers), Minmatar (freed slaves).
    *   Describe the development of capsuleer technology and its cost in human lives.
    *   Explain the Jovian Directorate's rise, genetic modifications, and mysterious decline.
    *   Detail Sansha's Nation formation and their abduction of "true citizens."
    *   Write about CONCORD—the police force maintaining law in high-security space.
    *   Describe the wormholes and Sleeper technology found in W-space.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Caldari State** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Corporate profit, expansion, military supremacy through efficiency.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Megacorporations run the state, with CEOs as de facto rulers.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Free market meritocracy benefits all.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Corporate oligarchy seeks total control through economic dominance.
        *   **Assets:** Advanced industry, massive navy, economic power.
        *   **Relationships:** At war with Gallente; trades with Amarr; neutral to Minmatar.
    *   **Gallente Federation** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Spread democracy and freedom, oppose slavery.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Elected government with president and senate.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Liberty and justice for all sentient beings.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Some factions use "spreading democracy" as cover for imperialism.
        *   **Assets:** Diverse population, drone technology, cultural influence.
        *   **Relationships:** At war with Caldari; allied with Minmatar; hostile to Amarr.
    *   **Amarr Empire** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Religious supremacy, expansion through conquest and conversion.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Theocratic empire led by Empress and Privy Council.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Bring all humanity into God's grace.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Maintain slavery as economic foundation while claiming religious necessity.
        *   **Assets:** Largest territory, energy weapons, vast slave labor force.
        *   **Relationships:** Allied with Caldari; hostile to Gallente and Minmatar.
    *   **Minmatar Republic** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Free all enslaved Minmatar, rebuild after Amarr occupation.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Tribal council with elected officials.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Freedom and self-determination for all tribes.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Some extremist tribes seek revenge, not just liberation.
        *   **Assets:** Guerrilla tactics, speed-focused ships, resourcefulness.
        *   **Relationships:** Allied with Gallente; at war with Amarr; neutral to Caldari.
    *   **Sansha's Nation** (Major Enemy)
        *   **Goals:** Create "true citizens" through mind control, unite humanity perfectly.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by Sansha Kuvakei, with enslaved administrators below.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Perfect unity without conflict or suffering.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Sansha seeks validation for his methods and revenge on those who exiled him.
        *   **Assets:** Mind-control technology, advanced ships, enslaved populations.
        *   **Relationships:** Enemy of all empires; conducts incursion raids.

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **Jita 4-4 (Trade Hub):** The busiest station in New Eden, where trillions of ISK flow daily.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** Market terminals, corporate offices, docking bays, CONCORD station.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Capsuleers, traders, corporate agents, CONCORD police.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Every item in game, contracts, corporate offices, market manipulation.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Market scam, suicide ganker caught, corporate merger announcement, spy recruitment, massive trade deal.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Someone's cornering the market on ship components." "A rival corp is hiring mercenaries." "Sansha incursion predicted nearby."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (massive station, ship traffic, holographic markets), Sound (market alerts, comms chatter, docking requests), Smell (recycled air, nothing—space station sterile).
    *   **Null-Sec Territory (Player Sovereignty):** Lawless space claimed by capsuleer alliances.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** Player-built stations, territorial claim units, defensive bubbles, wreck fields.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Alliance members, invaders, pirates, neutrals passing through.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Sovereignty warfare, territory control, unlimited PvP.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Gate camp, roaming gang, sovereignty timer battle, spy infiltration, diplomatic envoy.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "A major alliance is collapsing from internal betrayal." "Valuable moon mining rights are up for grabs." "A spy sold sovereignty intel to rivals."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (dark space, distant stars, territorial markers, wreckage), Sound (comms discipline, FC commands, silence), Smell (none—vacuum).
    *   **Wormhole System (W-Space):** Hidden systems accessed via unstable wormholes, no local chat.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** Sleeper structures, resource sites, unstable wormholes, player POS (player-owned structures).
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Sleeper AI, W-space corporations, resource harvesters.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Advanced Sleeper technology, high-value resources, extreme danger.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Sleeper AI attack, ambush (no local warning), wormhole collapse stranding players, profitable data site, C5/C6 eviction war.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Sleepers guard Jovian secrets." "A corp controls multiple wormhole systems." "Someone found a static connection to Jita."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (strange nebulae, Sleeper structures, isolation), Sound (eerie silence, no comms indicators, Sleeper weapon sounds), Smell (none—ship interior only).
    *   **Sansha's Mothership (Incursion Site):** Massive carrier where abducted citizens are converted.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** Mind-control chambers, True Citizen factories, Sansha's throne, abduction bays.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Sansha Kuvakei, enslaved "citizens," Sansha's fleet.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Final battle, high-value loot, liberation of citizens.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Sansha fleet patrol, mind-control attempt, citizen conversion ritual, fleet coordination challenge, Kuvakei's monologue.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Sansha was once a respected capsuleer." "The converted citizens feel no pain or fear." "Destroying the mothership ends the incursion."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (sterile white corridors, conversion pods, fleet displays), Sound (Sansha's broadcasts, screams turning to silence, engine hums), Smell (antiseptic, ozone, nothing human).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** The players' corporation declares war on a rival.
    *   **THEN:** All corp members become legal targets. Must defend assets and manage war costs weekly.
    *   **IF:** The players claim sovereignty in null-sec.
    *   **THEN:** They must defend it 24/7. Generates passive income but attracts invaders.
    *   **IF:** The players corner a market (buy 80%+ of an item).
    *   **THEN:** They can manipulate prices. Rival traders attempt to break the monopoly.
    *   **IF:** The players lose expensive ships repeatedly (bankruptcy).
    *   **THEN:** They're stuck in starter ships. Must rebuild wealth from scratch.
    *   **IF:** The players successfully defend against Sansha incursions multiple times.
    *   **THEN:** They're invited to assault Sansha's Mothership. High-risk, high-reward final mission.
    *   **IF:** The players betray alliance members for profit.
    *   **THEN:** They're blacklisted from future alliances. Must operate as solo pirates or mercenaries.
