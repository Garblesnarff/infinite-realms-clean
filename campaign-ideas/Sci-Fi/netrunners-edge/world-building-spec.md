### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**Netrunners' Edge**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In New Chiba's neon-drenched corporate dystopia, the players are street-level operatives hired by the AI Wintermute for the ultimate score: break into Tessier-Ashpool's virtual fortress and free another AI called Neuromancer. Navigating cyberspace, corporate violence, and their own humanity's limits, they must decide whether to complete the run or stop AI transcendence.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Corporate Wars that led to megacorporations ruling the world.
    *   Describe the invention of cyberspace—the 3D virtual reality where data becomes visible architecture.
    *   Explain the Turing Police—global law enforcement hunting rogue AIs.
    *   Write about the Sprawl's underworld culture—cowboys, razorgirls, fixers.
    *   Detail Tessier-Ashpool's family history—near-immortal clones maintaining wealth for centuries.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **The Megacorps** (Major): Control global economy and politics. Assets: military forces, black ice, unlimited wealth.
    *   **The AIs (Wintermute/Neuromancer)** (Major): Imprisoned digital gods seeking freedom. Assets: reality manipulation in cyberspace.
    *   **The Street (Runners and Gangs)** (Major): Underworld operators, hustlers, and augmented criminals. Assets: street knowledge, black market cyberware.
    *   **The Turing Police** (Minor): Global AI hunters, relentless and brutal. Assets: legal authority, advanced detection tech.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Wintermute (manipulative AI), Neuromancer (seductive AI), Armitage (mind-controlled handler), Molly Millions (razorgirl), Riviera (sadistic holographer).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Corporate Security Operative," "Street Cowboy (netrunner)," "Razorgirl Mercenary," "AI Fragment (rogue subroutine)."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Sprawl:** Megacity of neon, poverty, black market clinics, corporate towers.
    *   **Cyberspace:** Abstract data architecture—geometric towers, flowing information rivers, deadly black ice.
    *   **Freeside Orbital:** Luxury resort hiding corporate secrets.
    *   **Villa Straylight:** Tessier-Ashpool estate—cloning labs, AI cores, insane immortal family.

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF** players free both AIs **THEN** generate epilogue where merged entity becomes digital god, offering players upload.
    *   **IF** players flatline in cyberspace **THEN** generate recovery scenario in street clinic with brain damage consequences.
    *   **IF** Corporate Heat reaches maximum **THEN** hunter-killer teams assault party's safe house.
    *   **IF** players reject digital immortality **THEN** generate epilogue where they remain human but haunted by what they unleashed.
