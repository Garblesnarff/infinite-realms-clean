### **World-Building Specification Brief: Who Knows What Evil**

**1. Core Concept:**
By day respected members of society, by night the vigilante Shadow and agents who cloud men's minds and strike fear into criminal hearts, battling organized crime and occult threats in a 1930s noir metropolis.

**2. Lore & History:**
*   Detail The Shadow's origin—training in the Orient, learning to cloud minds, return to fight crime
*   Explain the city's power structure—corrupt officials, crime families, legitimate business fronts
*   Describe the occult underground—real magic exists but is rare and dangerous
*   Write about The Shadow's agent network and how it was built
*   Detail high society and its dark underbelly
*   Explain the international tensions of the era (equivalent to pre-WWII)

**3. Factions:**
*   **The Shadow's Network** (Major) - Vigilantes, agents, informants across all social classes
*   **The Crime Syndicate** (Major) - Five families controlling different criminal enterprises
*   **The Police** (Major) - Mix of honest cops and corrupt officials
*   **High Society** (Minor) - Wealthy elite, some innocent, some complicit in crime
*   **Foreign Intelligence** (Minor) - Spies and saboteurs from rival nations
*   **The Occult Underground** (Minor) - Practitioners of genuine dark magic
*   **The Press** (Minor) - Journalists seeking truth or sensationalism

**4. NPCs:**
*   **Tier 1:** Lamont Cranston/The Shadow, Margo Lane, Commissioner Weston, Shiwan Khan, The Voodoo Master, Dr. Tam
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Crime Boss," "Corrupt Detective," "Society Dame," "Cab Driver Informant," "Nightclub Singer," "Occult Practitioner," "Street Thug," "Mad Scientist"

**5. Locations:**
*   **The Sanctum** - Hidden base, criminal files, communications center
*   **Cranston Estate** - Opulent mansion, maintains cover identity
*   **The Blue Jade** - Nightclub/information hub secretly owned by Shadow
*   **City Hall** - Corrupt government seat, police headquarters
*   **Chinatown** - Mysterious district, Shadow's allies and training ground
*   **The Docks** - Smuggling operations, warehouse battles
*   **The Penthouse** - Villain lairs, society parties, rooftop confrontations

**6. Causality:**
*   IF secret identity exposed THEN all enemies converge, civilian life destroyed
*   IF crime boss defeated publicly THEN power vacuum creates gang war
*   IF Shadow kills rather than intimidates THEN becomes what he fights
*   IF agent network compromised THEN must rebuild intelligence from scratch
*   IF corruption exposed fully THEN city-wide chaos as government collapses
*   IF Shiwan Khan's psychic power matches Shadow's THEN mental battle with reality itself at stake

**7. MacGuffins & Artifacts:**
*   **The Fire Opal Ring (Girasol)** - Glows near danger, Shadow's signature
*   **Mind-Clouding Talisman** - Enhances psychic invisibility powers
*   **The Criminal Files** - Complete dossiers on city's underworld
*   **Stolen Death Ray** - Mad science weapon crime lords seek
*   **Mystic Scrolls** - Teach advanced mental techniques

**8. Investigation Types:**
*   Murder mysteries, blackmail schemes, smuggling operations, occult rituals, political corruption, sabotage plots, kidnappings, heists, con games, spy networks
