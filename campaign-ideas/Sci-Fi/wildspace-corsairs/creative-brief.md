# Creative Brief: Wildspace Corsairs

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Age of Sail in Space (Spelljammer-inspired)

Inspired by the whimsical space fantasy of *Spelljammer*, classic age of sail naval adventure, and cosmic wonder. This style captures wooden ships sailing star-filled voids, exotic alien worlds, and the romance of nautical adventure transplanted to infinite space.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Space Sailing:** Wooden ships with sails in airless void, naval aesthetics in space
*   **Cosmic Wonder:** Star-filled backgrounds, colorful nebulae, planets of impossible variety
*   **Exotic Aliens:** Non-standard fantasy races adapted to space
*   **Nautical Adventure:** Age of sail romance, pirate flags, naval combat, crew camaraderie

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Swashbuckling adventure, cosmic exploration, age of sail in space, wonder, freedom, exotic frontiers
*   **Feeling:** The thrill of sailing between worlds combined with nautical tradition. Infinite possibility in every direction. Camaraderie of diverse crews.

**2. Color Palette:**
*   **Ships:** Natural wood, brass fittings, colorful sails, pirate paint schemes
*   **Space:** Deep blacks with brilliant stars, colorful nebulae (purples, blues, pinks)
*   **Planets:** Extreme variety—red fire worlds, blue water spheres, green garden planets
*   **The Phlogiston:** Rainbow colors—red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple swirling
*   **Prompt Example:** "...a color palette of natural wood ships against deep black space filled with brilliant stars. Colorful nebulae in purple and blue provide background. Red fire planets and blue water worlds visible. Brass fittings gleam. Pirate sails in crimson and black. Rainbow phlogiston swirls in the distance."

**3. Character Design:**
*   **Human Corsairs:** Age of sail clothing—long coats, tricorn hats, cutlasses, practical sailing gear
*   **Giff:** Humanoid hippo mercenaries in Victorian military uniforms with enormous muskets
*   **Hadozee:** Flying squirrel-people with skin flaps, acrobatic sailors
*   **Plasmoids:** Ooze-shaped beings taking humanoid form, translucent and colorful
*   **Neogi:** Evil eel creatures with spider legs, slave traders
*   **Prompt Example:** "A diverse ship crew on deck. Human captain in tricorn hat and long coat. Giff gunner—humanoid hippo in red military jacket polishing massive musket. Hadozee rigger—flying squirrel person hanging from rigging. Plasmoid navigator—translucent blue ooze in roughly humanoid shape. All on wooden deck with space visible beyond."

**4. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Wildspace:** Airless void filled with stars, planets visible as distant spheres, ships sailing in formation
*   **Rock of Bral:** Massive asteroid carved into city, docks extending into space, ships moored
*   **Crystal Sphere:** Impenetrable shell enclosing solar system, slightly visible from inside
*   **Alien Planets:** Fire world with lava oceans, water world with floating cities, garden world with impossible forests
*   **Prompt Example:** "The Rock of Bral—a massive asteroid carved into a city. Docks extend into space where spelljammer ships are moored. Wooden ships with colorful sails float in zero gravity. Markets and buildings carved into asteroid surface. Stars and nebulae visible beyond. Ships of all sizes and designs—galleons, squid ships, nautiloids—fill the port."

**5. Ships and Space Phenomena:**
*   **Spelljammer Ships:** Wooden vessels in various designs—galleons, hammerships, squid ships, nautiloids
*   **Magical Sails:** Sometimes translucent, glowing, or impossible colors
*   **Ship Gravity:** Visible gravity planes where crew stands on top and bottom
*   **Space Combat:** Ballista bolts, catapult fire, boarding actions with grappling hooks
*   **The Spelljammer:** Massive manta ray-shaped vessel the size of a city
*   **Prompt Example:** "A space naval battle. A wooden galleon fires ballista at a mind flayer nautiloid (shell-shaped organic ship). Both have glowing sails. Crew members board via magical grappling hooks. Stars and a ringed planet provide backdrop. Weapon fire trails across void. Zero gravity debris from damaged ships floats between vessels."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Starlight:** Bright pinpoint stars providing cold light
*   **Sunlight:** One strong directional light source from system's sun
*   **Nebula Glow:** Soft colored light from gas clouds
*   **Ship Lights:** Lanterns, magical illumination, warm interior glow
*   **Prompt Example:** "...lit by brilliant starlight and distant sunlight. A purple nebula provides soft ambient glow. Ship lanterns cast warm orange light on wooden decks. One side of the ship is brightly lit by sun, the other in deep shadow. Magic from spelljammer helm glows blue-white inside cabin."

## Voice

An enthusiastic naval captain's voice full of wanderlust and adventure. The pacing is energetic with wind-in-sails momentum. The tone shifts between swashbuckling excitement and quiet wonder at cosmic vistas. Occasional nautical terminology mixed with space terms. Underlying theme of freedom and exploration. The voice carries the spirit of age of sail adventure transplanted to infinite stars. Role: The voice of cosmic adventure and exploration.
