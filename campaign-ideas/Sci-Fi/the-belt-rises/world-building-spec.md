# The Belt Rises - World-Building Specification

## The Solar System Political Map

### Earth (United Nations)
**Population**: 30 billion
**Government**: United Nations bureaucracy
**Territory**: Earth, Luna, orbital stations
**Power**: Political legitimacy, largest population
**Military**: UN Navy, Marine Corps
**Issues**: Overpopulation, basic assistance dependency, environmental decay
**Perspective**: Old power trying to maintain dominance

### Mars Congressional Republic (MCR)
**Population**: 5 billion
**Government**: Parliamentary republic
**Territory**: Mars, Deimos, Phobos
**Project**: Terraforming Mars (generational effort)
**Military**: Mars Congressional Republic Navy (MCRN), powerful fleet
**Culture**: Discipline, sacrifice for future, pride
**Issues**: Brain drain after gates open, terraforming dream dying

### The Outer Planets Alliance (OPA/The Belt)
**Population**: 100+ million (scattered)
**Government**: Fragmented, multiple factions
**Territory**: Asteroid belt (Ceres, Eros, Pallas, etc.), outer moons
**Economy**: Mining, water harvesting, resource extraction
**Military**: Initially weak, grows throughout campaign
**Culture**: Belter creole language, independence, exploitation resentment
**Leader**: Fred Johnson ("Butcher of Anderson Station")

### The Free Navy (Later Development)
**Leader**: Marco Inaros
**Goal**: Belt independence through force
**Methods**: Terrorism, asteroid strikes on Earth
**Support**: Disillusioned Belters
**Opposition**: Other OPA factions, Earth, Mars

## Technology in Detail

### Propulsion
**Epstein Drive**: Fusion torch, constant acceleration
**Efficiency**: Allows weeks/months between planets (vs. years)
**Thrust Gravity**: Acceleration creates artificial gravity
**Flip and Burn**: Accelerate halfway, flip, decelerate
**Juice**: Performance-enhancing drug for high-G maneuvers

### Realistic Space Combat
**Torpedoes**: Self-guided missiles
**PDC (Point Defense Cannons)**: Rapid-fire anti-missile
**Rail Guns**: Kinetic impactors, devastating
**Laser Communication**: Light-speed limited
**Stealth**: Heat management, emissions control
**No Sound**: Space battles are silent

### Suits and Habitats
**Pressure Suits**: Essential for vacuum work
**Mag Boots**: Walking in zero-G
**Rotating Habitats**: Spin gravity for stations
**Radiation Shielding**: Crucial for survival
**Life Support**: Recycling air and water

### Communication
**Laser Tightbeam**: Secure, line-of-sight
**Radio**: Broadcast, detectable
**Delay**: Light-speed lag affects conversation
**Ansible**: Does not exist (realistic)

## The Protomolecule

### Origin
**Creators**: Ring Builders (extinct civilization)
**Purpose**: Von Neumann construction tool
**Age**: 2+ billion years
**Discovery**: Phoebe (Saturn moon)
**Activation**: Eros station experiment

### Capabilities
**Self-replication**: Uses any matter
**Terraforming**: Reprograms matter at molecular level
**Adaptation**: Learns and evolves
**Hybrid Creation**: Integrates with biology
**Higher-Dimensional**: Accesses physics we don't understand

### Effects on Humans
**Infection**: Hijacks nervous system
**Vomit Zombies**: Intermediate stage
**Biomass**: Individual consciousness lost
**Hybrids**: Partial human consciousness retained
**Protomolecule Plague**: Eros station horror

### The Ring and Gates
**Construction**: Built from Venus protomolecule
**Function**: Gateway to Slow Zone
**Slow Zone**: Bubble universe, altered physics
**1,373 Gates**: Access to habitable systems
**Station**: Central hub, Ring Builder tech

## Belter Culture (Detailed)

### Physiology
**Tall and Thin**: Low gravity adaptation
**Brittle Bones**: Can't visit high-G worlds
**Larger Heads**: Different cranial development
**Generational**: Takes decades of space living

### Language
**Belter Creole**: Mixture of languages (English, Chinese, Romance, Slavic)
**Grammar**: Simplified, distinct from Earth languages
**Cultural Identity**: Marker of Belt origin
**Code-switching**: Educated Belters speak Standard too

### Economy
**Resource Extraction**: Mining asteroids, ice
**Water Harvesting**: Most valuable commodity
**Contract Labor**: Often exploitative
**Black Market**: Smuggling, piracy
**Remittances**: Supporting families

### Political Factions
**Fred Johnson's OPA**: Legitimate, seeks recognition
**Anderson Dawes' OPA**: More radical
**Marco Inaros' Free Navy**: Terrorist/freedom fighters
**Pirates and Criminals**: Kenzo, others

### Social Issues
**Air Tax**: Corporations charge for breathing
**Water Rationing**: Strict controls
**Generational Ships**: Nauvoo, other projects
**Independence Dreams**: vs. practical limitations

## Major Historical Events

### Anderson Station Massacre
**Date**: 15 years before campaign
**Event**: Peaceful Belt protest violently suppressed
**Casualties**: 173 Belters killed
**Perpetrator**: Colonel Fred Johnson (UN Marine)
**Aftermath**: Johnson becomes "Butcher," later OPA leader
**Impact**: Radicalized Belt, created martyrs

### The Canterbury Destruction
**False Flag**: Stealth ship attacks ice hauler
**Survivors**: Holden, Naomi, Amos, Alex (main crew)
**Cover-up**: Blamed on Mars
**Purpose**: Escalate Earth-Mars tensions
**Revelation**: Corporate conspiracy (Protogen)

### Eros Incident
**Protogen Experiment**: 100,000 unwilling test subjects
**Infection**: Protomolecule spreads
**Lockdown**: Station quarantined
**Miller's Sacrifice**: Crashes Eros into Venus
**Trauma**: Survivors haunted

### The Ring Appears
**Venus Launch**: Protomolecule construction complete
**Gate Structure**: Appears beyond Uranus orbit
**Free Fire Zone**: All factions claim it
**Slow Zone Crisis**: Speed limit kills aggressors
**Opening**: Access to 1,373 systems

## Stations and Locations

### Ceres Station
**Largest Belt Station**: 6.5 million population
**Rotation Gravity**: Spins for 0.3 G
**Crime and Poverty**: Overcrowded, oppressed
**Star Helix**: Private security firm
**Anderson Dawes**: OPA power broker

### Eros Station
**Entertainment Hub**: Vice capital of Belt
**Population**: 1.5 million (before incident)
**Protomolecule Test Site**: Horror location
**Destruction**: Crashes into Venus
**Legacy**: Changed everything

### Tycho Station
**Fred Johnson's Base**: OPA headquarters
**Construction Facility**: Built Nauvoo and warships
**Strategic Location**: Between Earth and Belt
**Refugees**: Houses many after Eros

### Ganymede Station
**Agricultural Hub**: Feeds much of system
**Mirrors and Domes**: Advanced farming
**Protomolecule Child**: Mei Meng experiment
**Battle**: Destroyed in fighting
**Refugees**: Mass exodus

### Illus (New Terra)
**First Colony World**: Through Ring gate
**Lithium Rich**: Valuable resources
**RCE Corporation**: Charter holders
**Belter Refugees**: Squatters
**Alien Artifacts**: Defense systems activate

**Crew**: 4-6 comfortable

**Class**: Ice hauler (mid-range)
**Crew**: 50+
**Purpose**: Haul ice from Saturn's rings
**Fate**: Destroyed by stealth ship

**Class**: Battleship (MCRN flagship)
**Size**: Massive, over 500m
**Crew**: Thousands
**Armament**: Overwhelming firepower
**Fate**: Destroyed by stealth ships

### Nauvoo/Behemoth
**Original Purpose**: Generation ship for Mormons
**Size**: Largest ship humans built
**Repurpose**: Attempted Eros impact
**Later**: OPA flagship

## The Ring Builder War

### The Ring Builders
**Extinct**: Killed 2 billion years ago
**Technology**: Ring gates, advanced physics
**Extent**: Billions of systems, galactic empire
**Protomolecule**: Their tool
**Slow Zone**: Their security system

### Their Enemy
**Unknown**: Never seen
**Method**: Attacked through gates
**Effectiveness**: Killed builders instantly across galaxy
**Weapon**: Affects consciousness directly
**Threat**: Still exists outside spacetime

### The Gatebuilder Ruins
**Alien Structures**: On colony worlds
**Active Defenses**: React to human presence
**Physics**: Don't match our understanding
**Danger**: Can be triggered
**Mystery**: Purpose unclear
