# The Belt Rises - Creative Brief

## Art Style and Visual Tone
- **Primary Style**: Hard sci-fi realism, documentary feel
- **Ships**: Utilitarian, realistic, visible thrust mechanics
- **Stations**: Industrial, rotating habitats, magnetic boots
- **Belters**: Tall, thin from low gravity, distinctive appearance
- **Protomolecule**: Organic-tech horror, blue bioluminescence

## Color Palettes
- **Space**: Realistic blacks, no sounds, cold and unforgiving
- **Belter Stations**: Harsh LED lighting, industrial grays
- **Earth**: Blue-green, crowded, wealthy core
- **Mars**: Red deserts, dome cities, military discipline
- **Protomolecule**: Sickly blue-green corruption spreading

## Music and Sound Design
- **Main Theme**: Atmospheric, tension-building strings
- **Belter Culture**: Folk music, work songs, cultural identity
- **Combat**: Realistic (muffled in suits, silent in vacuum)
- **Protomolecule**: Disturbing organic sounds with tech undertones
- **Political**: Quiet, intimate conversations carrying weight

## Cinematic Moments
- **Canterbury Destruction**: Watching ship explode, friends dying
- **Donnager Battle**: Outmatched warship fighting desperately
- **Eros Horror**: Walking through infected station, nightmare
- **Miller's Walk**: Solo journey to Julie, love and sacrifice
- **Venus Event**: Protomolecule launching ring to orbit
- **Ring Activation**: 1,373 gates opening simultaneously
- **"Doors and corners, kid. That's where they get you."**

## Artistic Inspirations
- The Expanse (TV series)
- 2001: A Space Odyssey (realistic space)
- Apollo 13 (practical problem-solving)
- Gravity (dangerous vacuum)
