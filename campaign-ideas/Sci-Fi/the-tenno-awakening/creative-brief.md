# Creative Brief: The Tenno Awakening

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Bio-Tech Space Ninja (Warframe-inspired)

Inspired by Warframe's unique aesthetic blending sleek bio-mechanical designs, techno-organic forms, and graceful acrobatic combat. This style emphasizes the alien beauty of Orokin architecture and the terrifying elegance of living weapons.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Bio-Mechanical Fusion:** Technology should look organic, grown rather than built.
*   **Graceful Lethality:** Combat is balletic, ninjas in space moving with impossible fluidity.
*   **Orokin Opulence:** Ancient architecture is ornate, ivory and gold, both beautiful and unsettling.

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Bio-mechanical, space ninja, techno-organic, graceful, mysterious, faction warfare.
*   **Feeling:** Elegant violence, ancient mysteries awakening, warriors who are also living weapons.

**2. Line Work & Form:**
*   **Style:** Sleek, organic curves mixed with angular tech elements.
*   **Forms:** Warframes have biomechanical designs—synthetic muscle visible under armor plating.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A Tenno Warframe with sleek bio-mechanical armor, organic curves blended with technological components, in an acrobatic combat pose."

**3. Color Palette:**
*   **Style:** Faction-coded with energy accents.
*   **Tenno/Orokin:** Ivory whites, golds, blues with energy glow accents (customizable).
*   **Corpus:** Clean whites, blues, robotic chrome.
*   **Grineer:** Rusted reds, industrial grays, deteriorated metals.
*   **Infested:** Sickly greens, fleshy pinks, pulsating organs.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...a color palette of ivory white and gold Orokin architecture with blue energy accents, contrasted against rusty red Grineer industrial structures."

**4. Character Design:**
*   **Style:** Warframes are bio-mechanical suits with unique silhouettes, no visible faces.
*   **Variety:** Each Warframe has distinct designs—some bulky, some skeletal, some elegant.
*   **Motion:** Emphasis on acrobatic poses—mid-flip, wall-running, blade-spinning.
*   **Prompt Example:** "An Excalibur Warframe, sleek bio-mechanical design with no visible face, ivory and gold plating, wielding an energy blade while wall-running."

**5. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Style:** Varied—Orokin opulent ruins, Corpus clean facilities, Grineer industrial decay, Infested organic corruption.
*   **Orokin:** Ornate golden halls with impossible geometry, tree-like pillars, floating platforms.
*   **The Void:** Reality-bending space with inverted physics and ethereal beauty.
*   **Prompt Example:** "An Orokin tower interior, with ivory walls decorated in gold filigree, impossible architectural angles, energy streams flowing upward, tree-like support columns."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Style:** Clean with energy glow highlights.
*   **Energy:** Warframe abilities glow with customizable colors—blues, reds, purples.
*   **Orokin:** Soft golden light from unknown sources.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...dramatically lit by golden Orokin ambient light, with bright blue energy radiating from a Warframe's abilities."

**7. Composition:**
*   **Style:** Dynamic, emphasizing motion and grace in combat.
*   **Techniques:** Use diagonal lines showing movement, frame acrobatic poses, show scale against massive enemies.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A dynamic shot of a Tenno mid-bullet-jump, surrounded by enemy fire, energy blade extended, with Corpus robots in the background."

## Voice

A calm, mysterious female voice with maternal warmth but underlying enigma. The tone is guiding and protective but holds secrets. Slight digital processing suggesting communication through technology. The emotion is controlled compassion masking uncertainty. Audio quality is crisp with subtle sci-fi filtering. Role: The Lotus, guiding the Tenno through missions, or a Tenno reflecting on their nature.
