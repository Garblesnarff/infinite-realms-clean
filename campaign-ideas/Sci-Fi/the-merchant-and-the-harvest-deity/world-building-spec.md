**The Merchant and the Harvest Deity**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** A traveling merchant discovers an ancient harvest wolf deity in his cart. She asks him to take her to her distant homeland. They journey together through trade routes, engaging in economic adventures and intellectual debates while slowly falling in loveâ€”mortal and divine, practical and playful, time-limited and eternal.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the decline of the old gods as new faiths spread through economic rather than theological means.
    *   Write about the medieval economic systems: guilds, currencies, trade routes.
    *   Describe Yoitsu, Holo's legendary homeland, and its tragic fate.
    *   Explain how harvest deities like Holo were once worshipped but are now forgotten.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **The Merchant Guilds:** Control trade, set prices, navigate competition and politics.
*   **The Church of the New Faith:** Suppressing old gods, consolidating power through economic control.
*   **The Old Gods:** Scattered, forgotten deities like Holo seeking purpose.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:**
    *   **Holo:** Ancient wolf harvest deity, proud and playful, fears loneliness.
    *   **Lawrence:** Practical merchant, clever trader, dreams of settling down.
    *   **Zheren:** Rival merchant, charming and unscrupulous.
    *   **Norah Arendt:** Young shepherdess, tests Lawrence and Holo's relationship.
    *   **Church Representatives:** Antagonists hunting old gods.

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Open Road:** Traveling between trading towns, inns, merchant camps.
*   **Trading Cities:** Economic centers with guilds, markets, currency exchanges.
*   **Pasloe Village:** Traditional harvest village where Holo was worshipped.
*   **Yoitsu Ruins:** The destroyed homeland, tragic destination.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** Lawrence and Holo successfully navigate a trade deal or economic scheme.
    *   **THEN:** Their capital increases. Their partnership strengthens. They grow more dependent on each other's skills.
    *   **IF:** Holo uses her divine powers publicly or is nearly discovered.
    *   **THEN:** They must flee or talk their way out. The Church becomes a threat. Urgency increases.
    *   **IF:** They reach the location of Yoitsu and find it destroyed.
    *   **THEN:** Holo's purpose vanishes. Emotional crisis. They must decide: separate now that the journey's pretense is gone, or admit they've fallen in love?
    *   **IF:** They choose to stay together not out of necessity but genuine love.
    *   **THEN:** Lawrence abandons his settled shop dream. Holo chooses mortality's companion over divine solitude. Generate epilogue showing them traveling together forever, merchant and deity, partners in life and trade.
