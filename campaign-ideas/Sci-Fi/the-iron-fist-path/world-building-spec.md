**The Iron Fist Path**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** A bullied commoner discovers the Kamogawa Boxing Guild and begins training in magical pugilism where fighters channel spirit into devastating techniques. From knowing nothing to developing the legendary Dempsey Roll—a weaving attack generating unstoppable momentum—they climb from street fights to regional to national to world champion. Through brutal training and pushing beyond all limits, they transform from victim to champion while discovering what true strength means.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the history of magical boxing and how fighters channel inner spirit into physical techniques.
    *   Write Coach Kamogawa's history as a boxer who lost his world title chance to WWII.
    *   Describe the legendary Jack Dempsey and his weaving technique that inspired the protagonist.
    *   Explain the ranking system from amateur to professional to regional to national to world level.
    *   Detail famous boxing venues from small halls to championship arenas.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Kamogawa Boxing Gym** (Major): Small, traditional gym producing champions through brutal training and fundamentals.
    *   **Naniwa Boxing Club** (Major): Osaka-based gym known for aggressive, trash-talking fighters.
    *   **Otowa Boxing Gym** (Minor): Rival gym focusing on technical, defensive boxing.
    *   **International Boxing Commission** (Minor): Governing body managing rankings, matches, and titles.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Genji Kamogawa (coach/mentor), Mamoru Takamura (gym ace), Ichirō Miyata (fated rival), Ricardo Martinez (world champion).
    *   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Sparring Partner" (training opponents), "Journeyman Fighter" (professional but not elite), "Rising Contender" (climbers like protagonist), "Retired Legend" (sources of wisdom), "Cut Man" (corner team member).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Kamogawa Boxing Gym:** Two-story building with ground floor gym (ring, bags, equipment, coach's desk) and second floor living quarters for live-in fighters.
*   **Kōrakuen Hall:** Mid-size professional venue with 2,000 seats, where most professional fights occur.
*   **Ryōgoku Kokugikan:** Major arena for championship matches, 11,000 capacity, national stage.
*   **Training Camp:** Remote mountain facility for isolation training—running mountain roads, special conditioning.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Protagonist wins Rookie Tournament → **THEN:** Earns professional license, must choose boxing as career path.
*   **IF:** Dempsey Roll is revealed → **THEN:** Future opponents study footage and train counters specifically.
*   **IF:** Becomes national champion → **THEN:** Pressure increases, media attention, challengers study every fight, weight of expectations.
*   **IF:** Suffers serious injury → **THEN:** Must choose between continuing career with risk of permanent damage or retiring.
*   **IF:** Defeats world champion → **THEN:** Becomes target of international challengers, lonely at the top like mentor Takamura.
