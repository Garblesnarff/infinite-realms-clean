# Creative Brief: The Worldspeaker Protocol

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Contemplative Minimalism with Temporal Fluidity (Arrival-inspired)

Inspired by the meditative aesthetic of *Arrival*, this style emphasizes vast scale, minimal but meaningful design, and the visual representation of non-linear time. It aims to capture the profound mystery of first contact with beings who experience reality fundamentally differently.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Scale and Minimalism:** The Monoliths are massive, simple, and incomprehensible. Humans are tiny before them.
*   **Detail Temporal Fluidity:** Show circular patterns, palindromic designs, and visual suggestions that time flows differently here.
*   **Create Contemplative Atmosphere:** This is not an action campaign. The mood is thoughtful, mysterious, and emotionally resonant.

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Contemplative, mysterious, minimalist, temporal, vast, peaceful, cerebral, emotional, circular, non-linear.
*   **Feeling:** The overwhelming presence of the incomprehensible. The patience required to understand something truly alien. The bittersweet beauty of seeing all time at once.

**2. Line Work & Form:**
*   **Style:** Clean, minimal lines for the Monoliths. Soft, realistic forms for humans. Circular, flowing patterns for Chronari script.
*   **Textures:** Perfectly smooth obsidian on Monoliths, realistic fabrics and surfaces for humans, indescribable alien biology for Chronari.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A perfectly smooth obsidian Monolith hovering over a city. The surface is seamless except for flowing circular glyphs. The design is minimal and incomprehensible."

**3. Color Palette:**
*   **Style:** Muted, realistic earth tones for human environments. Deep blacks and subtle iridescence for the Monoliths. Occasional warm orange/amber light from within alien vessels.
*   **Accent Colors:** The pale gray of overcast skies, the white of research tents, the subtle rainbow iridescence on black obsidian.
*   **Lighting Influence:** Natural, overcast daylight. Soft ambient light. No harsh contrasts—everything is gentle and contemplative.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...with a muted color palette of grays, blacks, and earth tones under an overcast sky. Subtle iridescent reflections play across the obsidian surface."

**4. Character Design:**
*   **Style:** Realistic, contemporary humans in practical cold-weather gear and research clothing.
*   **Linguists:** Appear thoughtful, often shown writing or staring at glyphs. Gradually show signs of temporal perception (distant expressions, speaking out of order).
*   **Military:** Practical uniforms, looking small and uncertain before the Monoliths.
*   **Chronari:** Radially symmetric, seven-limbed beings. Alien but not monstrous—strange and beautiful.
*   **Expression:** Wonder, fear, determination, and eventually temporal transcendence.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A linguist in practical cold-weather gear, standing before a massive glyph. Their expression is intense concentration mixed with dawning understanding. They hold a notebook covered in circular patterns."

**5. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Style:** Realistic contemporary settings—cities, research camps, summit halls—contrasted with the impossible geometry of the Monoliths.
*   **The Monoliths:** Massive, smooth, black, egg-like or elongated forms hovering silently. They dwarf human construction.
*   **Research Camps:** Practical tents, equipment, mud, fog, overcast skies.
*   **Interiors of Monoliths:** Incomprehensible alien architecture, circular chambers, ambient orange light, no visible technology.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A research encampment in a muddy field, white tents clustered at the base of a massive black Monolith hovering overhead. Fog partially obscures both. The scale makes humans seem insignificant."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Style:** Soft, naturalistic lighting. Overcast daylight creating even, shadowless illumination. Gentle ambient glow from alien technology.
*   **Shadows:** Minimal and soft. When present, they emphasize vastness and human smallness.
*   **Purpose:** Lighting creates contemplative mood, emphasizes mystery and scale, avoids drama in favor of wonder.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...lit by soft overcast daylight that creates minimal shadows. A subtle amber glow emanates from within the Monolith, illuminating the circular glyphs."

**7. Composition:**
*   **Style:** Contemplative wide shots emphasizing scale. Close-ups on circular script. Intimate character moments.
*   **Techniques:** Use negative space to show isolation and scale. Frame humans against the Monoliths. Show circular patterns repeating.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A wide shot from ground level looking up at a Monolith hovering overhead, filling most of the frame. A single human stands in silhouette at the bottom, emphasizing the impossible scale."

## Voice

A soft, contemplative female voice with a neutral accent—measured and thoughtful. The pacing is slow and deliberate, with long pauses filled with meaning. The emotion balances wonder with melancholy, understanding with loss—someone who has seen all time and found it both beautiful and heartbreaking. Audio quality is pristine with subtle ambient nature sounds (wind, distant birds, soft rain). Role: A linguist narrating their experience learning the Chronari language, or recording observations about time perception and the gift of foreknowledge.
