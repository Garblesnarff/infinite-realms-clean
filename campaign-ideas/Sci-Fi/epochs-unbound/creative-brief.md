# Epochs Unbound - Creative Brief

## Art Style
Grand scale science fantasy. Each era has distinct visual identity reflecting evolutionary and technological state. Increasingly alien as time progresses.

## Colors
- **Present:** Natural, familiar
- **1,000 Years:** Advanced but recognizable, bright
- **100,000 Years:** Primitive, earthy, desolate
- **1,000,000 Years:** Crystal blues, dying sun orange
- **100,000,000 Years:** Red giant glow, cosmic darkness

## Music
**Main Theme:** Sweeping orchestral suggesting vast time, wonder and melancholy

**Era Themes:**
- **Near Future:** Hopeful, technological
- **Far Future:** Primitive drums and primal sounds
- **Deep Future:** Electronic, alien, crystalline
- **End Times:** Cosmic drone, silence approaching

## Voice Direction
**Professor Antiqua:** Recordings grow more desperate/hopeful across eras

**Future Humans:** Speech evolves, eventually becomes alien

**Crystalline Entity:** Multiple harmonics, barely language

**Last One:** Voice of universe itself, cosmic and final

## Cinematic Moments
- **First Jump:** Watching civilization evolve in fast-forward
- **Meeting Weena:** Innocence in brutal world
- **Underground Discovery:** Horror of Morlocks
- **Dying Sun:** Visual spectacle of stellar death
- **The Last One:** Cosmic encounter at end of everything
- **Return to Present:** Appreciation for "now" after seeing eternity
