# Eternal Sleeves - Creative Brief

## Visual Aesthetic
- Neo-noir meets far-future cyberpunk
- Stark class divide: Opulent Aerium vs. grimy ground level
- Blade Runner meets Altered Carbon (obviously)
- Holographic advertisements, vertical cities, flying vehicles
- Color palette: Warm neons (pink, purple) contrasting cold corporate spaces

## Music Direction
- Atmospheric synthwave with noir jazz elements
- Melancholic themes about loss and time
- Action scenes: Dark electronic beats
- Meth scenes: Classical music (showing disconnect from modern world)
- VR sequences: Surreal soundscapes breaking reality

- **Re-sleeving**: Consciousness downloading into new body, visceral rebirth
- **Stack Extraction**: Spine-removal horror, final death
- **Virtual Torture**: Time dilation effects, centuries in seconds
- **Aerium Arrival**: Ascending to clouds, entering paradise built on suffering
- **Final Choice**: Multiple endings showing consequences decades later
