# World-Building Specification: The Underground Stars

## The Constellation Mind - Complete Nature

### Biology
- Collective of 50 million individual glowworms
- Each glowworm = neuron in distributed brain
- Bioluminescence = synaptic firing made visible
- Consciousness emerges from pattern-making

### Purpose
- Placed here 50,000 years ago by cosmic wayfarers
- Designed as dimensional lighthouse/beacon
- Guides those lost in dimensional void
- Has been calling the refugees for 10,000 years

### Communication
- Creates star maps by arranging light patterns
- Each constellation = navigation instruction
- Can spell words in any language if taught the symbols
- Shows past, present, and future paths simultaneously

## The Refugees - Complete Profile

**Origin**: Dimension 7-Null (designation), collapsing into heat death
**Population**: 10,000 individuals (survivors from billions)
**Journey**: 10,000-year voyage through dimensional void
**Ships**: Living vessels grown from dimensional matter
**Appearance**: Humanoid but ethereal, made of solidified starlight
**Needs**: Physical anchor in stable dimension, unable to survive in void indefinitely
**Culture**: Nomadic, generation ships, collective memory of lost homeworld
**Technology**: Dimensional navigation, reality engineering, photonic matter

## The Pursuer - The Hunter

**Nature**: Dimensional predator, feeds on collapsing realities
**Hunt**: Following refugees, drawn to their dimensional wake
**Appearance**: Absence given form, negative space entity
**Motivation**: Not malicious—it's eating dying dimensions, refugees just in the way
**Power**: Can destabilize dimensions, accelerates collapse
**Threat**: Will damage this dimension trying to consume refugees

## The Lumari People

**Population**: 10,000 in main city, 3,000 in outlying cave settlements
**Culture**: Underground-adapted humans, pale skin, large eyes, bioluminescent symbiosis
**Society**: Peaceful, artistic, use glowworm patterns for art and navigation
**Economy**: Fungal agriculture, limited surface trade
**Government**: Council of Starkeepers who interpret constellations
**Crisis**: Divided on welcoming visitors—hope vs. fear

## The Previous Civilization

**Name**: The Lightwalkers (from glyphs)
**Era**: 5,000 years ago, surface civilization explored deep caves
**Contact**: Encountered previous wave of dimensional refugees
**Response**: Welcomed them, learned from them
**Conflict**: Refugees brought enemy, war in the depths
**Resolution**: Most evacuated with refugees, some stayed, built Lumari culture
**Evidence**: Ruins, glyphs warning of danger, but also showing wonder

## Three Choices - Complete Details

### Choice 1: Welcome the Refugees
- **Method**: Open gateway fully, provide sanctuary
- **Benefits**: Knowledge exchange, new allies, cultural enrichment
- **Costs**: Integration challenges, potential resource strain, Pursuer arrives
- **Long-term**: Successful multidimensional society if managed well

### Choice 2: Defend Together
- **Method**: Accept refugees, prepare for Pursuer
- **Benefits**: United against common threat, epic confrontation
- **Costs**: Battle could damage dimension, casualties expected
- **Long-term**: Victory creates permanent alliance, defeated Pursuer leaves dimension safe

### Choice 3: Guided Exodus
- **Method**: Help refugees find different refuge
- **Benefits**: Pursuer follows them elsewhere, this dimension stays safe
- **Costs**: Refugees must keep fleeing, never find home
- **Long-term**: Moral weight of turning away desperate people

## Magic System - Constellation Magic

**Starlight Path** (1st level): Follow glowworm patterns, cannot get lost in caves
**Beacon Call** (3rd level): Amplify or dampen dimensional beacon temporarily
**Dimensional Sight** (5th level): See into adjacent dimensions briefly
**Gateway** (7th level): Open small dimensional portal for short time
**Cosmic Exodus** (9th level): Transport willing beings to another dimension permanently

## Campaign Outcomes

**The Welcome**: Refugees integrated successfully, cultural renaissance, dimensional knowledge shared, Pursuer defeated through unity.

**The Defense**: Epic battle at threshold, refugees and locals fight together, victory but at cost, permanent alliance forged.

**The Exodus**: Party helps refugees reach better refuge, dimensional navigation knowledge gained, option to join cosmic journey, Constellation Mind continues calling.
