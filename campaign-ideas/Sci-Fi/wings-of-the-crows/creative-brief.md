# Creative Brief: Wings of the Crows

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Dynamic Sports Action with Magical Flight (Haikyuu-inspired)

Inspired by the kinetic energy and emotional intensity of *Haikyuu!!* combined with fantasy elements. This style captures soaring aerial movements, team synchronization, competitive tension, and the underdog spirit rising to meet legendary opponents.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Dynamic Motion:** Aerial trajectories, freeze-frames of perfect attacks, motion lines, dramatic angles from above and below
*   **Team Coordination Visual:** Highlight synchronized movements, magical links between teammates, formation patterns
*   **Competitive Atmosphere:** Crowded arenas, intense expressions, contrasting team colors, scoreboard tension
*   **Magical Sport Elements:** Glowing spheres, enchanted nets, flight magic effects, combination spell visuals

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Soaring, determination, underdog spirit, synchronization, nationals glory, team evolution, flight
*   **Feeling:** The rush of perfect coordination mixed with the weight of fallen reputation. High-speed aerial action contrasted with quiet moments of team bonding. The thrill of competition and proving doubters wrong.

**2. Color Palette:**
*   **Karasuno Colors:** Black and orange (crow motifs), representing the underdog rising
*   **Opponent Teams:** Each guild has distinctive colors (Aoba Johsai's white/teal, Shiratorizawa's purple/white, Nekoma's red/black)
*   **Magical Effects:** Golden light for successful attacks, blue trails for flight paths, impact bursts in team colors
*   **Prompt Example:** "...team uniforms in black with orange accents, crow-wing designs on shoulders. They're suspended mid-air around a glowing golden sphere. Orange magical trails follow their movements. The gymnasium below has polished wood floors reflecting the action. Opponent team in purple and white on the other side of the shimmering net."

*   **Uniforms:** Guild-specific jerseys with numbers, magical enhancement patterns woven in
*   **Expressions:** Intense focus, determined grins, frustrated concentration, triumphant celebration

**4. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Gymnasiums:** Polished wooden floors, high ceilings with mystical nets floating at regulation height, spectator stands
*   **Tournament Venues:** Massive arenas with multiple courts, screens showing matches, crowds of thousands
*   **Training Facilities:** Practice nets, serving machines shooting magical spheres, outdoor mountain courts
*   **Prompt Example:** "A massive national tournament arena with a central court surrounded by rising tiers of spectators. Multiple magical nets glow at different heights. Enormous screens display close-ups of players. Banners from different guilds hang from the ceiling. Magical lighting creates dramatic shadows. The atmosphere crackles with competitive energy."

**5. Aerial Combat & Sport Action:**
*   **Quick Attacks:** Spiker and setter moving in perfect synchronization, magical timing indicators
*   **Blocking:** Multiple players forming aerial walls, defensive magic creating barriers
*   **Receives:** Libero diving through the air to intercept impossibly fast spheres
*   **Serves:** Magical energy building then releasing in devastating projectile attacks
*   **Prompt Example:** "The setter levitates the glowing sphere with one hand, eyes tracking three different spikers. The main spiker approaches at incredible speed, their form blurred. Orange and blue magical energy swirls between them. The opposing blockers rise to meet them, creating a wall of purple energy. The sphere hangs in frozen moment before impact. All players suspended in dramatic aerial poses."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Spotlight Drama:** Court lighting creating strong shadows, players emerging from darkness into light
*   **Magical Glow:** Sphere pulses with energy, successful attacks burst with light
*   **Motion Blur:** Trails showing speed and trajectory, freeze-frame clarity on impact moments
*   **Prompt Example:** "...dramatically lit from above with harsh gymnasium lighting creating strong shadows. The magical sphere glows intensely white-gold at moment of impact. Motion trails in orange show the spiker's approach. The net shimmers with defensive magic. Expressions are lit from below by the sphere's glow, creating intense shadows."

## Voice

A voice full of determination and competitive fire, breathless from exertion but refusing to give up. The pacing accelerates during crucial moments, building to crescendos of team coordination. The tone shifts between rallying cries to teammates, strategic observations, and moments of self-doubt overcome by trust. There's an underlying theme of lifting each other up and proving the fallen can fly again. Includes signature phrases about reaching higher and not giving up. Role: The voice of unwavering team spirit and the will to soar.
