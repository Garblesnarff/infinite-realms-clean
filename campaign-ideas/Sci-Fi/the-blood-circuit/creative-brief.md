# The Blood Circuit - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Cyberpunk Sports Arena

**Visual Identity:** Sleek futuristic designs, neon lighting, dynamic action, corporate branding everywhere. Mix of high-tech and magical elements.

**Color Palette:**
- Neon accents: Electric blue (#00F0FF), hot pink (#FF006E), acid green (#39FF14)
- Corporate: Chrome silver (#C0C0C0), deep black (#000000), gold sponsors (#FFD700)
- Arena-specific: Each location has signature color theme
- Ability effects: Vibrant, immediately recognizable

**Character Design:**
- Diverse Legends from all cultures/species
- Unique silhouettes for instant recognition
- Tech-augmented bodies, cybernetic enhancements
- Personality through costume design
- Corporate logos on gear

## Music and Sound Design

**Lobby/Pre-Match:** Electronic pump-up music, hype commentary
**Drop Sequence:** Intense orchestral + electronic build
**Combat:** Fast-paced action themes, ability sound cues
**Victory:** Triumphant fanfare, crowd cheering
**Elimination:** Tactical HUD sounds, respawn beacon urgency
**Interviews:** Professional broadcast themes

**Voice Direction:**
- Legends: Confident, quippy, catchphrases
- Commentators: Sports caster energy, hype
- Corporations: Professional menace
- Fans: Adoring or hateful

## Cinematic Moments

- Squad drop deployment from dropship
- First team wipe, elimination quips
- Clutch respawn rescues
- Ultimate ability combo devastation
- Final ring showdown
- Championship victory or bitter defeat
- Corporate betrayal reveal
- Legend rebellion uprising
