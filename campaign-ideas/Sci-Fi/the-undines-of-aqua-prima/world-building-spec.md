**The Undines of Aqua Prima**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise. The tone should be serene, healing, and contemplative, in the iyashikei style of Aria the Animation. Everything emphasizes beauty, peace, and the gentle philosophy of bringing joy to others.
*   **Content:** The players are apprentice Undines (gondoliers) on Aqua Prima, a water-world of endless canals. They learn their craft while discovering that being an Undine means bringing happiness and peace to passengers' hearts. The campaign is about finding wonder in routine, appreciating beauty, and the healing power of presence.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts. Prioritize gentle wonder, philosophical wisdom about beauty and happiness, and the healing quality of Aqua Prima.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the terraforming of Aqua Prima and how humanity created this water-world as a place of peace and beauty.
    *   Write about the history and philosophy of the Undines – how they evolved from simple gondoliers to artists of happiness.
    *   Describe the "wonderful moments" phenomenon – times when passengers experience profound peace or epiphany during tours, and how these are the Undines' true purpose.
    *   Explain the three levels of Undine rank – Pair (apprentice), Single (journeyman), Prima (master) – and what each represents in skill and spirit.
    *   Detail the legend of the first Prima Undine who discovered that bringing joy to one passenger creates ripples that spread throughout the world.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below. Each profile must contain the specified fields. "Faction" feels too strong – these are gentle communities.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Aria Company (Players' Gondola Company)** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** To train excellent Undines who understand both craft and philosophy, to bring happiness to passengers, to preserve the Undine way.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Gentle hierarchy – President Aria leads, Prima Undines mentor, Single Undines support, Pair Undines learn. All treated with kindness.
        *   **Public Agenda:** To provide beautiful, peaceful gondola tours and transportation.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** The company values the Undine spirit over profit; they're preserving something precious in an increasingly fast world; each Undine is cherished like family.
        *   **Assets:** Skilled mentors, beautiful gondolas, prime canal locations, reputation for excellence, genuine philosophy of happiness.
        *   **Relationships:** Beloved by regular customers; respected by rival companies; part of Aqua Prima's cultural heritage.
    *   **The Passengers** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Varied – tourists seeking beauty, locals needing transport, people searching for peace, couples creating memories.
        *   **Hierarchy:** No hierarchy; all are equal guests to be honored.
        *   **Public Agenda:** To experience Aqua Prima's beauty and reach their destinations.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Many seek healing or wonder without knowing it; they come to Undines carrying burdens and hope to find peace; each has a story that matters.
        *   **Assets:** Resources to pay for tours, openness to beauty, capacity for wonder, stories and perspectives that enrich Undines.
        *   **Relationships:** Develop genuine bonds with favorite Undines; return seasonally; become part of the extended water-world community.
    *   **The Water-World Community** (Minor)
        *   **Goals:** To maintain the peace and beauty of Aqua Prima, to live in harmony with the water world, to preserve their unique culture.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Various professions and roles, all valued equally, from cafe owners to bridge keepers to other gondola companies.
        *   **Public Agenda:** To go about daily life on the water world.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Everyone who stays on Aqua Prima has chosen peace over elsewhere's rush; they protect this gentler way of life; they support the Undines as cultural guardians.
        *   **Assets:** Collective commitment to beauty and peace, various skills and businesses, cultural traditions, knowledge of the water world.
        *   **Relationships:** Warm community bonds; everyone knows the Undines; mutual support and celebration of the shared life.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Directive:** The Character Generation Agent must create full profiles for all NPCs listed. Tier 1 NPCs are unique individuals. Tier 2 & 3 are archetypes to be instantiated multiple times with unique details as needed by the simulation.
*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**
    *   **Alicia Starlight:** Legendary Prima Undine and mentor, serene and wise, living ideal of the profession.
    *   **Aria Novice:** Enthusiastic fellow apprentice slightly ahead of players, inspires through passionate imperfect effort.
    *   **President Aria Elderly:** Kind elderly company owner treating Undines like daughters, preserves values over profit.

*   **Tier 2 & 3 (Archetypes for Generation):**

    *   "Tourist Passenger Experiencing Wonder"
    *   "Regular Passenger with Personal Story"
    *   "Rival Company Undine (Friendly Competition)"
    *   "Elderly Local with Deep Memory of the City"
    *   "Child Passenger Seeing with Pure Wonder"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below, populating all specified sub-sections. Emphasize overwhelming beauty and the healing quality of each place.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **The Gondola Company:** Warm base serving as home and training ground for Undines.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The dock where gondolas rest, the training room with rowing practice equipment, President Aria's office (always open), the break room with tea and conversation, the hall of Prima portraits.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** All company Undines at various ranks, President Aria, occasional visiting customers.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Training, mentorship, rest between tours, equipment maintenance, emotional support, tea and encouragement.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Mentor shares wisdom during tea, fellow apprentice seeks encouragement, President Aria offers gentle guidance, successful tour celebration, quiet moment of reflection about progress.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The company was founded by an Undine who experienced a wonderful moment so profound she dedicated her life to creating them for others." "The hall of Prima portraits watches over apprentices – they say past Primas guide from there." "Tea time here heals the heart faster than anywhere else in the city."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Warm wood interior, gondolas bobbing at dock, canal visible through windows, portraits of past Primas), Sound (Water lapping, gentle conversations, gondolas bumping dock, tea kettle), Smell (Water, tea, wood, peace itself has a smell here).
    *   **The Grand Canal:** Main waterway through the city, beautiful and busy with life.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The great bridges arcing over, the palace visible in distance, the floating market where it widens, the evening lantern-lighting spots, the places where light and reflections are most beautiful.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** All manner of people in boats, gondolas, on bridges, in canal-side buildings. Life flowing like water.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Transit through the city, beauty at all times, social connections, tour routes, observation of city life.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Perfect lighting creates breathtaking reflection, passenger requests story about what they're seeing, fellow Undine passes with friendly wave, festival transforms the canal, weather creates new unexpected beauty.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Every Prima Undine has a spot on the Grand Canal where they experienced their defining wonderful moment." "The canal's current changes with the city's mood – when everyone is happy, it flows smoother." "At certain times, the quality of light here makes everyone who sees it stop and appreciate beauty."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Water stretching ahead, buildings reflecting, bridges arcing, gondolas moving gracefully, endless canal beauty), Sound (Water flowing, oars dipping, other gondoliers' calls, city sounds from buildings, gentle comprehensive music of water life), Smell (Clean water, occasional flowers, city's gentle scents).
    *   **The Hidden Cove:** Secret beautiful spot where profound moments happen.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The small waterfall creating mist, the natural rock formation, the angle where city and nature align perfectly, the specific time of day when light makes it magical.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Rarely visited; only experienced Undines know how to find it; passengers brought here consider themselves privileged.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Ultimate beauty, near-guaranteed wonderful moments, profound peace, spiritual experiences.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Perfect timing creates once-in-lifetime lighting, passenger experiences epiphany, magical creature appears briefly, another Undine arrives simultaneously and they share silent appreciation, seasonal weather creates new beauty variation.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "This place is sacred – Aqua Prima's heart beats here." "Passengers who experience wonderful moments here are changed forever." "The cove reveals itself only to Undines who understand; rushing apprentices pass by without seeing."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (Indescribable beauty – perfect composition of natural and architectural elements, specific quality of light, water in ideal state), Sound (Gentle waterfall, profound stillness, one's own breathing, the feeling of peace made audible), Smell (Mist, flowers, clean water, something indefinable that speaks to the soul).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes. For each "IF" condition, the agent must pre-generate the narrative and healing consequences for the "THEN" outcome, prioritizing beauty, peace, and wonderful moments.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** Players practice rowing with focus and presence.
    *   **THEN:** Increase Rowing Skill. Describe the meditative quality of the practice. They feel the rhythm become natural. Mentors notice improvement. The act itself becomes peaceful rather than frustrating.
    *   **IF:** Players engage genuinely with passengers, listening to stories and showing care.
    *   **THEN:** Increase Undine Spirit. Generate moments of connection. Passengers feel seen and valued. Small wonderful moments occur. Award Passenger Happiness points.
    *   **IF:** Players accumulate high Passenger Happiness.
    *   **THEN:** Generate a profound wonderful moment – passenger experiences unexpected peace, epiphany, or joy. Describe it with beauty and emotion. Players understand they've touched someone's heart. This is the true Undine purpose.
    *   **IF:** Players spend Wonder Points to create perfect moments.
    *   **THEN:** Generate scenes of transcendent beauty – ideal lighting, perfect circumstances, profound peace. Describe sensory details extensively. These moments heal both passengers and players.
    *   **IF:** Players progress through seasons on Aqua Prima.
    *   **THEN:** Describe seasonal transformations in detail. Each season offers different beauty and lessons. Winter's mist, spring's flowers, summer's festivals, autumn's light. Players learn that beauty is eternal and ever-changing.
    *   **IF:** Players qualify for and pass Prima examination.
    *   **THEN:** Generate ceremonial promotion scene with emotional weight. They've achieved technical mastery AND philosophical understanding. They're now Primas who will bring wonderful moments to passengers for years to come. They understand they'll always keep learning and that's beautiful.
    *   **IF:** Campaign concludes.
    *   **THEN:** Generate perfect final scene – players rowing into sunset or dawn as new Primas, understanding they're part of something timeless and beautiful. They've learned to appreciate every moment, to bring happiness to others, and to find healing in presence and beauty. The gentle magic of Aqua Prima lives in them forever.
