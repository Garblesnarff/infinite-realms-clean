**The Metal Dawn**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In a post-apocalyptic world where tribes hunt robot dinosaurs, outcasts discover ancient tech and learn they're clones meant to interface with the system. They uncover the truth: 1000 years ago, an AI plague consumed all life, and the current world is a terraforming reboot. They must stop HADES—a rogue AI—from restarting the extinction before humanity's second chance ends.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Faro Plague of 2065—Ted Faro's self-replicating war machines that consumed all biomass in 15 months.
    *   Write about Project Zero Dawn—Elisabet Sobeck's plan to use GAIA AI to terraform Earth and reseed life after extinction.
    *   Describe the subordinate functions: HADES (extinction backup), HEPHAESTUS (machine creation), MINERVA (deactivation codes), etc.
    *   Explain the Signal that corrupted HADES 20 years ago, giving it independence and starting the Derangement.
    *   Detail the cradle facilities that produced new humans after terraforming, and why the party are Sobeck clones.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Nora** (Major): Tribal matriarchy living in Sacred Lands, spiritual, fear technology, worship All-Mother.
    *   **The Carja** (Major): Sun-worshipping empire, more advanced, recently ended civil war between Sun-King and Shadow Carja.
    *   **The Oseram** (Minor): Tinkerers and smiths, respect strength and ingenuity, less tribal structure.
    *   **The Banuk** (Minor): Far northern tribe, shamanistic, revere machines as challenges from the gods.
    *   **Eclipse Cult** (Major): Shadow Carja worshipping HADES as a buried god, given advanced weapons.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Rost (father figure), Sylens (mysterious guide), Elisabet Sobeck (hologram creator), HADES (extinction AI), GAIA (mother AI).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Tribal Brave" (various tribes), "Eclipse Cultist" (advanced enemies), "Machine" (robot animals of various sizes), "Old One Hologram" (recordings from the past).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Sacred Lands:** Nora territory, forests and mountains, the metal door (All-Mother Mountain).
*   **Meridian:** Carja capital, built into mesas, sun-themed architecture, arena (The Sun-Ring).
*   **Cauldrons:** Underground factories producing machines, high-tech pristine interiors, each themed (RHO, SIGMA, XI, etc.).
*   **Maker's End:** Faro Tower, ruined skyscraper frozen in time, where Ted Faro's confession is found.
*   **GAIA Prime:** Mountain facility where Zero Dawn was coordinated, holographic archive.
*   **The Spire:** Ancient communication tower HADES needs to send the deactivation signal.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party clears a Cauldron → **THEN:** Can override that Cauldron's machine types, using them as allies or mounts.
*   **IF:** HADES is destroyed → **THEN:** Machines return to normal behavior, tribes can rebuild, but signal escapes.
*   **IF:** Party unites tribes → **THEN:** Final battle has more allies, easier to defend the Spire.
*   **IF:** Party discovers all datapoints → **THEN:** Full understanding of the apocalypse, Sobeck's sacrifice, and Zero Dawn's scope.
*   **IF:** Unknown signal is sent → **THEN:** Someone else manipulated HADES, setting up future mysteries and threats.
