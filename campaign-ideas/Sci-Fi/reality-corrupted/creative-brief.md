# Reality Corrupted: Creative Brief

## Art Style: Glitch Aesthetics
Digital corruption made physical, cyberpunk meets broken reality

**Color Palette:**
- **Glitch Pink/Cyan** (#FF00FF / #00FFFF): Chromatic aberration, corruption
- **Matrix Green** (#00FF00): Code visibility, hacking
- **Error Red** (#FF0000): System failures, danger
- **Void Purple** (#800080): Deep corruption, admin space
- **Static Gray** (#808080): Neutral corruption

## Music and Sound
- **Main Theme**: Electronic glitches building to orchestral
- **Glitch Sounds**: Digital corruption, reality breaking
- **Console Theme**: Deep electronic, god-like presence
- **Crash Theme**: Everything degrades to noise

**Voice Direction:**
- **Neo**: Mysteriously shifting, multiple voice actors
- **The Debugger**: Robotic, antivirus warnings
- **Chaos**: Corrupted audio, beautiful degradation

## Cinematic Moments
- **First Noclip**: Character phases through wall, sees code
- **Reality Cascade**: City block glitching like corrupted video
- **Reaching Console**: Impossible architecture, reality as data
- **The Crash/Fix**: Depending on ending, visual spectacle

**Tagline**: "Error 404: Consensus Reality Not Found"
