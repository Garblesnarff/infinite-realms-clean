**Commanders War**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party are Commanding Officers in a world of turn-based tactical warfare between nations. Each CO has unique personality and CO Power (special ability changing battles). They capture bases, manage resources, deploy diverse units, and use CO Powers strategically. When mysterious Black Hole faction emerges with superweapons threatening all nations, former enemies must cooperate or face mutual annihilation.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the founding of the four nations and their cultural identities.
    *   Write the history of previous Great War that established current borders.
    *   Describe the CO system—how commanders gain special powers through tactical mastery.
    *   Explain the emergence of Black Hole and their mysterious leader Sturm.
    *   Detail the superweapons: laser satellites, tsunami generators, volcanic activators, meteor silos.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Orange Star** (Major): Balanced nation, heroic, values fairness, versatile forces.
    *   **Blue Moon** (Major): Cold northern nation, strong defense, weather control specialists.
    *   **Green Earth** (Major): Professional military, balanced economy, combined arms doctrine.
    *   **Yellow Comet** (Major): Traditional eastern nation, powerful but expensive units, honor-focused.
    *   **Black Hole** (Major Enemy): Mysterious villain faction with advanced technology and superweapons.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Commander Nell (Orange Star balanced), Commander Olaf (Blue Moon winter), Commander Eagle (Green Earth air), Commander Kanbei (Yellow Comet power), Commander Sturm (Black Hole villain).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Infantry" (capture/scout), "Tank" (versatile combat), "Artillery" (long range), "Fighter" (air superiority), "Battleship" (bombardment), "Recon" (fog of war vision).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Battle Maps:** Grid-based tactical battlefields with diverse terrain: plains (open), forests (cover), mountains (high ground/obstacles), roads (fast movement), cities (income/capture), seas (naval combat).
*   **Nation HQs:** Each nation's command center where campaigns are planned and COs coordinate.
*   **Black Hole Facilities:** Enemy bases with superweapon controls: satellite uplinks, tsunami generators, volcano activators.
*   **The Final Fortress:** Black Hole's command center, massive fortified base requiring multi-front assault.
*   **Missile Silos:** Capturable locations on maps that grant one-time devastating attacks.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Capture enemy cities → **THEN:** Gain income each turn, can produce more units, economic advantage.
*   **IF:** CO Power meter full → **THEN:** Can activate for game-changing effect: damage boost, healing, weather control, etc.
*   **IF:** Lose all units or HQ captured → **THEN:** Mission defeat, must retry with different strategy.
*   **IF:** Enemy factory destroyed → **THEN:** Cannot produce units there, reduces enemy reinforcements.
*   **IF:** Fog of war active → **THEN:** Cannot see enemy units outside vision range, ambushes possible.
*   **IF:** Four nations ally → **THEN:** Can command combined armies, coordinate against Black Hole.
*   **IF:** Superweapon activated → **THEN:** Devastating attack hits region, must disable before activation.
*   **IF:** Defeat Commander Sturm → **THEN:** Black Hole threat ended, nations can establish lasting peace.
