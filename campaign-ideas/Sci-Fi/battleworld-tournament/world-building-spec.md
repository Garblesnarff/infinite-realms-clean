**Battleworld Tournament**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Champions and villains from across the multiverse wake on Battleworld, assembled by the Beyonder for cosmic entertainment. The players must navigate faction warfare, form unlikely alliances, and challenge the god-like being for freedom.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   Detail the Beyonder: its nature, power, and why it creates Battleworlds.
*   Write how Battleworld was assembled from reality fragments.
*   Describe previous games and their outcomes.
*   Explain the rules the Beyonder enforces.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **The Hero Coalition:** United champions who refuse to compromise with evil.
*   **The Villain Syndicate:** Pragmatic villains seeking power or escape.
*   **Doom's Alliance:** Mixed faction believing cooperation is the only path to victory.
*   **The Beyonder's Favored:** Those who entertain the cosmic being and receive rewards.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** The Beyonder, Lord Doom, Champion Valor, Scattered Soul Survivors.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Hero Champion", "Pragmatic Villain", "Battleworld Native", "Previous Game Survivor".

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Patchwork Territories:** Diverse landscape fragments with resources.
*   **Neutral Zone:** Combat-free negotiation area.
*   **Doom's Fortress:** Strategic command center.
*   **Beyonder's Keep:** Reality-warping cosmic fortress.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Players ally with Doom → Unlock conspiracy against the Beyonder.
*   **IF:** Hero Coalition refuses all villain alliances → They attack the party as "compromised."
*   **IF:** Players entertain the Beyonder → Gain reality-warping boons but become faction targets.
*   **IF:** Final challenge succeeds → Generate outcome based on approach (combat, negotiation, or theft).
