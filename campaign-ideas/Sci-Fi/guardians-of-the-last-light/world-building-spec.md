**Guardians of the Last Light**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** Humanity's Golden Age ended with the Collapse. The players are Guardians—undead warriors resurrected by Ghosts using the Traveler's Light. They wield paracausal powers and must defend the Last City while uncovering the truth about the cosmic war between Light and Darkness.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Golden Age when the Traveler uplifted humanity across the solar system.
    *   Write the history of the Collapse when the Darkness arrived and the Traveler fell silent.
    *   Describe the creation of Ghosts and the first Guardians from the dead.
    *   Explain the philosophy of the Light (complexity, growth, freedom) versus the Darkness (simplicity, strength, finality).
    *   Detail each alien faction: Fallen (scavengers), Cabal (empire), Vex (time-machines), Hive (death-worshippers), Taken (corrupted slaves).
    *   Write about the Sword Logic—the Hive's philosophy that only the strong deserve existence.
    *   Describe the Witness and its goal to achieve the Final Shape.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Vanguard (Guardian Command)** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Protect the Last City, push back alien threats, understand the Traveler.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by the Vanguard—Zavala (Titan), Ikora (Warlock), Cayde-6 (Hunter, deceased).
        *   **Public Agenda:** Defend humanity's last city and reclaim lost worlds.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Some question whether the Traveler is benevolent or merely using humanity.
        *   **Assets:** Immortal Guardian army, Golden Age technology, the Traveler's Light.
        *   **Relationships:** Allied with certain Fallen houses and Cabal defectors; at war with Hive and Vex.
    *   **The Hive** (Major Enemy)
        *   **Goals:** Feed their worm gods through killing, spread the Darkness, prove Sword Logic.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by Hive gods (Oryx-deceased, Savathûn, Xivu Arath), with wizards and knights below.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Existence through conquest—kill to survive.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Some Hive question the worm pact that enslaves them to endless killing.
        *   **Assets:** Necromancy, throne worlds (pocket dimensions), Sword Logic power growth, Taken slaves.
        *   **Relationships:** Servants of Darkness; enemies of all Light; manipulated by the Witness.
    *   **The Fallen (House of Light)** (Minor Ally)
        *   **Goals:** Survive, reclaim lost glory, some seek peaceful coexistence.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by Kells (kings), House of Light led by Mithrax the Forsaken.
        *   **Public Agenda:** We were once blessed by the Traveler and seek redemption.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Internal conflict between peaceful houses and pirate houses.
        *   **Assets:** Advanced technology, stealth capabilities, Servitor support drones.
        *   **Relationships:** Some allied with Guardians; most remain hostile scavengers.
    *   **The Witness's Fleet** (Major Enemy)
        *   **Goals:** Achieve the Final Shape—a universe of perfect, unchanging order.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Led by the Witness, with Disciples and the Black Fleet.
        *   **Public Agenda:** End the chaos and suffering of existence through finality.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** The Witness believes it's merciful, not evil, ending all pain through stasis.
        *   **Assets:** Pyramid ships, Darkness powers, corruption abilities, paracausal weapons.
        *   **Relationships:** Commands the Hive; tempts Guardians; opposed to the Traveler.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Directive:** The Character Generation Agent must create full profiles for all NPCs listed.
*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**
    *   **The Ghost:** AI companion made of Light, loyal and curious, resurrects Guardian.
    *   **The Witness:** Ancient being leading Darkness, calm and philosophical, seeks Final Shape.
    *   **Zavala:** Titan Vanguard Commander, stoic and duty-bound, questions Traveler.
    *   **Eris Morn:** Former Guardian, haunted Hive expert, walks line between Light and Dark.
    *   **Ikora Rey:** Warlock Vanguard, brilliant strategist, studies Darkness academically.
    *   **The Exo Stranger:** Time-traveling mysterious figure, warns of dark timelines.
*   **Tier 2 & 3 (Archetypes for Generation):**
    *   "Guardian Fireteam Member (Titan/Hunter/Warlock)"
    *   "Fallen Scavenger"
    *   "Hive Wizard"
    *   "Cabal Legionary"
    *   "Vex Mind-Unit"
    *   "Last City Civilian"
    *   "Corrupted Taken"
    *   "Darkness-Tempted Guardian"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **The Last City (The Tower):** Humanity's final stronghold beneath the Traveler, center of Guardian operations.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Tower (Vanguard HQ), Bazaar, Hangar, Courtyard, the Wall.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Humans, Exos (robot people), Awoken (space elves), Guardians.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Weapon smiths, armor vendors, ship mechanics, Vanguard bounties.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Guardian training, civilian celebration, alien attack alarm, exotic merchant, Vanguard briefing.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The Traveler is beginning to wake." "Darkness pyramids are approaching the system." "A Guardian went rogue using Darkness powers."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (white and gold architecture, massive Traveler above, Guardian patrols), Sound (ship engines, chatter, announcements), Smell (ozone from transmat, food vendors, clean air).
    *   **The Hellmouth (Moon):** A massive Hive fortress carved into the Moon, descending miles deep.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Gatehouse, Circle of Bones, World's Grave, Crota's Throne.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Hive thralls, knights, wizards, Crota's soul.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** None—pure combat zone.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Thrall swarm, Wizard ambush, Crota's sword-bearers, cursed treasure, Hive ritual.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Crota can kill Guardians permanently." "The Sword Logic makes Hive stronger through killing." "Eris Morn survived here for years without a Ghost."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (green Hive magic, bone architecture, darkness), Sound (chittering thralls, wizard screams, silence), Smell (decay, sulfur, death).
    *   **The Dreadnaught (Saturn):** Oryx's throne world, a massive ship carved from a dead worm god.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Founts, Court of Oryx, Altar of Oryx, Touch of Malice shrine.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Taken (corrupted slaves), Hive, Oryx the Taken King.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Ancient Hive knowledge, powerful weapons, throne world secrets.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Taken corruption wave, dimensional rift, Hive tribute ritual, echo of Oryx, geometry shifts.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Oryx's throne world has impossible physics." "Killing him with his own Sword Logic empowers the killer." "His daughters continue his work."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (chitin walls, Taken portals, impossibleangles), Sound (Taken whispers, reality bending, distant roars), Smell (ozone, void, wrongness).
    *   **Inside the Traveler (Final Battle):** A realm of pure paracausal Light beyond normal space.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Pale Heart, Light's garden, Reality's Edge, the Witness's gate.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Light constructs, memories made manifest, the Witness.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Final confrontation space, ultimate choice.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Memory of fallen Guardian, Light manifestation, Darkness temptation, reality reconstruction, final trial.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "This is the Traveler's true form." "The Witness seeks to enter and end all existence." "Both Light and Dark are tools, neither good nor evil."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (white Light, geometric patterns, cosmic beauty), Sound (harmonious tones, whispered truths, silence), Smell (nothing—beyond physical sensation).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** The players kill Crota.
    *   **THEN:** Oryx arrives seeking revenge. The Taken begin appearing. Generate escalation missions.
    *   **IF:** The players accept Darkness powers.
    *   **THEN:** They gain Stasis/Strand abilities but accumulate Corruption. Zavala questions their loyalty.
    *   **IF:** A player's Ghost is destroyed.
    *   **THEN:** That Guardian can die permanently. Generate desperate mission to find replacement Ghost or accept mortality.
    *   **IF:** The players unite Fallen, Cabal, and Human forces.
    *   **THEN:** The final assault on the Witness gains massive army support bonuses.
    *   **IF:** The players defeat the Witness by accepting the Final Shape.
    *   **THEN:** Dark ending—all conflict ends, but so does growth and change. Generate stasis epilogue.
    *   **IF:** The players defeat the Witness by rejecting both Light and Darkness.
    *   **THEN:** They forge a new path. Generate epilogue where Guardians become truly mortal but free.
