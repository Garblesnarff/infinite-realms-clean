# The Wyld Timeways
**Tagline:** *"Be excellent to each other. And party on through time, dudes!"*

## Campaign Overview

The party discovers a magical time-traveling phone booth (or mystical portal shaped suspiciously like one) that allows them to journey to any point in historyâ€”but only for fun, educational, and excellent adventures. Unlike dark time travel campaigns focused on preventing apocalypses, this lighthearted romp emphasizes comedy, historical tourism, meeting legendary figures, and forming a rock band that transcends time itself.

When the party accidentally disrupts history during a joyride through time, they must fix their mistakes by recruiting historical heroes, forming the most excellent adventuring party ever assembled, and saving reality through the power of friendship, music, and radical time travel shenanigans. The campaign celebrates the joy of adventure, learning from history, and the totally excellent power of being kind to one another across all of time and space.

Inspired by Bill & Ted's optimistic time travel comedy, this campaign is perfect for groups wanting lighter tone while still engaging with time travel mechanics and historical settings.

## Core Themes

- **The Power of Friendship**: Bonds transcending time
- **Learning is Excellent**: History as adventure playground
- **Music Unites**: Universal language across eras
- **Kindness Matters**: Being excellent to everyone
- **Adventure and Fun**: Joy of exploration
- **Found Family**: Building team across time periods

- Loves modern world

**Beethoven** (Neutral Good, Male Human Bard)

**Future Council** (Various Alignments)

## Unique Mechanics

**Historical Disruption Tracker**
- Measure how much timeline changed
- Humorous consequences
- Napoleon now sells pastries
- Lincoln invented telephone
- Party must fix funniest ones

**The Excellent Speech**
- Charisma checks to befriend anyone
- Being genuinely nice works on everyone
- Historical figures love sincerity
- Music and kindness universal
- Combat often avoided through friendship

**Time Booth Rules**
- Arrives exactly when plot needs
- Can travel anywhere/when
- Multiple booths can exist (time duplicates)
- Breaking it is temporary setback
- "Excellent!" is command word

**Band Performance**
- Music has magical properties
- Good performance affects reality
- Historical jam sessions
- Each era has musical challenges
- Final concert saves everything

**Death's Games**
- Challenge Death to contests
- Best two out of three
- Any game works
- Losing is temporary
- Death is actually cool

## Victory Conditions

**Optimal Victory: Most Triumphant!**
- Save timeline through excellent concert
- All historical friends remain in right eras
- Future utopia ensured
- Band goes platinum across all time
- Everyone learns to be excellent

**Standard Victory: Bodacious!**
- Fix major timeline problems
- Keep some historical friends
- Future mostly positive
- Band is local hit
- Good times had by all

**Pyrrhic Victory: Non-Heinous But...**
- Timeline saved but messy
- Lost some friends to proper eras
- Future okay but not utopian
- Band breaks up (reunion tour later)
- Bittersweet but still fun

## Defeat Conditions

**Bogus Timeline**
- Evil traveler wins
- Joyless future created
- Music banned
- Everyone is sad
- Most heinous outcome

**Trapped in Time**
- Booth destroyed
- Party stuck in wrong era
- Must live out lives there
- Could be fun depending on when
- Sequel potential

**Paradox Party Wipe**
- Too many timeline changes
- Reality can't handle it
- Everything resets
- Party forgets adventure
- Back to failing history

## Special Features

**Historical Cameos:** Real historical figures with D&D stats and personalities

**Musical Challenges:** Actual songs or descriptions, player creativity encouraged

**Moral Simplicity:** Good guys are good, bad guys redeemable, kindness wins

**Humor Mandatory:** Campaign should be fun, mistakes are opportunities for comedy

**Learning Through Play:** Actually teach history (sort of) while adventuring

**No Real Stakes:** Even death is temporary and fun; enjoy the ride

**Power of Friendship:** Mechanically effective; being nice works

**Time Travel Light:** Don't worry about paradoxes too much; rule of cool applies
