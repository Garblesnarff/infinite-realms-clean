# Creative Brief: The Rainbow Veil

## Visual Aesthetics
- **Color Palette**: Full spectrum (#FF0000 Red, #FF7F00 Orange, #FFFF00 Yellow, #00FF00 Green, #0000FF Blue, #4B0082 Indigo, #9400D3 Violet)
- **Visual Style**: Waterfall grandeur meets prismatic light refraction, reality shimmer between solid and spirit
- **Key Imagery**: Massive rainbow arcing over thundering falls, spirit creatures made of colored light, phasing village flickering between worlds

## Music and Sound
- **Main Theme**: Seven-part harmony, each Keeper has instrument (Red: drums, Orange: flute, Yellow: bells, Green: strings, Blue: cello, Indigo: theremin, Violet: choir)
- **Soundscape**: Constant waterfall roar, mist hiss, rainbow chimes (light as sound), spirit whispers
- **Key Scenes**: Each trial has color-specific music, Unity Ritual combines all seven themes

## Voice Acting
- **Red Keeper**: Fierce, passionate, like crackling fire
- **Orange Keeper**: Playful, laughing, musical lilt
- **Yellow Keeper**: Calm, wise, warm as sunlight
- **Green Keeper**: Balanced, diplomatic, gentle
- **Blue Keeper**: Deep, sad, oceanic resonance
- **Indigo Keeper**: Mysterious, whispered, echoing
- **Violet Keeper**: Ethereal, multiple voices, transcendent

## References
- Visual: Victoria Falls footage, rainbow light refraction, Studio Ghibli spirit realms
- Sound: Waterfall recordings, Enya (ethereal vocals), prism/crystal sounds
