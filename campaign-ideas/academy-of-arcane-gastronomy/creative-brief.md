# Academy of Arcane Gastronomy - Creative Brief

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Harry Potter meets culinary school, magical academia with food focus
- **Art Style**: Gothic architecture with warm kitchen lighting, contrast between ancient and modern
- **Tone**: Wonder of learning mixed with darker conspiracy undertones

## Color Palettes
- **House Flambe**: Reds, oranges, golds - fire and passion
- **House Glac√©**: Blues, silvers, whites - precision and cold
- **House Verde**: Greens, browns, earth tones - nature and growth
- **House Umami**: Purples, deep reds, balanced tones - harmony
- **Corruption**: Sickly greens, wrong purples, diseased yellows
- **Campus**: Warm stone, aged wood, magical glow

## Music Themes
- Main theme: Academic grandeur with culinary warmth
- House themes: Each distinct musical identity
- Study music: Quiet, focused, building tension
- Cooking duels: Intense, competitive, magical
- Corruption theme: Discordant, unsettling
- Victory: Triumphant graduation march

## Voice Direction
- **Headmaster Goldwhisk**: Kindly grandfather hiding desperation, Ian McKellen warmth becoming sinister
- **Professor Umeboshi**: Passionate teacher, encouraging, Michelle Pfeiffer energy
- **Dean Sourwood**: Strict but fair, softening over time, Patrick Stewart authority
- **Victoria Silverplate**: Aristocratic mean girl to genuine ally, Emma Watson arc
- **Remi Saltshaker**: Best friend energy, comic relief with heart, Neville Longbottom vibes

## Cinematic Moments
- Arrival at Academy: Sweeping shots revealing magical campus
- First successful flavor-binding: Magical food coming to life
- Cooking duels: Over-the-top Food Wars reaction shots
- Discovery of underground prison: Horror movie atmosphere
- Founding Feast ritual: Dark ceremony interrupted by heroes
- Final graduation: Bittersweet triumph, looking to future
