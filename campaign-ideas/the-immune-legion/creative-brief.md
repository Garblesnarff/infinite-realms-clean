# Creative Brief: The Immune Legion

## Visual Art Direction
- **Color Palette:** Blood reds, immune cell whites, pathogen sickly greens/purples, organ-specific colors
- **Osmosis Jones Inspiration:** Anthropomorphized cells with personality, city-like body interior
- **Immune Cells:** White blood cells as armored warriors/police, T-cells as elite assassins, B-cells as scholars
- **Pathogens:** Bacteria as enemy soldiers, viruses as infiltrators, magical construct as leader
- **Organs as Kingdoms:** Each organ has distinct architectural style and culture

## Music and Sound Design
- **Main Theme:** Heroic but intimate, fighting war inside fragile ecosystem
- **Immune Legion March:** Military precision, coordinated cellular response
- **Pathogen Theme:** Infection spreading, adaptive mutation sounds
- **Organ Themes:** Each location has unique musical identity (lungs=breathing rhythm, heart=percussion-heavy)
- **Battle Music:** Fast-paced cellular combat with biological weapon sounds
- **Heartbeat:** Constant rhythmic presence throughout campaign

## Voice Acting
- **General Phagocyte:** Gruff military commander, authoritative male voice
- **Captain Neutros:** Brave soldier, ready to sacrifice, young male voice
- **Assassin T:** Precise, controlled, deadly calm female voice
- **Intelligence B:** Scholarly, analytical, measured tone
- **Pathogen Collective:** Multiple overlapping voices, insectoid undertones
- **Dr. Hemo:** Enthusiastic scientist, professorial, supportive
