### **World-Building Specification Brief: Saviors of Mongo**

**1. Core Concept:**
Earthlings transported to planet Mongo must unite disparate alien kingdoms against tyrannical Emperor Ming the Merciless, navigating alien politics and exotic technology to save two worlds using rocket ships and raygun heroism.

**2. Lore & History:**
*   Detail planet Mongo's history—once unified, now divided under Ming's tyranny
*   Explain how Ming rose to power through superscience and manipulation
*   Describe each kingdom's culture, technology level, and relationship with Ming
*   Write about ancient Mongo before Ming—hints of greater past
*   Detail Earth-Mongo connection—why party was brought here (accident or fate?)
*   Explain Mongo's exotic technology—anti-gravity, ray weapons, mind control

**3. Factions:**
*   **Ming's Empire** (Major) - Tyrannical central government, advanced technology, brutal enforcement
*   **Arboria (Forest Kingdom)** (Major) - Resistance base, ruled by Prince Barin
*   **Sky City (Hawkmen Kingdom)** (Major) - Neutral but honorable, ruled by Vultan
*   **Frigia (Ice Kingdom)** (Major) - Isolationist, ruled by Queen Fria
*   **Undersea Kingdom (Shark Men)** (Minor) - Underwater realm, hostile to surface
*   **The Rebels** (Minor) - Underground resistance across all kingdoms
*   **Earth** (Minor) - Distant home world, may send aid or become target

**4. NPCs:**
*   **Tier 1:** Flash Gordon, Dale Arden, Dr. Zarkov, Ming the Merciless, Princess Aura, Prince Barin, Prince Vultan, Queen Fria
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Ming's Guard," "Hawkman Warrior," "Arboria Rebel," "Shark Man," "Arena Beast Master," "Palace Scientist," "Rebel Spy," "Ice Warrior"

**5. Locations:**
*   **Ming City** - Capital, palace, arena, atom furnace, death trap chambers
*   **Arboria** - Forest kingdom, tree cities, resistance headquarters
*   **Sky City** - Floating metropolis, aerial platforms, Hawkmen aeries
*   **Frigia** - Ice kingdom, crystal palaces, frozen wastelands
*   **Undersea Kingdom** - Domed cities, shark men territory
*   **The Arena** - Gladiatorial stadium, exotic beasts, death matches
*   **Rocket Ship Hangar** - Where Earth technology meets Mongo science

**6. Causality:**
*   IF kingdom allied with party THEN Ming targets them for destruction
*   IF Aura betrays Ming THEN she becomes hunted but valuable ally
*   IF arena combat lost THEN execution or re-education in pleasure chambers
*   IF atom furnace destroyed THEN Ming's power crippled but may use doomsday weapon
*   IF party returns to Earth THEN Ming may follow to conquer new world
*   IF kingdoms united THEN can challenge Ming's military might

**7. MacGuffins & Artifacts:**
*   **The Atom Furnace** - Powers Ming City, destroying it cripples empire
*   **Mind Control Rings** - Enslave victims to Ming's will
*   **Anti-Gravity Technology** - Allows flight and space travel
*   **Ray Weapons** - Various types, each with distinct effect
*   **The Rocket Ship** - Earth technology key to victory
*   **Crown of Mongo** - Ancient artifact legitimizing rightful ruler

**8. Exotic Technology:**
*   Disintegrator rays, paralysis beams, pleasure chambers, execution devices, rocket ships, anti-gravity platforms, mind control apparatus, death traps, force fields, communication arrays, teleportation chambers
