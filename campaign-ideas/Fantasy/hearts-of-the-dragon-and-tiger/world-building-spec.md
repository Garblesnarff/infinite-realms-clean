**Hearts of the Dragon and Tiger**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** Two adventurers with fearsome reputations but gentle hearts form an alliance to help each other win the affections of their respective crushes. Through schemes, mishaps, and growing intimacy, they discover that true love was beside them all along. Set in a magical academy where social dynamics are as important as combat training.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts. These entries will form the historical and cultural bedrock of the world.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the history of Palmtop Heights Academy and its founding principles of balanced training in combat and social graces.
    *   Write the legend of the "Dragon's Eyes Curse" - a superstition about half-orcs with sharp eyes being dangerous, and how it affects Ryuji.
    *   Describe the cultural significance of the Winter Festival and its tradition of gift-giving that reveals true feelings.
    *   Explain the social hierarchy within the academy and how reputations are formed and maintained.
    *   Detail the story of a past legendary couple from the academy whose romance became the stuff of legend.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create a detailed profile for each faction listed below. Each profile must contain the specified fields.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Student Council** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** To maintain order and organize academy events that foster both combat prowess and social development.
        *   **Hierarchy:** President (Sumire), Vice President (Kitamura), various department heads.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Create a thriving academy community through festivals, tournaments, and social programs.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** The president is preparing to leave the academy to pursue advanced studies, which will impact several key relationships.
        *   **Assets:** Authority over academy events, budget for festivals, respect of faculty.
        *   **Relationships:** Allied with faculty, somewhat at odds with the more rebellious student factions.
    *   **The Softball Team** (Minor)
        *   **Goals:** Win the inter-academy championship while maintaining their reputation as the most energetic group in school.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Captain (Minorin), various positions.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Train hard, play hard, and bring glory to the academy.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Several members use their athletic reputation to avoid confronting their real emotional issues.
        *   **Assets:** Athletic facilities, loyal team spirit, popular social status.
        *   **Relationships:** Generally well-liked, friendly rivalry with other sports teams.
    *   **The Popular Crowd** (Minor)
        *   **Goals:** Maintain social status and influence academy culture.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Loosely organized around Ami and other attractive, influential students.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Set trends and define what's "in" at the academy.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Many hide deep insecurities behind their perfect facades.
        *   **Assets:** Social influence, wealth, connections to important families.
        *   **Relationships:** Sometimes clash with the student council, dismissive of "lesser" students.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Directive:** The Character Generation Agent must create full profiles for all NPCs listed. Tier 1 NPCs are unique individuals. Tier 2 & 3 are archetypes to be instantiated multiple times with unique details as needed by the simulation.
*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**
    *   **Kitamura Yusaku:** Kind-hearted paladin, student council VP, secretly in love with the council president.
    *   **Kushieda Minori (Minorin):** Energetic cleric and softball captain, perceptive about others' feelings, hiding her own struggles.
    *   **Ami Kawashima:** Beautiful but jaded bard who sees through social facades and forces truth upon others.
    *   **Yasuko Takasu:** Ryuji's mother, former adventurer turned tavern keeper, works multiple jobs with endless energy.
    *   **Sumire:** Student council president, graceful and accomplished, planning to leave for advanced studies.
*   **Tier 2 & 3 (Archetypes for Generation):**
    *   "Gossip-prone academy student who spreads rumors"
    *   "Intimidated classmate who avoids Ryuji due to his appearance"
    *   "Faculty member who understands student romance struggles"
    *   "Popular student who bullied Taiga until she fought back"
    *   "Teammate who tries to set up Minorin with various suitors"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below, populating all specified sub-sections.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **Palmtop Heights Academy - Main Building:**
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The grand entrance hall with founder statues, the student council room, the dining hall, various classrooms, the rooftop (a secret meeting spot).
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Students of all races and classes, faculty members, support staff.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Education, training facilities, library resources, meal services.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Overhear gossip about the main characters, witness a confession scene between other students, accidental collision in hallway leading to romantic comedy moment, surprise quiz or training exercise, festival preparation chaos.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The rooftop is where the legendary couple first confessed their love." "The student council president is leaving at the end of the year." "Ami Kawashima is only pretending to be nice."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (polished floors, notice boards covered in announcements, students in varied uniforms), Sound (chatter and laughter, bells marking class periods, footsteps echoing), Smell (chalk, books, food from the dining hall).
    *   **The Dragon's Eye Tavern:**
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The main bar area, the cozy kitchen where Ryuji cooks, the small apartment upstairs, the wall covered in Yasuko's old adventuring trophies.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Yasuko, Ryuji, regular customers from all walks of life.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Home-cooked meals, drinks, a warm atmosphere, motherly advice from Yasuko.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Yasuko embarrassing Ryuji in front of guests, a regular customer sharing wisdom about relationships, Taiga showing up unexpectedly for dinner, Yasuko falling asleep at the bar after a long shift, cooking lesson turning into heart-to-heart talk.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Yasuko was once a famous adventurer known as 'The Night Butterfly.'" "Ryuji does all the cooking and cleaning because his mother works three jobs." "The tavern is struggling financially but Yasuko hides it with her smile."

        *   **Key Landmarks:** The empty refrigerator, the expensive but unused furniture, the large windows overlooking the city, the single futon in an otherwise empty room.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Taiga (though she often eats at Ryuji's tavern).
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Solitude, luxury amenities that go unused, a space that reveals emotional state.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Discovering Taiga can't cook and survives on takeout, finding evidence of her wealthy but absent family, interrupted moment of vulnerability, surprise visit from Ryuji bringing food, thunderstorm revealing Taiga's fear of being alone.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Taiga's family is incredibly wealthy but she lives alone." "She hasn't spoken to her father in years." "Despite the luxury, she seems happier eating instant noodles at Ryuji's tavern."

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes. For each "IF" condition, the agent must pre-generate the narrative and environmental consequences for the "THEN" outcome.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** Taiga and Ryuji successfully help each other have one-on-one time with their respective crushes.
    *   **THEN:** Their affection for Kitamura/Minorin increases slightly, but their affection for each other increases more. They feel vaguely disappointed by the results but can't articulate why.
    *   **IF:** The players witness or participate in Ami's arrival and her exposure of social pretenses.
    *   **THEN:** Social dynamics shift across the academy. Ami becomes a catalyst forcing the main characters to confront their true feelings. Relationship progress accelerates but becomes more complicated.
    *   **IF:** The Winter Festival occurs and the characters must decide who to give their handmade gifts to.
    *   **THEN:** The recipient of each character's gift reveals their subconscious feelings. If they give gifts to each other instead of their original crushes, their relationship reaches a tipping point.
    *   **IF:** Kitamura announces his plan to follow Sumire and confesses his unrequited love publicly.
    *   **THEN:** Taiga's fantasy is shattered. She experiences a emotional crisis that Ryuji must help her through, dramatically accelerating their bond.
    *   **IF:** The final confession occurs during the snowstorm.
    *   **THEN:** Both characters achieve true love status with each other. Generate an epilogue showing their life together, Yasuko's tearful happiness, and Minorin's knowing smile as she watches them walk hand-in-hand.
