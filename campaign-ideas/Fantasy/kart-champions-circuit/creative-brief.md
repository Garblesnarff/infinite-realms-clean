# Creative Brief: Kart Champions Circuit

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Whimsical Cartoon Racing (Mario Kart-inspired)

Inspired by the vibrant, colorful aesthetics of *Mario Kart* with exaggerated proportions, expressive characters, and impossible tracks. This style captures the joyful chaos of kart racing, magical item effects, competitive spirit, and fantastical kingdom diversity.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Dynamic Motion:** Speed lines, drifting smoke, item trails, exaggerated poses showing acceleration and turns
*   **Vibrant World Design:** Each kingdom has distinct visual identity—mushroom forests, lava castles, rainbow cosmos, ghost mansions
*   **Item Effect Clarity:** Magical items have distinctive visual signatures—banana yellow, shell red/green/blue, star rainbow shimmer
*   **Whimsical Architecture:** Tracks that defy physics—loops, jumps, floating sections, antigravity zones

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Whimsical, competitive, colorful, chaotic fun, magical racing, kingdom adventure, cartoon energy
*   **Feeling:** The excitement of a close race mixed with comedic chaos from items. Bright optimism even when a blue shell is incoming. Pure racing joy.

**2. Color Palette:**
*   **Kingdom Variety:** Mushroom Kingdom (red, white, green), Bowser's Castle (orange, red, black), Rainbow Road (full spectrum), Ghost Valley (purple, gray, ethereal blue)
*   **Kart Design:** Bold primary colors with racing stripes, each character's signature color scheme
*   **Item Effects:** Yellow bananas, red/green/blue shells, orange mushrooms, rainbow stars, purple lightning
*   **Prompt Example:** "...vibrant color palette with a red kart with white racing stripes drifting around a corner leaving blue smoke trails. Green shells fly through the air with green energy trails. The track is bright pink and purple checkerboard floating in a starfield. Item boxes glow with golden question marks."

**3. Character Design:**
*   **Proportions:** Large heads, expressive faces, smaller bodies in go-kart style vehicles
*   **Expressive Reactions:** Determined racing faces, shocked expressions when hit, triumphant victory poses
*   **Costume Variety:** Racing suits, overalls, princess gowns adapted for racing, helmets and goggles
*   **Prompt Example:** "A determined racer with a large mushroom-shaped helmet, blue overalls, white gloves gripping a steering wheel. Their face shows intense concentration. Red kart with flame decals. A green shell is about to hit them from behind, visible in a comedic moment of impending doom."

**4. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Impossible Tracks:** Roads that loop vertically, spiral through clouds, plunge underwater, traverse lava
*   **Kingdom Landmarks:** Giant mushrooms, castle towers with eyes, floating islands connected by rainbow bridges
*   **Track Furniture:** Item boxes, boost panels, jump ramps, hazard obstacles (thwomps, piranha plants, boulders)
*   **Prompt Example:** "A racing track made of rainbow-colored bricks floating in space with no guardrails. The track curves up and over in an impossible loop. Stars and nebulas fill the background. Boost panels glow blue on the track surface. Item boxes float above the track emitting golden sparkles."

**5. Racing Action:**
*   **Speed Effects:** Motion blur on backgrounds, wind effects on characters, speed lines, boost flames from exhausts
*   **Item Chaos:** Multiple items in flight—shells spinning, bananas on track, star invincibility rainbow trails
*   **Drifting:** Blue/orange sparks flying from wheels during drift boosts, skid marks on track
*   **Prompt Example:** "Three karts racing neck-and-neck around a tight corner. The lead kart drifts left with orange sparks flying from its wheels. A red shell homes in from behind with a red energy trail. A banana peel sits in the racing line. Motion blur emphasizes speed. Characters have determined racing expressions."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Bright and Clean:** Cartoon shading with clear highlights and shadows
*   **Item Glow:** Magical items emit soft glowing light in their signature colors
*   **Environmental Lighting:** Lava glow, star shine, underwater caustics, rainbow prismatic effects
*   **Prompt Example:** "...brightly lit with clear cel-shading. The blue shell glows with intense blue light illuminating nearby karts. Rainbow Road's surface reflects starlight. Characters have simple but clear shadow definitions. Speed boost flames cast orange light."

## Voice

An enthusiastic racing announcer voice full of energy and excitement, like someone commentating the most thrilling race ever. The pacing is rapid during action, building hype for items and position changes. The tone shifts between playful comedy ("And there goes another banana victim!"), dramatic racing calls ("They're neck and neck on the final lap!"), and triumphant celebration. There's an underlying sense of pure fun—this is competitive but never mean-spirited. Slightly breathless from the excitement. Role: The voice of joyful competition.

## Music & Sound Direction

*   **Musical Style:** Upbeat, catchy melody racing themes with strong rhythm. Each track has its own musical identity matching the kingdom (orchestral for castle, tropical for beach, cosmic for space).
*   **Sound Effects:** Kart engines revving, item box jingles, shell hits, drift boosts, the distinctive "blue shell incoming" alarm, victory fanfares.
*   **Dynamic Music:** Music intensifies on final lap, slows briefly when hit by items, triumphant flourish when using stars.
*   **Ambient Sounds:** Crowd cheering, kingdom-specific atmosphere (lava bubbling, ghost whispers, underwater echoes).
