### **World-Building Specification Brief: The Camorr Game**

**Core Concept:** In fantasy Venice (Camorr), elite con artists execute elaborate heists against nobility while navigating criminal underworld codes, until betrayal forces them to pull their most audacious con yet.

**Key Factions:**
- **The Gentlemen Bastards (Players):** Elite thieves trained in the Thieves' Prosper, rob nobility through long cons.
- **The Camorri Gangs:** Criminal organizations under various Capas, bound by the Secret Peace.
- **The Bondsmagi of Karthain:** Mage organization selling services for gold, holding grudges forever.
- **The Nobility:** Wealthy targets, corrupt and decadent, control the city officially.
- **The Spider's Network:** Secret intelligence organization manipulating both legal and criminal worlds.

**Key Locations:**
- **Camorr:** Canal city built among indestructible Elderglass ruins, criminal territories, noble estates, the Five Towers.
- **The Sinspire (Tal Verrar):** Nine-floor gambling tower with escalating security.
- **The Thieves' Prosper:** Secret meeting houses protected by criminal code.
- **Tal Verrar:** Pirate city with different power structure and opportunities.

**Magic Systems:**
- **Bondsmagi:** Powerful wizards with mind control, elemental magic, near-invincibility. Expensive to hire, eternal grudges.
- **Alchemy:** Limited - mostly poisons, antidotes, and practical substances.
- **Elderglass:** Ancient indestructible material, glows faintly, basis of city architecture.

**Unique Mechanics:**
- **Long Con Planning:** Multi-phase heists with flashback mechanics for clever preparation.
- **Multiple Identities:** Tracking various disguises and their social connections.
- **The Secret Peace:** Criminal code with serious consequences for violation.
