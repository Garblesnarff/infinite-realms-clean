### **World-Building Specification Brief: Journey of the Monkey King**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party joins an epic pilgrimage led by monk Tripitaka to retrieve sacred scriptures, accompanied by the legendary Monkey King Sun Wukong serving penance for rebelling against heaven. They face demons, spirits, celestial bureaucracy, and moral challenges on the path to enlightenment.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail Sun Wukong's rebellion against heaven and his 500-year imprisonment
    *   Explain the Three Realms (Heaven, Earth, Underworld) and their relationship
    *   Describe the sacred scriptures and why they're needed in the mortal realm
    *   Write about the celestial bureaucracy's structure and politics
    *   Detail the nature of demons, spirits, and immortals in this world
    *   Explain karma, reincarnation, and the path to enlightenment

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:**
    *   **The Pilgrims** (Major) - The party plus companions, goals: complete pilgrimage, redemption
    *   **The Celestial Bureaucracy** (Major) - Heavenly officials, maintain cosmic order, some oppose mortal enlightenment
    *   **Demon Kings** (Major) - Rule territories, want immortality through eating Tripitaka, some sympathetic
    *   **The Buddha's Followers** (Major) - Support pilgrimage, test and guide pilgrims
    *   **Rogue Immortals** (Minor) - Former celestials now in hiding, variable agendas

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Sun Wukong, Tripitaka, Zhu Bajie, Sha Wujing, Guanyin (compassionate bodhisattva), Erlang Shen (celestial warrior), The Buddha
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Demon King with Unique Power," "Celestial Bureaucrat," "Mountain Spirit," "Shapeshifting Fox Spirit," "Dragon King"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Silk Road** - Mortal realm pilgrimage path
*   **Flame Mountain** - Demon territory of impossible heat
*   **Cloud Palace of Heaven** - Celestial bureaucracy headquarters
*   **The Western Paradise** - Buddha's enlightened realm
*   Each location has supernatural encounters, transformation challenges, and moral tests

**6. Causality Chains**
*   IF Sun Wukong abandons the quest THEN celestial punishment and loss of party's strongest member
*   IF party shows compassion to demons THEN enlightenment increases, some demons become allies
*   IF party solves challenges through violence alone THEN enlightenment decreases, harder trials ahead
*   IF they navigate celestial bureaucracy successfully THEN heavenly allies provide crucial aid in final act
