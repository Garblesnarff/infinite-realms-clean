# Creative Brief: Spirit Detective Chronicles

## Art Style: Urban Fantasy with Spirit Energy (Yu Yu Hakusho-inspired)

Inspired by *Yu Yu Hakusho*'s blend of modern urban settings with supernatural elements, spirit energy visualization, and demon aesthetic ranging from horrific to beautiful, emphasizing character-driven martial arts with mystical powers.

### Core Principles:

*   **Spirit Energy Weapons:** Each character's life force manifests uniquely (finger guns, swords, whips, armor)
*   **Demon Diversity:** Designs range from monstrous to elegant humanoid
*   **Modern Urban:** Contemporary Japanese setting for early arcs, fantastical Demon Realm later
*   **Tournament Drama:** Arena battles with clear rings and boundaries

---

### Style Guide:

**Color Palette:**
*   **Spirit Energy:** Blue-white for pure spirit energy, varies by character (gold, green, red)
*   **Demon Auras:** Dark colors—purple, black, blood-red
*   **Modern Setting:** Realistic urban colors for living world
*   **Demon Realm:** Harsh reds, purples, alien sky colors

**Character Design:**
*   **Spirit Detectives:** Modern casual clothing (school uniforms, street wear, pompadour hairstyles)
*   **Demons:** Range from monstrous creatures to beautiful humanoids, often with elegant fashion sense
*   **Transformations:** Demon forms add markings, horns, enlarged muscles, changed eye colors
*   **Combat Wear:** Practical fighting clothes that can take punishment

**Spirit Energy Effects:**
*   **Spirit Gun:** Blue energy concentrated at fingertip, fired as beam
*   **Spirit Sword:** Aura shaped into blade form
*   **Spirit Wave:** Expanding sphere or wave of energy
*   **Prompt Example:** "A fighter points their finger like a gun, blue-white spirit energy concentrating at the fingertip. They shout and fire—a massive blue energy beam explodes forward with trailing spirals. The blast impacts their demon opponent, creating an explosion of light. Smoke rises from the crater."

**Demon Aesthetic:**
*   **Low-Class:** Monstrous, animalistic, grotesque
*   **High-Class:** Elegant, refined, dangerous beauty
*   **Transformations:** Releasing power shows true demonic form
*   **Prompt Example:** "An elegant demon in flowing robes releases their seal. Dark purple energy erupts around them. Demonic markings appear on their face. Their eyes turn golden. Horns emerge from their forehead. Their aura becomes oppressive, visible as purple flames surrounding their body."

**Tournament Scenes:**
*   **Arena:** Raised platform with clear boundaries, spectators surrounding
*   **Intensity:** Close-quarters martial arts mixed with energy attacks
*   **Crowd Reactions:** Demon spectators cheering for blood
*   **Prompt Example:** "A tournament arena on a floating platform. Two fighters clash—one wreathed in blue spirit energy, the other in dark demonic aura. They exchange rapid punches, each impact creating shockwaves. The crowd of demons roars in excitement. The sky above is dark and stormy."

## Voice

A street-smart voice with barely concealed aggression and surprising depth. Shifts from cocky during victories to introspective during moral dilemmas. Theme of "proving yourself through strength" mixed with "protecting what matters." Role: The voice of spirit and determination manifested through willpower.
