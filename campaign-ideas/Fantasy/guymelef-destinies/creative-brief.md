# Creative Brief: Guymelef Destinies

## Art Style: Fantasy Medieval Meets Ornate Mecha (Escaflowne Aesthetic)

Romantic fantasy with elegant mechanical knights. This style blends medieval European fantasy, steampunk machinery, tarot mysticism, and soaring mecha battles.

### Core Principles:

*   **Romantic Fantasy:** Flowing capes, ornate armor, dramatic poses, painterly backgrounds.
*   **Elegant Mecha:** Guymelefs designed like mechanical knights—swords, shields, capes, ornate decoration.
*   **Tarot and Destiny:** Mystical imagery—cards, prophecies, fate threads visualized.

---

### Style Guide:

**Color Palette:**
*   **Fanelia:** Warm golds and reds—dragon kingdom aesthetic.
*   **Asturia:** Royal blues and whites—elegant and diplomatic.
*   **Zaibach:** Blacks, silvers, deep reds—ominous and industrial.
*   **Escaflowne:** White with gold/red accents, dragon motifs.

**Character Design:**
*   **Van:** Short dark hair, red cape, dragon-scale pattern on clothes, intense eyes showing determination and pain.
*   **Hitomi:** Earth school uniform (green tracksuit) early, fantasy clothes later. Brown hair, expressive face.
*   **Allen:** Long blonde hair, knight's armor, perpetually heroic pose, charming smile.
*   **Folken:** White hair, missing right arm (prosthetic), black Zaibach uniform, tragic eyes.

**Guymelef Design:**
*   Knight-like humanoid mechs with medieval weapons (swords, lances).
*   **Escaflowne:** White armored Guymelef with dragon wings, can transform from dragon form. Gold and red trim, elegant curves.
*   **Scherazade:** Blue with ornate decoration, slim profile, rapier weapon.
*   **Zaibach Units:** Angular, black/red, brutal and functional.

**Voice:** Romantic fantasy protagonist—speaking with passion about destiny, love, honor. Dramatic declarations, poetic metaphors, melodramatic but sincere. Mix of medieval speech patterns and modern isekai confusion.

## Music Palette:
*   Orchestral fantasy with choir—sweeping strings for romance, triumphant brass for battles.
*   Mystical harp and flute for tarot/prophecy scenes.
*   Electric guitar for dramatic moments (anime influence).
*   Iconic: "Dance of Curse" (haunting vocals), "Epistle" (emotional piano).
