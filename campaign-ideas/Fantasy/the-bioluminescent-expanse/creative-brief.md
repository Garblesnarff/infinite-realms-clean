# The Bioluminescent Expanse - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Vibrant Bioluminescent Fantasy
**Inspiration**: Avatar (Pandora), Finding Nemo/Dory, Life of Pi jellyfish scene, nature documentaries of deep sea creatures
**Aesthetic**: Explosion of color, every surface glows, psychedelic beauty grounded in real biology
**Tone**: Optimistic wonder transforming to desperate preservation of beauty

## Color Palettes
**Healthy Reef**: Neon blues, electric greens, vibrant oranges, hot pinks, brilliant purples
**Coraline People**: Opalescent skin with customizable bioluminescent patterns, rainbow spectrum
**Blight Zones**: Grays, blacks, dull browns, colors draining away
**Void Dwellers**: Absolute black silhouettes, occasional red bioluminescence (only color they produce)

## Voice Direction
**Radiant Meridian**: Calm authority, musical quality, each word precisely chosen
**Shimmer**: Youthful excitement, rapid speech, curious questions, warm
**Umbral Scholar**: Contemplative, somber, delivers hard truths gently
**Phosphor**: Ancient, resonant, speaks in poetic metaphor, gentle wisdom

## Cinematic Moments
- Arrival: Reef vista reveals hundreds of miles of glowing beauty
- Festival: Synchronized light show, reef becomes living art visible from space
- Blight Spreads: Time-lapse of color draining, horror of beauty dying
- First Void Encounter**: Massive shadow blots out light, terror in darkness
- Final Festival: Light explosion drives back darkness, reef reborn
