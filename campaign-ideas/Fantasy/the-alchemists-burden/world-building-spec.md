**The Alchemist's Burden**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In militaristic Amestris where alchemy is science, the party committed human transmutation's taboo and lost body parts. Now State Alchemists seeking restoration, they uncover a conspiracy: the nation is a transmutation circle for mass sacrifice. They must master alchemy, face homunculi, and choose what to sacrifice to stop Father from becoming god.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail Amestris' formation and the truth that Father orchestrated its borders as a transmutation circle.
    *   Write the history of the Ishvalan War of Extermination and State Alchemists' role as weapons.
    *   Describe the first Philosopher's Stone created from Xerxes' entire population centuries ago.
    *   Explain the Gate of Truth and why human transmutation is taboo.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** Military of Amestris (corrupt government), Father's Homunculi (seven sin-themed immortals), Ishvalan Survivors (seeking revenge), Xingese Empire (rival nation seeking immortality).

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Father, Seven Homunculi, Colonel Mustang, Van Hohenheim, Truth, Fuhrer King Bradley (Wrath).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "State Alchemist," "Military Officer," "Ishvalan Warrior," "Homunculi Minion."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Central City:** Capital with military headquarters, underground tunnels to Father's lair.
*   **Father's Lair:** Laboratory containing the Gate, homunculi creation chambers, nationwide transmutation circle control.

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Party uses Philosopher's Stone → **THEN:** Gains immense power but consumes human souls, moral consequences.
*   **IF:** Party exposes conspiracy → **THEN:** Military civil war, homunculi accelerate plans.
*   **IF:** The Promised Day eclipse arrives → **THEN:** Nationwide transmutation begins, must stop Father within time limit.
*   **IF:** Party offers their gate knowledge → **THEN:** Can restore lost body parts by giving up alchemy itself.
