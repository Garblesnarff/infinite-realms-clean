# Creative Brief: Crisis of Infinite Realms

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Cosmic Horror Surrealism (Multiverse Apocalypse)

Inspired by the reality-bending visuals of works like *Doctor Strange*, *Everything Everywhere All at Once*, and classic cosmic horror art. This style aims to capture the overwhelming scale of a multiversal collapse, where realities fracture, merge, and die in visually spectacular and horrifying ways.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Reality Breaking:** Show fractured dimensions, impossible geometry, overlapping worlds, and visual paradoxes.
*   **Cosmic Scale:** Depict vast, incomprehensible entities, waves of antimatter consuming worlds, and heroes dwarfed by cosmic forces.
*   **Surreal Juxtaposition:** Mix different realities in the same frame—medieval knights next to futuristic soldiers, castles floating in space, multiple versions of the same location overlapping.

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Apocalyptic, cosmic, surreal, reality-breaking, infinite, overwhelming, heroic defiance, multiversal chaos.
*   **Feeling:** A sense of reality falling apart at the seams. The atmosphere should be one of beautiful cosmic horror, where the end of everything is both terrible and awe-inspiring. The world(s) feel unstable and dreamlike.

**2. Line Work & Form:**
*   **Style:** A mix of sharp, precise lines for solid objects and fluid, dissolving edges for reality-breaking effects. Lines should fracture, duplicate, or phase into other dimensions.
*   **Forms:** Characters and objects should sometimes have multiple overlapping versions, ghostly echoes, or partially phased-out portions. Architecture bends and overlaps impossibly.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A highly detailed digital painting with surrealist elements. A hero stands firm, their form overlapped by translucent versions of themselves from other realities, each wearing different armor and wielding different weapons."

**3. Color Palette:**
*   **Style:** Vibrant, otherworldly colors that clash and blend—deep purples and blacks for the void, bright neon colors for reality fractures, shifting prismatic effects for merged worlds.
*   **Antimatter:** Pure black with white negative-space edges, consuming color wherever it touches.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...a color palette of deep cosmic purples and blacks shot through with neon blue and pink reality fractures. The antimatter wave is pure black with glowing white edges, erasing color from the world."

**4. Character Design:**
*   **Style:** Characters should look battle-worn but determined. Multiple versions of the same character may appear simultaneously, each reflecting different choices and realities.
*   **Alternate Versions:** Show visual diversity—the same hero as a knight, a cyborg, a wizard, a villain—all recognizably the same person but different.
*   **Expression:** Faces show awe, horror, determination, and the weight of cosmic knowledge. Eyes sometimes glow with multiversal awareness.
*   **Prompt Example:** "The player character, a warrior, surrounded by ghostly, translucent versions of themselves—one in wizard robes, one in demonic armor, one as a celestial being—all converging into a single, empowered form."

**5. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Style:** Surreal landscapes where multiple realities overlap. A medieval castle might have futuristic towers growing from it, or a forest floor might transition seamlessly into a starfield.
*   **Dying Worlds:** Show worlds in various states of collapse—frozen in time, consumed by darkness, breaking into geometric fragments, or melting into abstract chaos.
*   **Details:** Floating debris from destroyed realities, ghostly echoes of civilizations, antimatter waves advancing like liquid shadow, reality itself crackling with visible energy.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A panoramic view of the Merged Reality: a landscape where a medieval village, a futuristic city, and an alien jungle all occupy the same space, phasing in and out of each other. The sky shows three different times of day simultaneously."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Style:** Dramatic and impossible lighting. Light sources from multiple realities create contradictory shadows. Antimatter casts "negative light" that makes things darker.
*   **Shadows:** Multiple overlapping shadows from different light sources, shadows that move independently, or absence of shadow where there should be one.
*   **Purpose:** Lighting emphasizes the surreal, unstable nature of reality. It creates a dreamlike, unsettling atmosphere.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...lit by impossible light sources from multiple merged realities—one side in daylight, one in moonlight, one in alien bioluminescence. The hero casts three different shadows, each pointing in a different direction."

**7. Composition:**
*   **Style:** Often uses layered, translucent compositions to show multiple realities at once. Can also use split-screen effects or kaleidoscopic patterns to represent fractured reality.
*   **Techniques:** Layer multiple versions of the same scene with different details. Use spiraling or fractal patterns to represent infinite realities. Frame the cosmic entities at impossible scales.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A split composition showing the same location in three different realities simultaneously: a lush forest, a dead wasteland, and a crystalline alien structure, all overlapping and bleeding into each other. Heroes from each reality face the same threat."

## Voice

A vast, echoing voice that sounds like multiple speakers talking in imperfect unison, creating a haunting harmony. The tone is ancient and otherworldly, speaking with the authority of someone who perceives all realities simultaneously. There's a quality of barely-contained cosmic knowledge, as if the speaker exists outside normal time and sees all possible pasts and futures. The delivery is measured and ominous, with subtle audio distortions that suggest reality itself bending. Role: The Monitor, narrator of infinite stories, bearing witness to the end of all things.
