# Lightning's Chosen - Creative Brief

## Visual Style
**Electric Divinity** - Lightning as divine force, Lichtenberg scars as holy marks, storm symbolism

**Colors**: Electric blues, brilliant whites, storm purples, silver scars on skin, darkness of void as pure black

**Character Design**: Lightning-Touched have silver branching scars, crackling auras, eyes that flash with power

**Locations**: Storm Citadel wreathed in perpetual lightning, arenas of divine trial, void zones of absolute darkness

## Music
- Thunder Voice: Orchestral mixed with electronic, voices in storm
- Lightning Power: High-energy electric guitar and drums
- Dark Gaps: Silence broken by whispers, uncomfortable absence
- Trial Themes: Epic boss music with lightning sound effects

## Voice Acting
- Thunder Voice: Multiple voices speaking in unison, punctuated by thunder
- High Conductor Marcus: Zealot's passion, divine certainty
- Rogue Striker Zara: Bitter freedom fighter, warning others
- Void Speaker: Soft whisper promising peace, seductive absence

**Tagline**: "The Storm Decides Who Is Worthy"

**Key Art**: Silhouette of heroes struck by lightning from above, scars glowing, with massive storm deity face in clouds looking down, and creeping darkness around edges
