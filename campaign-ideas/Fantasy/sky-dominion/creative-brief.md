# Sky Dominion - Creative Brief

## Art Style and Visual Tone

### Overall Aesthetic
Studio Ghibli-inspired wonder meets steampunk adventure. Emphasis on vertical scale, endless skies, and the contrast between industrial surface world and ethereal floating cities. Romantic nostalgia for lost civilizations mixed with ecological warnings.

### Surface World Design
- **Industrial Revolution aesthetic**: Coal smoke, steam engines, brass machinery
- **Mining towns**: Vertical shaft architecture, company housing, oppressive
- **Color Palette**: Greys, browns, smoke, occasional rust orange
- **Mood**: Grounded, heavy, realistic hardship

### Sky Aesthetic
- **Floating islands**: Impossible geology, waterfalls flowing to nowhere, crystal formations
- **Airships**: Combination of sailing ships and dirigibles, ornate and practical
- **Cloud layers**: Different environments at different altitudes
- **Color Palette**: Blues, whites, golden sunlight, rainbow refraction through crystals

### Laputa Design
- **Architecture**: Greco-Roman meets Art Deco with crystal integration
- **Gardens**: Lush overgrowth, gentle disorder of nature reclaiming civilization
- **Technology**: Sleek brass and crystal, organic curves, Ancient aesthetic
- **Robots**: Ghibli-style gentle giants with egg-shaped bodies, spindly limbs

## Music and Sound Design

### Main Theme
Sweeping orchestral adventure with Celtic influences. Emphasis on strings and woodwinds creating sense of flight and freedom. Leitmotifs for sky (ascending scales) versus earth (descending, heavy).

**Instrumentation**: Full orchestra, harp, pan flute, accordion, music box for Sheeta's theme.

### Location Themes

**Surface/Mining Towns**: Industrial percussion (hammers on metal), work songs, oppressive drones.

**Sky Travel**: Wind instruments, strings playing ascending arpeggios, sense of movement and freedom.

**Laputa**: Ethereal choir, ancient instruments, combination of majesty and melancholy.

**Combat**: Dramatic brass, percussion, strings creating urgency and danger.

### Character Themes

**Sheeta**: Music box playing simple melody, representing innocence and lost childhood.

**Muska**: Organ music with discordant notes, classical turned sinister.

**Dola**: Jaunty accordion with pirate swagger, maternal warmth underlying.

**Robot Guardian**: Gentle chimes and low brass, representing peaceful power.

## Voice Direction for Key NPCs

### Sheeta
- **Vocal Quality**: Young soprano, gentle but with core of steel
- **Key Traits**: Whispers secrets, shouts defiance, never whines
- **Reference**: Hayao Miyazaki heroines
- **Sample**: "Laputa is my home... but home isn't worth the world's destruction."

### Colonel Muska
- **Vocal Quality**: Smooth baritone, cultured menace
- **Key Traits**: Never loses composure, treats violence as poetry
- **Reference**: Raul Julia, Alan Rickman
- **Sample**: "Three minutes. That's how long it takes to erase a city. Efficient, wouldn't you say?"

### Dola
- **Vocal Quality**: Gruff alto, maternal authority
- **Key Traits**: Barks orders, hidden tenderness, creative cursing
- **Reference**: Judi Dench, Kathryn Janeway
- **Sample**: "Listen up, you sky-brained idiots! That girl's worth more than all Laputa's gold combined!"

### Robot Guardian
- **Vocal Quality**: No speech—musical tones, mechanical sounds
- **Key Traits**: Different tones for emotions (gentle hum vs. warning siren)
- **Reference**: Wall-E, Iron Giant
- **Sample**: [Questioning chime rising in pitch] [Protective low bass rumble]

## Cinematic Moments

### Sheeta's Fall
- **Visual**: Slow-motion descent from miles up, pendant glowing, clouds parting
- **Audio**: Wind rushing, then silence as magic activates, gentle descent music
- **Direction**: Perspective from ground looking up, tiny figure becoming clear

### First Sight of Flying Island
- **Visual**: Ship breaks through cloud layer, impossible island floating above
- **Audio**: Music swells, party's gasps of wonder
- **Direction**: Long establishing shot emphasizing scale and impossibility

### Robot Guardian Awakens
- **Visual**: Ancient automaton's eyes glow, dust falling from joints, gentle rise
- **Audio**: Mechanical activation sounds becoming musical
- **Direction**: Close-up on eye lighting up, pull back to reveal full scale

### Airship Battle in Storm
- **Visual**: Lightning illuminating ships, rain lashing, pirates swinging on ropes
- **Audio**: Thunder, cannon fire, shouted orders, wood creaking
- **Direction**: Dynamic camera following action, multiple perspective cuts

### Entering Laputa
- **Visual**: Hurricane wall parts before Sheeta's pendant, reveal of pristine gardens
- **Audio**: Storm fades to birdsong and wind chimes
- **Direction**: Dark chaos to brilliant paradise, slow push into wonderland

### Orbital Strike Demonstration
- **Visual**: Laputa's crystal weapon charges, beam of light, distant city vanishing
- **Audio**: Building hum, then devastating silence
- **Direction**: Watch Muska's cold face, then cut to destruction, return to his smile

### Speaking the Destruction Spell
- **Visual**: Sheeta and helper reciting ancient words, city trembling, crystals cracking
- **Audio**: Ancient language echoing, destruction beginning gently then cascading
- **Direction**: Close on speakers' faces, wide shots of Laputa breaking apart

## Environmental Storytelling

### Altitude Indicators
- **Surface**: Heavy, polluted, industrial
- **Low Sky**: Transitional, some floating debris
- **Mid Sky**: Comfortable, exotic flying wildlife
- **High Sky**: Thin air, dangerous weather, approaching Laputa
- **Laputa**: Perfect preserved environment, eerie perfection

### Laputan Decay
Show passage of 700 years through:
- Nature reclaiming architecture
- Robots showing wear but still functioning
- Texts preserved but fading
- Technology dormant but reactivatable

### Cultural Contrast
- **Surface**: Survival, exploitation, no beauty
- **Pirates**: Freedom but lawlessness
- **Military**: Order through oppression
- **Laputa**: Harmony abandoned for peace

## Player Experience Goals

### Emotional Journey
Wonder → Adventure → Temptation → Horror → Sacrifice → Bittersweet Hope

### Sensory Immersion
Emphasize the sensation of flight, vertigo of heights, clean air versus coal smoke, beauty versus industry.

### Memorable Moments
- First time ship leaves ground
- Discovering floating island
- Meeting Robot Guardian
- Seeing Laputa's gardens
- Witnessing weapon's power
- Making final choice
