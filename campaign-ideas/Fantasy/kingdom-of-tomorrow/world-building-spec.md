### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

**Kingdom of Tomorrow**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Old generation heroes return to restore traditional heroism as the new generation fights with brutal pragmatism. Guardian's increasingly authoritarian methods and the new generation's resistance push toward war. The players must choose sides or unite both generations before a manipulated conflict destroys the realm.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   Detail the Golden Age: when the old heroes were at their peak.
*   Write what caused the old heroes' retirement and public loss of faith.
*   Describe the rise of the new generation and their brutal methods.
*   Explain Justice Light: Guardian's orbital compliance weapon.
*   Detail previous generational conflicts in hero history.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **The Justice Alliance (Old Heroes):** Traditional heroes enforcing classic values.
*   **The New Guard:** Young metahumans resisting authoritarianism.
*   **The Mediators:** Heroes trying to prevent war.
*   **The Human League:** Civilians who fear all metahumans.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Guardian, Magog, Wonder Knight, The Manipulator.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Retired Legend", "Reckless New Hero", "Traumatized Civilian", "Extremist from Either Side".

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Gulag:** Prison for uncontrolled metahumans.
*   **Orbital Platform:** Justice Light weapon construction site.
*   **The Sanctuary:** Old heroes' retirement refuge.
*   **The Battleground:** Neutral zone for final confrontation.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Escalation Clock reaches maximum → Generate the apocalyptic battle.
*   **IF:** Players expose the Manipulator → Both sides temporarily unite.
*   **IF:** Guardian uses Justice Light → Creates tyranny or prevents war depending on context.
*   **IF:** Players broker peace → Generate the reconciliation and new code that honors both generations.
