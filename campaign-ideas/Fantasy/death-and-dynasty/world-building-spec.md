### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

This document is the primary instruction set for the world-building AI pipeline. Each section provides explicit directives for specialized agents to generate the necessary assets for a complete and dynamic campaign world.

**Death and Dynasty**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** The party are master assassins from another world who died and were offered a deal by a goddess: be reincarnated into a fantasy realm as members of a noble house, retain their skills and memories, and prevent a prophesied calamity—the rise of an invincible Hero who will become a tyrant and destroy the world. Born as children of House Tuatha Dé, they must use their past-life expertise in assassination, tactics, and intelligence work to navigate court politics, eliminate key threats, and ultimately decide the fate of the prophesied Hero. Every kill must be perfect, every plan must be flawless, because failure means not just death, but the end of everything.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts. These entries will form the historical and cultural bedrock of the world.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the history of the Kingdom and the establishment of the noble houses.
    *   Write about the ancient prophecy of the Hero who will become a tyrant and destroy the world.
    *   Describe the legend of the Demon Lord of Despair and his imprisonment behind the Demon Gate.
    *   Explain the nature of reincarnation magic and why the Goddess of Fate would intervene.
    *   Detail the political structure of the kingdom and the relationships between major noble houses.
    *   Write about past Heroes and how their stories ended—establishing a pattern of corruption.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create detailed profile for each faction listed below. Each profile must contain the specified fields.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **House Tuatha Dé** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Protect family honor, maintain noble standing, protect the kingdom.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Lord and Lady Tuatha Dé lead, with the party as children/heirs.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Serve the crown with honor and strengthen the house's position.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** The party secretly works to prevent the calamity while protecting the family from threats.
        *   **Assets:** Noble estate, military forces, political connections, the party's assassination skills.
        *   **Relationships:** Respected by the crown; rivals with other houses; secretly opposed to the Cult of Ruin.
    *   **The Royal Court** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Maintain the kingdom's stability and prosperity.
        *   **Hierarchy:** The King rules, with various dukes and advisors below.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Govern justly and protect the realm.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Many factions within the court have competing interests; some are infiltrated by the cult.
        *   **Assets:** Military might, legal authority, the prophesied Hero Cain.
        *   **Relationships:** Complex web of alliances; some members are allies, others are cultists.
    *   **The Cult of Ruin** (Major)
        *   **Goals:** Orchestrate Cain's corruption to bring about the prophesied destruction.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Duke Blackthorn leads, with cells throughout the kingdom.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Unknown; they operate in complete secrecy.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** Create tragedies that will break Cain's faith in humanity and turn him into the tyrant.
        *   **Assets:** High-ranking members in court, cursed artifacts, demonic pacts, hidden resources.
        *   **Relationships:** Secretly opposed to the crown; opposed by the party; serves the Demon Lord.
    *   **Cain's Circle** (Minor)
        *   **Goals:** Support Cain in his heroic journey and protect the innocent.
        *   **Hierarchy:** Cain leads, with childhood friends and loyal retainers.
        *   **Public Agenda:** Train to become strong enough to protect the kingdom.
        *   **Secret Agenda:** None—they're genuinely good people, which makes them vulnerable.
        *   **Assets:** Cain's growing power, the support of common folk, genuine friendship.
        *   **Relationships:** Supported by the crown; admired by the people; targeted by the cult.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Directive:** The Character Generation Agent must create full profiles for all NPCs listed. Tier 1 NPCs are unique individuals. Tier 2 & 3 are archetypes to be instantiated multiple times with unique details as needed by the simulation.
*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**
    *   **The Goddess of Fate:** Distant deity who reincarnated the party and set their mission.
    *   **Cain the Hero:** Genuinely kind prophesied hero destined to become a tyrant unless saved.
    *   **Lord Tuatha Dé:** Honorable warrior father who loves his unusual children.
    *   **Lady Tuatha Dé:** Cunning political operator mother who suspects the truth.
    *   **Duke Blackthorn:** Charming cult leader orchestrating Cain's corruption.
    *   **Elena:** Cain's childhood friend destined to betray him under manipulation.
    *   **Sir Gareth:** Legendary honorable knight who may become an ally.
    *   **The Demon Lord of Despair:** Patient manipulator feeding on corruption and destruction.

    *   "Loyal House Tuatha Dé Retainer"
    *   "Cult Member (Various Ranks)"
    *   "Royal Court Politician"
    *   "City Guard Captain"
    *   "Information Broker"
    *   "Hired Bodyguard"
    *   "Innocent Bystander"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create a detailed blueprint for each location below, populating all specified sub-sections.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **House Tuatha Dé Estate:** The party's noble home and operations base.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The main hall, training grounds, library, secret chambers (converted to ops rooms), family crypts.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** The party, Lord and Lady Tuatha Dé, household staff, guards.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Training facilities, intelligence gathering, safe planning space, family resources.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Training session with father, political discussion with mother, loyal retainer offering help, infiltrator discovered, family gathering.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The family crypts have secret passages." "Mother suspects we're not ordinary." "A servant has been asking suspicious questions."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (stone walls, family banners, weapon racks), Sound (training sounds, formal conversations, footsteps on stone), Smell (oil and steel, old parchment, candle wax).
    *   **The Royal Capital:** Sprawling city of intrigue and danger.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The Royal Palace, noble district, merchant quarters, slums, the underground (criminal network).
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Nobles, merchants, commoners, criminals, guards, spies.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Everything from legal trade to black market goods, information, hired help.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Cult agent spotted, political assassination attempt, chance meeting with Cain, noble social event, criminal offering services.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Strange disappearances in the slums." "A noble house is secretly backing the cult." "The palace has hidden rooms and passages."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (varied architecture from opulent to squalid, crowds, guards), Sound (city noise, merchant calls, whispered plots), Smell (varied—perfume to sewage depending on district).
    *   **The Royal Palace:** Center of political power and intrigue.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The throne room, the war room, noble receiving halls, the king's private chambers, secret passages.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** The king, court nobles, guards, advisors, servants, spies.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Political audiences, court events, access to power (if earned).
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Court ceremony, overhearing conspiracy, meeting Cain formally, assassination opportunity, political negotiation.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Duke Blackthorn has unusual influence with the king." "Secret meetings occur in the old tower." "The king is dying and doesn't know who to trust."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (opulent decorations, guards in livery, power on display), Sound (formal announcements, whispered politics, echoing footsteps), Smell (incense, perfume, power).
    *   **Cain's Village:** Peaceful settlement targeted by the cult.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The village square, Cain's family home, the small temple, the inn, surrounding farmland.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Simple farmers, Cain's family, Elena, village priest.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** Simple goods, peaceful rest, connection to Cain's origins.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Meeting Cain in his element, village festival, cult scouts observing, Elena seeking advice, peaceful daily life.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "Strange figures have been seen in the woods." "The priest senses dark magic nearby." "This village is scheduled for destruction by the cult."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (simple buildings, farmland, peaceful life), Sound (farm animals, children playing, village bell), Smell (fresh earth, cooking fires, hay).
    *   **The Cult's Hidden Temple:** Secret sanctum beneath the capital.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The altar to the Demon Lord, the ritual chamber, the planning room, cells for victims, the secret entrance.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Duke Blackthorn, cult members, prisoners, demonic manifestations.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** None for the party—this is enemy territory.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Cult ritual in progress, guards patrolling, overhearing plans, discovering prisoners, demonic manifestation.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The cult's full plan is written in the planning room." "They're preparing a major ritual soon." "Duke Blackthorn meets with a demonic envoy here."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (dark stone, demonic symbols, ritual circles), Sound (chanting, screams, demonic whispers), Smell (blood, incense, sulfur).
    *   **The Demon Gate:** Ancient sealed portal in a cursed forest.
        *   **Key Landmarks:** The massive sealed gate, protective wards (weakening), the clearing (unnaturally dead), remnants of ancient battles.
        *   **Primary Inhabitants:** Nothing living—only demonic whispers and manifestations.
        *   **Available Goods & Services:** None—pure danger and information.
        *   **Potential Random Encounters (x5):** Demonic manifestation, cult pilgrims, ward weakening event, vision of the future, ancient guardian remnant.
        *   **Embedded Plot Hooks & Rumors (x3):** "The gate is weakening—Cain's corruption would shatter it." "The Demon Lord can be communicated with here." "Destroying the gate might prevent the calamity."
        *   **Sensory Details:** Sight (massive stone gate, dead vegetation, wrongness), Sound (whispers in unknown language, unnatural silence, heartbeat-like pulses), Smell (decay, sulfur, absence of life).

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Directive:** The World Simulation Agent must implement the following trigger-based state changes. For each "IF" condition, the agent must pre-generate the narrative and environmental consequences for the "THEN" outcome.
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** The party successfully prevents a cult plot targeting Cain.
    *   **THEN:** The Corruption Track doesn't advance. Generate consequences: cult adapts plans, Cain remains innocent, party gains time.
    *   **IF:** The party fails to prevent a tragedy affecting Cain.
    *   **THEN:** The Corruption Track advances. Generate scenes showing Cain's growing darkness and despair.
    *   **IF:** The party is discovered to be reincarnated assassins.
    *   **THEN:** Generate crisis: some allies become enemies, reputation destroyed, must operate entirely in shadows or flee.
    *   **IF:** The party successfully assassinates Duke Blackthorn.
    *   **THEN:** The cult is temporarily disrupted. Generate power vacuum, new leader emerging, and consequences of the kill.
    *   **IF:** The party chooses to kill Cain to prevent the calamity.
    *   **THEN:** Generate the perfect assassination mission and the aftermath: guilt, world saved but at cost, reactions from allies.
    *   **IF:** The party chooses to save Cain from corruption.
    *   **THEN:** Generate the complex operation to eliminate all threats and the epilogue showing whether it worked.
    *   **IF:** The party chooses to prepare Cain for his trials.
    *   **THEN:** Generate scenes of training and revelation, then show whether trust was justified or misplaced.
