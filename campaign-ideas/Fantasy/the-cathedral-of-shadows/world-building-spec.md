# World-Building Specification: The Cathedral of Shadows

## Core Concept
Gothic dimension as infinite cathedral. Divine light fading as Architect-God loses faith. Party must ascend through architectural theology to restore light or accept darkness.

## Lore
- **Origin**: Manifestation of Gothic theology as physical realm
- **The Architect-God**: Divine entity who built reality as cathedral, now doubting
- **Light Fading**: Divine illumination consumed by shadow (God's doubt)
- **Vertical Theology**: Physical ascension toward heaven = spiritual salvation

## Factions
- **The Last Monks** (maintain what remains)
- **The Seven Sins** (embody temptation)
- **The Seven Virtues** (test worthiness)
- **Gargoyle Guardians** (protect holy spaces)
- **Shadow Creatures** (divine doubt made manifest)

## NPCs
- **Brother Grimwald**: Monk guide
- **Pride, Envy, Wrath, Sloth, Greed, Gluttony, Lust**: Sin embodiments
- **Faith, Hope, Charity, Prudence, Justice, Temperance, Fortitude**: Virtue trials
- **The Architect-God**: Final entity

## Locations
Infinite cathedral: Crypt → Nave → Transepts → Flying Buttresses → Bell Towers → Spires → Rose Window.

## Mechanics
- **Virtue Score**: Moral tracking
- **Vertical Gravity**: Shifts toward divine light
- **Stained Glass Prophecy**: Windows reveal truth
- **Living Architecture**: Responds to faith
- **Light/Shadow Sanity**: Exposure to extremes damages psyche
