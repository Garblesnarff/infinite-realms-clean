# Taste of the Divine - Creative Brief

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Hyper-saturated flavor worlds meets the emptiness of the Void
- **Art Style**: Synesthetic visuals showing taste as color, sound, feeling
- **Tone**: Wonder and existential philosophy, beautiful and contemplative

## Color Palettes
- **Sweet Domain**: Pinks, pastels, candy colors, warm happiness
- **Salty Domain**: Ocean blues, crystal whites, tear-drop silvers
- **Sour Domain**: Electric yellows, lime greens, lightning whites
- **Bitter Domain**: Deep browns, dark greens, shadow purples
- **Umami Domain**: Rich reds, satisfying golds, balanced earth tones
- **Void Territory**: Complete absence of color, gray-white nothingness
- **Balanced Magic**: Rainbow spectrum in perfect harmony

## Music
- Each flavor has musical signature
- Sweet: Major keys, strings, joyful
- Salty: Ocean sounds, melancholic melodies
- Sour: Sharp notes, percussion, energetic
- Bitter: Minor keys, wisdom, deep bass
- Umami: Harmonies bringing others together
- Void: Silence, white noise, absence of music
- Climax: All flavors in perfect symphony

## Voice Direction
- **Sage Umami**: Gentle teacher, Mr. Rogers wisdom
- **Divine Palates**: Each has distinct voice matching flavor
- **Dulcia**: Childlike joy, innocent happiness
- **Salazar**: Deep and melancholic, ocean's weight
- **Acidalia**: Sharp and energetic, lightning crack
- **Amaros**: Profound wisdom, serious gravitas
- **Savora**: Chorus of voices in harmony
- **Flavorless Void**: Flat affect, emotionless calm, seductive emptiness

## Cinematic Moments
- First flavor-spell casting with food
- Each Divine Palate's liberation, flavor flooding back
- Void territory contrast with flavor domains
- Party achieving perfect five-flavor balance
- Final choice between destruction and acceptance
- World transformed by restored flavors
