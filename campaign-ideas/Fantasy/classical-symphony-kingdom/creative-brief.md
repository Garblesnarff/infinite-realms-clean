# Classical Symphony Kingdom - Creative Brief

## Art Style and Visual Tone

**Overall Aesthetic:** Baroque architecture meets crystal palace fantasy. Everything is elegant, proportioned, and mathematically beautiful. Think Versailles crossed with crystal cathedrals, rendered in perpetual golden hour light. Every element has purpose and perfect placement.

**Visual Influences:**
- Versailles Palace and baroque architecture
- Crystal Palace and glass architecture
- Classical paintings (Vermeer, Claude Lorrain)
- Fantasy crystal cities
- Sheet music as decorative element
- Geometric perfection

## Color Palettes

**Primary:** Gold (#D4AF37), ivory (#FFFFF0), royal blue (#4169E1), burgundy (#800020)
**Accents:** Silver (#C0C0C0), emerald (#50C878), sapphire (#0F52BA)
**Lighting:** Warm golden hour constantly

**District Palettes:**
- **Baroque:** Rich golds, deep reds, ornate
- **Classical:** Balanced whites, blues, proportioned
- **Romantic:** Passionate purples, reds, dramatic
- **Impressionist:** Soft pastels, watercolor effects
- **Modern:** Harsh contrasts, geometric patterns
- **Minimalist:** Whites, grays, simple
- **Neo-Classical:** Blended traditional and modern

## Music and Sound Design

**Ambient:** Constant orchestral music, changes by district
**Baroque Quarter:** Bach, Vivaldi, Handel
**Classical Quarter:** Mozart, Haydn
**Romantic Quarter:** Tchaikovsky, Brahms
**Impressionist Quarter:** Debussy, Ravel
**Modern Quarter:** Stravinsky, Schoenberg
**Minimalist Quarter:** Glass, Reich
**Neo-Classical:** Contemporary classical

**NPC Leitmotifs:**
- **Maestro Primo:** Wagner's "Ride of the Valkyries" authority
- **Maestro Allegro:** Mozart's lightness and hope
- **Cadenza:** Beethoven's "Moonlight Sonata" tragedy
- **The Composer:** Mathematical patterns becoming music

## Voice Direction

**Maestro Primo:** Authoritative, measured, operatic bass
**Maestro Allegro:** Warm, encouraging, tenor
**Cadenza:** Can't speak; violin speaks for her
**The Composer:** Ethereal, mechanical becoming organic

## Cinematic Moments

**Session 3 - The Wrong Note:** Reality glitches in slow motion, everything inverts
**Session 13 - Truth Revealed:** Montage of kingdom's history
**Session 19 - Final Battle:** Musical duel where notes manifest as attacks
**Session 21 - First Free Performance:** Kingdom transforms, citizens react
