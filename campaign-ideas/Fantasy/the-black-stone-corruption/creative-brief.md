# Creative Brief: The Black Stone Corruption

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Dark Fantasy Realism (Black Desert-inspired)

Inspired by Black Desert Online's gorgeous but dark aesthetic—a style blending realistic character models, beautiful landscapes, and creeping corruption. This style captures both the economic prosperity and the dark horror lurking beneath.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Realistic Beauty:** Characters and environments are highly detailed and realistic.
*   **Corruption Contrast:** Show pristine beauty being corrupted by dark, crystalline growth.
*   **Economic Prosperity vs Dark Horror:** Trade cities vs rift-torn wastelands.

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Dark fantasy, economic empire, corruption, realistic beauty, ambition-costs-humanity.
*   **Feeling:** The tension between building prosperity and the creeping cost of dark power.

**2. Line Work & Form:**
*   **Style:** Highly detailed, realistic rendering with emphasis on texture.
*   **Forms:** Medieval-inspired architecture, realistic armor and weapons, Black Stone crystalline corruption.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A warrior in realistic medieval armor with Black Stone-enhanced sword showing dark crystalline growth along the blade."

**3. Color Palette:**
*   **Style:** Rich, earthy realism for uncorrupted areas; dark purples and blacks for corruption.
*   **Uncorrupted:** Warm browns, greens, golden sunlight, vibrant markets.
*   **Corrupted:** Sickly purples, deep blacks, unnatural dark crystal growth.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...a color palette of warm medieval browns and golds for the trade city, with creeping black-purple corruption visible at the edges."

**4. Character Design:**
*   **Style:** Highly realistic character models with detailed costumes.
*   **Progression:** Show visual progression of corruption—starting normal, gradually showing dark veins, crystalline growths.
*   **Classes:** Variety of roles—warriors, rangers, sorceresses, each with distinct aesthetic.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A female ranger in realistic fantasy clothing, with subtle black veins visible on exposed skin, eyes showing hints of purple glow, holding a Black Stone-enhanced bow."

**5. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Style:** Beautiful realistic medieval fantasy cities and trade routes, contrasted with corrupted rift zones.
*   **Cities:** Bustling markets, worker NPCs, node connections visible.
*   **Rifts:** Reality tears showing dark dimension, monster emergence, corruption spreading.
*   **Prompt Example:** "The trade city of Heidel, realistic medieval architecture with bustling markets and worker NPCs, with an ominous dark rift visible in the sky above."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Style:** Realistic lighting with dramatic corruption glow effects.
*   **Natural:** Warm sunlight, realistic shadows, vibrant market lighting.
*   **Corruption:** Unnatural purple-black glow from Black Stones and rifts.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...dramatically lit by warm afternoon sunlight, contrasting against the ominous purple glow emanating from a dark rift opening in the distance."

**7. Composition:**
*   **Style:** Emphasize the contrast between prosperity and corruption.
*   **Techniques:** Frame beautiful scenes with corruption encroaching at edges. Show before/after comparisons.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A split composition showing a prosperous trade route on the left with workers and merchants, and the same route corrupted on the right with dark rifts and monsters."

## Voice

A smooth, slightly Middle-Eastern or Mediterranean-accented voice suggesting exotic trade routes. The tone balances merchant enthusiasm with growing unease about corruption. The emotion shifts from optimistic ambition to creeping dread. Audio quality is warm but gradually gains echoing distortion suggesting corruption. Role: A merchant who has built an empire but begins questioning the cost.
