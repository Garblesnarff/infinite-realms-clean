# The Eternal Feast - Creative Brief

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Restaurant to Another World meets Doctor Who, cozy impossible space
- **Art Style**: Normal restaurant interior opening onto infinite vistas
- **Tone**: Warm hospitality in context of cosmic stakes

## Color Palettes
- **Main Restaurant**: Warm woods, golden lighting, inviting earth tones
- **Each Dimension**: Distinct color identity
- **Fire Dimension**: Reds, oranges, volcanic
- **Ice Dimension**: Blues, whites, crystalline
- **Dream Dimension**: Shifting pastels and impossible colors
- **The Void**: Absolute black with hints of cosmic hunger
- **Finale**: All dimensions' colors bleeding together

## Music
- Main Theme: Cozy restaurant ambiance with mysterious undertones
- Service Rush: Coordinated chaos, kitchen symphony
- Dimensional Travel: Reality-bending transition sounds
- Customer Themes: Each major customer has leitmotif
- The Hunger: Bass so deep it's felt not heard
- Finale Feast: Orchestral building to crescendo
- Credits: Warm, satisfied, infinite possibility

## Voice Direction
- **Remy**: Professional manager, hiding deeper knowledge, mysterious calm
- **Balthazar**: Passionate chef, explosive temper, Gordon Ramsay as fire elemental
- **Whisper**: Ethereal, echoing, speaks in riddles and truths
- **Dishwasher Prime**: Cheerful, innocent seeming, occasional ancient wisdom
- **Lord Diabolo**: Surprisingly polite, well-mannered demon
- **The Hunger**: Thousand voices asking to be fed, cosmic loneliness
- **Previous Owner (flashbacks)**: Warm, wise, tired but hopeful

## Cinematic Moments
- First doorway opening to reveal restaurant
- Cooking dish that bridges dimensions
- Customer from party member's homeworld
- Kitchen on fire (literally and figuratively)
- Millennial Feast preparation montage
- Confronting the Hunger face-to-face
- Feeding the Hunger the perfect meal
- Epilogue: New staff arriving for first day

## Episode Highlights (Customer Showcases)
- The time traveler who's their own grandfather
- Gods negotiating peace treaty over dinner
- Death taking a break for good soup
- Dragon who just wants birthday cake
- The couple married across dimensional war
- Embodiment of Hunger trying not to eat everything
- Previous customer returning to say thank you
- Party member's deceased loved one visiting
