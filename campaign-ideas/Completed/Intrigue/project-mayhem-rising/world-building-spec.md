**Project Mayhem Rising**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In a city consumed by consumer culture, dissatisfied adventurers meet Tyler, a charismatic anarchist who leads them into underground fighting rings that evolve into Project Mayhem—a revolutionary movement to destroy financial institutions and reset society. As reality fractures, they discover Tyler might be a shared delusion, and they must decide whether to stop the chaos they created or let it burn the world.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the rise of consumer culture in the city and how it replaced meaning with material goods.
    *   Write the history of support groups as substitute communities for those disconnected from real relationships.
    *   Describe the formation of the first fight club and how it spread through word of mouth.
    *   Explain Tyler Durden's philosophy of personal destruction leading to enlightenment.
    *   Detail Project Mayhem's cell structure and how it evolved from pranks to terrorism.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Project Mayhem** (Major): Anarchist organization structured in cells, dedicated to destroying consumer culture.
    *   **The Space Monkeys** (Major): Faceless foot soldiers of Project Mayhem, identity-less followers.
    *   **Corporate Structure** (Minor): The establishment—banks, credit companies, media—target of the revolution.
    *   **Support Group Network** (Minor): Various groups where the disconnected seek meaning.
    *   **Fight Club Chapters** (Minor): Underground fighting rings in various city locations.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Tyler Durden (revolutionary leader/shared delusion), Marla Singer (chaos anchor), The Detective (investigator), Robert Paulson (martyr).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Space Monkey" (interchangeable Project Mayhem member), "Support Group Member" (seeking connection), "Corporate Drone" (the enslaved), "Fight Club Regular" (seeking authenticity through violence).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Paper Street House:** Dilapidated Victorian mansion, Project Mayhem headquarters, constantly deteriorating.
*   **Lou's Tavern Basement:** Original fight club location, grimy concrete, bloodstained floor.
    *   **Corporate Towers:** Glass and steel skyscrapers targeted for destruction, filled with debt records.
*   **The Soap Factory:** Tyler's legitimate business, using stolen liposuction fat for high-end products.
*   **Support Group Church:** Where the disconnected gather to share manufactured suffering.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party joins fight club → **THEN:** Access to underground network, physical scars accumulate, feel authentically alive.
*   **IF:** Tyler's influence grows → **THEN:** Lost time episodes increase, party commits actions they don't remember.
*   **IF:** Project Mayhem escalates → **THEN:** From pranks to vandalism to terrorism, no turning back.
*   **IF:** Truth about Tyler revealed → **THEN:** Identity crisis, space monkeys continue operations anyway, must stop own creation.
*   **IF:** Financial buildings destroyed → **THEN:** Debt records erased, society reset, chaos or liberation.
