# Brick by Brick - Creative Brief

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Style**: Blocky LEGO realism meets existential crisis
- **Colors**: Primary colors, high saturation, plastic sheen
- **Scale**: Grid-based movement, everything modular
- **Lighting**: Bright, optimistic colors hiding dark themes

## Character Design
- Classic minifigure proportions (cylindrical heads, claw hands)
- Customization through parts (different heads, torsos, legs)
- Faction identification through color schemes
- Wear shown as scratches on plastic, fading print

## Environment
- Everything built from LEGO bricks
- Visible studs, connection points, modularity
- Buildings mid-construction or mid-destruction
- Parts Bin: Chaos of mixed pieces, nightmare realm

## Music Direction
- Building sounds as percussion
- Plastic clicking rhythm
- Optimistic adventure theme with underlying tension
- Rebuild: Reality-warping audio effects

## Voice Acting
- Minifigures: Enthusiastic but existentially aware
- Master Builder Gray: Wise, weary, LEGO commercials voice
- Commander Set: Rigid, instruction-manual precise
- Mix Master: Chaotic,enthusiastic, multiple accents

## Marketing Tagline
"Everything is awesome. Until it's disassembled."
