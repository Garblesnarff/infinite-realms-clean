# Blood of the Einherjar - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Norse Mythology Meets Historical Viking Age

**Visual:** Viking Age realism (historical clothing, weapons, ships) meets mythological grandeur (giants, dragons, divine manifestations).

**Palettes:**
- **Midgard:** Ice blue, forest green, iron gray, blood red, snow white
- **Valhalla:** Gold, amber (mead), weapon-silver, fire-warm
- **Jotunheim:** Frozen white-blue, stone gray, ancient ice
- **Ragnarok:** Fire orange, ash black, blood red, destruction

**Music:** Scandinavian folk (tagelharpa, lyre, drums) meets epic metal. Wardruna-style throat singing. Battle drums.

**Character Themes:**
- **Odin:** Ravens cawing, mysterious, one note missing (one eye)
- **Thor:** Thunder and hammer percussion, heroic brass
- **Loki:** Shapeshifting unpredictable music, trickster motifs

**Voices:**
- **Odin:** Ian McShane—wise, dangerous, knowingly doomed
- **Thor:** Chris Hemsworth meets Brian Blessed—booming, heroic
- **Loki:** Tom Hiddleston—charming, dangerous, chaos
- **Norns:** Three women speaking in unison, fate-voice

**Cinematic Moments:**
- Valkyries descending to choose the slain
- Thor battling Jormungandr (god vs. world-serpent)
- Heimdall blowing Gjallarhorn
- Fimbulwinter descending
- Vigrid plain battle—gods dying
- World tree burning
