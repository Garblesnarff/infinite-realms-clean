# Campaign Bible: Belly of the Beast

## 1. DEEP LORE & HISTORY
[TAG: LORE_CORE]

### Origin Myth: The Primordial Hunger
In the beginning, there was only the **Primordial Hunger**, a colossal, living maw that consumed all of existence. It did not create the universe; it devoured it, meticulously grinding every star, every planet, every atom into a single, perfect **Cosmic Chyme**. The world is not a planet; it is a single, intricate morsel in a vast, cosmic digestive system, constantly churning, constantly dissolving according to the Hunger's grand design. The "Belly of the Beast" is not just a metaphor; it is a literal place, a living, breathing ecosystem within the Primordial Hunger, where the remnants of devoured worlds struggle to survive, constantly being broken down and re-formed. The conflict arises because the Primordial Hunger has grown bored, and its attention is drifting, causing its digestive processes to fray, and the cosmic chyme to unravel into chaos.

### Timeline of Events
1.  **-10,000 Y (The First Bite):** The Primordial Hunger devours the universe, creating the Cosmic Chyme.
2.  **-8000 Y (The Age of Digestion):** The first sentient beings emerge, embodying archetypal roles: The Prey, The Predator, The Parasite, The Symbiote.
3.  **-6000 Y (The Grand Digestion):** The Belly of the Beast is formed, a living ecosystem within the Primordial Hunger, where remnants of devoured worlds struggle to survive.
4.  **-4000 Y (The Indigestion Plague):** The Primordial Hunger begins to fray, causing "indigestion" to appear in reality. Worlds crumble, civilizations collapse, and logic breaks down.
5.  **-2000 Y (The Master Predator Protocol):** The Belly of the Beast develops a universal "Master Predator" template, forcing certain individuals into archetypal roles to maintain digestive integrity.
6.  **-1000 Y (The Unreliable Digestion Incident):** A rogue Predator attempts to rewrite the Cosmic Chyme, causing history to become fluid and contradictory.
7.  **-500 Y (The Fourth Wall Breach):** A powerful Predator, frustrated with their role, wishes to "speak to the devourer." They briefly glimpse the fading Cosmic Chyme, driving them mad.
8.  **-100 Y (The Fading Morsels):** The Cosmic Chyme's designs grow weaker, causing prey to lose their motivation, predators to lose their drive, and reality to unravel into meaningless fragments.
9.  **-10 Y (The Missing Nutrient):** A vital nutrient, the **Essence of Sustenance**, is stolen from the Belly of the Beast, causing all conflicts to become endless.
10. **Present Day:** The party are new inhabitants of the Belly of the Beast, each with their own unique connection to survival. They are caught in a crisis as the Cosmic Chyme fades, and the world threatens to unravel into meaningless fragments.

### The "Why": The Meaning of Consumption
The central conflict is the struggle between **Consumption and Preservation**. The Primordial Hunger seeks to consume all of existence, reducing it to a single, undifferentiated mass. But the Belly of the Beast, in its diversity, represents the richness and complexity of existence. The inhabitants of the Belly of the Beast fight to preserve their existence, to remind the world of its purpose, and to prevent reality from unraveling into meaningless fragments. The party's struggle is not just to become Master Survivors, but to defend the very concept of individuality, to prove that diversity is strength, and to prevent the universe from becoming utterly, irrevocably consumed by hunger.

## 2. FACTIONS
[TAG: FACTION_DATA]

### 1. The Gastric Guardians
**Type:** Scholarly Institution / Digestive Mages
**Leader:** The Grand Digestor, a legendary figure who has memorized the entire Cosmic Chyme.
**Asset:** **The Chyme Archive:** A magical archive that stores the designs of every known devoured world in existence.
**Rivals:** The Nihilists, The Indigestion Patrol.

### 2. The Nihilists
**Type:** Anti-Consumption Cult / Meaningless Zealots
**Leader:** The Blank Slate, a being whose presence drains all meaning from its surroundings.
**Asset:** **Anti-Consumption Auras:** They can create zones where all actions become meaningless, and all structures are forgotten.
**Rivals:** The Gastric Guardians.

### 3. The Indigestion Patrol
**Type:** Janitorial Service / Reality Repairmen
**Leader:** The Continuity Cop, a being who can instantly spot inconsistencies.
**Asset:** **Retcon Hammers:** Hammers that can "un-happen" minor events, fixing indigestion.
**Rivals:** The Gastric Guardians (who sometimes create indigestion).

### 4. The Archetype Predators Guild
**Type:** Professional Performers / Role-Players
**Leader:** The Tragic Predator, a man doomed to always fail.
**Asset:** **Method Hunting:** They can perfectly embody any archetype, gaining temporary abilities associated with that role.
**Rivals:** The Fourth Wall Breakers (who expose their "hunting").

### 5. The Fourth Wall Breakers
**Type:** Anarchist Cult / Meta-Aware Rebels
**Leader:** The Narrator, a disembodied voice that speaks directly to the party.
**Asset:** **Audience Awareness:** They know they are characters in a story and can sometimes appeal directly to the "audience" (the players) for help.
**Rivals:** The Gastric Guardians (who try to "edit" them out of existence).

### 6. The Unreliable Chyme
**Type:** Rogue Predators / Reality Distorters
**Leader:** The Liar, a predator whose every word changes reality.
**Asset:** **Subjective Truth:** Their words can temporarily alter the perception of reality for those who hear them.
**Rivals:** The Indigestion Patrol.

### 7. The Foreshadowing Fanatics
**Type:** Prophetic Cult / Obsessive Predictors
**Leader:** The Seer of the Coming Collapse, a woman who speaks only in ominous warnings.
**Asset:** **Pre-Cognitive Visions:** They can see glimpses of future indigestion, allowing them to prepare.
**Rivals:** The Deus Ex Machina Collective (who often invalidate their predictions).

### 8. The Character Development Committee
**Type:** Bureaucratic Therapists / Emotional Manipulators
**Leader:** The Therapist, a being who forces characters to confront their flaws.
**Asset:** **Emotional Arcs:** They can subtly manipulate a character's emotions to force them into a specific emotional arc.
**Rivals:** The Fourth Wall Breakers (who resist their manipulation).

### 9. The Deus Ex Machina Collective
**Type:** Mechanical Cult / Accidental Saviors
**Leader:** The Great Crane, a massive, sentient mechanical arm.
**Asset:** **Convenient Coincidence:** They can subtly manipulate probability to create highly improbable, but narratively convenient, events.
**Rivals:** The Wish-Weavers (who see them as crude).

### 10. The Primordial Hunger's Agents
**Type:** Unknowing Servants / Plot Devices
**Leader:** None (They are simply tools of the Hunger).
**Asset:** **Digestive Immunity:** They are immune to minor reality alterations, as they are essential to the digestion.
**Rivals:** Anyone who tries to deviate from the chyme.

## 8. MECHANICS
[TAG: RULES_CUSTOM]

### Subsystem: Digestive Cohesion
*   **Cohesion Score (0-10):** Represents the stability of the Cosmic Chyme. Starts at 5.
*   **Gaining Cohesion:** Following digestive tropes, resolving design points, making digestively satisfying choices.
*   **Losing Cohesion:** Breaking digestive tropes, creating indigestion, making illogical or unexpected choices.
*   **Effects:**
    *   *High Cohesion:* Reality is stable, predictable. GNEA agents are less active.
    *   *Low Cohesion:* Reality is unstable, unpredictable. Indigestion appears, Architectural Golems spawn.
    *   *0 Cohesion:* The Cosmic Chyme collapses, leading to a chaotic, nonsensical reality.

### Subsystem: Archetype Points
*   **Archetype Points:** Players earn Archetype Points by leaning into specific archetypes (e.g., "Prey," "Predator," "Parasite").
*   **Spending Points:** These points can be spent to gain temporary abilities associated with that archetype (e.g., a "Prey" can spend a point to gain advantage on a saving throw against being consumed).
*   **Breaking Archetype:** Players can choose to defy their archetype, but doing so costs Archetype Points and may lead to a loss of Digestive Cohesion.

## 9. SESSION GUIDE
[TAG: DM_GUIDE]

*   **Session 1: The Enrollment.** The party enrolls in the Gastric Guardians. They meet the Grand Digestor and their fellow students. *Social:* A digestive challenge to prove their worth.
*   **Session 2: The Fading Designs.** The Cosmic Chyme begins to fray, causing prey to lose their motivation. The party investigates the Guild, suspecting the Nihilists. *Heist:* Infiltrate the Chyme Archive.
*   **Session 3: The Missing Nutrient.** A vital nutrient, the Essence of Sustenance, is stolen from the Belly of the Beast. *Combat:* Rescue an enslaved Archetype Predator.
*   **Session 4: The Indigestion Patrol.** The party discovers the Indigestion Patrol is spreading meaninglessness. *Exploration:* Navigate a colorless forest.
*   **Session 5: The Forgetting's Threat.** The Forgetting Collective attempts to erase all memories from existence. *Social:* A high-stakes emotional manipulation.
*   **Session 6: The Archetype Predators' Dilemma.** The party must convince a group of Archetype Predators to break character and help them. *Heist:* A chase through the Guild Library.
*   **Session 7: The Fourth Wall Breach.** The party seeks out the Fourth Wall Breakers for guidance on how to communicate with the Primordial Hunger. *Combat:* A battle against digestive purists.
*   **Session 8: The Unreliable Chyme's Truth.** The party must confront the Liar, whose words are changing reality. *Puzzle:* A balance-restoring challenge.
*   **Session 9: The Foreshadowing Fanatic's Warning.** The Seer of the Coming Collapse reveals a cryptic prophecy about the Cosmic Chyme's fate. *Social:* A philosophical discussion about meaning.
*   **Session 10: The Character Development Committee's Intervention.** The Therapist attempts to force the party into a predetermined emotional arc. *Boss Fight:* A battle against the Blank Slate.
*   **Session 11: The Nihilists' Gambit.** The Blank Page and his cultists attempt to erase all meaning from existence. *Combat:* A massive battle against the Nihilists.
*   **Session 12: The Cosmic Chyme's Rebirth.** The party must rewrite the missing section, restoring meaning to existence and shaping the future of the Cosmic Chyme. *Climax:* The party must awaken the Primordial Hunger from its boredom, restoring digestion to the Ash Wastes.
