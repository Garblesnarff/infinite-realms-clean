### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**Wrath of Achilles**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Warriors in the legendary Trojan War must choose between glory through death or obscurity through survival, participating in epic siege sparked by Helen's theft. Through divine intervention and hero duels, they witness whether fame justifies ten years of bloodshed.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Trojan War's cause—Paris' judgment, Helen's abduction, Greek unity.
    *   Write Achilles' prophecy—stay and die gloriously, or leave and live anonymously.
    *   Describe the gods' involvement—why they take sides in mortal war.
    *   Explain Bronze Age warfare—chariots, phalanx, hero duels.
    *   Chronicle key events: Achilles' wrath, Patroclus' death, Hector's fall, Trojan Horse.
    *   Detail Troy's defenses and why siege lasts ten years.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** Greek Alliance (Major), Trojan Defenders (Major), Olympian Gods (Major, divided), Myrmidons (Minor), Trojan Allies (Minor)

**4. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Achilles refuses to fight → **THEN:** Trojans push Greeks to ships.
*   **IF:** Patroclus dies → **THEN:** Achilles' rage returns him to battle.
*   **IF:** Hector dies → **THEN:** Troy's greatest defender gone, morale breaks.
*   **IF:** Trojan Horse accepted → **THEN:** City falls through deception.
*   **IF:** Party chooses glory → **THEN:** Death but eternal fame.
