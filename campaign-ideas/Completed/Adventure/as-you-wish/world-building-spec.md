### **World-Building Specification Brief: As You Wish**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party must rescue their beloved companion from a scheming prince's political marriage plot while navigating a fairy tale world where true love, revenge, and miracles are real forces. What begins as a simple rescue becomes an epic tale testing whether love and loyalty can triumph over power and treachery.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the history of the Dread Pirate Roberts and how the legacy is passed between successors
    *   Explain the political tensions between Florin and Guilder that the Prince exploits
    *   Describe the legend of the Fire Swamp and why it's considered impassable
    *   Write about the nature of "mostly dead" versus "all dead" and how miracles work
    *   Detail the code of honor among master swordsmen and the significance of revenge quests
    *   Explain the Storyteller's relationship to the worldâ€”is it a dream, a tale being told, or something else?

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:**
    *   **The Royal Court of Florin** (Major) - Prince Humperdinck's faction, goal: start war with Guilder for glory
    *   **True Love's Champions** (Major) - The party and allies, goal: rescue and expose the Prince's plot
    *   **The Thieves' Guild** (Minor) - Vizzini's former associates, neutral mercenaries
    *   **The Brute Squad** (Minor) - Prince's enforcement arm, loyal through fear
    *   **The Guild of Master Swordsmen** (Minor) - Honorable duelists following ancient codes
    *   **Guilder's Court** (Background) - Neighboring kingdom unaware of being framed

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Prince Humperdinck, Count Rugen, Inigo Montoya, Fezzik, Miracle Max, The Storyteller
    *   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Royal Guard Captain," "Brute Squad Enforcer," "Miracle Max's Wife Valerie," "Albino Torturer," "Castle Servant with Secret Knowledge," "Florin Noble," "Guilder Ambassador"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Kingdom of Florin** - Fairy tale kingdom with class divisions, beautiful but hiding darkness
*   **The Cliffs of Insanity** - Impossible vertical cliff face testing physical limits
*   **The Fire Swamp** - Three terrors: flame spurts, lightning sand, rodents of unusual size
*   **The Pit of Despair** - Underground torture chamber/laboratory beneath an abandoned tree
*   **Castle Florin** - Grand palace with secret passages, multiple defensive layers
*   Each location has theatrical combat opportunities, environmental hazards, and dramatic reveals

**6. Causality Chains**
*   IF the party fails to stop the wedding THEN war begins between Florin and Guilder
*   IF Inigo finds Count Rugen THEN his revenge quest culminates in a dramatic duel
*   IF a character dies with true love's motivation THEN Miracle Max can resurrect them
*   IF the party gains Fezzik's loyalty THEN they have incredible strength for the castle assault
*   IF the Prince's plot is exposed publicly THEN the war can be prevented and he faces justice
*   IF the party embraces the theatrical nature of the world THEN the Storyteller provides subtle aid
