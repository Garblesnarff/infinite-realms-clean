### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

**Court of Redemption**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** A delinquent with a violent reputation joins the declining Shohoku High basketball team to impress someone they admire, despite never having played competitively. Possessing natural athletic genius but lacking discipline, they must learn teamwork and commitment. Alongside a talented but troubled team—including hot-headed ace, gentle giant, and regretful captain—they transform from laughingstock to championship contenders. Their journey from knowing nothing to executing perfect slam dunks becomes a path of personal redemption.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail Shohoku High's basketball history—former glory now faded into mediocrity.
    *   Write the protagonist's delinquent past and 50 middle school fight victories.
    *   Describe the Inter-High tournament structure and National Championship qualification.
    *   Explain Sannoh Kogyo's dynasty—undefeated champions representing the insurmountable peak.
    *   Detail the basketball positions and fundamental skills required for each role.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Shohoku High** (Major): Declining program with dedicated captain, home of misfits and redemption seekers.
    *   **Ryonan High** (Major): Tactical powerhouse with veteran coach and talented ace Sendoh.
    *   **Kainan High** (Major): Elite program with 17 straight National appearances, perfect fundamentals.
    *   **Sannoh Kogyo** (Major): Undefeated champions, overwhelming favorites, the final boss of high school basketball.
    *   **Other Regional Schools** (Minor): Various schools with unique playstyles and star players.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Takenori Akagi (captain/center), Kaede Rukawa (ace genius), Hisashi Mitsui (prodigal shooter), Akira Sendoh (complete player rival).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Team Captain" (leadership roles), "Star Player" (team aces), "Tactical Coach" (strategic masterminds), "Veteran Player" (experienced competitors), "Bench Player" (supporting sixth man).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Shohoku High Gymnasium:** Traditional school gym with wooden floor, retractable bleachers, worn equipment showing age, championship photos from past glory days.
*   **Inter-High Arena:** Regional tournament venue with multiple courts, elevated spectator seating, tournament bracket boards, team warm-up areas.
*   **National Championship Arena:** Tokyo's premier venue, massive capacity, professional broadcasting equipment, represents the peak of high school basketball.
*   **Street Courts:** Outdoor concrete courts with chain nets where protagonist first learned to dunk, represents their roots.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Protagonist masters fundamentals → **THEN:** Natural athleticism combines with technique, becomes dangerous player.
*   **IF:** Team develops chemistry → **THEN:** Unlock combination plays, cover each other's weaknesses, become greater than sum of parts.
*   **IF:** Win Inter-High qualifiers → **THEN:** Advance to Nationals, face elite competition, seniors' dreams alive.
*   **IF:** Face Sannoh Kogyo → **THEN:** Must play perfect game, every mistake is costly, ultimate test of growth.
*   **IF:** Protagonist fouls out → **THEN:** Team must win without their energy, proves they've developed beyond one player.
