**The Academy of Three Lions**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party joins Garreg Mach Monastery's Officer Academy, training future leaders of three rival nations (Empire, Kingdom, Alliance). They choose which house to lead, navigating school life while uncovering conspiracy involving the Church and ancient dragon magic. A five-year time skip transforms their students into war leaders, forcing the party to fight former friends in a continent-spanning war where their choices determine Fodlan's future.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the War of Heroes where Saint Seiros defeated Nemesis the Liberation King 1000 years ago.
    *   Write the history of how Fodlan split into three nations after the Empire's power waned.
    *   Describe the Crest system—bloodlines granting power—and how it dominates society.
    *   Explain the truth: Crests come from dragon blood; Relics are made from dragon bones; the Church hides genocide.
    *   Detail the ancient Agarthan civilization, destroyed by dragons, now operating in shadows as "Those Who Slither in the Dark."

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Adrestian Empire** (Major): Southern power seeking to reunify Fodlan through military might and meritocracy.
    *   **Holy Kingdom of Faerghus** (Major): Northern nation valuing chivalry and tradition, loyal to Church.
    *   **Leicester Alliance** (Major): Eastern federation of merchant houses seeking independence through trade.
    *   **Church of Seiros** (Major): Religious organization controlling Garreg Mach, hiding ancient secrets.
    *   **Those Who Slither in the Dark** (Major): Shadow organization manipulating all sides, seeking dragon extinction.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Edelgard von Hresvelg (Emperor/Flame Emperor), Dimitri Alexandre Blaiddyd (King turned vengeful), Claude von Riegan (Alliance leader/schemer), Lady Rhea (Archbishop/ancient dragon), Thales (Agarthan mastermind).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "House Student" (future generals), "Knight of Seiros" (monastery defenders), "Death Knight" (mysterious dark cavalier), "Agarthan Mage" (shadow operatives), "Demonic Beast" (transformed humans).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Garreg Mach Monastery:** Central monastery with cathedral (Rhea's throne), classrooms, training grounds, knight's hall, market, dormitories, cemetery, underground Holy Tomb with goddess's resting place.
*   **Enbarr:** Imperial capital with massive palace, opera house, military academies, will be site of final battle in some paths.
*   **Fhirdiad:** Kingdom capital with castle, cold climate, falls into civil war and madness.
*   **Shambhala:** Hidden underground Agarthan city with ancient technology, missile silos, and superweapons.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party chooses Black Eagles → **THEN:** Can side with Edelgard's revolution or oppose it; determines ally/enemy factions.
*   **IF:** Party chooses Blue Lions → **THEN:** Must help Dimitri recover from madness or watch him become monster.
*   **IF:** Party chooses Golden Deer → **THEN:** Focus on uniting nations and exposing Church secrets.
*   **IF:** Edelgard's identity is revealed early → **THEN:** Can recruit her to Church path or cement her as enemy.
*   **IF:** Students die in battle (Classic Mode) → **THEN:** Permanently lost, affects morale and available units.
*   **IF:** Support levels maximized → **THEN:** Students unlock paired endings and special dialogue.
*   **IF:** Church is defeated → **THEN:** Crest system can be abolished; dragons lose power over Fodlan.
*   **IF:** Edelgard is defeated → **THEN:** Empire collapses; traditional power structures remain or democracy emerges.
