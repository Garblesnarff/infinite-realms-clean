### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**The Grandmaster: Legacy of Schools**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** During the twilight of Republican China, the party are martial arts masters navigating the unification of Northern and Southern traditions, the romance between Ip Man and Gong Er that cannot be, Ma San's betrayal and murder of his master, the Japanese invasion, and ultimately the question of what legacy they leave when the old martial arts world ends.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the differences between Northern (formal, acrobatic) and Southern (practical, close-quarters) martial arts.
    *   Write about the political situation in 1930s-1950s China—warlords, invasion, civil war, exile.
    *   Describe the tradition of grandmasters and the significance of unifying schools.
    *   Explain the philosophy of various schools: Wing Chun, Baguazhang, Xingyi, etc.
    *   Detail the code of honor that prevents Gong Er from romance, marriage, or teaching after her father's death.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:**
    *   **Northern Schools** - Goal: Preserve traditional forms and philosophical depth.
    *   **Southern Schools** - Goal: Maintain practical effectiveness and adaptability.
    *   **Collaborators (Ma San's faction)** - Goal: Survive by serving occupiers.
    *   **The Resistance** - Goal: Preserve Chinese culture through the war.
    *   **Hong Kong Exiles** - Goal: Keep martial arts alive in the modern world.

**4. NPC Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Ip Man, Gong Er, Gong Yutian, Ma San, The Razor.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Northern master (various styles)," "Southern master (various styles)," "Japanese officer," "Hong Kong student," "War refugee."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Golden Pavilion (1930s):**
        *   Landmarks: Grand dining hall, private challenge rooms, rooftop garden.
        *   Encounters: Philosophical debates, formal challenges, political maneuvering.
    *   **The Train Station (1940s):**
        *   Landmarks: Platform where final duel occurs, waiting room, steam and snow.
        *   Encounters: Gong Er vs Ma San, witnesses to the end of an era.
    *   **Hong Kong Rooftop Schools (1950s):**
        *   Landmarks: Cramped training spaces, student quarters, view of modern city below.
        *   Encounters: Teaching new generation, adapting techniques, final gatherings.

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** The party successfully unifies Northern and Southern schools → Legacy of cooperation continues.
*   **IF:** Gong Er achieves revenge but dies → Bittersweet victory, her techniques die with her.
*   **IF:** The party successfully teaches the next generation → Martial arts survive into modernity.
*   **IF:** Ma San is defeated with honor → His students can be redeemed.
*   **IF:** The party chooses exile over collaboration → They survive but lose their homeland.
*   **IF:** Ip Man and Gong Er's romance is acknowledged before her death → Emotional closure, she dies at peace.
