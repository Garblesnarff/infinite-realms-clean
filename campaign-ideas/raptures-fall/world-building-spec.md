**Rapture's Fall**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party crashes and discovers Rapture—an underwater city built on objectivist principles but fallen to ADAM-addicted chaos. Guided by Atlas using "Would you kindly," they fight through Splicers and Big Daddies. They discover they're mind-controlled sleeper agents created by Fontaine. They must break conditioning and choose their own path: save the Little Sisters and escape, or harvest them and rule Rapture.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail Andrew Ryan's philosophy—objectivism, rejection of government/religion, "No Gods or Kings."
    *   Write Rapture's construction in 1946—a city for "great men" to work free of parasites.
    *   Describe the discovery of ADAM—genetic modification substance from sea slugs.
    *   Explain the Rapture Civil War—Frank Fontaine vs. Andrew Ryan, leading to collapse.
    *   Detail the creation of Little Sisters and Big Daddies to harvest ADAM.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Ryan Industries** (Major): Andrew Ryan's corporate empire, now oppressive security force.
    *   **Fontaine Futuristics** (Major): Crime empire disguised as genetics company, creator of Plasmids.
    *   **The Splicers** (Major): ADAM-addicted citizens, mutated and insane, various types.
    *   **The Little Sisters & Big Daddies** (Minor): ADAM harvesting operation, tragic figures.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Andrew Ryan (tyrant), Frank Fontaine/Atlas (manipulator), Dr. Tenenbaum (redeemed scientist), Sander Cohen (mad artist).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Splicer" (various types—Thuggish, Leadhead, Spider, Houdini), "Big Daddy" (Bouncer, Rosie, Rumbler), "Little Sister" (ADAM collector), "Security Bot."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Welcome Center:** Bathysphere arrival, Ryan's statue and speech, art deco magnificence.
*   **Medical Pavilion:** Steinman's surgical theater, Plasmid introduction, medical horror.
*   **Fort Frolic:** Sander Cohen's entertainment district, frozen in artistic nightmare.
*   **Hephaestus:** Industrial core with geothermal power, Ryan's workshop.
*   **Ryan's Office:** Golden sanctum, site of the "Would you kindly" twist.
*   **Point Prometheus:** Fontaine's headquarters, ADAM processing, final battle.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party saves Little Sisters → **THEN:** Gain less ADAM, unlock good ending, Tenenbaum provides gifts, Sisters help in finale.
*   **IF:** Party harvests Little Sisters → **THEN:** Gain more ADAM/power, unlock evil ending, alone in finale.
*   **IF:** Party reaches Ryan → **THEN:** "Would you kindly" twist revealed, forced to kill him, loss of agency.
*   **IF:** Party breaks conditioning → **THEN:** Can resist Fontaine's commands, reclaim free will.
*   **IF:** Party defeats Fontaine → **THEN:** Escape Rapture (good) or rule it (evil), prove free will exists.
