### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**The Invasion Within**

**1. Core Concept:** Parasitic aliens infiltrate by consuming human brains. Party members are partial infectionsâ€”symbiotic rather than replaced. Must hunt full parasites while hiding their own infections, questioning what defines humanity when you're part alien.

**2. Lore Primer:** Parasites arrived as spores, target brainstem, perfect mimicry of hosts, lifecycle requires human consumption, some develop consciousness beyond instinct, origin unknown (evolution? alien invasion? Earth's immune response to humanity?).

**3. Factions:**
*   **Symbiotic Hosts (Players):** Hybrid human-parasites seeking to protect humanity.
*   **Full Parasites:** Range from mindless predators to philosophical questioners.
*   **Human Society:** Unaware of invasion; would kill infected if discovered.
*   **Parasite Extremists:** Believe humanity must be culled.
*   **Human Extremists:** Hunt all infected; no mercy.

**4. NPCs:**
*   **Tier 1:** Player Symbiotes (Migi, etc.), Reiko (philosopher parasite), Gotou (composite predator), Hirokawa (human collaborator), Uragami (human monster).
*   **Tier 2-3:** "Full Parasite," "Suspected Infected," "Human Victim," "Extremist Hunter."

**5. Locations:** Urban neighborhoods, schools, feeding grounds (isolated areas), parasite gathering sites, extremist hideouts, the infection origin site.

**6. Triggers:**
*   **IF symbiote starves:** Weakens; may try to take control.
*   **IF party exposed:** Hunted by both humans and parasites.
*   **IF party kills humanely:** Humanity tracker increases.
*   **IF party becomes predatory:** Humanity tracker decreases; symbiote relationship strengthens.
*   **IF encounter parasite seeking peace:** Coexistence path opens.
