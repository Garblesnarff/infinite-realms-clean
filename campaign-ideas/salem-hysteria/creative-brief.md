# Creative Brief: Salem Hysteria

## Art Style: Puritan Horror (The Crucible meets The VVitch)

Inspired by the paranoid intensity of *The Crucible* and the folk horror of *The VVitch*. This style captures Puritan austerity, communal paranoia, and the horror of witch hunt hysteria.

### Core Principles:
*   **Emphasize Claustrophobia:** Small village, everyone watching everyone, no escape.
*   **Puritan Aesthetic:** Black clothing, stern faces, religious severity, communal judgment.
*   **Hysteria Visualization:** Show the escalating madness through body language and expressions.

### Color Palette:
Puritan blacks and grays, New England autumn colors, courtroom wood browns, the stark white of accusers' fits, blood red of execution.

### Character Design:
Puritan clothing (severe, black, modest), afflicted girls showing dramatic fits, accused showing fear and dignity, judges showing certainty and coldness.

## Voice
