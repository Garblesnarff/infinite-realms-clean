**Reapers of the Veil**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party dies and becomes Soul Reapers—warriors with zanpakuto (sentient spirit swords) who guide souls and fight Hollows. They join the Thirteen Court Guard Squads. When a captain betrays Soul Society stealing godlike power, they must master shikai and bankai to prevent reality's collapse and face the truth about Soul Society's founding.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail Soul Society's creation and the truth about the Soul King.
    *   Write the history of Hollows and how souls become corrupted.
    *   Describe the creation of the Hogyoku and why it's forbidden.
    *   Explain the balance between worlds and what happens if it breaks.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** The Thirteen Court Guard Squads (Soul Society military), Arrancar Army (Hollows with Soul Reaper powers), Royal Guard (protects Soul King), Quincy Survivors (humans who destroy rather than purify Hollows).

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Head Captain, Sosuke Aizen (betrayer), Yoruichi (exile), Ten Espada, Zanpakuto Spirits.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Squad Captain," "Lieutenant," "Squad Member," "Hollow," "Arrancar."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Soul Society:** Seireitei (noble court), Rukongai (commoner afterlife), squad barracks, execution grounds.
*   **Hueco Mundo:** Endless white desert, Forest of Menos, Las Noches palace.

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Party achieves shikai → **THEN:** Unlock unique abilities, recognized as competent fighters.
*   **IF:** Party achieves bankai → **THEN:** Recognized as captain-level, can face Espada.
*   **IF:** Aizen fuses with Hogyoku → **THEN:** Becomes nearly godlike, transcends Soul Reaper/Hollow boundaries.
*   **IF:** Balance between worlds breaks → **THEN:** Emergency endgame—realities merging, must stabilize or all existence ends.
