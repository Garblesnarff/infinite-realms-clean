# Creative Brief: Blades of the Holy War

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Epic Holy War (Kingdom of Heaven meets The Message)

Inspired by the moral complexity of *Kingdom of Heaven* and the respectful portrayal of both sides in *The Message*. This style aims to capture religious fervor meeting brutal reality, the clash and unexpected commonality between cultures, and the tragedy of holy war.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Duality:** Focus on crusader and Saracen perspectives equally, showing honor and atrocity on both sides.
*   **Religious Iconography:** Show crosses and crescents, holy sites, religious conviction driving violence.
*   **Cultural Clash:** Highlight European crusaders encountering sophisticated Islamic civilization.

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Zealous, complex, brutal, honorable, tragic, faith-driven, culturally rich.
*   **Feeling:** The weight of religious conviction meeting moral complexity. Epic holy war scale with intimate human tragedy.

**2. Color Palette:**
*   **Style:** Crusader whites and reds (crosses), Islamic greens and golds, desert earth tones, Jerusalem stone colors, blood red shared by both sides.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...a Holy Land color palette: crusader whites and reds contrasting with Islamic green and gold, desert tans and Jerusalem stone, blood red staining both Christian and Muslim banners."

**3. Character Design:**
*   **Style:** Crusader knights in chainmail with red crosses, Saracen warriors in flowing armor, religious leaders from both faiths, mixed cultural elements showing complexity.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A crusader knight and Saracen warrior showing mutual respect after battle, both wounded, both bearing religious symbols, both recognizing shared humanity despite being enemies."

## Voice

A voice capturing the tragedy of religious warfare from all perspectives. Respectful of genuine faith while unflinching about violence done in God's name. The narrator speaks with the weight of someone recognizing that all sides believed they fought for the right. Audio features: church bells and calls to prayer, battle cries in multiple languages, Jerusalem's stones, the clash of faiths and cultures.
