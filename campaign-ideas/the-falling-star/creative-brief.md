# The Falling Star - Creative Brief

## Art Style
**Armageddon meets Cosmic Horror** - Emphasis on scale, astronomy, space travel. Night sky dominated by growing asteroid. Contrast between familiar world and alien space. Crystalline beauty mixed with cosmic terror.

## Color Palettes
- **Earth**: Normal colors fading to panic reds and desperate greys
- **Space**: Void black, star white, cosmic purple, nebula colors
- **Asteroid**: Crystal blue, alien silver, prismatic refractions
- **Colossus**: Reality-warping cosmic colors, titan gold, ancient power

## Music & Sound
**Main Theme**: "The Falling Star" - Orchestral with cosmic synth, countdown ticking, building to explosive crescendo. **Space**: Eerie silence punctuated by radio static, cosmic hums, alien sounds. **Impact**: Overwhelming percussion, reality-breaking sounds, apocalyptic.

## Cinematic Moments
- **Session 2**: Night sky shot, tiny dot growing larger—the asteroid
- **Session 23**: First view of Colossus inside asteroid—titan filling cavern
- **Session 39**: Asteroid exploding/deflecting, visible from planet surface
- **Victory**: World celebrating, night sky beautiful again, monument to heroes
