### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**Metaverse Ronin**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In a corporate-fractured America, the players investigate Snow Crash—a drug/virus exploiting ancient Sumerian linguistic programming. Navigating both the Metaverse (VR) and meat-space, they must stop media mogul L. Bob Rife from enslaving humanity through neurological reprogramming.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   Detail the collapse of federal government and rise of franchise-states (corporate nations).
*   Explain Metaverse creation—virtual reality social space.
*   Describe Sumerian me (mental programs) and their rediscovery by modern neurolinguistics.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Franchise-States** (corporate territories), **L. Bob Rife's Empire** (media/religion monopoly), **The Mafia** (CosaNostra Pizza), **The Raft** (refugee collective).

**4. NPC Roster**
*   Hiro Protagonist (hacker), Y.T. (courier), L. Bob Rife (villain), Juanita (linguist), Uncle Enzo (mafia don).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   The Metaverse (VR space), LA Burbclaves (corporate territories), The Raft (floating city), The Library (AI archive).

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF** party stops Snow Crash **THEN** prevent global mind control but franchise-states remain.
*   **IF** party exposed to Snow Crash **THEN** resist or become infected spreaders.
*   **IF** party destroys the Raft **THEN** thousands of refugees die—morally complex victory.
