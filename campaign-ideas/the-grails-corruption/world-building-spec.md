### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**The Grail's Corruption**

**1. Core Concept:** Seven mages summon legendary heroes to battle for the Holy Grail which grants any wish. Party fights in battle royale while discovering the Grail is corrupted and twists wishes into nightmares. Must choose between victory, ideals, and preventing catastrophe.

**2. Lore Primer:** Holy Grail Wars occur every 60 years, seven Masters chosen by Grail, each summons heroic spirit from Throne of Heroes, last Master standing wins, Grail supposedly grants any wish, actually corrupted by Angra Mainyu (all world's evils), wishes become nightmares, previous wars ended in disaster.

**3. Factions:**
*   **Seven Master-Servant Teams:** Each fighting for different wish.
*   **The Mage Association:** Oversees war from shadows.
*   **The Church:** Provides neutral overseer.
*   **The Grail/Angra Mainyu:** Corrupting force wanting to be born.

**4. NPCs:**
*   **Tier 1:** Six rival Masters (Kiritsugu, Kirei, Tokiomi, Kariya, Waver, Kayneth), their Servants (legendary heroes), Overseer, Angra Mainyu.
*   **Tier 2-3:** "Mage Assassin," "Innocent Bystander," "Master's Family," "Servant Manifestation."

**5. Locations:** Modern city, each Master's base, summoning locations, urban battlefields, Grail's chamber, Einzbern castle.

**6. Triggers:**
*   **IF Servant defeated:** Master vulnerable; must flee or use command seals.
*   **IF Master killed:** Servant fades unless finds new contract.
*   **IF command seal used:** Master-Servant relationship changes.
*   **IF Grail corruption discovered:** Party must choose path.
*   **IF final wish made on corrupted Grail:** Nightmare outcome.
