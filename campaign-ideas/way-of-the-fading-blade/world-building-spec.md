### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**Way of the Fading Blade**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Samurai in Meiji-era Japan face abolishment of their class as the nation modernizes. Training traditionalist armies and facing modern Imperial forces, they must decide whether honor requires dying for fading traditions or adapting them for a changing world.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Meiji Restoration—Emperor's return to power, modernization drive, Western influence.
    *   Write history of samurai class—centuries of warrior culture, code of bushido.
    *   Describe the abolishment of samurai—sword bans, conscript army, loss of status.
    *   Explain Western imperialism threat—why Japan modernizes or faces colonization.
    *   Chronicle samurai rebellions—traditionalists resisting change.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** Traditionalist Samurai (Major), Imperial Government (Major), Western Advisors (Minor), Merchant Class (Minor), Peasant Army (Minor)

**4. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Party follows pure bushido → **THEN:** Earns samurai respect but tactical disadvantages.
*   **IF:** Japan fails to modernize → **THEN:** Becomes Western colony like China.
*   **IF:** Tradition adapts → **THEN:** Bushido values survive in modern form.
*   **IF:** Final charge occurs → **THEN:** Beautiful death or survival shapes legacy.
