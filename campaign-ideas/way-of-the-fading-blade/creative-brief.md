# Creative Brief: Way of the Fading Blade

## Art Style: Traditional Japanese Aesthetic Meets Meiji Modernization

Inspired by *The Last Samurai* and historical Meiji period. Captures the austere beauty of samurai culture, the clash between traditional Japan and Western modernity, and the tragic elegance of a dying way of life.

### Core Principles:

*   **Emphasize Traditional Beauty:** Cherry blossoms, bamboo forests, traditional architecture, sword artistry
*   **Cultural Contrast:** Samurai armor vs Western uniforms, katana vs rifles, tea ceremony vs industrialization
*   **Seasonal Metaphors:** Cherry blossoms falling, autumn leaves, winter snow, spring renewal
*   **Disciplined Violence:** Precise sword techniques, archery form, beautiful death poses

### Color Palette:

*   **Traditional:** Muted earth tones, black lacquer armor, white and red samurai banners
*   **Cherry Blossoms:** Soft pinks against dark bark, petals falling in wind
*   **Imperial Modern:** Western military blues and reds, factory grays, steel rifles
*   **Natural:** Forest greens, mountain grays, rice field golds, misty mornings
