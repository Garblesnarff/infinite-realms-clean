# The Demon King's Shadow - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Hindu Epic Meets High Fantasy

**Palettes:** Gold and saffron (divinity), deep green (forest), crimson (rakshasas), ocean blue, Lanka gold

**Music:** Indian classical (sitar, tabla, bansuri) meets epic orchestra. Ragas for different moods. Hanuman Chalisa chanting.

**Voices:**
- Rama: Dignified, measured, divine (Cillian Murphy)
- Hanuman: Playful yet powerful (Andy Serkis)
- Ravana: Scholarly evil, ten voices layered (Benedict Cumberbatch)

**Cinematic Moments:**
- Hanuman's leap across ocean
- Sita's agni pariksha (fire ordeal)
- Rama's final divine arrow piercing all ten heads
- Bridge construction montage
