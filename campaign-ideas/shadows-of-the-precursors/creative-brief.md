# Shadows of the Precursors - Creative Brief

## Art Style and Visual Tone
- **Primary Style**: Sleek sci-fi with alien mystery elements
- **Normandy Design**: Cutting-edge, blue/white lighting, holographic interfaces
- **Precursor Ruins**: Ancient stone mixed with impossible technology, blue-green glow
- **Reaper Aesthetic**: Organic-mechanical horror, red and black, indoctrination effects

## Color Palettes
- **Citadel**: Bright, hopeful, multi-cultural (diverse species visible)
- **Combat**: Orange muzzle flares, blue biotic powers, tech yellow
- **Reaper Presence**: Red lighting, organic corruption, dread atmosphere
- **Space**: Deep blacks with colorful nebulas, emphasizing scale

## Music and Sound Design
- **Main Theme**: Epic orchestral with synth elements (Mass Effect style)
- **Combat**: Electronic percussion with orchestra
- **Emotional**: Piano and strings for character moments
- **Reaper Theme**: Ominous brass and synthetic sounds (the "BWAAM")

## Cinematic Moments
- **Eden Prime Beacon**: Vision of galactic harvest, overwhelming
- **Sovereign Reveal**: Reaper ship speaking, cosmic horror
- **Virmire Choice**: Heartbreaking decision, timer counting down
- **Battle of Citadel**: Fleet combat while ground team fights inside
- **Human Reaper**: Body horror on massive scale
- **"We fight or we die. That's the plan."**

## Artistic Inspirations
- Mass Effect trilogy (BioWare)
- Star Trek (exploration optimism)
- Lovecraft (cosmic horror of Reapers)
- Blade Runner (neon-noir cityscapes)
