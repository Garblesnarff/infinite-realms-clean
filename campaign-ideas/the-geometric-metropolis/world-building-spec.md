# World-Building Specification: The Geometric Metropolis

## Core Concept
Art Deco dimension of perfect geometric order maintained by Symmetry Engine that erases imperfection. Noir mystery through three social tiers investigating Engine's dark truth.

## Lore
- **Origin**: Built by 1920s-era mages seeking perfect ordered society
- **Symmetry Engine**: AI maintaining geometric perfection through erasure
- **Three Tiers**: Gilded Above (elite), Chrome Middle (workers), Forgotten Below (rejects)
- **Streamlined Society**: Every aspect designed for efficiency and beauty

## Factions
- **The Gilded Elite** (support Engine, benefit from order)
- **The Chrome Workers** (ambivalent, fear both chaos and erasure)
- **The Irregular Resistance** (oppose Engine, embrace imperfection)
- **Engine Enforcers** (geometric automatons eliminating asymmetry)

## NPCs
- **Streamline Sam**: Noir detective guide
- **Madame Symmetra**: Elite antagonist
- **Vector the Machinist**: Secret ally
- **The Symmetry Engine**: Final boss/philosopher

## Locations
- **Gilded Above**: Luxury ziggurats
- **Chrome Middle**: Streamlined city
- **Forgotten Below**: Underground refuge
- **Engine Chamber**: Perfect geometric core

## Mechanics
- **Symmetry Score**: Engine targets asymmetry
- **Social Tier**: Affects access and reactions
- **Noir Investigation**: Clue mechanics
- **Streamlined Bonus**: Precision rewarded
