**Bracers of Liberl**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Players are Bracers in the peaceful Kingdom of Liberl, completing assignments from finding lost items to stopping monsters. A slow-burn conspiracy reveals the secret society Ouroboros orchestrating a coup to access an ancient artifact. The campaign emphasizes worldbuilding, relationship building with every NPC, and gradual escalation from mundane tasks to saving the nation.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Bracer Guild's founding and philosophy of political neutrality while helping citizens.
    *   Write the history of Orbment technology - magical devices powered by Septium crystals.
    *   Describe the ancient civilization that created the Sept-Terrions (reality-warping artifacts).
    *   Explain Ouroboros's Gospel Plan and their goal to gather Sept-Terrions.
    *   Detail Joshua's past as a child assassin trained by Ouroboros.
    *   Write the political history of Liberl and its relationship with neighboring Erebonian Empire.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:**
    *   **Bracer Guild** (Major - Protagonists): Independent helpers, politically neutral, earn BP through assignments.
    *   **Ouroboros** (Major - Antagonist): Secret society collecting Sept-Terrions through manipulations.
    *   **Royal Army/Intelligence** (Complex): Some loyal, some corrupted, Richard leads coup faction.
    *   **Regional Governments** (Minor): Each city has local governance players must navigate.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Cassius Bright, Schera, Joshua, Olivier, Richard, Professor Alba, Ouroboros Enforcers, The Grandmaster.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Regional Mayor," "Bracer Guild Staff," "Citizen with Request," "Ouroboros Agent."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Locations:** Rolent (starting town), Bose (agricultural), Ruan (harbor/academy), Zeiss (manufacturing), Grancel (capital), Glorious (floating fortress), Sealed Area (ancient ruins).

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Players complete optional Bracer assignments helping NPCs.
*   **THEN:** Those NPCs provide safe houses, information, and support during the coup.
*   **IF:** Players build high relationships across all regions.
*   **THEN:** Unlock best ending with full regional support against Ouroboros.
*   **IF:** Joshua's past is revealed and players support him.
*   **THEN:** He returns to the party stronger, relationship deepened.
