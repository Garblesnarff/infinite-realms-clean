# Creative Brief: Bracers of Liberl

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Pastoral Fantasy Intrigue (Trails in the Sky-inspired)

Inspired by the warm, detailed aesthetic of *The Legend of Heroes: Trails in the Sky*, featuring cozy European-inspired towns, Orbment technology blending magic and machinery, and a color palette emphasizing peaceful beauty contrasted with shadowy conspiracy.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Warmth and Detail:** Every location and NPC should feel lived-in and real, with attention to small touches.
*   **Magitech Aesthetic:** Blend Victorian/steampunk technology with magical glowing Orbments seamlessly.
*   **Contrast Peace and Conspiracy:** Beautiful pastoral settings hide political machinations and ancient mysteries.

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Hopeful, methodical, warm, mysterious, political intrigue, slice-of-life, conspiracy, coming-of-age, pastoral fantasy.
*   **Feeling:** Cozy adventures in detailed world gradually revealing darker mysteries. Balance everyday kindness with epic scope.

**2. Color Palette:**
*   **Style:** Warm, pastoral colors - golden fields, blue skies, green countryside. Orbment technology glows with distinctive colors. Ouroboros uses purples and blacks. Each region has signature colors.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...rendered in warm pastoral colors - golden wheat fields, blue sky, a small European-style town with colorful roofs. A character's Orbment device glows with blue magical light."

**3. Character & Environment Design:**
*   **Style:** Anime-inspired characters in early 1900s European clothing mixed with fantasy elements. Detailed town designs - each building has purpose and character.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A detailed anime illustration of a small fantasy kingdom town square with European architecture, market stalls, citizens going about daily life, and a guild building with a bracer emblem. Warm, welcoming atmosphere."

## Voice

Warm, thoughtful narration like a veteran Bracer recounting adventures. Pacing is measured, building slowly to revelations. Emotion emphasizes hope, growth, and the value of helping others.
