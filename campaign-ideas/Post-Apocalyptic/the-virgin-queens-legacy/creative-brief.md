# The Virgin Queen's Legacy - Creative Brief

## Art Style
**Tudor Elegance + Court Drama**: Portraits style (Nicholas Hilliard), elaborate costumes, pageantry. Inspired by: Elizabethan portraits, films like *Elizabeth* (1998).

## Visual Design
**Elizabeth**: Red hair, white face paint (lead-based, toxic), elaborate ruffs, jeweled gowns. Evolution: Youthful princess → Ageless icon → Aged but still crowned.
**Court**: Opulent costumes, intrigue-filled chambers, theater and spectacle.

## Color Palettes
- **Royal**: Tudor green and white, gold thread
- **Protestant**: Black and white (Puritan influence)
- **Catholic**: Rich reds, papal gold
- **Naval**: Ocean blue, Spanish galleon brown, English grey

## Music
**Tudor/Elizabethan**: Lute, virginals, madrigals. Tallis, Byrd, Dowland. Court masques, dramatic flourishes.

## Voice Direction
**Elizabeth**: English (RP), imperious, intelligent, capable of warmth and ice. Evolution: Uncertain youth → Commanding monarch → Tired but unbowed.

## Cinematic Moments
- Tower imprisonment: Fear of execution
- Tilbury speech: "Heart and stomach of a king"
- Armada victory: Fire-ships, Protestant Wind
- Essex's execution: Personal tragedy
- Death scene: Refuses to lie down (won't yield even to death)
