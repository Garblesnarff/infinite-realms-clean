# The Eternal Court War [CAMPAIGN: Post-Apocalyptic]

## Core Concept [CONCEPT: High]
In a world scarred by an unknown cataclysm, humanity clings to survival within the ruins of a once-great metropolis. Society has fractured into numerous "Courts," each claiming direct lineage and divine right from pre-Collapse royalty. These Courts are locked in a perpetual, brutal struggle for control over dwindling resources, forgotten technologies, and the very definition of what it means to be civilized in a broken world. The players navigate this treacherous landscape, forced to choose allegiances or forge their own path amidst the endless conflict.

## Setting [SETTING: Urban Ruin]
The campaign unfolds within the sprawling, shattered remains of a vast city, now known only as the "Shattered Crown." Towering skyscrapers, half-collapsed and overgrown with mutated flora, pierce a perpetually hazy sky. Streets are choked with debris, forming labyrinthine alleys and treacherous canyons. Settlements are built within the shells of old buildings, fortified with scavenged metal and makeshift defenses. Beyond the immediate Court territories lie the "Dead Zones" â€“ areas of extreme radiation, mutated creatures, or inexplicable phenomena, holding both immense danger and the promise of invaluable pre-Collapse relics.

### Locations [LOCATION: Specific]
*   **The Iron Throne District:** [LOCATION: District] The heavily fortified, central district controlled by the Iron Court. It boasts the most intact infrastructure, including a functional (though heavily modified) water purification plant and a few operational pre-Collapse generators. Its walls are made of reinforced concrete and scavenged vehicle plating.
*   **The Verdant Sprawl:** [LOCATION: Wilds] A vast, overgrown park and botanical garden district, now a dangerous jungle teeming with mutated plant life and predatory creatures. The Verdant Court claims this territory, utilizing its unique ecosystem for food and bio-engineered resources.
*   **The Ash Wastes:** [LOCATION: Wasteland] The industrial sector, now a desolate expanse of toxic dust and skeletal factories. The Ash Court, composed primarily of scavengers and mechanics, braves its dangers for rare metals and fuel.
*   **The Whispering Archives:** [LOCATION: Ruin] The remains of a grand library or data center, now a perilous maze of collapsed shelves and corrupted data banks. Rumored to hold vast amounts of pre-Collapse knowledge, it is a neutral (and highly contested) zone.
*   **The Gutter Market:** [LOCATION: Bazaar] A sprawling, chaotic black market operating in the sewers and lower levels of the city, where all Courts and independent factions trade goods, information, and secrets.

## Factions [FACTION: Political]
*   **The Iron Court:** [FACTION: Authoritarian] Descendants of military and industrial leaders, they believe in strict hierarchy, order, and the re-establishment of a powerful, centralized government. They are well-armed and disciplined, but often ruthless.
*   **The Verdant Court:** [FACTION: Ecological] Composed of those who adapted to the mutated environment, they prioritize ecological balance and sustainable living. They are skilled in bio-engineering and guerrilla warfare, often seen as radical by other Courts.
*   **The Ash Court:** [FACTION: Pragmatic] A loose confederation of engineers, mechanics, and scavengers. They value ingenuity, self-reliance, and the practical application of technology. They are less concerned with lineage and more with survival and progress.
*   **The Silent Watchers:** [FACTION: Mysterious] A secretive group of scholars and archivists who operate from hidden enclaves, attempting to piece together the true history of the Collapse and the pre-Collapse world. They rarely interfere directly but possess invaluable knowledge.
*   **The Free Folk:** [FACTION: Independent] Nomadic groups, independent traders, and small, unaligned settlements who try to survive outside the Court system, often caught in the crossfire.

## NPCs [NPC: Key]
*   **Queen Isolde "Ironheart" Vane:** [NPC: Leader] The stern, unyielding matriarch of the Iron Court. A brilliant tactician and administrator, she rules with an iron fist, convinced that only absolute control can save humanity. (Female, 50s, imposing, pragmatic)
*   **Elder Thistlewick:** [NPC: Sage] The ancient, enigmatic leader of the Verdant Court. He communicates through cryptic parables and seems to possess an uncanny connection to the mutated flora. (Male, indeterminate age, gaunt, wise)
*   **Jax "The Wrench" Kincaid:** [NPC: Engineer] A charismatic and resourceful leader of a prominent Ash Court scavenging crew. He's known for his mechanical genius and his ability to fix almost anything. (Male, 30s, jovial, inventive)
*   **Archivist Seraphina:** [NPC: Scholar] A young, brilliant member of the Silent Watchers, obsessed with uncovering the truth behind the Collapse. She is often found poring over ancient texts or deciphering corrupted data. (Female, 20s, studious, driven)
*   **"Ghost" Rourke:** [NPC: Rogue] A legendary independent scout and smuggler who knows the Dead Zones better than anyone. He's a neutral party, always looking for the next score, but possesses a hidden moral compass. (Male, 40s, elusive, cynical)
*   **Kaelen, The Whispering Blade:** [NPC: Assassin] A highly skilled assassin in the employ of the Iron Court, known for their silent movements and deadly precision. They are a feared enforcer, rarely seen but always felt. (Androgynous, 20s, silent, deadly)

## Quests [QUEST: Primary]
*   **The Water Wars:** [QUEST: Resource Control] The Iron Court's primary water purification plant is failing. Players must secure a rare pre-Collapse filtration component from a heavily guarded Dead Zone or negotiate a water-sharing agreement with the Verdant Court, risking betrayal.
*   **Seeds of Dissent:** [QUEST: Espionage] The Verdant Court tasks players with infiltrating the Iron Throne District to spread propaganda among the populace, highlighting the Iron Court's resource hoarding and environmental destruction.
*   **The Lost Schematic:** [QUEST: Retrieval] The Ash Court needs a legendary pre-Collapse schematic for a powerful energy generator, rumored to be hidden deep within the Whispering Archives, guarded by automated defenses or mutated creatures.
*   **Echoes of the Past:** [QUEST: Investigation] The Silent Watchers hire the players to investigate a newly discovered pre-Collapse data cache that hints at the true cause of the Collapse, but it's located in a highly unstable Dead Zone.
*   **The Gutter King's Gambit:** [QUEST: Negotiation] The Gutter Market's leader, a shadowy figure known as the "Gutter King," offers a lucrative reward for players to mediate a truce between two minor warring factions, threatening to destabilize the entire market.
*   **The Mutagenic Bloom:** [QUEST: Containment] A new, rapidly spreading mutated plant strain from the Verdant Sprawl threatens to overwhelm a neutral settlement. Players must find a way to contain or eradicate it, potentially requiring cooperation from multiple Courts.

## Threats [THREAT: Environmental]
*   **Radiation Storms:** [THREAT: Environmental] Frequent, unpredictable storms of residual radiation that sweep through the Shattered Crown, forcing all inhabitants to seek shelter or risk severe mutation.
*   **Mutated Wildlife:** [THREAT: Creature] The Dead Zones and Verdant Sprawl are home to grotesque, aggressive creatures warped by the Collapse, ranging from giant insects to feral, intelligent beasts.
*   **Automated Defenses:** [THREAT: Technological] Remnants of pre-Collapse security systems, still active in some ruins, ranging from laser grids to robotic sentinels, often malfunctioning and indiscriminate.
*   **Resource Scarcity:** [THREAT: Social] The constant struggle for food, clean water, and fuel fuels conflict between Courts and leads to desperate acts by individuals.
*   **Internal Strife:** [THREAT: Political] The constant power struggles, betrayals, and shifting alliances between and within the Courts pose a significant threat to any who get involved.
*   **The Whispering Sickness:** [THREAT: Psychological] A mysterious, mind-altering affliction that sometimes manifests in those who spend too long in the deepest Dead Zones or consume tainted resources, leading to madness or violent outbursts.

## World-Changing Events [EVENT: Cataclysmic]
*   **The Great Thaw:** [EVENT: Environmental] A sudden, inexplicable shift in the global climate causes massive ice caps to melt, leading to widespread flooding and the emergence of new, previously submerged ruins and dangers.
*   **The Signal:** [EVENT: Mysterious] A powerful, coherent signal broadcasts across all frequencies from deep within a Dead Zone, containing fragments of pre-Collapse data or a message from an unknown entity, sparking a desperate race to decipher its meaning.
*   **The Rise of the Undercity:** [EVENT: Social] A massive, organized movement of the Free Folk and disaffected citizens from all Courts rises from the Gutter Market, threatening to overthrow the established order and forge a new society.
*   **The Awakening of the Machine God:** [EVENT: Technological] An ancient, dormant pre-Collapse AI or super-weapon reactivates, its purpose unknown, potentially offering salvation or total annihilation.
*   **The Factional Collapse:** [EVENT: Political] One of the major Courts suffers a catastrophic internal betrayal or external defeat, leading to its complete dissolution and a power vacuum that plunges the Shattered Crown into unprecedented chaos.
*   **The Sky-Fall:** [EVENT: Cosmic] Fragments of a decaying orbital station or a newly discovered celestial body begin to fall from the sky, impacting the Shattered Crown and revealing alien technologies or new threats.

## Possible Endings [ENDING: Ambiguous]
*   **The New Hegemony:** [ENDING: Authoritarian] One Court successfully conquers or absorbs all others, establishing a new, albeit potentially oppressive, unified government over the Shattered Crown.
*   **The Fragile Alliance:** [ENDING: Cooperative] The Courts, perhaps with player intervention, forge a tenuous alliance to face a greater external threat or to rebuild society, but old rivalries simmer beneath the surface.
*   **The Exodus:** [ENDING: Exploratory] The players discover a way to leave the Shattered Crown and lead a portion of humanity to a new, unknown land, leaving the old conflicts behind.
*   **The Cycle Continues:** [ENDING: Cyclical] Despite player efforts, the warring factions remain entrenched, and the struggle for power continues indefinitely, with only minor shifts in the balance.
*   **The Truth Revealed:** [ENDING: Revelatory] The true cause of the Collapse is uncovered, forcing humanity to confront a devastating truth about their past or their future, fundamentally altering their understanding of the world.
*   **The Wild Frontier:** [ENDING: Anarchic] All established Courts collapse, and humanity disperses into smaller, independent groups, leading to a more anarchic but potentially freer existence in the wilds.
