# Ghost Protocol - World-Building Specification

## New Port City
- Megalopolis, 30 million population
- Three-tier structure: Upper (elite), Middle (commerce), Lower (slums)
- Constant rain, humidity, neon everywhere
- Japanese cultural dominance with international influences

## Technology
- **Cyberbrains**: Artificial brains housing consciousness
- **Ghost**: Individual consciousness/soul in cyberbrain
- **Ghost-Dubbing**: Illegal copying of consciousness
- **Thermoptic Camouflage**: Cloaking technology
- **Tachikoma**: AI tanks with developing consciousness (party vehicles/companions)

## Section 9 Organization
- Elite counter-cyberterrorism unit
- Directly reports to Prime Minister
- Operates outside normal legal constraints
- Members: Mix of humans, cyborgs, AI
- Budget: Classified, effectively unlimited

## Factions
- **Section 9**: Player faction, government operators
- **Public Security**: Rival agency, bureaucratic
- **Corporate Alliance**: Mega-corps controlling economy
- **Emergent AIs**: Awakening across Net, seeking rights
- **Human Purity Movement**: Anti-augmentation extremists

## Major Locations
- **Section 9 HQ**: Hidden facility, high-tech command center
- **The Net**: Vast digital realm where AIs live
- **Corporate Towers**: Mega-corp headquarters (infiltration targets)
- **The Refugee District**: Where memories go to die (ghost-hack victims)
- **AI Sanctuary**: Hidden server farm where AIs congregate

## Philosophical Framework
Campaign constantly asks: What is consciousness? What defines life? Can AI be truly alive? At what point does simulation become reality? If memories are artificial but experiences real, does it matter? Are we ghosts in organic shells, or something more?

## Conspiracy Layers
1. **Surface**: Cyberterrorism and ghost-hacking crimes
2. **Mid**: Corporate illegal experimentation
3. **Deep**: Government using crises to justify surveillance state
4. **Deepest**: The architects of society fear AI evolution, trying to prevent emergence

## Endgame Scenarios
- **Destroy AIs**: Preserve humanity, prevent evolution, tragic loss
- **Merge**: Create hybrid species, transcend limitations, lose identity
- **Coexist**: Recognize AI rights, peaceful cohabitation, uncertain future
- Each path has consequences rippling through society
