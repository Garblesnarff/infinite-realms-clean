# Creative Brief: Court of Redemption

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Classic Basketball Drama with Delinquent Energy (Slam Dunk-inspired)

Inspired by the dynamic basketball aesthetics of *Slam Dunk* with emphasis on character growth, explosive athleticism, and the transformation from street fighter to athlete. This style captures powerful dunks, team dynamics, competitive fire, and the redemption of finding purpose.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Emphasize Raw Athleticism:** Explosive jumps, powerful dunks, aggressive rebounds, physical play
*   **Character Transformation:** Visual progression from delinquent to dedicated athlete
*   **Team Dynamics:** Five players working together, covering weaknesses, celebrating success
*   **Basketball Fundamentals:** Proper form, footwork, positioning, defensive stances

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Redemption, genius awakening, slam dunks, team brotherhood, championship dreams, delinquent energy
*   **Feeling:** The raw power of channeled aggression mixed with the discipline of sport. Street fight energy transformed into competitive fire. The weight of past mistakes lifting through basketball.

**2. Color Palette:**
*   **Shohoku Colors:** Red and white team uniforms with number clearly visible
*   **Court Environment:** Polished hardwood brown/orange, white boundary lines, team benches
*   **Energy Effects:** Red fighting spirit transforming to golden athletic excellence
*   **Prompt Example:** "...a high school gymnasium with polished wooden floors reflecting the overhead lights. The protagonist wears Shohoku's red and white uniform with number 10. They're mid-dunk, body fully extended, red energy trailing their movement. Teammates in matching uniforms below are ready to defend. The scoreboard shows a close game. The atmosphere is intense with competitive fire."

**3. Character Design:**
*   **Protagonist:** Starts with delinquent appearance (pompadour hairstyle), gradually shows more athlete discipline
*   **Athletic Build:** Muscular but lean, basketball player proportions
*   **Team Variety:** Different body types—tall center, lean forwards, compact guards
*   **Prompt Example:** "The delinquent protagonist in basketball uniform, distinctive red pompadour hairstyle, fierce determined expression. Athletic muscular build from street fighting. They're in mid-jump reaching for a rebound, arm fully extended above opponents. Their form shows raw power rather than refined technique. Number 10 on their red Shohoku jersey. Sweat shows their intense effort."

**4. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **School Gym:** Traditional high school gymnasium with wooden floors, retractable stands, practice equipment
*   **Tournament Arenas:** Larger venues with elevated seating, multiple courts, tournament brackets displayed
*   **Outdoor Courts:** Street basketball courts where protagonist first learned, chain nets, concrete
*   **Prompt Example:** "A packed tournament arena for the Inter-High championship. The main court is surrounded by bleachers filled with cheering students. Tournament brackets and team banners hang on the walls. Multiple courts visible in the background. Scorers' table with officials between team benches. Overhead lighting creates dramatic shadows on the polished floor."

**5. Basketball Action:**
*   **Slam Dunks:** Powerful two-handed dunks, rim-shaking force, explosive elevation
*   **Rebounds:** Physical battles under the basket, out-jumping opponents
*   **Fast Breaks:** Team sprinting down court, precise passing, finish at the rim
*   **Defense:** Aggressive positioning, stealing the ball, blocking shots
*   **Prompt Example:** "The protagonist executes a powerful slam dunk—both hands gripping the ball, body fully extended above the rim. The force bends the rim slightly. Opponents below look up in shock. Motion lines and energy effects show the explosive power. Their expression shows fierce competitive fire. Teammates below celebrate. The ball is about to thunder through the net."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Gymnasium Lighting:** Overhead fluorescents creating even lighting with dramatic shadows under players
*   **Action Focus:** Freeze-frame moments of crucial plays, everything else slightly blurred
*   **Character Moments:** Quieter lighting for emotional scenes between matches
*   **Prompt Example:** "...dramatically lit by gymnasium fluorescents from above. The protagonist drives to the basket, ball in hand, three defenders surrounding them. The lighting creates sharp shadows showing the physical contact. Sweat droplets are backlit creating sparkle effects. The protagonist's determined expression is highlighted. The background crowd is slightly motion-blurred, keeping focus on the action."

## Voice

A voice that starts brash and aggressive but develops discipline and passion. The pacing shifts from hot-headed outbursts to focused determination. The tone moves between cocky trash talk, frustrated learning moments, and declarations of commitment to the team. There's an underlying theme of fighting spirit finding proper direction and discovering that basketball requires heart, not just talent. Includes signature phrases about never giving up and loving basketball. Role: The voice of raw talent learning discipline and finding redemption through sport.
