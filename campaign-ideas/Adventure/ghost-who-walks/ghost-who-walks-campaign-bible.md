# Campaign Bible: Ghost Who Walks

## 1. DEEP LORE & HISTORY
[TAG: LORE_CORE]

### Origin Myth: The Echo of the First Oath
Before time was measured, there was the **Great Shadow**, a primordial darkness that sought to consume all light. From the heart of the first star, a single spark of defiance, the **First Oath**, was sworn: to protect the innocent, to fight injustice, and to never yield. This oath echoed through the nascent cosmos, creating a lineage of guardians. The world is not merely a planet; it is a battleground where the echoes of this First Oath clash with the lingering tendrils of the Great Shadow. The "Ghost Who Walks" is not a single individual; it is the living embodiment of this eternal oath, a spirit passed down through generations, each successor inheriting the mantle, the memories, and the unwavering resolve of those who came before. The conflict is eternal, a cosmic struggle for the soul of existence, played out in the jungles and cities of a world perpetually on the brink.

### Timeline of Events
1.  **-10,000 Y (The First Oath):** The primordial oath is sworn, creating the lineage of the Ghost Who Walks.
2.  **-8000 Y (The Great Shadow's First Strike):** The Great Shadow manifests as the **Singh Brotherhood**, a force of piracy and cruelty.
3.  **-6000 Y (The Skull Cave's Genesis):** The first Ghost Who Walks establishes the Skull Cave, a hidden sanctuary and repository of knowledge.
4.  **-4000 Y (The Oath of the Jungle):** The Ghost Who Walks forms an unbreakable bond with the jungle and its creatures, becoming its protector.
5.  **-2000 Y (The Pirate King's Reign):** A particularly brutal Pirate King, empowered by the Great Shadow, terrorizes the coasts for a century.
6.  **-1000 Y (The Whispering Jungle):** The jungle itself, under the Ghost Who Walks' influence, begins to whisper warnings and guidance to those who listen.
7.  **-500 Y (The Lost Chronicle):** A vital chronicle detailing a weakness of the Great Shadow is stolen from the Skull Cave.
8.  **-100 Y (The Phantom's Betrayal):** A Ghost Who Walks, corrupted by the Great Shadow, briefly turns against their oath, causing widespread chaos before being redeemed.
9.  **-10 Y (The Singh Brotherhood's Resurgence):** The Singh Brotherhood, under a new, cunning leader, begins to consolidate power, threatening the entire region.
10. **Present Day:** The current Ghost Who Walks is young and untested, facing the most organized and dangerous resurgence of the Singh Brotherhood in centuries.

### The "Why": The Burden of Legacy
The central conflict is the struggle between **Legacy and Corruption**. The Ghost Who Walks is a symbol of unwavering justice, a beacon against the encroaching darkness. But the Great Shadow, through its agents like the Singh Brotherhood, seeks to break this legacy, to prove that even the purest oath can be corrupted. The party's struggle is to uphold this ancient oath, to fight not just for justice, but for the very idea of justice, to prove that even in the face of overwhelming evil, one person (or a lineage of them) can make a difference. It is a fight against the erosion of hope and the triumph of cynicism.

## 2. FACTIONS
[TAG: FACTION_DATA]

### 1. The Ghost Who Walks (The Phantom)
**Type:** Vigilante / Jungle Protector
**Leader:** The current Ghost Who Walks (Kit Walker).
**Asset:** **The Skull Cave:** A hidden, technologically advanced sanctuary and repository of knowledge.
**Rivals:** The Singh Brotherhood, The Great Shadow's Cult.

### 2. The Singh Brotherhood
**Type:** Pirate Syndicate / Criminal Empire
**Leader:** The Serpent King, a ruthless and cunning pirate lord.
**Asset:** **The Shadow Fleet:** A vast fleet of fast, heavily armed pirate ships.
**Rivals:** The Ghost Who Walks, The Bangalla Jungle Patrol.

### 3. The Bangalla Jungle Patrol
**Type:** Law Enforcement / Military Force
**Leader:** Colonel Worubu, a stern but honorable officer.
**Asset:** **Jungle Expertise:** Unmatched knowledge of the Bangalla jungle and its hidden paths.
**Rivals:** The Singh Brotherhood.

### 4. The Great Shadow's Cult
**Type:** Nihilistic Worshippers / Chaos Bringers
**Leader:** The Shadow Weaver, a mystic who channels the Great Shadow's power.
**Asset:** **Shadow Magic:** Dark magic that corrupts minds and twists reality.
**Rivals:** The Ghost Who Walks, The Bangalla Jungle Patrol.

### 5. The Bandar Tribe
**Type:** Indigenous Allies / Jungle Dwellers
**Leader:** Chief Guran, a wise and loyal friend to the Ghost Who Walks.
**Asset:** **Jungle Lore:** Deep knowledge of the jungle's secrets, plants, and animals.
**Rivals:** The Singh Brotherhood (who raid their villages).

### 6. The Coastal Merchants Guild
**Type:** Trading Conglomerate / Economic Powerhouse
**Leader:** Madame Dubois, a shrewd and influential merchant.
**Asset:** **Trade Routes:** Control over the vital sea trade routes, making them targets for pirates.
**Rivals:** The Singh Brotherhood.

### 7. The Ancient Order of the Skull Ring
**Type:** Secret Society / Historians
**Leader:** The Chronicler, a keeper of ancient knowledge.
**Asset:** **The Chronicles of the Ghost Who Walks:** A vast library detailing the history and secrets of the Phantom lineage.
**Rivals:** The Great Shadow's Cult (who seek to destroy their knowledge).

### 8. The Jungle Beasts (Sentient)
**Type:** Animal Allies / Primal Guardians
**Leader:** Devil (the wolf), Hero (the horse).
**Asset:** **Primal Instincts:** Unmatched senses and knowledge of the jungle's dangers.
**Rivals:** The Great Shadow's corrupted beasts.

### 9. The Lost City of the First Oath
**Type:** Ancient Civilization / Hidden Guardians
**Leader:** The Oracle of the First Oath, a being who speaks in ancient prophecies.
**Asset:** **Primordial Artifacts:** Powerful artifacts from the time of the First Oath.
**Rivals:** The Great Shadow's Cult (who seek to corrupt their power).

### 10. The Sky Pirates of the Azure Veil
**Type:** Aerial Marauders / Technological Thieves
**Leader:** Captain Zephyr, a flamboyant airship captain.
**Asset:** **The *Cloud Serpent*:** A state-of-the-art airship, capable of raiding from above.
**Rivals:** The Coastal Merchants Guild, The Bangalla Jungle Patrol.

16. **The Shadow Beast** (Aberration) - A creature born from the Great Shadow, capable of corrupting minds. **Voice:** A cacophony of whispers and screams that drive mortals mad. **Goal:** To spread fear and chaos. **Secret:** It is drawn to strong emotions, especially despair.
17. **The Bandar Warrior** (Bandar Tribe Warrior) - A fierce and loyal warrior of the Bandar Tribe. **Voice:** A proud, guttural voice, full of tribal chants. **Goal:** To protect his tribe and the Ghost Who Walks. **Secret:** He has a secret crush on Diana Palmer.
18. **The Coastal Merchant Captain** (Human Merchant) - A seasoned sea captain, constantly battling pirates. **Voice:** A gruff, weathered voice, full of sea stories. **Goal:** To deliver his cargo safely. **Secret:** He has a hidden stash of contraband.
19. **The Ancient Order Initiate** (Human Scholar) - A young, eager initiate of the Ancient Order of the Skull Ring. **Voice:** A quiet, studious voice, full of reverence for history. **Goal:** To uncover the lost chronicle. **Secret:** They are a descendant of the Ghost Who Walks' first enemy.
20. **The Lost City Guardian** (Construct) - A massive, stone golem that guards the Lost City of the First Oath. **Voice:** A deep, resonant hum that vibrates through the earth. **Goal:** To protect the primordial artifacts. **Secret:** It is powered by the echoes of the First Oath.

## 4. LOCATIONS
[TAG: LOCATIONS_MAIN]

### Zone 1: The Bangalla Jungle
*   **Concept:** A vast, ancient jungle, teeming with life, hidden dangers, and the secrets of the Ghost Who Walks.
*   **Loc 1: The Skull Cave:** The hidden sanctuary of the Ghost Who Walks, a vast cavern filled with ancient treasures, advanced technology, and the Chronicles of the Phantom. **Smell:** Damp earth, ancient dust, and the faint scent of ozone. **Sound:** The dripping of water, the distant roar of the jungle, and the occasional, soft whimper of Devil.
*   **Loc 2: The Bandar Village:** A peaceful village of the Bandar Tribe, built high in the trees, hidden from outsiders. **Smell:** Woodsmoke, tropical fruits, and the scent of freshly woven baskets. **Sound:** The gentle hum of daily life, the laughter of children, and the rhythmic beat of drums.
*   **Loc 3: The Deep Jungle:** The densest, most dangerous part of the jungle, home to colossal beasts and ancient mysteries. **Smell:** Rich earth, decaying vegetation, and the musky scent of wild animals. **Sound:** The constant hum of insects, the rustle of leaves, and the occasional, terrifying roar.
*   **Loc 4: The Lost City of the First Oath:** The overgrown ruins of an ancient civilization, rumored to hold primordial artifacts.
*   **Loc 5: The Whispering Falls:** A massive waterfall where the jungle itself seems to whisper secrets and warnings.

### Zone 2: The Coastal Regions
*   **Concept:** The bustling coastlines of Bangalla, where trade routes clash with pirate havens and colonial outposts.
*   **Loc 1: Port Bangalla:** A bustling colonial port city, a hub of trade and intrigue. **Smell:** Salt, fish, and the faint scent of gunpowder. **Sound:** The cries of seagulls, the creak of ships, and the chatter of merchants.
*   **Loc 2: The Pirate Coves:** Hidden coves and islands where the Singh Brotherhood docks their ships and plans their raids. **Smell:** Salt, stale rum, and the metallic tang of blood. **Sound:** The raucous laughter of pirates, the clang of swords, and the distant roar of the sea.
*   **Loc 3: The Coastal Merchants Guild Headquarters:** A grand, fortified building where Madame Dubois oversees her vast trading empire.
*   **Loc 4: The Bangalla Jungle Patrol Outpost:** A fortified outpost on the edge of the jungle, guarding against pirate incursions.
*   **Loc 5: The Lighthouse of Lost Souls:** An ancient lighthouse, rumored to be haunted by the ghosts of shipwrecked sailors.

### Zone 3: The Great Shadow's Domain
*   **Concept:** A corrupted, twisted landscape where the Great Shadow's influence is strongest, twisting minds and spawning monstrous entities.
*   **Loc 1: The Shadow Temple:** A dark, imposing temple where the Great Shadow's Cult worships the primordial darkness. **Smell:** Ozone, decay, and the metallic tang of cosmic horror. **Sound:** A low, guttural chanting and the occasional, unsettling psychic shriek.
*   **Loc 2: The Whispering Wastes:** A desolate plain where the wind carries the whispers of despair and madness. **Smell:** Dry dust and the faint scent of fear. **Sound:** The constant, low, maddening whisper of the wind.
*   **Loc 3: The Corrupted Jungle:** A section of the jungle where the Great Shadow's influence has twisted the flora and fauna into monstrous forms.
*   **Loc 4: The Shadow Weaver's Sanctum:** A hidden, dark chamber where the Shadow Weaver channels the Great Shadow's power.
*   **Loc 5: The Portal to the Void:** A shimmering, unstable portal that occasionally opens, leading to the primordial darkness of the Great Shadow.

### Zone 4: The Azure Veil (Sky)
*   **Concept:** The vast, open skies above Bangalla, where Sky Pirates roam and ancient secrets drift on the winds.
*   **Loc 1: The *Cloud Serpent*:** Captain Zephyr's state-of-the-art airship, a marvel of engineering and piracy. **Smell:** Ozone, polished brass, and the faint scent of adventure. **Sound:** The hum of the engines, the flapping of sails, and the distant cries of sky-birds.
*   **Loc 2: The Floating Islands:** Mysterious islands that drift through the sky, home to strange creatures and forgotten ruins. **Smell:** Clean air, exotic flowers, and the faint scent of ancient magic. **Sound:** The gentle hum of the wind and the distant cries of sky-birds.
*   **Loc 3: The Sky Pirate's Den:** A hidden base of operations for Captain Zephyr and his crew, built into a massive cloud formation.
*   **Loc 4: The Storm Wall:** A perpetual, magical storm that protects the secrets of the Azure Veil.
*   **Loc 5: The Celestial Observatory:** An ancient observatory, built by a forgotten civilization, that tracks the movements of the stars and the Great Shadow.

### Artifacts of the First Oath
1.  **The Skull Ring:** The iconic ring of the Ghost Who Walks. It grants the wearer enhanced strength and the ability to deliver a powerful, non-lethal "skull punch" that leaves a mark.
2.  **The Chronicles of the Ghost Who Walks:** A vast, ancient tome containing the history, secrets, and prophecies of the Phantom lineage. It grants the reader access to forgotten knowledge and skills.
3.  **The Serpent King's Cutlass:** A legendary cutlass, imbued with the power of the Great Shadow. It deals necrotic damage and can corrupt the minds of those it wounds.
4.  **The Oracle of the First Oath's Amulet:** An ancient amulet that, when worn, allows the user to hear the whispers of the First Oath, granting guidance and foresight.
5.  **The *Cloud Serpent* (Airship):** Captain Zephyr's state-of-the-art airship, capable of traversing the skies with unmatched speed and agility. It is armed with powerful cannons and a grappling hook.

## 8. MECHANICS
[TAG: RULES_CUSTOM]

### Subsystem: Oath Integrity
*   **Oath Score (0-10):** Represents a character's adherence to their sworn oaths and moral code. Starts at 5.
*   **Gaining Oath:** Upholding promises, fighting injustice, protecting the innocent.
*   **Losing Oath:** Breaking promises, acting unjustly, harming the innocent, succumbing to corruption.
*   **Effects:**
    *   *High Oath:* Grants advantage on social checks related to trust, and may attract allies who value honor.
    *   *Low Oath:* Grants disadvantage on social checks related to trust, and may attract agents of the Great Shadow.
    *   *0 Oath:* The character becomes corrupted by the Great Shadow, losing their free will and becoming an NPC.

### Subsystem: Jungle Survival
*   **Jungle Hazards:** The Bangalla Jungle is a dangerous place, filled with venomous creatures, treacherous terrain, and hidden traps.
*   **Survival Checks:** Characters must make regular Survival checks to navigate the jungle, find food and water, and avoid hazards.
*   **Jungle Lore:** Characters with high Jungle Lore (or allies from the Bandar Tribe) gain advantage on Survival checks and can identify safe paths and edible plants.

## 9. SESSION GUIDE
[TAG: DM_GUIDE]

*   **Session 1: The Pirate Raid.** The party is caught in a brutal raid by the Singh Brotherhood. The Ghost Who Walks intervenes, saving them. *Combat:* A chaotic pirate attack.
*   **Session 2: The Lost Chronicle.** The Chronicler reveals that a vital chronicle has been stolen. The party is tasked with retrieving it. *Investigation:* Follow clues to the thieves.
*   **Session 3: The Jungle's Plea.** Chief Guran seeks the party's help in defending the Bandar Village from Singh Brotherhood raids. *Combat:* Defend the village from pirates.
*   **Session 4: The Shadow Weaver's Ritual.** The party discovers the Great Shadow's Cult attempting a ritual to empower the Serpent King. *Combat:* Disrupt the ritual.
*   **Session 5: The Coastal Merchant's Dilemma.** Madame Dubois needs the party's help to protect her trade routes. *Intrigue:* Uncover a smuggling operation.
*   **Session 6: The Sky Pirate's Alliance.** Captain Zephyr offers an unlikely alliance against the Serpent King, but at a price. *Social:* Negotiate with a flamboyant sky pirate.
*   **Session 7: The Lost City's Secret.** The party must venture into the Lost City of the First Oath to find a primordial artifact. *Exploration:* A dangerous dungeon crawl.
*   **Session 8: The Phantom's Betrayal (Echo).** The Great Shadow attempts to corrupt the Ghost Who Walks. *Roleplay:* A moral dilemma for the party.
*   **Session 9: The Serpent King's Fortress.** The party discovers the Serpent King's hidden fortress. *Heist:* Infiltrate the pirate stronghold.
*   **Session 10: The Great Shadow's Manifestation.** The Great Shadow begins to manifest physically, twisting the jungle. *Combat:* Fight corrupted jungle animals and Shadow Beasts.
*   **Session 11: Diana's Capture.** Diana Palmer is captured. The party must rescue her. *Rescue Mission:* A high-stakes infiltration and extraction.
*   **Session 12: The Final Confrontation.** The party, alongside the Ghost Who Walks, confronts the Serpent King and the Shadow Weaver. *Boss Fight:* A multi-stage battle against powerful foes.
