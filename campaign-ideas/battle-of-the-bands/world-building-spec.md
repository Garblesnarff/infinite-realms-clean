### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

**Battle of the Bands**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Struggling musician-adventurers in Toronto discover reality operates on video game logic when they must defeat Ramona Flowers' seven Evil Exes in boss battles to earn the right to date her. Between fights, they compete in Battle of the Bands competitions, unlock power-ups, and level up through the power of love and sick guitar solos.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail how reality came to operate on video game logic and physics in this Toronto.
    *   Write the history of the League of Evil Exes and Gideon's control over it.
    *   Describe subspace highways and how they connect emotional landscapes.
    *   Explain the Vegan Police and how vegan powers work.
    *   Detail the Toronto indie music scene hierarchy and Battle of the Bands tradition.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The League of Evil Exes** (Major): Seven former partners organized by Gideon to control Ramona.
    *   **Toronto Indie Scene** (Major): Competing bands, venues, and music politics.
    *   **The Vegan Police** (Minor): Enforcers of the vegan code who can strip powers.
    *   **Subspace Travelers** (Minor): Those who can navigate between emotional dimensions.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Gideon Graves (final boss), Ramona Flowers (love interest), The Seven Evil Exes (each unique boss).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Indie Musician" (band competitor), "Evil Ex Minion" (themed henchmen), "Venue Owner" (quest giver), "Vegan Enforcer" (special mechanics).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Second Cup:** Safe zone coffee shop, party's base of operations.
*   **The Rockit:** Main venue for boss fights and band competitions.
*   **Subspace Highways:** Fast-travel system through emotional dream corridors.
*   **The Chaos Theatre:** Gideon's nightclub, multi-floor final dungeon.
*   **Honest Ed's:** Item shop for purchasing power-ups and equipment.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Evil Ex defeated → **THEN:** Coins dropped, EXP gained, can date Ramona, next Ex appears.
*   **IF:** Band wins competition → **THEN:** Fame increases, unlock venues, gain resources.
*   **IF:** Power of Love/Self-Respect earned → **THEN:** Special weapon appears, stat boost.
*   **IF:** All seven Exes defeated → **THEN:** Gideon appears for final boss fight.
*   **IF:** Vegan code broken → **THEN:** Vegan Police strip powers, boss becomes vulnerable.
