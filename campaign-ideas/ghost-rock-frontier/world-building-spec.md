**Ghost Rock Frontier**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In 1879 where the Civil War still rages and ghost rock (screaming supernatural fuel) powers steampunk technology, cowboys and gunslingers face manitous (evil spirits) possessing the dead as Harrowed. The party discovers the Reckoners—four demonic entities—manipulate humanity through fear, and ghost rock mining thins reality letting them through.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the discovery of ghost rock in California and how it prevented Civil War's end.
    *   Write the history of the Reckoners—War, Famine, Pestilence, Death—feeding on humanity's fear.
    *   Describe how manitous possess the dead to create Harrowed.
    *   Explain the fracturing of America: Union, Confederacy, Native nations, independent territories.
    *   Detail the true nature of ghost rock—crushed souls of the damned.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Agency** (Major): Secret organization fighting supernatural threats.
    *   **Union and Confederacy** (Major): Both sides continuing Civil War, need ghost rock.
    *   **The Reckoners' Servants** (Major): Cultists, corrupted officials, possessed leaders serving demonic entities.
    *   **Native Tribes** (Minor): Fighting to protect Spirit World from ghost rock corruption.
    *   **Mad Scientists** (Minor): Inventors creating impossible ghost rock-powered devices.
    *   **The Harrowed** (Minor): Undead fighting their possessing manitous for control.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Marshal Holloway (Agency leader), Colonel Chamberlain (Union peacemaker), Dr. Hellstromme (mad scientist), Stone Calf (shaman), Reverend Grimme (false prophet).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Gunslinger" (quick-draw fighter), "Harrowed Outlaw" (undead gunman), "Steam Automaton" (robot), "Manitou" (possessing demon), "Confederate/Union Soldier" (war veteran).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Gomorra:** Ghost rock boomtown mixing Western and steampunk aesthetics.
*   **The Deadlands:** Nightmare zones where reality is thin, supernatural manifests.
*   **Lost Angels:** California city controlled by Reverend Grimme's cult.
*   **The Great Maze:** Shattered California coastline creating labyrinth of islands.
*   **Contested Battlefields:** Where both armies use ghost rock war machines.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Character dies → **THEN:** May return as Harrowed, must fight manitou for control.
*   **IF:** Ghost rock mining increases → **THEN:** Reality barriers weaken, more supernatural manifestations.
*   **IF:** Widespread fear spreads → **THEN:** Reckoners grow stronger, manifestation approaches.
*   **IF:** Union and Confederacy negotiate peace → **THEN:** War (one Reckoner) weakens significantly.
*   **IF:** Mad science device malfunctions → **THEN:** Explosion, corruption, or worse depending on power level.
*   **IF:** Reckoners fully manifest → **THEN:** Reality permanently warped, humanity faces extinction.
