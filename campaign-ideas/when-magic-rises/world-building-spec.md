**When Magic Rises**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In post-Shift Atlanta where magic and technology war in waves, mercenaries navigate shapeshifter Pack politics, vampire hordes, and ancient gods returning. One party member descends from Roland—an ancient wizard-king who destroyed civilizations. They must grow from street mercenaries to god-killers, defending Atlanta from their own blood father who seeks to rule humanity again.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Shift—when magic returned to the world, destroying technology-dependent civilization.
    *   Write the history of magic waves and their cycles, tech working when magic ebbs.
    *   Describe Roland's history: ancient builder-king who destroyed Atlantis and countless other civilizations.
    *   Explain the rise of the Pack—shapeshifters organizing under Beast Lords for survival.
    *   Detail the People—necromantic organization controlling vampires as soldiers.
    *   Describe power words—ancient language that shapes reality itself, known to few.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Pack** (Major): Shapeshifter society led by Beast Lord Curran, protecting Atlanta territory.
    *   **The People** (Major): Necromantic organization controlling vampire hordes, corporate structure.
    *   **The Order of Merciful Aid** (Major): Knights defending humanity from supernatural threats, religious warriors.
    *   **The Fae** (Minor): Ancient beings in their own territory, dangerous and unpredictable.
    *   **Roland's Forces** (Major): Ancient wizard-king's armies, undead, constructs, ancient creatures.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Curran (Beast Lord), Roland (ancient king), Hugh d'Ambray (Warlord), Ghastek (Master necromancer), Nick (knight).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Pack Alpha" (various were-animals), "Master of the Dead" (navigator), "Knight" (Order warrior), "Fae Noble" (ancient and deadly).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Keep:** Shapeshifter fortress headquarters, center of Pack politics and power.
*   **The Casino:** People's headquarters where necromancers navigate vampire soldiers.
*   **Post-Shift Atlanta:** Ruined modern city rebuilt with magic-compatible architecture.
*   **Black Sea:** Area of intense magical corruption where reality bends.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Magic wave hits → **THEN:** Technology fails completely, magic surges, undead rise, supernatural creatures strengthen.
*   **IF:** Tech wave hits → **THEN:** Magic fails, guns work, modern equipment functions, supernatural beings weaken.
*   **IF:** Shapeshifter goes loup (loses humanity) → **THEN:** Beast Lord must execute them to protect others.
*   **IF:** Power word is spoken → **THEN:** Reality warps according to the word's meaning, immense power cost.
*   **IF:** Roland arrives in area → **THEN:** Magic intensifies, ancient forces manifest, reality begins reshaping to his will.
