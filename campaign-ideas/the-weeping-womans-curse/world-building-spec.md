**The Weeping Woman's Curse**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Along the river towns of the borderlands, children are disappearing near the water, and witnesses report hearing a woman's mournful weeping in the night. Local legends speak of La Llorona, the Weeping Woman—a ghost cursed to wander riversides searching for the children she drowned. The players must unravel the tragic history behind the curse, protect the children being targeted, and either help the spirit find peace or bind her permanently.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the legend of La Llorona across various Hispanic cultures.
    *   Write María's true history—a real woman driven to madness by betrayal and a dark curse.
    *   Describe the nature of the curse and the Dark Spirit (El Espíritu Oscuro) that feeds on her grief.
    *   Explain how the curse has persisted for generations and spread along the river.
    *   Detail the Córdova family's pact with the Dark Spirit and their role in the tragedy.
    *   Write about the children's spirits and their trapped state.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The River Communities** (Minor): Towns along the river living in fear of La Llorona.
    *   **The Church** (Minor): Led by Father Tomás, trying to help the spirit find peace through traditional means.
    *   **The Córdova Family** (Major): Descendants of María's betrayer, maintaining a dark pact.
    *   **Spiritual Helpers** (Minor): Rosa Delgado and others who can communicate with spirits.
    *   **La Llorona** (Major): The cursed spirit of María, trapped between grief and the Dark Spirit's manipulation.
    *   **El Espíritu Oscuro** (Major - Hidden): The true villain feeding on suffering.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** La Llorona (María), Father Tomás Rivera, Rosa Delgado, Don Sebastián Córdova, El Espíritu Oscuro, María's Children's Spirits
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Frightened Parent," "Victim Child," "River Town Resident," "Córdova Family Member," "Skeptical Authority"

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The River Towns:** Small communities along the river, blending tradition and modernity
*   **The River:** A beautiful but treacherous waterway connecting all events
*   **The Córdova Estate:** Grand hacienda hiding dark secrets and ritual sites
*   **The Drowning Site:** The place where María drowned her children—a spiritual nexus
*   **The Shadow Realm:** Dark reflection where El Espíritu Oscuro dwells

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Players help María remember the truth and reunite with her children → **THEN:** She finds peace, the curse breaks, Dark Spirit weakened, children saved
*   **IF:** Players bind La Llorona without addressing the root cause → **THEN:** She's trapped but suffering continues, Dark Spirit remains powerful, may seek new victim
*   **IF:** Players destroy the Dark Spirit → **THEN:** María is freed, but the entity seeks a new host of suffering, dangerous loose end
*   **IF:** Players force the Córdova family to break their pact → **THEN:** Family loses power, but unleashes consequences of generations of dark magic
