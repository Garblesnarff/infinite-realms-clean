# Wheel of Fate - Creative Brief

## Visual Art Direction
- Carnival chaos: Spinning wheels, bright colors, games of chance everywhere
- Fortuna: Multiple arms spinning wheels, mad eyes seeing all probabilities, crowned with fortunes
- Pattern Keeper's realm: Sacred geometry, clockwork precision, sterile perfection
- Chaos Serpent: Kaleidoscopic madness, Escher impossibilities, beautiful disorder
- The Wheel: Massive cosmic spinner, four evangelists in corners (sphinx, snake, angel, lion)

## Music and Sound
- **Carnival Theme**: Chaotic carnival music, multiple songs at once, dizzying
- **Fortuna's Theme**: Mad laughter over probability calculations, chaos and math
- **Pattern Theme**: Perfect mechanical precision, clockwork, sterile
- **Chaos Theme**: No pattern, pure noise becoming beauty, random harmony
- **The Wheel**: Deep grinding, cosmic spinning, fate turning

## Voice Acting
- **Fortuna**: Mad prophet, laughing and crying, speaking in probabilities, "Forty-two percent chance of doom, ha ha HA!"
- **Pattern Keeper**: Calm, certain, believes fully in his mission, "I saved everyone by ensuring outcomes"
- **Chaos Serpent**: Joyful randomness, no pattern to speech, beautiful madness
- **The Wheel**: Deep, ancient, tired of being manipulated

## Taglines
- "Spin the Wheel, Accept Your Fate"
- "Fortune Favors the Bold, Then Abandons Them"
- "The Only Certainty Is Change"
