**Spirit Detective Chronicles**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** After heroic death, the party becomes Spirit Detectives hunting rogue demons with spirit energy weapons. They're forced into the Dark Tournament, survive deadly matches, and eventually enter Demon Realm politics. They must decide who rules demons and whether demons and humans can coexist.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the three realms—Spirit World (afterlife bureaucracy), Living World (Earth), Demon Realm (chaos).
    *   Write the history of the barrier between realms and why it weakens.
    *   Describe the class system for demons (D to S) and how power is measured.
    *   Explain King Enma's corruption and manipulation of both realms for control.
    *   Detail the Three Kings' origins and their philosophies for ruling.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** Spirit World (afterlife government), Spirit Detectives (enforcers), Dark Tournament Organization (criminal entertainment), Three Kings' Armies (demon territories).

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Koenma (prince), Hiei (fire demon), Kurama (fox spirit), Toguro (antagonist), Three Kings (Yomi, Mukuro, Raizen).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Demon Hunter," "Tournament Fighter," "Demon Lord," "Spirit World Bureaucrat."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Spirit World:** Bureaucratic afterlife with judgment halls, detention cells, Spirit Detective HQ.
*   **Dark Tournament Island:** Hidden island with arena, spectator stands, contestant barracks.
*   **Demon Realm:** Three territories with distinct cultures (strategic city, industrial wasteland, primal forest).

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Party develops spirit weapon → **THEN:** Can fight B-class demons, recognized as competent detectives.
*   **IF:** Party wins Dark Tournament → **THEN:** Gains territory, wealth, reputation; sponsors offer allegiance.
*   **IF:** Party enters Demon Realm → **THEN:** Face S-class threats, must choose which King to support.
*   **IF:** Party achieves S-class power → **THEN:** Can influence realm politics, compete in final tournament, decide future.
