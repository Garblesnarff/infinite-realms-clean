# Spirit Detective Chronicles

*A Spirit Detective / Demon Realm campaign — Long Campaign (18-24 sessions) — Medium to High difficulty*

---

## Campaign Overview

**Title:** Spirit Detective Chronicles
**Campaign Type / Genre:** Spirit Detective / Demon Realm / Tournament / Urban Fantasy
**Estimated Length:** Long Campaign — 18-24 sessions
**Player Level Range:** Start **2** → Finish **15-18**
**Tone (keywords):** spirit detective, demon realm, tournament arcs, spirit energy, redemption, demon world politics
**Core Premise (2-4 sentences):**
After a selfless act leads to their death, the party is resurrected by Spirit World to serve as Spirit Detectives—agents who hunt rogue demons and supernatural threats in the living world. Armed with spirit energy and aided by a spirit guide, they'll face increasingly powerful demons, compete in the Dark Tournament where fighters battle to the death, and eventually discover the Demon Realm's complex politics. The campaign culminates in determining who will rule the Demon Realm and whether demons and humans can coexist.

---

## Story Arc (Long Campaign — 18-24 sessions)

*   **Act I — Spirit Detective Work (Sessions 1-6):** The party awakens from death as Spirit Detectives. They hunt demons threatening the living world, learning to manifest spirit energy as weapons. They face the Four Saint Beasts who plan to open a portal for demon invasion, and rescue a demon who becomes their reluctant ally.
*   **Act II — The Dark Tournament (Sessions 7-14):** The party is kidnapped and forced to compete in the Dark Tournament—a deadly competition where demon teams fight to the death for their sponsors. They must win five matches against increasingly powerful teams while bonding with enemy fighters and learning about demon society's complexity.
*   **Act III — The Three Kings (Sessions 15-20):** The party enters the Demon Realm to face the Three Kings—ancient demon lords who rule different territories. They discover that Spirit World has manipulated both realms. A tournament is proposed to determine Demon Realm's next ruler peacefully. The party must decide which king to support and what future demons and humans will share.
*   **Act IV — Unification (Sessions 21-24):** The Demon Realm Tournament brings together the strongest fighters from across dimensions. The party competes alongside their former enemies and allies, showing that strength and honor transcend species boundaries. They determine the new order and forge lasting peace.

## Major NPCs

1.  **Koenma** — *The Spirit Prince*
    *   **Role:** Prince of Spirit World and the party's boss, coordinates Spirit Detective operations
    *   **Personality:** Appears as toddler with pacifier but is actually 700 years old, wants to prove himself
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** His father is the corrupt King Enma who views demons as mere tools

2.  **Hiei** — *The Forbidden Child*
    *   **Role:** S-class demon with fire powers and a third eye, starts as enemy becomes ally
    *   **Personality:** Cold, antisocial, values strength, secretly protective of his lost sister
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** Born as forbidden male to an all-female ice demon race, cast out as infant

3.  **Kurama** — *The Spirit Fox*
    *   **Role:** Ancient S-class demon reborn in human body, master of plant manipulation
    *   **Personality:** Intelligent, elegant, ruthless when needed, conflicted between human and demon nature
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** His human mother's love changed him—he'd sacrifice everything for her

4.  **Toguro** — *The Unbreakable*
    *   **Role:** Main antagonist of Dark Tournament, chose to become demon for power
    *   **Personality:** Nihilistic, believes strength is everything, seeks worthy death
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** Became demon after failing to protect students—power consumed his humanity

5.  **Yomi, Mukuro, Raizen** — *The Three Kings*
    *   **Role:** Ancient S-class demons ruling Demon Realm territories, each with unique philosophy
    *   **Personality:** Yomi (blind strategist), Mukuro (scarred cyborg warrior), Raizen (honorable beast)
    *   **Secret/Flaw:** Their war threatens to destroy both realms, seeking successors

## Key Locations

1.  **Spirit World:** Bureaucratic afterlife where King Enma judges souls and coordinates Spirit Detectives
2.  **The Living World:** Modern-day Earth where demons cause supernatural incidents
3.  **Hanging Neck Island:** Remote location hosting the Dark Tournament's fighting arena
4.  **Demon Realm:** Three territories ruled by the Kings, varying from civilized cities to savage wastelands
5.  **Tournament Grounds:** Various arenas for deadly competition—rings over lava, collapsing floors, dimensional barriers

---

## Central Conflict

This campaign explores themes of redemption, the nature of good and evil, strength versus morality, and whether different species can coexist. It asks: Are demons inherently evil or shaped by circumstances? Can warriors retain humanity while pursuing power? Is peace through strength possible? The central conflict shifts from hunting demons to understanding them to determining their future.

## Unique Mechanics

### 1. **Spirit Energy System**
*   Life force that can be weaponized through training
*   Manifests differently per person (projectiles, melee weapons, barriers, detection)
*   Characters develop signature techniques (Spirit Gun, Spirit Sword, Rose Whip, etc.)
*   Advanced users create Spirit Armor and massive area attacks

### 2. **Spirit Detective Arsenal**
*   Spirit items provided by Koenma: compasses that detect demons, communication devices, sealing artifacts
*   Develop personalized spirit weapons through emotional breakthrough
*   Can sense demon energy levels to gauge threats
*   Some gain demon abilities through transformations

### 3. **Demon Transformations**
*   Characters with demon heritage can release limiters
*   Each transformation has unique appearance (horns, markings, aura, size)
*   Power multiplies but may affect personality/control
*   S-class transformations rival natural disasters

### 4. **Tournament Rules**
*   Teams of five fight in sequence
*   Fight until death, surrender, or ring-out
*   Winning team advances bracket
*   Sponsors bet fortunes and territories
*   No outside interference—honor system surprisingly respected

### 5. **Territory System**
*   Demon Realm divided into territories controlled through strength
*   Challenging a territory lord requires proving combat ability
*   Ruling requires not just strength but leadership and vision
*   Territory wars can be waged or settled through proxy battles

### 6. **Demon Classes**
*   D-class: Weak demons (enhanced human level)
*   C-class: Dangerous demons (require spirit weapons)
*   B-class: Master demons (city-threatening)
*   A-class: Elite demons (nation-threatening)
*   S-class: Legendary demons (world-threatening, rare)
*   Characters start detecting then reaching each tier
