# Agents of the Concordat - Creative Brief

## Art Style and Visual Tone
- **Primary Style**: Retro-futuristic elegance meets alien bizarre
- **The Intrepid**: Sleek curves, art deco influences, comfortable luxury
- **Alien Worlds**: Each uniquely designed, no repeating aesthetics
- **Diversity**: Every species visually distinct and interesting
- **Wonder**: Emphasize the strange and beautiful

## Color Palettes
- **Concordat**: White and gold, diplomatic elegance
- **Each Alien World**: Unique palette (ocean world: blues, crystal world: prismatic, etc.)
- **Architects**: Sterile white shifting to ominous red
- **Convergence**: Blending all colors into unified spectrum
- **Space**: Varied nebulas, colorful phenomena

## Music and Sound Design
- **Main Theme**: Sweeping orchestral with exotic instruments
- **Each World**: Musical identity reflecting culture
- **Convergence**: Harmonies that shouldn't work but do
- **Action**: Energetic, varied styles depending on location
- **Discovery**: Wonder-filled, inspiring brass and strings

## Cinematic Moments
- **First Contact**: Each a unique visual and auditory experience
- **The Probability Storm**: Reality kaleidoscoping, visual impossibility
- **Archive Discovery**: Holographic records of extinct civilizations
- **Convergence Vision**: All minds connecting, beautiful and terrifying
- **The Choice**: Visualizing different futures simultaneously
- **"To seek out new life and new civilizations..."**

## Artistic Inspirations
- Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets (visual variety)
- Star Trek (exploration optimism)
- Doctor Who (diverse alien worlds)
- Fifth Element (retro-futuristic aesthetic)
