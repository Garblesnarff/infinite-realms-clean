**Dead Witch Walking**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** A witch bounty hunter in Cincinnati draws power from ley lines while partnering with a living vampire and pixie warrior. After breaking from corrupt I.S., they face assassination attempts, demon deals that mark their soul, and discovery of Wild Magic heritage. Elves want them dead, demons want their soul, and vampires want their blood. Found family of different species navigates Inderland politics while embracing dangerous power.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Turn—plague that killed human population, forcing Inderland species to reveal themselves.
    *   Write the history of Wild Magic and how it destroyed elven civilization.
    *   Describe ley lines—earth energy network that witches channel for power.
    *   Explain demon imprisonment in the ever-after and why they're desperate to escape.
    *   Detail vampire society—living vampires, undead masters, bloodlust and turning.
    *   Describe Cincinnati's position as ley line nexus and Inderland political center.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **I.S. (Inderland Security)** (Major): Law enforcement for supernatural species, corrupt and dangerous.
    *   **Vampire Houses** (Major): Undead masters ruling living vampires, complex politics.
    *   **Elven Community** (Major): Genetic purists, fear Wild Magic, conduct illegal experiments.
    *   **Demons** (Major): Imprisoned in ever-after, making deals to gain power and escape.
    *   **Witch Covens** (Minor): Traditional witches, secret knowledge, earth magic practitioners.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Jenks (pixie), Ivy (living vampire), Algaliarept (demon), Trent (elf), Newt (demon librarian).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Living Vampire" (struggling with bloodlust), "Undead Master" (terrifying power), "Demon" (various specialties), "Were" (pack member), "Witch" (coven).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Church:** Ley line node base, graveyard, defensible position.
*   **The Ever-After:** Demon prison dimension, twisted reality, dangerous to visit.
*   **Piscary's Pizza:** Vampire neutral ground, political meeting place.
*   **Kalamack Industries:** Elven business empire, genetic lab beneath.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Witch makes demon deal → **THEN:** Gains power but receives demon mark (smut), attracts demonic attention.
*   **IF:** Living vampire drinks from master → **THEN:** Can be turned undead, loses free will and humanity.
*   **IF:** Wild Magic is revealed → **THEN:** Elves send assassins, demons seek to control, becomes target for all factions.
*   **IF:** Too much smut accumulates → **THEN:** Demon can claim soul completely, other witches shun the corrupted.
*   **IF:** Demons escape ever-after → **THEN:** Reality threatened, species must unite or face destruction.
