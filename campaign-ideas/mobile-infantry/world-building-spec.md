### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**Mobile Infantry**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The players are Mobile Infantry recruits fighting the Arachnid threat in powered armor. Through brutal war, they navigate a fascistic society where citizenship requires military service, eventually discovering Arachnid intelligence and questioning whether their war is defense or genocide.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   Detail the Terran Federation's founding after nuclear wars unified Earth under military rule.
*   Explain powered armor technology and Mobile Infantry doctrine.
*   Describe the Arachnid Quarantine Zone and first contact war.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Terran Federation** (militaristic government), **Mobile Infantry** (elite troops), **Arachnids** (hive-mind species).

**4. NPC Roster**
*   Lt. Rasczak (mentor), Sgt. Zim (drill instructor), Sky Marshal Dienes (warmonger), Brain Bug (intelligent enemy).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   Camp Currie (training base), Klendathu (Arachnid homeworld), Planet P (outpost), Valley of Death.

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF** party captures Brain Bug **THEN** discover Arachnid intelligence.
*   **IF** party completes genocide **THEN** fascistic government strengthens.
*   **IF** party negotiates peace **THEN** court-martial but historical redemption.
