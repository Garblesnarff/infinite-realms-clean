### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

This document is the primary instruction set for the world-building AI pipeline. Each section provides explicit directives for specialized agents to generate the necessary assets for a complete and dynamic campaign world.

**Masks and Shadows**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Directive:** This is the foundational context. All generated content must align with this core premise.
*   **Content:** A popular student and a gloomy outcast accidentally discover each other's secret lives outside school. As they help each other navigate their dual identities, they fall in love and learn that their authentic selves are far more lovable than their carefully constructed facades.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Directive:** The Lore Generation Agent must create detailed entries for each of the following prompts.
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the academy's social hierarchy and the pressure to maintain certain personas.
    *   Write about the cultural importance of reputation and public image in this society.
    *   Describe how tattoos and piercings are viewed in this culture (potentially rebellious or taboo).
    *   Explain the common family structure where parents work long hours, leaving older siblings responsible.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Directive:** The Faction Generation Agent must create detailed profiles for each faction listed below.
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Popular Group** (Minor): Hori's school friends who know her public persona but not her private life.
    *   **The Outsider Group** (Minor): Students like Miyamura who are isolated or misunderstood.
    *   **The Friend Circle** (Major): Toru, Yuki, Sakura, Sengoku, and others who become genuine friends to both protagonists.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1 (Unique, Major NPCs):**
    *   **Kyoko Hori:** Perfect at school, domestic at home, sharp-tongued but caring.
    *   **Izumi Miyamura:** Gloomy-seeming at school, actually kind and gentle, hidden tattoos and piercings.
    *   **Toru Ishikawa:** Friendly fighter, initially crushing on Hori.
    *   **Yuki Yoshikawa:** Romantic ranger, supports the couple.
    *   **Souta Hori:** Hori's young brother, loves Miyamura.
    *   **Kakeru Sengoku & Sakura Kono:** Class president couple, earnest and sweet.

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Directive:** The Environment Generation Agent must create detailed blueprints for each location.
*   **Location Roster:**
    *   **The Academy:** School setting where public personas are maintained.
    *   **Hori's House:** Warm, modest home. Primary setting for relationship development. Kitchen, living room, Souta's room important.
    *   **The Park:** Friend group hangout spot.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** Miyamura reveals his tattoos/piercings to someone new.
    *   **THEN:** Test their reaction. Acceptance strengthens his confidence. His Social Comfort increases.
    *   **IF:** Hori and Miyamura's relationship becomes public.
    *   **THEN:** Initial surprise from classmates, but generally positive reactions. Their friend group is supportive.
    *   **IF:** Enough domestic scenes occur with genuine connection.
    *   **THEN:** Both characters' dual identities begin to merge. They show more of their authentic selves at school.
    *   **IF:** Final session shows full integration.
    *   **THEN:** Both are comfortable being themselves everywhere. Relationship is open. Friend group knows and loves their real selves. Generate epilogue of comfortable, authentic life together.
