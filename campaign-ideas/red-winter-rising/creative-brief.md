# Creative Brief: Red Winter Rising

## Art Style: Revolutionary Epic (Reds meets Doctor Zhivago)

Inspired by the revolutionary fervor of *Reds* and the epic scope of *Doctor Zhivago*. This style captures revolutionary idealism meeting brutal reality, civil war's horror, and the transformation of Russia from empire to Soviet state.

### Core Principles:
*   **Emphasize Contradiction:** Show revolutionary promises versus revolutionary methods.
*   **Red Winter:** Capture Russian winter as backdrop to revolutionary and civil war.
*   **Ideological Intensity:** Portray genuine belief driving brutal actions.

### Color Palette:
Revolutionary red dominating, Russian winter whites and grays, aristocratic golds fading, Soviet red stars and banners, the blood of civil war, the gray of Soviet bureaucracy emerging.

### Character Design:
Bolshevik revolutionaries in leather coats and caps with red stars, White Army officers in Imperial uniforms, workers and peasants in revolutionary fervor, aristocrats fallen, Lenin and Trotsky as revolutionary icons, secret police emerging.

## Voice
