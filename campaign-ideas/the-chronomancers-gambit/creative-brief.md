# The Chronomancer's Gambit - Creative Brief

## Art Style
Increasingly desperate and apocalyptic. Clean present deteriorates into ruined future. Ancient past mythological and grand.

## Colors
- **Present:** Normal, realistic
- **Future:** Desaturated grays, plague-green
- **Ancient Past:** Epic golds and reds
- **Temporal Effects:** Distortion and blur

## Music
**Main Theme:** Ticking clock with desperate strings, builds urgency

**Jump Sound:** Disorienting rush, reality tearing

**Plague Theme:** Creeping horror, biological wrongness

**Ancient War:** Epic orchestral, mythological scale

## Voice Direction
**Cassian:** Progressively more desperate and unhinged

**Vortigern:** Ancient authority, convinced of righteousness

**Harbinger:** Multiple voices, inhuman, disease given sound

## Cinematic Moments
- **Future Apocalypse:** Ruins and death, motivation established
- **Jumps Back:** Visual regression through time
- **Bootstrap Realization:** Horror of creating own problem
- **Ancient War:** Epic scope and moral complexity
- **Final Choice:** Weight of decision
