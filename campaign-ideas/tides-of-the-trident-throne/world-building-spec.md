# Tides of the Trident Throne - World Building Specification

## The Seven Kingdoms Complete

### 1. Atlantis (Capital, Neutral Territory)
**Ruler**: High King Thalassor (deceased), regent Queen Coralline
**Population**: 500,000 mixed races
**Location**: Central ocean, mid-depth
**Specialty**: Diplomacy, magic, trade
**Culture**: Cosmopolitan, meritocratic, center of learning
**Military**: 10,000 Royal Guard (elite mixed-race force)
**Stance**: Neutral mediator, weakened by assassination

### 2. Kingdom of Corallia (Merfolk)
**Ruler**: Princess Naia (heir), Council of Tides (regency)
**Population**: 2 million merfolk
**Location**: Tropical shallow reefs
**Specialty**: Art, healing magic, aquaculture
**Culture**: Peaceful, artistic, environmental stewards
**Military**: 50,000 Coral Guard (defensive specialists)
**Current Crisis**: Coral blight killing reefs, suspected sabotage

### 3. Typhon's Gate (Tritons)
**Ruler**: Warlord Kaelan
**Population**: 800,000 tritons
**Location**: Volcanic trenches, deep geothermal vents
**Specialty**: Metalwork, military might, engineering
**Culture**: Martial, honor-based, militaristic
**Military**: 150,000 Legion of the Deep (best trained force)
**Ambition**: Believe only military strength can unite kingdoms

### 4. The Verdant Shelves (Sea Elves)
**Ruler**: Archdruid Silvanus
**Population**: 600,000 sea elves
**Location**: Kelp forests, underwater groves
**Specialty**: Druidic magic, reconnaissance, archery
**Culture**: Isolationist, naturalist, ancient traditions
**Military**: 40,000 Kelp Rangers (guerrilla specialists)
**Concern**: Surface world deforestation affecting ocean

### 5. The Scaled Dominion (Sea Dragonborn)
**Ruler**: Emperor Dracos the Gold
**Population**: 300,000 dragonborn
**Location**: Underwater mountains, cave systems
**Specialty**: Arcane magic, treasure hoarding, sorcery
**Culture**: Hierarchical, wealth-based status, ancient pride
**Military**: 30,000 Dragon Guard (elite sorcerer-soldiers)
**Secret**: Hoarding legendary weapons, including alternatives to Trident

### 6. The Benthic Alliance (Various Deep Races)
**Ruler**: Council of Species (no single leader)
**Population**: 1.5 million (mix of many races)
**Location**: Mid-to-deep ocean, scattered settlements
**Specialty**: Trade, diversity, adaptability
**Culture**: Democratic, multicultural, practical
**Military**: 80,000 Allied Defense Force (largest but disorganized)
**Position**: Wants democratic reform, end to monarchy

### 7. The Twilight Reaches (Underdark Connection)
**Ruler**: Duchess Velara (Drow)
**Population**: 400,000 (drow, duergar, others)
**Location**: Deep caverns connecting to Underdark
**Specialty**: Subterfuge, assassination, poison-craft
**Culture**: Secretive, survivalist, distrusted by others
**Military**: 20,000 Shadow Swimmers (assassins and spies)
**Wildcard**: Could be ally or enemy depending on party diplomacy

## The Kraken Lords: Ancient Enemy

### History
- **12,000 years ago**: Kraken Lords ruled oceans through fear
- **10,000 years ago**: First Atlantis united kingdoms, imprisoned Kraken Lords in Abyss
- **Present**: Aboleth manipulation weakens prison, Kraken Lords awakening

### The Three Kraken Lords
**Tethys the Strangler**: Massive kraken, physical powerhouse, commands lesser krakens
**Charybdis the Devourer**: Creates whirlpools and maelstroms, chaos incarnate
**Scylla the Many**: Multiple heads, each different magic type, most cunning

### Kraken Forces
- Lesser krakens (kaiju-size creatures)
- Sahuagin armies (corrupted by Kraken Lords)
- Deep sea aberrations
- Controlled leviathans

## Factions and Politics

### Reformist Coalition (Pro-Democracy)
- Led by Benthic Alliance
- Supported by some Merfolk
- Wants to abolish monarchy, create elected council
- Sees assassination as opportunity for change

### Traditionalist Bloc (Pro-Monarchy)
- Led by Atlantis loyalists
- Supported by Dragonborn, some Tritons
- Princess Naia as rightful heir
- Maintain status quo with reforms

### Militarist Faction (Pro-Conquest)
- Led by Duke Kaelan and Typhon's Gate
- Some Dragonborn support
- Unify by force under military rule
- "Strength is only language everyone understands"

### Isolationists (Withdrawal)
- Led by Sea Elves
- Some Twilight Reaches support
- Close borders, each kingdom independent
- Risk: Leaves all vulnerable to external threats

### The Shadow Conspiracy (Hidden)
- Led by Deep Sage (Aboleth)
- Manipulating all sides
- Goal: Weaken kingdoms for Kraken Lord return
- Members in every faction

## The Trident of Sovereignty

### Properties
- Legendary artifact, symbol of rightful rule
- Only responds to "worthy" leader (DM defined criteria)
- Grants: Water control, command over ocean creatures, weather manipulation
- Proves legitimacyâ€”whoever wields it is recognized as rightful ruler
- Cannot be stolen, only claimed by worthy or given freely

### Worthiness Criteria (Options)
- Pure of heart (Princess Naia route)
- Strongest warrior (Duke Kaelan route)
- Most intelligent (Sage route)
- Chosen by kingdoms (Party mediates vote)
- Party member proves worthy (Player as ruler)
- Destroyed to create democracy (Reform ending)

## Surface World Threat

### United Surface Kingdoms
**Motivation**: Ocean kingdoms appear weak, opportunity for expansion
**Forces**: 200 warships, 100,000 marines
**Leader**: Admiral Grimfin (sympathetic, can be negotiated with)
**Technology**: Surface ships, arcane cannons, anti-water magic

**Diplomacy Options**:
- Prove ocean kingdoms united and strong (deters invasion)
- Ally with surface against Kraken Lords (temporary alliance)
- Defeat surface fleet (creates lasting enmity)
- Negotiate treaty (requires strong position)

## Environmental Mechanics

### Ocean Health System
- Kingdoms' political stability affects ocean environment
- War = pollution, overfishing, coral death
- Peace = thriving ecosystem, magical bounty
- Party can see direct consequences of their diplomatic failures

### The Coral Blight
- Mysterious disease killing Corallia's reefs
- Investigation reveals: Aboleth bio-weapon
- Cure requires cooperation from multiple kingdoms
- If uncured: Merfolk kingdom collapses, refugees flood others

## Extended NPC Roster

**Captain Maris** (Triton warrior): Kaelan's rival, believes in honor over conquest, potential ally
**Scholar Deepthought** (Aboleth, not evil): Helps party understand ancient history, reveals Deep Sage's conspiracy
**Prince Finneus** (Merfolk): Naia's brother, young, kidnapped to pressure Corallia
**Ambassador Krill** (Surface human): Peaceful envoy, tries to prevent war, assassinated by extremists
**The Prophetess** (Ancient Sea Hag): Cryptic warnings about past repeating, optional oracle
**Commodore Razorfin** (Sahuagin): Commander of Kraken Lord forces, redeemable or irredeemable villain

## Historical Timeline

- **12,000 years ago**: Kraken Lord tyranny
- **10,000 years ago**: Seven Kingdoms unite, first High King, imprison Kraken Lords
- **8,000 years ago**: Golden age of Atlantis
- **5,000 years ago**: Civil war nearly destroys empire, reforms instituted
- **2,000 years ago**: Surface-Ocean treaty established
- **500 years ago**: Last external threat defeated
- **50 years ago**: Thalassor crowned, era of peace begins
- **Present**: Thalassor assassinated, empire in crisis

## Causality Chains

### Assassination Conspiracy
1. Deep Sage wants Kraken Lords freed (promised power as reward)
2. Needs kingdoms weakened and divided
3. Manipulates extremists in multiple kingdoms
4. Provides poison to Twilight Reaches assassin
5. Assassin kills Thalassor during public summit
6. Kingdoms immediately suspect each other
7. While they fight, prison weakens
8. Deep Sage performs rituals to accelerate Kraken Lord awakening
9. If kingdoms unite, rituals fail
10. If kingdoms stay divided, Kraken Lords escape fully

### Path to Unity
1. Party investigates, discovers multiple conspiracies
2. Reveal true enemy to all kingdoms
3. Ancient threat forces cooperation
4. Old prejudices challenged by necessity
5. Heroes from each kingdom work together
6. Success against Krakens proves unity works
7. New government structure reflects cooperation
8. Ocean enters new golden age or fragments again based on choices
