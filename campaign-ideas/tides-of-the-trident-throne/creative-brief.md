# Tides of the Trident Throne - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Aquatic High Fantasy
**Inspiration**: Aquaman, Game of Thrones, Lord of the Rings underwater
**Aesthetic**: Regal, colorful, diverse kingdoms each with distinct visual identity
**Tone**: Political drama meets epic fantasy warfare

## Color Palettes by Kingdom
- **Atlantis**: Royal blues, gold trim, marble white, imperial purple
- **Corallia**: Vibrant coral pinks, oranges, tropical yellows
- **Typhon's Gate**: Volcanic reds, obsidian black, bronze metals
- **Elven Shelves**: Emerald greens, natural browns, silver accents
- **Kraken Depths**: Deep purple, bioluminescent blue, void black

## Voice Direction
**Princess Naia**: Youthful idealism, genuine hope, British accent, grows stronger through campaign
**Duke Kaelan**: Military authority, controlled aggression, slight gruffness, Russian accent
**Lady Seraphine**: Silky, dangerous, knows more than she says, French accent
**Queen Coralline**: Maternal grief, quiet strength, dignified, African accent
**Deep Sage (Aboleth)**: Multiple voices layered, ancient, manipulative, no accent (alien)

## Cinematic Moments
- **The Assassination**: Slow-motion as Thalassor falls, party catches him, dying words
- **First Kingdom Vista**: Atlantis revealed in full grandeur, spires reaching toward light
- **The Uniting**: Seven banners raise together, kingdoms alliance forged
- **Kraken Rising**: Massive tentacles emerge from abyss, ancient horror returns
- **Coronation**: New ruler crowned or democracy established, new era begins

## Inspirational Media
Visual: Aquaman (2018), Avatar underwater scenes, Finding Nemo/Dory
Audio: Game of Thrones OST, Lord of the Rings OST, Pirates of the Caribbean
Thematic: Game of Thrones (politics), Avatar: The Last Airbender (unity)
