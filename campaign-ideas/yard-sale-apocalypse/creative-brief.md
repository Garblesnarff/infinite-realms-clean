# Yard Sale Apocalypse - Creative Brief

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Style**: Suburban apocalypse, end-of-world countdown
- **Colors**: Normal suburbia with doomsday orange price tags
- **Lighting**: Summer morning brightness masking darkness
- **Scale**: Lawn becomes battlefield, price tags like gravestones

## Character Design
- Toys showing wear from years of play
- Price tags attached = marked for sale
- Boxed toys = packed for moving (safe)
- Dirty toys = headed for trash
- Each toy's condition determines value/fate

## Environment
- **Playroom**: Being dismantled, boxes everywhere
- **Garage**: Sorting center, toys judged and priced
- **Front Lawn**: Market of souls, blankets with toys displayed
- **Moving Truck**: Escape or salvation, depending on perspective
- **The Collector's Car**: Clean, organized, horrifying

## Music Direction
- **Countdown Theme**: Clock ticking, time running out
- **Garage Sorting**: Industrial, cold, judgment
- **Sale Day**: Chaotic marketplace, voices, cash register
- **Separation Scenes**: Heartbreaking strings, goodbyes
- **Collector Theme**: Music box, pristine, lifeless

## Voice Acting
- **Major Tom**: Desperate leader, military bearing cracking
- **Scout**: Revolutionary, freedom fighter, hopeful
- **Bella**: Optimistic despite fear, maternal
- **Rust**: Philosophical, accepting, weary
- **The Collector**: Enthusiastic but tone-deaf to toy consciousness
- **Timmy**: Sad child forced to choose

## Marketing Tagline
"One family's trash. One collector's treasure. Every toy's apocalypse."
