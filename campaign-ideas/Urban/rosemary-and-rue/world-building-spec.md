**Rosemary and Rue**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** A changeling knight and PI navigates Bay Area faerie courts while cursed to solve a countess's murder in three days or die. They use blood magic to see truth, navigate fae politics where lying is impossible but deception thrives, and uncover conspiracies involving the false queen. Discovery of hidden bloodline connects them to Firstborn—original children of Oberon—granting unexpected power and making them target.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the structure of fae courts—nobility, races, hierarchy, and political systems.
    *   Write the history of Oberon and Titania's departure, leaving Firstborn to watch over fae.
    *   Describe blood magic—how tasting blood reveals memories and truth at a cost.
    *   Explain changeling status—half-fae, half-human, belonging to neither world.
    *   Detail hope chests and their role in fae inheritance and political power.
    *   Describe the false queen's century-long reign and how she replaced the true queen.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Kingdom of the Mists** (Major): Bay Area fae courts under (false) Queen's rule.
    *   **The Cait Sidhe** (Major): Cat-fae with their own kingdom, proud and territorial.
    *   **Daoine Sidhe** (Major): High fae nobility, rulers of most courts, complex politics.
    *   **The Undersea** (Minor): Merfolk courts, distinct from land fae.
    *   **Firstborn** (Minor): Original children of Oberon/Titania/Maeve, godlike power.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Tybalt (Cait Sidhe King), The Luidaeg (sea witch Firstborn), Sylvester (duke), False Queen, Evening (deceased Firstborn).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Fae Noble" (various races and ranks), "Changeling" (various heritages), "Cait Sidhe" (cat-fae), "Selkie" (seal-folk).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Shadowed Hills:** Sylvester's knowe, elegant and welcoming fae domain.
*   **The Court of Cats:** Tybalt's territory in San Francisco shadows.
*   **The Luidaeg's Apartment:** Sea witch's home where truth is told and debts collected.
*   **The Summerlands:** Deeper Faerie, beautiful and deadly, alien rules.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Blood magic is used → **THEN:** Truth revealed but physical cost to user, attracts attention.
*   **IF:** Fae speaks direct lie → **THEN:** Impossible; they die or are magically prevented.
*   **IF:** Hope chest is opened → **THEN:** Reveals inheritance and artifacts, triggers political consequences.
*   **IF:** Changeling's true heritage revealed → **THEN:** Social status changes dramatically, becomes target or power.
*   **IF:** False queen is exposed → **THEN:** Kingdom restructures, civil war risk, party gains or loses everything.
