### **Template: World-Building Specification Brief**

**Can You Dig It?**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Street gang framed for killing visionary leader Cyrus must journey 30 miles through hostile New York City to reach home while every gang in the city hunts them. Survive the night through each gang territory, prove innocence, and earn reputation as the best. Can you dig it?

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the gang structure of 1970s New York and territorial divisions.
    *   Write Cyrus's history and his vision to unite all gangs.
    *   Describe the creation and codes of the major gangs.
    *   Explain the Warriors' reputation before and after the summit night.
    *   Detail the DJ's role and how radio connects all gang territories.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Warriors** (Major): Party's gang from Coney Island, framed for murder.
    *   **The Gramercy Riffs** (Major): Most powerful gang, Cyrus's people, seeking justice.
    *   **The Rogues** (Major): Villain gang who killed Cyrus, framing Warriors.
    *   **Various Themed Gangs** (Minor): Baseball Furies, Lizzies, Punks, Orphans, Turnbull ACs—each controls territory.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Cyrus (visionary leader), Luther (mad dog killer), Masai (Riffs war chief), The DJ (omniscient voice), Swan (acting Warriors leader).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Gang Member" (themed to their gang), "Neutral Observer" (civilians), "Police" (external threat), "Mercedes" (witness/ally).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Van Cortlandt Park:** Northern Bronx, summit location, ground zero.
*   **Various Gang Territories:** Each neighborhood with distinct gang control and aesthetics.
*   **The Subway System:** Underground network connecting all territories.
*   **Riverside Park:** Baseball Furies territory, trees and open fields.
*   **Coney Island Beach:** Warriors' home turf, final confrontation site.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Cyrus killed → **THEN:** All gangs mobilize, Warriors framed, city-wide manhunt.
*   **IF:** Party enters gang territory → **THEN:** Fight or negotiate based on gang and reputation.
*   **IF:** Unity maintained → **THEN:** Combo attacks, saves allies, stronger together.
*   **IF:** Reputation earned → **THEN:** Some gangs become neutral or helpful.
*   **IF:** Truth revealed → **THEN:** Riffs acknowledge innocence, target shifts to Rogues.
*   **IF:** Dawn arrives → **THEN:** Survived the night, safety, status as legends.
