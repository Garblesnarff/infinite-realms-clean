**Sharn: City of Towers**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In Sharn, the vertical City of Towers where magic fuels industry, the party works as inquisitives investigating a murder. They uncover a conspiracy involving warforged rights, dragonmarked house monopolies, and a plot to restart the devastating Last War. They must navigate noir intrigue, house politics, and questions of artificial sentience.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Last War—century-long conflict that devastated Khorvaire and destroyed nation of Cyre.
    *   Write the history of dragonmarked houses and their monopolistic control of services.
    *   Describe the creation of warforged by House Cannith as soldiers, now seeking personhood.
    *   Explain the Treaty of Thronehold that ended the war and banned warforged creation.
    *   Detail the Day of Mourning when Cyre became the Mournland—unexplained magical catastrophe.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Twelve Dragonmarked Houses** (Major): Noble families with hereditary magical marks, control commerce.
    *   **House Cannith** (Major): Artificers and makers, split into three factions, created warforged.
    *   **The Lord of Blades** (Major): Warforged nationalist building independent nation in Mournland.
    *   **The Emerald Claw** (Minor): Terrorist organization serving the Blood of Vol.
    *   **Sharn City Watch** (Minor): Law enforcement struggling with corruption and house influence.
    *   **Warforged Rights Movement** (Minor): Activists seeking legal personhood for constructs.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Merrix d'Cannith (house leader), Lord of Blades (warforged revolutionary), Aarren d'Cannith (mad artificer), Captain Kalaes (terrorist), Iron (warforged ally).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "House Agent" (marked operative), "Warforged Laborer" (seeking freedom), "City Watch" (corrupt or honest), "Artificer" (maker), "War Veteran" (traumatized survivor).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Upper Sharn:** Wealthy towers with sky gardens, airship docks, floating restaurants, house enclaves.
*   **Middle Dura:** Working-class district with party's office, markets, honest living.
*   **The Cogs:** Industrial underbelly with forges, warforged ghettos, criminals, darkness.
*   **House Cannith Enclave:** Fortified tower complex with workshops, laboratories, and secrets.
*   **Mournland Border:** Dead-gray wasteland where Cyre was, reality warped and dangerous.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party supports warforged rights → **THEN:** Gains warforged allies but angers conservative houses.
*   **IF:** Dragonmark research is stolen → **THEN:** Houses become paranoid, increase security, hunt thieves.
*   **IF:** Conspiracy exposed publicly → **THEN:** Political crisis, possible treaty collapse, war risk.
*   **IF:** Marked warforged are created → **THEN:** Houses lose monopolies, economic chaos, legal battles.
*   **IF:** Aarren's plot succeeds → **THEN:** Sharn destroyed, war restarts, thousands die.
*   **IF:** Treaty preserved → **THEN:** Fragile peace continues but underlying tensions remain.
