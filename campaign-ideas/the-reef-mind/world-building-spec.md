# World-Building Specification: The Reef Mind

## The Prismatic Barrier - Complete Ecology

### Physical Specifications
- **Length**: 1,500 miles along tropical coast
- **Width**: 30-150 miles from shore
- **Depth**: Surface to 300 feet
- **Total area**: ~130,000 square miles
- **Coral polyp count**: Estimated 500 trillion individual organisms
- **Age**: 10,000 years of continuous growth
- **Core temperature**: 78-82°F (healthy), currently rising to 85°F+ in affected areas

### The Reef as Organism

**Consciousness Architecture:**
- Each coral polyp = neuron analog
- Chemical signals between polyps = neurotransmitters
- Electrical impulses through calcium carbonate = neural firing
- Consciousness emerges from network of trillions
- Processing speed: One "thought" takes days to weeks
- Memory storage: Geological record in coral skeleton

**Intelligence Level:**
- IQ equivalent: Unmeasurable (thinks differently than humans)
- Specializations: Ecological management, pattern recognition, long-term planning
- Limitations: Thinks very slowly, struggles with rapid change
- Strengths: Perfect memory, parallel processing, spatial reasoning across thousands of miles

### Historical Development

**10,000 Years Ago: The First Thought**
- Single coral colony reached critical mass (~1 million polyps)
- First emergent consciousness: "I grow"
- Took 100 years to develop sense of self

**9,000-5,000 Years Ago: Expansion**
- Reef spreads along coast, consciousness grows with it
- Develops sub-minds to manage different functions
- Learns to shape its own growth deliberately
- First partnerships with fish species, creating symbiotic relationships

**5,000-1,000 Years Ago: Stewardship**
- Reef takes active role in ocean management
- Controls fish migrations, nutrient cycles, storm energy
- Protects coastline from erosion and tsunamis
- Becomes aware of surface dwellers, begins subtle communication attempts

**1,000-100 Years Ago: First Contact**
- Human Coralseers develop communion techniques
- Reef learns language, concepts, begins true dialogue
- Partnership forms: Reef protects coast, humans protect Reef
- Golden age of cooperation

**100 Years Ago-Present: The Decline**
- Human populations explode, cities expand
- Pollution increases, ocean temperature rises
- Reef tries to adapt but changes happen too fast
- 20 years ago: First bleaching event, Reef doesn't understand
- 5 years ago: Bleaching becomes chronic, sub-minds begin fracturing
- Present: Reef is dying and fragmenting simultaneously

## The Sub-Minds - Detailed Specifications

### The Navigator (Order/Control)
**Function**: Manages water flow, fish migrations, nutrient distribution
**Location**: Northern 400 miles of Reef
**Manifestation**: Geometric coral patterns, perfect mazes, symmetrical growth
**Psychology**: Obsessive-compulsive, terrified of chaos, believes control = survival
**Problem**: Trying to micromanage everything, strangling natural processes
**Communication**: Speaks in precise measurements, directions, coordinates
**Domain Features**:
- Current Labyrinths: Tunnels that guide fish exactly where Navigator wants
- The Perfect Spiral: Mile-wide coral formation in perfect Fibonacci sequence
- Flow Gates: Coral structures that regulate current speed

**Reconciliation Requirements**:
1. Navigate the impossible maze it created (Intelligence/Wisdom challenge)
2. Prove that some chaos is healthy (break symmetry to save trapped fish)
3. Show trust by letting it guide you somewhere without knowing destination
4. Final acceptance: "I cannot control everything. And that is... acceptable."

### The Gardener (Growth/Creation)
**Function**: Manages coral reproduction, growth, species diversity
**Location**: Southern 400 miles of Reef
**Manifestation**: Explosive overgrowth, every color imaginable, chaotic beauty
**Psychology**: Panicking, trying to grow fast enough to replace what dies
**Problem**: Overgrowth choking itself, invasive species taking over, no pruning
**Communication**: Rapid, excited, talks about growth rates and spawn cycles
**Domain Features**:
- The Spawn Fields: Annual coral spawning grounds, now happening monthly
- Overgrowth Canopy: Coral growing on top of coral, three layers deep
- The Chokepoint: Where overgrowth has completely blocked a channel

**Reconciliation Requirements**:
1. Help prune overgrowth manually (difficult physical challenge)
2. Show that old, stable coral is beautiful too (Nature check to identify ancient formations)
3. Introduce concept of sustainable cycles (Persuasion to teach patience)
4. Final acceptance: "Growth without wisdom is cancer. I will learn restraint."

### The Dreamer (Memory/History)
**Function**: Stores long-term memory, learns from past cycles, maintains identity
**Location**: Central 400 miles, the oldest reef sections
**Manifestation**: Visions of the past, ghostly re-enactments, crystal-clear memories
**Psychology**: Depressed, living in past when Reef was whole and world made sense
**Problem**: Ignoring present reality, letting its domain die through neglect
**Communication**: Speaks of past events as if they're happening now
**Domain Features**:
- Memory Grottos: Caves where coral patterns record historical data
- The Vision Pools: Water that shows the past when touched
- Fossil Fields: Ancient dead coral that holds earliest memories

**Reconciliation Requirements**:
1. Experience key memories (roleplay historical moments Reef shares)
2. Find and preserve most important memory in permanent form (Quest item)
3. Show present danger that requires its attention (Dramatic confrontation)
4. Final acceptance: "The past shapes us, but we live in now. I remember... and I wake."

### The Fury (Defense/Survival)
**Function**: Protects Reef from predators, disease, physical threats
**Location**: Eastern 300 miles, most damaged bleached sections
**Manifestation**: Violent currents, sharp coral spikes, aggressive predator summoning
**Psychology**: Wounded, betrayed, sees all surface dwellers as enemies
**Problem**: Can't distinguish between guilty and innocent, wants genocide
**Communication**: Roars, accusations, demands for vengeance
**Domain Features**:
- The Scarred Reaches: Miles of bleached white coral bone
- Spike Fields: Defensive coral grown into lethal formations
- The Graveyard: Where Fury piles bodies of its victims

**Reconciliation Requirements**:
1. Survive its initial attacks without killing its defenders (Combat restraint)
2. Show evidence of surface dwellers trying to help (Bring witnesses, proof)
3. Punish the truly guilty (Bring justice to polluters, showing you're on its side)
4. Final acceptance: "Not all surface-breathers are killers. Some... some are healers."

## Coastal Civilization

**Population**: 100,000 (60% human, 20% merfolk, 15% sea elf, 5% other)

**Government**:
- Merchant Senate (15 elected representatives)
- Current leader: Senator Marlowe (human male, 55, pro-business, anti-regulation)

**Economy**:
- 40%: Fishing and aquaculture
- 25%: Coral trade (jewelry, construction material, magical components)
- 20%: Shipping and trade hub
- 10%: Tourism to the Reef
- 5%: Other industries

**Districts**:
1. **The High Terraces**: Wealthy estates on highest coral platforms, above storm surge
2. **Coral Market Square**: Commercial center, shops selling reef products
3. **The Docks**: Industrial port, fishing fleets, cargo ships
4. **Temple District**: Coralseer Temple, various ocean deity shrines
5. **The Bleached Quarter**: Slums built on dead coral, poorest citizens
6. **Merchant Row**: Banking, trading companies, guild halls
7. **The Grotto**: Underwater merfolk section, accessible via magic/diving

**Key Locations**:
- **Coralseer Temple**: Monastery where communion is taught, houses Magister Delphine
- **The Senate Hall**: Coral-carved amphitheater for government meetings
- **Saltborn Shipping Company**: Captain Theros's employer, major polluter
- **The Reef Museum**: Educational center, currently in denial about crisis
- **Stormbreak Wall**: Massive coral barrier that protects harbor (maintained by Reef)

## The Bleaching Plague - Scientific Detail

### Cause: Sunheart Sphere Malfunction

**The Artifact**:
- Created 5,000 years ago by ancient civilization (pre-dates current surface cultures)
- Purpose: Regulate ocean temperature in region
- Mechanism: Draws geothermal energy, releases it or contains it
- Original setting: Maintain 78-80°F
- Current state: Corrupted to release massive heat, water now 85-88°F

**How Sahaugin Corrupted It**:
- 10 years ago: Prophet Ss'thakara discovers Sunheart in deep reef
- She interprets it as divine artifact
- Experiments with controls, accidentally increases temperature
- Notices coral bleaching follows, interprets as "purification"
- Establishes cult around it, actively prevents it from cooling

**Bleaching Process**:
1. Elevated temperature stresses coral polyps
2. Polyps expel symbiotic algae (zooxanthellae) that give coral color and food
3. Without algae, coral appears white (bleached)
4. Coral can survive weeks without algae, but slowly starves
5. Dead coral becomes brittle, breaks apart, ecosystem collapses

**Chain Reaction**:
- Dead coral = lost neurons in Reef Mind
- As sections die, consciousness fragments
- Stress causes remaining sections to malfunction
- Sub-minds separate to preserve local function
- If not stopped, complete death is inevitable

### The Cure

**Three-Part Solution Required**:

1. **Restore Sunheart**:
   - Reclaim from sahaugin (combat/diplomacy)
   - Recalibrate to original settings (Arcana/History checks to decipher ancient controls)
   - Stabilize temperature to 78-80°F
   - Result: Bleaching stops spreading

2. **Reunify Sub-Minds**:
   - Reconcile all four (individual quest lines)
   - Perform communion ritual linking them
   - Re-establish unified consciousness
   - Result: Reef can coordinate healing efforts

3. **Surface Cooperation**:
   - Reduce pollution (requires city cooperation)
   - Stop destructive coral harvesting
   - Establish sustainable practices
   - Result: Remove ongoing stress factors, allow natural recovery

**Timeline**:
- With all three: Full healing in 5-10 years
- With two of three: Stabilization but permanent scars
- With one of three: Slow death delayed but inevitable
- With none: Death within 6 months

## Sahaugin Cult - Details

### The Heat Worshippers

**Beliefs**:
- The cold darkness of the deep is death
- Heat is life, light, purity
- The Reef Mind is false god that keeps ocean in eternal stagnation
- Bleaching is purification, returning ocean to primal chaos
- From chaos, true freedom emerges

**Structure**:
- Prophet Ss'thakara: Charismatic leader, true believer
- The Inner Circle: 12 sahaugin who operate the Sunheart
- The Faithful: ~200 sahaugin cultists who guard the Abyssal Gardens
- The Converted: Other deep-sea species recruited to the cause

**Their City: Thermia**:
- Built in bleached coral graveyards
- Architecture: Sharp, aggressive, anti-organic
- Population: 500
- Features: The Hot Springs (geothermal vents they worship), The Sunheart Temple, Barracks

**Combat Tactics**:
- Use heated water as weapon (scalding jets)
- Summon heat-adapted predators (thermal vent worms, fire coral)
- Fanatic willingness to die for cause
- Will destroy Sunheart rather than surrender it

**Redemption Path**:
- Reveal that Sunheart is ancient tool, not divine
- Show that bleaching kills ALL ocean life, including sahaugin food sources
- Confront Prophet with truth about her clutch's death (Gardener's accidental overgrowth)
- Offer alternative: Help calibrate Sunheart to optimal temperature for ALL species
- Outcome: Some cultists can be redeemed, join healing effort

## Magic System Integration

### Coral Magic

**Reef-Granted Spells** (Available to attuned characters):

**Cantrip: Coral Growth**
- Cause small piece of coral to grow rapidly
- Can create handholds, seal small leaks, etc.
- 1-foot growth per casting

**2nd Level: Commune with Reef**
- Enhanced version of communion, can ask specific questions
- Reef responds with visions or emotions
- Range: Must be touching living coral

**4th Level: Summon Reef Guardian**
- Call forth construct made of living coral
- Use Earth Elemental statistics but appears as coral formation
- Duration: 1 hour
- Granted by Fury after reconciliation

**6th Level: Memory Vision**
- Experience specific event from Reef's 10,000-year history
- Grants knowledge, potential skill proficiency for 24 hours
- Granted by Dreamer after reconciliation

**8th Level: Tidal Shaping**
- Control ocean currents in 1-mile radius
- Can create calm water, riptides, or localized whirlpools
- Concentration, up to 10 minutes
- Granted by Navigator after reconciliation

### Consequences of Failure
- If Reef dies, all Reef-granted magic ceases functioning permanently
- Characters with Breathing Coral lose water breathing
- Coralseers with coral-lung die within days (coral dies inside them)
- Tsunami destroys all coastal magical infrastructure
