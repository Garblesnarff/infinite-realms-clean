### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**Defender of the Holy City**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Knights in Crusader Jerusalem must defend the Holy City during Saladin's siege, navigating moral complexity where the Muslim enemy proves more honorable than Christian zealots. Through siege warfare and political choices, they decide whether Jerusalem's stones or its people matter more.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Crusades—why Europeans invaded Holy Land, establishment of Crusader kingdoms.
    *   Write Baldwin IV's reign—leper king who maintained peace through wisdom.
    *   Describe Jerusalem's importance to three faiths—Christian, Muslim, Jewish.
    *   Explain Saladin's rise—uniting Muslim world to reclaim Jerusalem.
    *   Chronicle Guy's incompetence and Reynald's provocations leading to war.
    *   Detail the Battle of Hattin—devastating Crusader defeat.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** Moderate Crusaders (Major), Zealot Crusaders (Major), Saladin's Army (Major), Jerusalem Citizens (Major), Knights Templar (Minor), Muslim Moderates (Minor)

**4. Causality Chains**
*   **IF:** Party shows mercy to civilians → **THEN:** Saladin offers better terms.
*   **IF:** Party commits atrocities → **THEN:** Saladin swears to massacre city.
*   **IF:** Walls fall → **THEN:** Final choice of suicide defense vs negotiation.
*   **IF:** Party respects enemy → **THEN:** Mutual respect develops across battle lines.
