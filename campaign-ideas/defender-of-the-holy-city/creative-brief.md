# Creative Brief: Defender of the Holy City

## Art Style: Epic Medieval with Middle Eastern Influences

Inspired by *Kingdom of Heaven* and historical Crusades. Captures the golden architecture of Jerusalem, the brutal reality of siege warfare, the cultural blend of East and West, and the moral ambiguity of religious conflict.

### Core Principles:

*   **Emphasize Sacred Beauty:** Jerusalem's golden stone, religious architecture, sacred sites
*   **Cultural Blend:** Crusader and Muslim aesthetics mixing, both beautiful and martial
*   **Siege Scale:** Massive walls, armies, engines of war, human cost
*   **Moral Complexity:** No clear villains in main conflict, sympathetic characters on both sides

### Color Palette:

*   **Jerusalem:** Golden-white stone, blue sky, green gardens within walls
*   **Crusaders:** Steel armor, white surcoats with red crosses, practical earth tones
*   **Muslims:** Curved scimitars, flowing robes, greens and golds, ornate decoration
*   **Siege:** Fire and smoke, blood on stone, dust clouds, sunset oranges
