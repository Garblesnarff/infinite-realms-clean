# The Leviathan Hunt - Creative Brief

## Art Style: Monster Hunter Oceanic
**Inspiration**: Monster Hunter series, Moby Dick, The Meg, Pacific Rim, Shadow of the Colossus
**Aesthetic**: Hunters vs. massive creatures, David vs. Goliath scale, epic scope
**Tone**: Adventure and danger, respect for prey, brutal beauty of the hunt

## Color Palettes
- **Tidebreakers**: Practical blues, grays, worn leather, salt-stained gear
- **Leviathans**: Each unique—Great Whites (gray/white), Krakens (purple-black), Dragon Turtle (emerald-gold)
- **Ocean Combat**: Churning water, blood in water, storm grays, intense blues
- **Victory**: Sunset oranges as hunters return with trophy

## Music
- **Main Theme**: "The Hunt Begins"—drums and horns, epic and primal
- **Preparation**: Tense strings, focus and planning
- **The Chase**: Increasing tempo, percussion building
- **Boss Battle**: Full orchestral, choirs, massive scale
- **Victory**: Triumphant but respectful, honoring worthy prey
- **Tragedy**: When prey becomes sympathetic, mournful strings

## Voice Direction
**Captain Mara**: Gruff authority, war veteran, female voice, tough love mentor
**Finn Hookhand**: Jovial, storyteller, slight pirate accent, hides pain with humor
**Scholar Deepwhisper**: Calm, intelligent, passionate about conservation, melodic
**Leviathan King**: Deep resonant, ancient, patient, not hostile but firm
**Admiral Stormwright**: Military precision, cold calculation, sees monsters not animals

## Cinematic Moments
- **First Major Hunt**: Small boat facing massive beast, scale and danger
- **The Leviathan King Reveal**: Miles-long dragon turtle emerges, party is tiny
- **Victory Feast**: Guild celebrates with song, food, and glory
- **Moby's Appearance**: Legendary white whale breaches, unstoppable force
- **Final Battle**: Ultimate hunt or peace negotiation, fate of ocean decided

## Inspirational Media
Visual: Monster Hunter games, Moby Dick, The Meg, Pacific Rim, God of War (sea serpent)
Audio: Monster Hunter OST, Pirates of the Caribbean, Jaw's theme (suspense)
Thematic: Moby Dick (obsession), Princess Mononoke (nature vs. humanity)
