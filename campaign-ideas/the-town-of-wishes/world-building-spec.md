**The Town of Wishes**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** A delinquent helps a fragile girl achieve her dream, falling in love and discovering purpose. Their story continues beyond school into marriage, tragedy, and redemption, while a supernatural town grants wishes to those who help others. Collecting enough "light orbs" through kindness allows for a miracle that can change everything.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the legend of the town itself as a living entity that grants wishes.
    *   Write about the illusionary world and how it parallels the real world.
    *   Describe Nagisa's mystical connection to the town from her childhood accident.
    *   Explain the light orbs as manifestations of gratitude and happiness.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:**
    *   **The Furukawa Family:** Loving, supportive, the emotional center.
    *   **The Drama Club:** Students helping each other overcome struggles.
    *   **The Town:** Not a faction but a living entity responding to happiness and suffering.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:**
    *   **Nagisa Furukawa:** Fragile cleric, dreams of drama, deeply loving.
    *   **Tomoya Okazaki:** Delinquent fighter, cynical but kind, broken relationship with father.
    *   **Sanae & Akio Furukawa:** Wonderfully supportive parents.
    *   **Ushio Okazaki:** Tomoya and Nagisa's daughter, embodiment of hope and redemption.
    *   **Youhei Sunohara:** Best friend, comic relief, loyal.

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Academy:** Where romance begins, under cherry blossoms.
    *   **The Furukawa Bakery:** Warm family home, central gathering place.
    *   **The Illusionary World:** Stark white planes, endless winter, where the lonely girl and doll collect light orbs.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** Players successfully help a heroine resolve her personal crisis.
    *   **THEN:** Earn a light orb. That heroine becomes a supportive friend. The town becomes slightly healthier.
    *   **IF:** Tomoya and Nagisa's relationship deepens.
    *   **THEN:** Nagisa's health improves temporarily. The town flourishes.
    *   **IF:** Nagisa dies in childbirth (Part Two begins).
    *   **THEN:** Tomoya breaks completely. Five-year time skip. Ushio is being raised by grandparents. The town falls into subtle decay.
    *   **IF:** Tomoya reconnects with Ushio and becomes a father.
    *   **THEN:** His purpose returns. The town begins healing. More light orbs can be earned through fatherhood.
    *   **IF:** At the climax, Tomoya has earned enough light orbs and chooses to use them for a miracle.
    *   **THEN:** Time resets to Nagisa's childbirth. This time she survives. Ushio is born healthy. Generate epilogue showing the family together, happy, under cherry blossoms. The illusionary world is revealed to be Ushio's consciousness thanking her father for choosing to live.
