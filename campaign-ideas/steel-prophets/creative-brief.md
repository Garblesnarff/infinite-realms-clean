# Steel Prophets - Creative Brief

## Art Style
**Dark techno-fantasy**: Gritty present with chrome assassins from dystopian future. Heavy shadows, neon accents, industrial settings.

**Terminator Design:** Chrome endoskeletons, glowing red eyes, liquid metal morphing, damaged reveals mechanical interior

**Future War:** Ruins and ash, plasma fire, desperate bunkers, blue vs. red laser combat

## Color Palettes
- **Present Day:** Natural colors with chrome reflections
- **Terminators:** Silver metal, glowing red eyes/cores
- **Future War:** Desaturated ruins, neon weapon fire
- **Hope Moments:** Warm golds breaking through darkness

## Music
**Main Theme:** Industrial percussion with ominous electronics, building tension

**Terminator Theme:** Mechanical rhythm, relentless and unstoppable, minor key menace

**Future War:** Desperate orchestral with electronic warfare sounds

**Hope Theme:** Human voice choir over determined drums

## Voice Direction

**T-800:** Emotionless, mechanical precision, Austrian accent homage, learning inflection slowly

**Marcus (Allied Terminator):** Starts mechanical, gains humanity, internal conflict audible

**Sarah:** Strong determination, fear underneath, grows into leadership

**Magnus:** Enthusiastic scientist, naive optimism, gradual realization of danger

**Jaina:** Battle-hardened but compassionate, scarred by future

## Cinematic Moments

- **Terminator Revelation:** Skin burns away revealing chrome skeleton
- **Time Displacement:** Naked arrival in lightning sphere
- **Chase Sequences:** Relentless pursuit through city
- **Endoskeleton Rising:** "Killed" Terminator stands up
- **Foundry Battle:** Molten metal and industrial warfare
- **Future Vision:** Apocalyptic revelation of what's coming
- **The Stand:** Party protects innocent against impossible odds
