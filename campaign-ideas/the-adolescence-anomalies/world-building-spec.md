**The Adolescence Anomalies**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In a coastal city, teenagers' intense emotional issues manifest as supernatural "Adolescence Syndrome." A cynical artificer helps various people solve their syndrome manifestations, falling in love with an actress who's literally fading from perception. A modern supernatural romance about overcoming the gap between how we're perceived and who we truly are.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail Adolescence Syndrome as a supernatural phenomenon rooted in quantum mechanics and observation theory.
    *   Write about famous historical cases of the syndrome.
    *   Describe the coastal city of Fujisawa and its significance.
    *   Explain the social pressures of modern adolescence that trigger manifestations.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **The Academy:** Normal students unaware of the supernatural around them.
*   **Syndrome Experiencers:** Those affected by and investigating the phenomenon.
*   **The Entertainment Industry:** Mai's world, representing fame's pressure.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:**
    *   **Mai Sakurajima:** Famous actress experiencing invisibility, mature and sarcastic.
    *   **Sakuta Azusagawa:** Cynical artificer, approaches supernatural rationally.
    *   **Tomoe Koga:** Girl in time loop, avoids difficult choices.
    *   **Rio Futaba:** Scientist studying syndromes, splits into two.
    *   **Nodoka Toyohama:** Mai's sister, swaps bodies due to inferiority.
    *   **Kaede Azusagawa:** Sakuta's sister, recovering from severe syndrome.

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **The Academy Library:** Where Mai first appears in bunny costume, visible only to Sakuta.
*   **The Beach:** Fujisawa coast, where emotional conversations and resolutions occur.
*   **Sakuta's Apartment:** Shows his caring for Kaede, his responsible side.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **Triggers:**
    *   **IF:** Sakuta identifies the emotional root of a syndrome manifestation and helps resolve it.
    *   **THEN:** The syndrome ends. The person returns to normal. They become an ally. Sakuta and Mai's relationship deepens.
    *   **IF:** Mai's perception count drops below a threshold.
    *   **THEN:** She begins to fade physically. Urgency increases. Sakuta must act.
    *   **IF:** In the final arc, Mai is erased from existence to save Sakuta.
    *   **THEN:** Everyone forgets her. Use meta-techniques: players feel something is missing. Sakuta must gather evidence and use logic to remember her against supernatural erasure.
    *   **IF:** Sakuta successfully proves Mai's existence through love and evidence.
    *   **THEN:** She returns to everyone's perception. Their love has transcended supernatural erasure. The world acknowledges their bond. Generate epilogue of their future together.
