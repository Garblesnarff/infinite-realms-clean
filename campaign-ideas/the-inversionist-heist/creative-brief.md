# The Inversionist Heist - Creative Brief

## Art Style
Sleek, modern, espionage aesthetic. Forward footage normal, backward footage runs in reverse. Split-screen showing both temporal directions.

## Colors
- **Forward Time:** Natural colors, warm tones
- **Inverted Time:** Cool blues, slight desaturation
- **Turnstile:** Red/orange, liminal space
- **Temporal Clash:** Both palettes colliding

## Music
**Main Theme:** Orchestra building tension, can be played forward or backward

**Inversion Effect:** Music runs backward, sounds otherworldly

**Heist Theme:** Rhythmic precision, clockwork coordination

**Combat Theme:** Dual tracks, forward and backward simultaneously

## Voice Direction
**Inverted Speech:** Played backward, requires translation

**The Protagonist:** Calm, analytical, masterful

**The Antagonist:** Cultured villain, enjoys the game

**Neil:** Mysterious knowledge, sad undertones

## Cinematic Moments
- **First Inversion:** World running backward, disorientation
- **Temporal Pincer Visual:** Split screen showing both teams
- **Inverted Combat:** Spectacular reversed action
- **The Turnstile:** Transition between temporal directions
- **Final Heist:** Three teams, multiple timelines, perfect coordination
