# World-Building Specification: The Eternal Shores of Tír na nÓg

**1. Core Concept:** Players are caught in the Celtic Otherworld where time stands still and beauty is eternal. They must resist paradise's seduction, solve why they were brought, and escape before Fading completely or while time destroys their mortal world.

**2. Lore Primer:**
*   Celtic concepts of the Otherworld (Tír na nÓg, Mag Mell, Tech Duinn)
*   Time dilation between worlds
*   The Tuath Dé (gods of Ireland) and their nature
*   Fae/sidhe characteristics and rules
*   The story of Oisin and Niamh
*   Samhain and Beltane as boundary times
*   Sidhe mounds as doorways
*   Geis (sacred obligations) and their power

**3. Factions:**
*   **The Shining Court** - Manannan's beautiful court, seductive paradise-keepers.
*   **The Fae Folk** - Various sidhe with alien morality, needing mortal emotion.
*   **The Trapped Mortals** - Humans who stayed too long, warnings and allies.
*   **The Dark Otherworld** - Tech Duinn's shadows, those who didn't fit paradise.
*   **The Mortal World** - Distant, aging, calling players home.

**4. NPCs:**
*   **Tier 1:** Manannan mac Lir, Niamh, The Dagda, The Morrigan, Oisin.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** Various fae nobles, trapped mortals in different stages of Fading, spirits of nature.

**5. Locations:** Blessed Shores, Crystal Palace, Eternal Orchard, Mist Between Worlds, Tech Duinn, Sidhe Mounds.

**6. Mechanics:**
*   Time dilation tracking
*   Fading Humanity stat
*   Otherworld food binding
*   Time-specific escape windows
*   Fae bargain system
*   Memory Anchors

**7. Themes:** Paradise as trap, timelessness vs. growth, immortality's costs, what makes us human, the value of mortality, contentment vs. striving, memory and identity.
