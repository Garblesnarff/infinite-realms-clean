**Guilty Pleasures**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** In St. Louis, a necromancer serves as legal vampire executioner while their power escalates exponentially. They navigate vampire kisses, werewolf packs, and necromantic cults while being targeted by master vampires who seek to bind them through marks. As they grow powerful enough to control even master vampires, the Mother of All Darkness seeks to possess them. Power always has a price in blood and freedom.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the legal status of preternatural beings—vampires are citizens, executioners are licensed killers.
    *   Write the history of the Vampire Council and their rule over all vampirekind.
    *   Describe the Mother of All Darkness—first vampire, source of all vampire powers, ancient goddess.
    *   Explain vampire marks—four stages that progressively bind human to vampire.
    *   Detail lycanthropy as disease—different strains for different animals, pack structures.
    *   Describe necromancy's evolution from raising zombies to controlling vampires and death itself.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **St. Louis Vampire Kiss** (Major): Jean-Claude's court, master vampires and their servants.
    *   **The Vampire Council** (Major): Ancient vampires who govern all vampires, deadly and political.
    *   **Werewolf Pack (Lukoi)** (Major): St. Louis shapeshifter pack, hierarchical and violent.
    *   **Animators Inc.** (Minor): Legal necromancy business raising dead for clients.
    *   **The Harlequin** (Minor): Vampire secret police serving the Mother of All Darkness.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Jean-Claude (Master), Richard (Ulfric), Edward (assassin), Marmee Noir (Mother), Asher (lieutenant).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Master Vampire" (various powers), "Werewolf" (pack hierarchy), "Necromancer" (animator), "Vampire Servant" (marked human).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Guilty Pleasures:** Vampire strip club, neutral ground, meeting place.
*   **Circus of the Damned:** Underground vampire headquarters, gothic carnival.
*   **The Lunatic Cafe:** Shapeshifter restaurant and territory.
*   **Animator Inc.:** Business where legal necromancy occurs.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Vampire places mark on human → **THEN:** Binding progresses through four stages, each granting power but costing freedom.
*   **IF:** Necromancer's power reaches critical mass → **THEN:** Can control vampires, raise armies, attract cosmic threats.
*   **IF:** Triumvirate forms → **THEN:** Three beings share power, amplifying all abilities but binding fates.
*   **IF:** Ardeur manifests → **THEN:** Must feed on life energy or risk killing innocents.
*   **IF:** Mother of All Darkness possesses host → **THEN:** Ancient vampire goddess walks again, apocalyptic threat.
