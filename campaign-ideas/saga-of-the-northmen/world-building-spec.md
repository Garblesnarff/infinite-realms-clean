### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**Saga of the Northmen**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** Warriors in Ragnar Lothbrok's warband evolve from raiders to conquerors as they invade England. Through shield-wall battles, political intrigue, and favor of Norse gods, they forge their own saga and decide whether they fight for gold, glory, land, or their place in Valhalla.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail Viking Age Scandinavia—fjords, harsh climate, raiding culture.
    *   Write history of early raids on England and why they began.
    *   Describe Norse religion—Odin, Thor, Freya, Valhalla, Ragnarok.
    *   Explain Saxon kingdoms of England—Northumbria, Mercia, Wessex, East Anglia.
    *   Chronicle Ragnar Lothbrok's legend—historical vs mythic.
    *   Detail Great Heathen Army's invasion and conquest.
    *   Describe Viking society—jarls, karls, thralls, shield-maidens, Thing assembly.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions:** Ragnar's Warband (Major), Great Heathen Army (Major), Saxon Kingdoms (Major), Ragnar's Sons (Major), Christian Church (Minor), Rival Jarls (Minor)

**4. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party remains pagan → **THEN:** Norse gods aid, but Christian kingdoms refuse alliance.
*   **IF:** Ragnar executed → **THEN:** Revenge invasion unites Vikings.
*   **IF:** Party conquers land → **THEN:** Must defend settlements, culture clash with Saxons.
*   **IF:** Party supports specific son → **THEN:** Shapes Viking political structure.
*   **IF:** Alfred unites Saxons → **THEN:** Viking expansion halted, Danelaw established.
