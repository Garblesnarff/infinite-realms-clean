**The Lightning Runner**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** A timid errand-runner becomes secret identity "Eyeshield 21"—a masked lightning-fast running back for the newly formed Deimon Devil Bats football team. Wearing a distinctive eyeshield to hide their identity, they possess supernatural speed but must learn American football's complexity. The team starts with three members and must recruit and train from scratch while facing established powerhouses. Their journey from absolute novices to championship contenders proves strategy and heart overcome raw power.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail American football's introduction to Japan and its growing popularity in high schools.
    *   Write the legend of the "real" Eyeshield 21—Notre Dame's secret player the mask originally honored.
    *   Describe the Christmas Bowl—Japan's high school football championship tournament.
    *   Explain football positions, formations, and basic strategic concepts.
    *   Detail the recruiting process—finding hidden talent in unexpected places.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **Deimon Devil Bats** (Major): Underdog team built from misfits, relies on speed and strategy.
    *   **Ojo White Knights** (Major): Elite team with perfect execution and genius quarterback.
    *   **Shinryuji Naga** (Major): Undefeated champions with overwhelming talent at every position.
    *   **Seibu Wild Gunmen** (Minor): Western-themed team with impenetrable defense.
    *   **Various Regional Teams** (Minor): Schools with established programs representing different challenges.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Yoichi Hiruma (devil strategist), Ryokan Kurita (gentle giant), Seijuro Shin (perfect player), Agon Kongo (natural genius).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Quarterback" (field generals), "Running Back" (ball carriers), "Linebacker" (defensive leaders), "Wide Receiver" (pass catchers), "Coach" (tactical minds).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Deimon High School:** Underdog school with worn practice field, minimal equipment, scrappy atmosphere.
*   **Practice Field:** Where Devil Bats train—lacks proper facilities but full of determination.
*   **Tokyo Stadium:** Major venue for playoff and championship games.
*   **Christmas Bowl Arena:** National championship stadium in Tokyo, represents the ultimate dream.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Eyeshield 21's identity is discovered → **THEN:** Opponents can specifically counter their plays, school life becomes complicated.
*   **IF:** Team successfully recruits specialized player → **THEN:** New tactical options unlock, can counter specific opponent strengths.
*   **IF:** Hiruma's trick play succeeds → **THEN:** Opponents become wary, must vary strategies, momentum shifts.
*   **IF:** Protagonist develops beyond pure speed → **THEN:** Become complete player, harder to counter, can contribute in multiple ways.
*   **IF:** Reach Christmas Bowl → **THEN:** Face absolute elite, national recognition, prove rookies can achieve impossible.
