# Empire of Endless Courses - Creative Brief

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Modern restaurant drama meets The Bear and Chef's Table, fast-paced business tension
- **Art Style**: Sleek restaurant interiors, bustling kitchen chaos, elegant fine dining
- **Tone**: Aspirational success story with cutthroat business undertones

## Color Palettes
- Starting Rusty Spoon: Worn browns, tarnished metals, humble
- Success Phase: Golds, polished surfaces, luxury
- Rival Empires: Each has signature color scheme
- Guild: Imperial gold and crimson, oppressive wealth
- Finale: Blending all colors showing empire scope

## Music
- Main Theme: Building orchestral, success and ambition
- Service Rush: Fast-paced kitchen chaos, coordinated panic
- Business Meetings: Tense negotiation music
- Inspection: Nerve-wracking suspense
- Victory: Triumphant fanfare when earning Plates
- Rivalry: Competing themes clashing and resolving

## Voice Direction
- **Margaux DuBois**: Cold corporate CEO, Meryl Streep in Devil Wears Prada
- **Chef Giovanni**: Passionate Italian chef, explosive temper, Gordon Ramsay energy
- **Helena Coinsworth**: Practical accountant, voice of reason, sensible
- **Inspector Thyme**: Impartial critic, Anton Ego gravitas
- **Saffiya**: Revolutionary energy, passionate about food access

## Cinematic Moments
- First successful service: Kitchen ballet
- Earning first Plate: Ceremony with stakes
- Hostile takeover attempt: Corporate thriller
- Empire montage: Multiple locations operating simultaneously
- Final inspection: Everything on the line
- Legacy epilogue: Empire continuing after party
