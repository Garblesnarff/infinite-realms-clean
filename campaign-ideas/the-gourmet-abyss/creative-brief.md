# The Gourmet Abyss - Creative Brief

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Delicious in Dungeon meets Made in Abyss, beautiful dungeon horror with appetizing food
- **Art Style**: Detailed monster anatomy showing edible parts, contrast between grotesque creatures and perfect dishes
- **Tone**: Wonder and horror in balance, adventure with existential dread undertones

## Color Palettes
- Upper Floors (1-10): Stone grays, torch oranges, familiar dungeon
- Twilight Zone (11-25): Bioluminescent blues/greens, alien beauty
- Abyssal Depths (26-40): Flesh reds, disturbing organic tones
- Impossible Deep (41-50): Colors that shouldn't exist, reality-breaking
- Food: Always appetizing despite monstrous source

## Music
- Exploration: Wonder mixed with tension
- Combat/Hunting: Fast-paced with cooking anticipation
- Cooking Scenes: Warm, domestic, comforting despite setting
- Descent: Music gets stranger with each floor
- Floor 50: Grand, terrible, transcendent
- Transformation Theme: Body horror beautiful

## Voice Direction
- **Chef Delvin**: Survivalist veteran, Bear Grylls meets Gordon Ramsay
- **Marcotte Twins**: Enthusiastic rivals, competitive but friendly
- **Fungalmancer Mykol**: Alien intelligence, David Attenborough observing humans
- **Mad Chef Ramsay**: Descent into madness, calculating to raving
- **Gourmet King**: Ancient exhaustion, wants death but can't die

## Cinematic Moments
- First monster kill and cooking scene
- Transformation sequence showing changes
- Descending to new floor, reveal shots
- Feast scenes with impossible ingredients
- Final choice at the Feast of Ages
- Return to surface changed forever
