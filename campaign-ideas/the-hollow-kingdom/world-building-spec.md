**The Hollow Kingdom**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party are wandering bugs arriving in Hallownest, a once-great insect kingdom fallen to the orange Infection plague. They discover the Pale King sealed the source (the Radiance, a forgotten moth god) inside the Hollow Knight vessel, but the seal is failing. The party must explore the depths, fight through infected hordes, and confront the Radiance in the Dream Realm.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the age of Radiance worship—when moths revered the light goddess.
    *   Write the Pale King's arrival and how he made bugs sapient, causing them to forget the Radiance.
    *   Describe the Radiance's revenge—the Infection plague driving bugs mad.
    *   Explain the Pale King's solution—create mindless vessels to contain the Radiance.
    *   Detail the sealing and the Hollow Knight's imprisonment.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Pale Court** (extinct): Pale King's government, now dead.
    *   **The Infected** (major): Orange-eyed bugs driven to rage.
    *   **Deepnest Spiders** (minor): Independent horrors in the depths.
    *   **The Dreamers** (minor): Three bugs maintaining the seal with their sleep.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Hornet, The Hollow Knight, The Radiance, The Pale King (dead).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Infected Bug," "Deepnest Spider," "Dream Warrior," "Failed Vessel."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Dirtmouth:** Surface town, safe hub.
*   **Forgotten Crossroads:** Starting area that becomes infected.
*   **City of Tears:** Flooded capital with rain.
*   **Deepnest:** Spider-infested nightmare tunnels.
*   **The Abyss:** Void sea where vessels were born.
*   **Temple of the Black Egg:** Hollow Knight's prison.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party kills Dreamers → **THEN:** Black Egg opens.
*   **IF:** Party enters Black Egg → **THEN:** Must choose ending.
*   **IF:** Forgotten Crossroads reached → **THEN:** Becomes infected later.
*   **IF:** Dream Nail acquired → **THEN:** Can access true ending path.
