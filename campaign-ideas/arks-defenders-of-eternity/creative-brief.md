# Creative Brief: ARKS: Defenders of Eternity

This document outlines the core creative direction for the assets of this campaign, ensuring a consistent and fitting tone across all visuals, audio, and more.

## Art Style: Sci-Fi Anime (Phantasy Star-inspired)

Inspired by Phantasy Star Online 2's vibrant anime aesthetic—a style blending colorful sci-fi, anime character designs, flashy combat effects, and cosmic horror elements. This style captures both the hope of heroic defenders and the existential dread of facing primordial darkness.

### Core Principles for Prompts:

*   **Anime Energy:** Characters and effects should be vibrant, dynamic, and stylized.
*   **Photon Brilliance:** Light-based powers should be spectacular and colorful.
*   **Parallel World Variety:** Show diverse settings from fantasy to sci-fi.

---

### Detailed Style Guide for Image Generation:

**1. Overall Vibe & Mood:**
*   **Keywords:** Anime sci-fi, photon powers, parallel worlds, cosmic horror, hopeful-yet-desperate.
*   **Feeling:** The excitement of anime action combat mixed with the existential weight of fighting universal darkness.

**2. Line Work & Form:**
*   **Style:** Clean anime-style lines with dynamic action poses.
*   **Forms:** Sleek sci-fi armor, flashy weapons, energy effects everywhere.
*   **Prompt Example:** "An ARKS Hunter in anime style, wearing sleek futuristic armor with glowing photon accents, wielding a massive sword that trails light energy."

**3. Color Palette:**
*   **Style:** Vibrant, high-saturation colors for heroes; dark purples and blacks for Darkers.
*   **Photon:** Bright blues, whites, golds, rainbow energy effects.
*   **Darkers:** Deep purples, blacks, corrupting red-purple energy.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...a color palette of vibrant blues and whites for photon energy effects, contrasting against deep purple-black Darker corruption."

**4. Character Design:**
*   **Style:** Anime character designs with diverse aesthetics—humans, androids, newman (space elves), deuman (demon-people).
*   **Classes:** Distinct silhouettes—Hunters in armor, Rangers in tactical gear, Forces in robes.
*   **Customization:** Highly varied—from realistic to fantastical, showing player creativity.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A female Force class character in anime style, with newman elf ears, wearing flowing futuristic robes with glowing circuits, casting colorful photon magic."

**5. Backgrounds & Environments:**
*   **Style:** Diverse parallel worlds—fantasy forests, futuristic cities, volcanic wastelands, corrupted darker nests.
*   **Oracle Fleet:** Massive white spacecraft interiors with holographic interfaces.
*   **Darker Realms:** Twisted, corrupted versions of normal spaces with purple-black corruption.
*   **Prompt Example:** "The Oracle Fleet interior, clean white sci-fi architecture with glowing blue interfaces, bustling with anime-style ARKS operatives of various races."

**6. Shading & Lighting:**
*   **Style:** Anime cel-shading with dramatic light effects.
*   **Photon Effects:** Brilliant, almost overwhelming light-based attacks and abilities.
*   **Darker Corruption:** Ominous purple-black shadows and energy.
*   **Prompt Example:** "...dramatically lit by brilliant white photon energy explosions, with ominous purple Darker corruption energy creating contrast."

**7. Composition:**
*   **Style:** Dynamic anime action compositions—mid-combat poses, energy effects, team formations.
*   **Techniques:** Use speed lines, energy trails, dramatic angles. Show team coordination.
*   **Prompt Example:** "A dynamic action shot of an ARKS team—Hunter, Ranger, Force—in mid-combat against Darkers, with photon energy effects and speed lines emphasizing movement."

## Voice

An energetic, heroic voice with anime enthusiasm balanced by occasional seriousness. Slight Japanese accent reflecting the game's origins. The tone shifts from upbeat action to contemplative when discussing cosmic horror themes. The emotion ranges from triumphant to melancholic. Audio quality is crisp with sci-fi processing for comms. Role: An ARKS veteran narrating missions, or the Oracle Fleet's AI announcer.
