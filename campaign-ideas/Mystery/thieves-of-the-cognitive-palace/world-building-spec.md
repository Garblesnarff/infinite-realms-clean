**Thieves of the Cognitive Palace**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party discovers the Metaverse, a supernatural realm where corrupt adults' twisted desires manifest as Palaces. As the Phantom Thieves, they infiltrate these cognitive dungeons to steal crystallized hearts (Treasures), forcing villains to confess in reality. Balancing school life, social relationships, and meticulously planned heists, they must unmask a conspiracy before time runs out on their deadlines.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Metaverse—the collective unconscious made manifest, where cognition becomes reality.
    *   Write the history of Igor and the Velvet Room across different timelines and their role in rehabilitation.
    *   Describe how Personas are awakened through acts of rebellion and the tearing of metaphorical masks.
    *   Explain the Conspiracy's plan to use cognitive psience to control society and eliminate free will.
    *   Detail Yaldabaoth's birth from humanity's desire to be controlled and avoid responsibility.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Phantom Thieves** (Major): Vigilante group stealing hearts to reform corrupt adults, operates from the shadows.
    *   **The Conspiracy** (Major): Shadow government using the Metaverse to create psychotic breakdowns and control society.
    *   **Police Investigation** (Major): Law enforcement hunting the Phantom Thieves, some corrupt, some genuine.
    *   **The Public** (Minor): Society's opinion shifts between supporting and fearing the Thieves, ultimately crucial to defeating god.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Morgana (guide), Velvet Room Attendants (fate wardens), The Detective (pursuer-turned-ally), Conspiracy Leader (false messiah), Yaldabaoth (god of control).
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Confidant" (social link NPCs granting abilities), "Palace Ruler" (corrupt adults with distorted desires), "Shadow" (cognitive enemies), "Velvet Room Resident" (Persona fusion masters).

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **Shibuya:** Urban hub with shops, arcades, cafés, social activities, bustling crowds.
*   **School:** Daily life setting, social links, exams, part-time jobs, maintaining cover.
*   **The Velvet Room:** A prison cell between dream and reality where Personas are fused and guidance is given.
*   **Palaces:** Unique cognitive dungeons for each target (castle, museum, pyramid, bank, spaceport, casino, cruiser, temple).
*   **Mementos:** The collective unconscious Palace, a vast subway dungeon representing the public's distorted desires.
*   **Leblanc Café:** Party's safe house, serves curry and coffee, located in back alleys.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party steals a treasure → **THEN:** Target has a change of heart in reality and confesses their crimes publicly.
*   **IF:** Party maxes a confidant social link → **THEN:** They unlock ultimate abilities (survive fatal damage, switch party members, etc.).
*   **IF:** Party misses the deadline → **THEN:** The target acts on their plans, resulting in tragedy and potential game over.
*   **IF:** Party raises their stats (knowledge, charm, guts, etc.) → **THEN:** New confidants and dialogue options unlock.
*   **IF:** Security level reaches 100% in a Palace → **THEN:** The party is forcibly ejected and loses a day of time.
*   **IF:** The public's support for the Phantom Thieves grows → **THEN:** Their power increases, culminating in ability to fight gods.
