**The White Wolf's Hunt**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The party are Witchers—mutant monster slayers—hunting for Ciri, the Child of Elder Blood whose prophesied power can save or doom the world. The Wild Hunt—spectral riders from another dimension—also pursues her. Through morally complex contracts, political intrigue, and impossible choices between lesser evils, they must find Ciri before the Hunt and decide whether to let her fulfill her destiny or protect her from it.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   **Prompts:**
    *   Detail the Conjunction of the Spheres—when monsters entered the world from other dimensions.
    *   Write the history of the first Witchers—their creation through brutal mutations and training.
    *   Describe the decline of Witchers as monsters become rare and humans fear mutants.
    *   Explain Ciri's bloodline—the Elder Blood grants dimension-crossing powers.
    *   Detail the War between Nilfgaard and the Northern Kingdoms and its impact.

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Factions Roster:**
    *   **The Witchers** (Minor): Monster hunters enhanced by mutations, dying profession,各school (Wolf, Cat, Bear, Griffin).
    *   **Nilfgaard Empire** (Major): Southern empire conquering the north, disciplined military, Emperor Emhyr.
    *   **Northern Kingdoms** (Major): Alliance of human kingdoms resisting Nilfgaard (Temeria, Redania, Kaedwen).
    *   **The Lodge of Sorceresses** (Major): Cabal of powerful mages manipulating politics.
    *   **The Wild Hunt** (Major): Spectral elven riders from dying world seeking Ciri's power.
    *   **Church of the Eternal Fire** (Minor): Religious zealots burning mages and non-humans.

**4. NPC Generation Roster**
*   **Tier 1:** Cirilla (Child of Elder Blood), Yennefer (Sorceress), Eredin (Wild Hunt King), Emperor Emhyr, The Bloody Baron.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** "Monster" (various types from bestiary), "Witch Hunter" (zealots), "Wild Hunt Rider" (spectral cavalry), "Sorceress" (Lodge member), "Nilfgaardian Soldier."

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   **White Orchard:** Tutorial village with inn, smith, herbalist. Griffin nest in nearby ruins.
*   **Velen/No Man's Land:** Swampy war zone with Bloody Baron's fortress, Crookback Bog, scattered villages.
*   **Novigrad:** Massive free city with districts: Temple Quarter, merchant areas, docks, underground.
*   **Skellige:** Island archipelago with Ard Skellig (main island), jarls' keeps, druid groves, monster caves.
*   **Kaer Morhen:** Mountain Witcher fortress with courtyard, keep, lab, walls, training grounds.

**6. Causality Chains & Dynamic World States**
*   **IF:** Party helps the Bloody Baron redeem himself → **THEN:** He survives and aids in final battle.
*   **IF:** Party is harsh/dismissive to Ciri → **THEN:** She loses confidence, doesn't survive White Frost.
*   **IF:** Party trusts Ciri's choices → **THEN:** She becomes a Witcher (good ending).
*   **IF:** Party brings Ciri to Emperor Emhyr → **THEN:** She becomes Empress (bittersweet ending).
*   **IF:** Party recruits all allies → **THEN:** More survive the Battle of Kaer Morhen.
*   **IF:** Party kills both Crones early → **THEN:** Velen remains cursed, Baron's family dies.
