### **World-Building Specification Brief**

**Winter's Ambassadors**

**1. Core Concept & Narrative Hook**
*   **Content:** The players are Ekumen envoys to Winter, a frozen world of ambisexual humans who shift gender monthly. Navigating political intrigue between monarchist Karhide and totalitarian Orgoreyn, they must earn trust and survive an epic ice journey while confronting assumptions about gender, culture, and humanity.

**2. Lore & History Primer**
*   Detail the Ekumen's mission of peaceful first contact across scattered human worlds.
*   Explain Gethenian biology—ambisexual humans with monthly kemmer cycles.
*   Describe Winter's cultures—Karhide (honor-based feudalism), Orgoreyn (totalitarian bureaucracy).

**3. Faction Deep-Dive**
*   **Karhide** (feudal monarchy), **Orgoreyn** (totalitarian state), **The Ekumen** (peaceful confederation).

**4. NPC Roster**
*   Estraven (loyal exile), King Argaven (paranoid monarch), Orgota conspirators.

**5. Location Blueprints**
*   Karhide (stone cities, hearth culture), Orgoreyn (surveillance state), Gobrin Ice (deadly glacier).

**6. Causality Chains**
*   **IF** party understands Shifgrethor **THEN** gain Karhide allies.
*   **IF** party survives ice journey **THEN** earn Estraven's ultimate trust.
*   **IF** Estraven dies **THEN** bittersweet victory—contact established but at tragic cost.
