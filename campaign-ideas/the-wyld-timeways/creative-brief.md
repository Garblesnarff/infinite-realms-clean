# The Wyld Timeways - Creative Brief

## Art Style
Bright, colorful, comic book energy. Highly anachronistic mixing of eras. 80s/90s aesthetic meets fantasy. Everything is slightly exaggerated and fun.

## Colors
- **Primary Palette:** Bright, vibrant, no muted tones
- **Time Booth:** Electric blue, neon pink, chrome
- **Each Era:** Exaggerated historical color schemes
- **Future:** Gleaming chrome and rainbow neon
- **Overall:** Maximum visual energy

## Music
**Main Theme:** Power ballad rock anthem, guitar-heavy, uplifting

**Era Themes:**
- Ancient Greece: Lyre meets electric guitar
- Medieval: Lute and drums meet rock
- Wild West: Banjo and harmonica rock fusion
- Future: Synthesizer paradise

**Battle Music:** Hair metal intensity

**Emotional Moments:** Power ballad sincerity

**Final Concert:** All eras' music combined, glorious chaos

## Voice Direction

**Party Members:** Enthusiastic, genuine, excited about everything

**Rufus:** Chill surfer-philosopher, wise but casual

**Historical Figures:** Distinctive but endearing, slightly modern despite era

**Socrates:** Curious wonder, questions everything

**Joan:** Fierce determination, mall-loving warrior

**Death:** Surprisingly friendly skeleton, good sport

**Malus:** Comic villain energy, redeemable sad underneath

## Cinematic Moments

**Time Booth Arrival:** Landing in different eras with style, possibly crashes

**Historical Recruitment:** Montage of gathering friends

**Mall Sequence:** Historical figures experiencing modernity

**Death's Games:** Various gaming challenges, comedic

**Robot Duplicates:** Evil party vs. real party

**The Time Pool:** Meeting all versions of selves

**Final Concert:** Epic performance across all time

**Group Photo:** Entire cast in freeze-frame ending

## Set Pieces

**Ancient Greek Forum:** Philosophical debates become argument for time travel

**Medieval Tournament:** Anachronistic jousting with modern commentary

**Wild West Showdown:** Six-shooter versus time booth

**Afterlife Game Room:** Various challenges against Death

**Future Concert Hall:** Massive venue for final performance

**The Time Vortex:** Swirling colors, seeing all eras at once

## Costume Design

**Party:** Mix of eras they've visited, increasingly eclectic

**Historical Figures in Present:** Hilariously contemporary versions of historical garb

**Future People:** Comfortable future fashion, lots of chrome

**Death:** Classic skeleton in robe, maybe sunglasses

**Time Booth:** Interior is bigger than exterior, psychedelic

## Tone and Atmosphere

**Optimistic:** Everything can be fixed with friendship

**Humorous:** Jokes matter more than realism

**Energetic:** Keep action and comedy flowing

**Sincere:** Earnest emotion underneath comedy

**Inclusive:** Everyone can join the party

**Educational:** Learn history through fun

**Musical:** Songs enhance everything

**Excellent:** Above all, be excellent
